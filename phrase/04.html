English Expressions with "Make"

  1. Make a decision - 做出决定
    例句：You need to make a decision about whether to accept the job offer.
  2. Make a difference - 产生影响
    例句：Volunteering can make a difference in the community.
  3. Make an effort - 做出努力
    例句：She made an effort to arrive on time for the meeting.
  4. Make friends - 结交朋友
    例句：He quickly made friends with his classmates.
  5. Make a living - 谋生
    例句：Many artists struggle to make a living from their work.
  6. Make a mistake - 犯错误
    例句：Even the best of us can make a mistake occasionally.
  7. Make a point - 阐明观点
    例句：He made a point that we should consider the environmental impact.
  8. Make a reservation - 预订
    例句：We need to make a reservation at the restaurant for tonight.
  9. Make a promise - 许诺
    例句：She made a promise to help her friend with the project.
  10. Make a suggestion - 提建议
    例句：I'd like to make a suggestion for improving the process.
  11. Make an appointment - 预约
    例句：Please make an appointment with the doctor for next week.
  12. Make the most of - 充分利用
    例句：You should make the most of your time while studying abroad.
  13. Make up (for something) - 补偿
    例句：He made up for being late by bringing donuts for everyone.
  14. Make up one's mind - 决定
    例句：She hasn't made up her mind about which dress to wear.
  15. Make it - 成功；按时到达
    例句：I'm not sure if I can make it to the party on time.

这些固定搭配在日常生活中非常实用，可以帮助你更自然、准确地表达自己的想法。记住这些搭配，并在适当的情境中使用它们，可以提高你的英语沟通能力。

移动或行进

"Go" 最基本的含义是指从一个地方移动到另一个地方。

  • 例句： I need to go to the store to buy some milk.（我需要去商店买些牛奶。）

去往特定的目的地

"Go" 可以用来指代去往一个特定的地点或目的地。

  • 例句： We are going to the beach this weekend.（我们这个周末要去海滩。）

变得或变成

"Go" 可以用来描述事物的状态变化或转变。

  • 例句： The milk went bad after being left out of the fridge.（牛奶放在冰箱外变质了。）

开始或进行

"Go" 可以用来表示开始或进行某个活动或过程。

  • 例句： Let's go for a walk in the park.（让我们去公园散步。）

流逝或经过

"Go" 可以用来描述时间的流逝或经过。

  • 例句： How long does it go from morning to evening?（从早上到晚上要经过多长时间？）

说或发出声音

"Go" 有时也用来描述说话或发出声音的行为。

  • 例句： The baby goes "coo" when he's happy.（婴儿开心时会发出“咕咕”声。）

参与或出席

"Go" 可以用来表示参与某个事件或出席某个场合。

  • 例句： Are you going to the party tonight?（你今晚去参加派对吗？）

有效的或可行的

"Go" 还可以用来形容某物是有效的、适用的或可行的。

  • 例句： This ticket will go for both events.（这张票适用于两个活动。）
Common Phrasal Verbs with "Go" in English

Common Phrasal Verbs with "Go" in English

  1. Go ahead - 继续进行，去做吧
    例句：You can go ahead and start the meeting without me.
  2. Go after - 追求，追逐
    例句：She went after her dream job in the big city.
  3. Go against - 违反，违背
    例句：Going against the rules could lead to serious consequences.
  4. Go around - 四处走动，绕过
    例句：The rumor went around the office quickly.
  5. Go away - 离开，走开
    例句：I told him to go away and never come back.
  6. Go back - 返回，回想
    例句：I need to go back to the store to return this shirt.
  7. Go beyond - 超出，超越
    例句：Her kindness went beyond what anyone expected.
  8. Go by - 经过，流逝
    例句：Time goes by so quickly when you're having fun.
  9. Go down - 下降，被记载
    例句：The sun goes down in the west.
  10. Go for - 选择，决定做
    例句：I'm going to go for a run after work.
  11. Go in for - 参与，喜欢
    例句：She goes in for outdoor sports like hiking and cycling.
  12. Go off - 爆炸，响起
    例句：The alarm went off at 6:00 AM.
  13. Go on - 继续
    例句：Please go on with your story.
  14. Go out - 出去，熄灭
    例句：We're going out for dinner tonight.
  15. Go over - 复习，仔细检查
    例句：We need to go over the plan one more time.
  16. Go through - 经历，通过
    例句：They went through a lot of challenges before starting their business.
  17. Go under - 沉没，失败
    例句：The ship went under during the storm.
  18. Go up - 上升，增长
    例句：Prices are going up next month.
  19. Go with - 伴随，选择
    例句：I'll go with you to the party.
  20. Go without - 没有，缺少
    例句：We had to go without electricity for two days.

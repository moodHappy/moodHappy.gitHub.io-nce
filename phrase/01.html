A Helping Hand

援助之手

Bob: Hey Alice, could you spare me a bit of sugar?

嘿，爱丽丝，你能借我点糖吗？

Alice: Sure, here you go. Just a bit though, I'm running low myself.

当然，给你。但只能借你一点，我自己也快没了。

Bob: Thanks! I'll return it later.

谢谢！我会还给你的。

Alice: No problem. By the way, could I borrow a copy of that book you mentioned?

没问题。顺便问一下，你能借我那本你提到的书吗？

Bob: Of course, I have a copy of it at home. I'll bring it tomorrow.

当然，我家有一本。我明天带过来。

Alice: Great, thanks! Oh, and do you have a bottle of water? I forgot mine today.

太好了，谢谢！哦，你有一瓶水吗？我今天忘带了。

Bob: Yeah, here's a bottle of water. Just make sure to return it.

有的，这是一瓶水。记得还给我。

Alice: Thanks, you're a lifesaver!

谢谢，你真是个救星！

Bob: No worries, glad to help.

别客气，帮助你很高兴。

under the weather 身体不适

1. "I'm feeling under the weather today."（我今天感觉身体不舒服。）

burning the midnight oil 熬夜学习或工作

2. "I'm burning the midnight oil to finish this project."（我正在加班加点完成这个项目。）

cloud nine 狂喜状态, 极乐的心境

3. "I'm on cloud nine after getting that promotion."（我因为晋升感到非常高兴。）

hit the sack 睡觉

4. "I need to hit the sack, I have an early start tomorrow."（我得睡觉了，明天得早起。）

grab a bite 随便吃几口垫垫肚子；吃点东西；随便吃点

5. "Let's grab a bite to eat, I'm starving."（我们去吃点东西吧，我饿坏了。）

all ears 洗耳恭听

6. "I'm all ears, tell me what's on your mind."（我洗耳恭听，说说你在想什么。）

killing time 杀戮时刻；杀时间；消磨时间

7. "I'm just killing time until the movie starts."（我只是在打发时间，等电影开始。）

over the moon 欣喜若狂；月亮之上；乐疯了

8. "I'm over the moon about my new job offer."（我因为新工作的邀约而欣喜若狂。）

in a pickle 处于困境之中；身陷困境；混乱的境遇

9. "I'm in a pickle, I accidentally locked my keys in the car."（我陷入了困境，我不小心把钥匙锁在车里了。）

on the fence 犹豫未决的；中立的

10. "I'm on the fence about which vacation spot to choose."（我对选择哪个度假地犹豫不决。）

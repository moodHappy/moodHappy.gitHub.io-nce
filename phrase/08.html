Common Phrases with "Know"

  1. Know about - 了解某事
    例句：I know about the new policy that will be implemented next month.
  2. Know of - 听说过某事
    例句：Do you know of any good restaurants in the area?
  3. Know better - 更清楚，更了解
    例句：You know better than to make such a mistake.
  4. Know for sure - 确信，有把握
    例句：We won't know for sure until the results are announced.
  5. Know how - 知道怎样做某事
    例句：She knows how to fix the computer.
  6. Know if - 知不知道某事是否会发生
    例句：I don't know if I'll be able to attend the meeting.
  7. Know of someone/something - 知道某人/某事
    例句：I know of a great mechanic who can fix your car.
  8. Know on - 对...有深入了解
    例句：She knows on the subject of economics.
  9. Know one's way around - 熟悉...的周围环境
    例句：He knows his way around the city very well.
  10. Know someone/something by heart - 非常熟悉某人/某事
    例句：She knows the entire script by heart.
  11. Know something inside out - 非常了解某事
    例句：He knows the company inside out after working there for 20 years.
  12. Know the ropes - 熟悉某项工作的技巧或程序
    例句：After a month on the job, he finally knows the ropes.
  13. Know what you're talking about - 知道你在说什么
    例句：You really know what you're talking about when it comes to cars.

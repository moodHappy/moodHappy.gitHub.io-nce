"Get" Phrasal Verbs

  1. Get up - 起床
    • Example: "I find it hard to get up early on weekends."
  2. Get to - 到达（某地）
    • Example: "It usually takes me an hour to get to work."
  3. Get along with - 与...相处融洽
    • Example: "She always gets along well with her colleagues."
  4. Get rid of - 摆脱，丢弃
    • Example: "We need to get rid of these old clothes."
  5. Get into - 进入；开始对...感兴趣
    • Example: "He's really gotten into photography lately."
  6. Get out of - 从...出来；逃避（责任等）
    • Example: "I can't wait to get out of this stuffy room."
  7. Get over - 克服；恢复（从疾病或失望中）
    • Example: "It took her weeks to get over the flu."
  8. Get ahead - 取得进步；成功
    • Example: "She works hard to get ahead in her career."
  9. Get together - 聚会；相聚
    • Example: "Let's get together for coffee sometime."
  10. Get in touch with - 与...取得联系
    • Example: "I'll get in touch with you as soon as I have the information."
  11. Get used to - 习惯于
    • Example: "It took me a while to get used to the new job."
  12. Get across - 传达（意思等）
    • Example: "I'm trying to get my point across in this presentation."
  13. Get around to - 找时间做某事
    • Example: "I've been meaning to get around to cleaning the garage."
  14. Get on with - 继续做某事；与...相处
    • Example: "Let's get on with the meeting, shall we?"
  15. Get through - 完成；通过；接通电话
    • Example: "I'm trying to get through my work before the weekend."
  16. Get to know - 了解；熟悉
    • Example: "I'm looking forward to getting to know everyone at the party."
  17. Get in shape - 健身；恢复体型
    • Example: "I've started a new workout routine to get in shape."
  18. Get away with - 逃脱惩罚
    • Example: "He tried to get away with stealing, but was caught by the security."
  19. Get down to - 开始认真对待；着手做某事
    • Example: "Let's get down to business and discuss the contract details."
  20. Get back to - 回到；重新联系
    • Example: "I'll get back to you with the answer as soon as possible."

这些固定搭配在日常生活中非常实用，能够帮助你更流畅地使用英语进行沟通。掌握它们可以让你的英语表达更加自然和地道。

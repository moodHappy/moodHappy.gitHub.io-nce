二、若干表达法示例

1.put out 不高兴

----1) He seemed greatly put out by the arrival of the new workers.

----2) She never gets put out even by the most difficult maters.

2.on the spot 当场

----1) No one could look at that dear sweet little face of yours without falling in love with you on the spot.

----2) He did it on the spot.

----3) He died on the spot.

3.convey to 把什么一种...使人理解

----1) How can I convey these idea to the children?

----2) The phrase does not convey this meaning.

----3) This description, picture ,sketch will convey some idea of the scenery.

----4) It conveyed to me the idea of a battlefield.

(idea是概况的意思)

4 lit up 显出愉悦的神情

----1) Her face lit up with joy when she heard the good news.

----2) A smile lit up his face.

5 beat a retreat 退却

----1) They beat a retreat when they saw that they were too few.

----2) The cat beat a hasty retreat when he saw the dog coming.

----3) He beat a retreat ,when he saw me.

----4) The enemy beat a hasty retreat when they found the place occupied.

----5) Seeing that his rival was outwitting him ,he beat a hasty retreat by saying that he was not feeling well and had to go to the doctor's.

状语从句类型 常用引导词 例句
时间状语从句 when, as, while, as soon as, before, after, since, till, until 等 When I came into the office, the teachers were having a meeting.（当我走进办公室，老师们已经在开会了）¹
地点状语从句 where, wherever, anywhere, everywhere 等 Where there is a will, there is a way.（有志者事竟成）²
原因状语从句 because, since, as, for 等 He is disappointed because he didn't get the position.（因为没有得到这个职位，他很失望）
目的状语从句 so that, in order that, such that 等 Speak clearly， so that they may understand you.（说清楚点，以便他们能理解）
结果状语从句 so...that, such...that 等 He got up so early that he caught the first bus.（他起得如此的早，以至于他赶上了第一班车）
条件状语从句 if, unless, whether 等 You will certainly succeed so long as you keep on trying.（只要你不断尝试，你一定会成功的）
方式状语从句 as, as if, how 等 He behaved as if he were the boss.（他表现得好像他是老板）
比较状语从句 as, than 等 The house is three times as big as ours.（这个房子是我们的三倍大）
让步状语从句 though, although, even if, even though 等 Though he is a child, he knows a lot.（尽管他还是个孩子，他懂得很多）

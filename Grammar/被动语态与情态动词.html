Examples of Passive Voice with Modal Verbs

Modal Verb English 中文
can (能够) The cookies can be eaten by anyone. 这些饼干谁都能吃。
could (能够，过去式) The message could be understood by everyone. 这条消息可以被每个人理解。
may (可能) The report may be finished by tomorrow. 这份报告明天可能会完成。
might (可能，也许) The answer might be found in the textbook. 这个答案也许能在教科书中找到。
shall (应该) The chores shall be completed before dinner. 家务活应该在晚饭前完成。
should (应该) The package should be delivered today. 这个包裹今天应该会送到。
will (将会) The project will be finished by the end of the week. 这个项目将会在这周结束前完成。
would (将会，愿意) The invitation would be accepted by most people. 大多数人将会接受邀请。
must (必须) The rules must be followed by everyone. 所有人都必须遵守规则。
ought to (应该) The document ought to be reviewed before signing. 在签署前应该审查文件。

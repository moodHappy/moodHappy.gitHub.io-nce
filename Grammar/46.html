1. Accustom(ed) to：

  • 表示习惯于某事物或情况。
  • 例句：It took a while, but I've become accustomed to the noise from the construction site next door.

2. Amount to：

  • 表示总计达到或意味着。
  • 例句：The costs of the project amounted to more than we initially estimated.

3. Appeal to：

  • 表示吸引或向某人或团体提出请求。
  • 例句：The charity event appealed to many people to donate to the cause.

4. Apply to /for：

  • 表示适用于或申请某事物。
  • 例句：You can apply for the job online through our website.

5. Attach(ed) to：

  • 表示连接或附属于某事物。
  • 例句：The documents should be attached to the email when you send it.

6. Attend to：

  • 表示处理或照顾。
  • 例句：Please attend to the customer's needs promptly.

7. Belong to：

  • 表示属于某人或某团体。
  • 例句：This book belongs to the library, so you cannot keep it.

8. Challenge to：

  • 表示向某人提出挑战。
  • 例句：He challenged me to a chess match.

9. Compare to /with：

  • 表示将事物与其它事物进行比较。
  • 例句：You can't compare me to my brother; we are very different.

10. Condemn(ed) to：

  • 表示被判决或注定要遭受某种命运。
  • 例句：The criminal was condemned to life in prison.

11. Confess to：

  • 表示承认或坦白某事。
  • 例句：He confessed to the police that he had stolen the money.

12. Confine to：

  • 表示限制在某个范围内。
  • 例句：The illness confined her to bed for a week.

13. Consent to：

  • 表示同意或允许某事。
  • 例句：The parents gave their consent to their daughter's marriage.

14. Convert to：

  • 表示改变信仰或改变某事物的形式。
  • 例句：Many people convert to a healthier lifestyle after a health scare.

15. Entitle(d) to：

  • 表示有权利或资格获得某事物。
  • 例句：As a member, you are entitled to use all the facilities.

16. Listen to：

  • 表示聆听某人或某事物。
  • 例句：You should listen to your teacher when she is explaining the lesson.

17. Mention to：

  • 表示向某人提及某事。
  • 例句：I'll mention to my boss that you called.

18. Object to：

  • 表示反对某事。
  • 例句：The residents objected to the construction of the new highway.

19. Occur to：

  • 表示突然想到或出现在脑海中。
  • 例句：It suddenly occurred to me that I forgot to lock the door.

20. Prefer to：

  • 表示更倾向于选择某事物。
  • 例句：I prefer to drink tea over coffee.

21. React to /against：

  • 表示对某事物做出反应或反对。
  • 例句：The public reacted against the new policy.

22. Reply to：

  • 表示回答某人或某事物。
  • 例句：She replied to the email promptly.

23. Respond to：

  • 表示对某事物做出回应或反应。
  • 例句：The company needs to respond to customer complaints more effectively.

24. See to：

  • 表示负责或处理某事。
  • 例句：Can you see to the arrangements for the meeting?

25. Submit to：

  • 表示服从或提交给某人或某事物。
  • 例句：The employee submitted his report to the manager.

26. Surrender to：

  • 表示向某人或某事物投降或屈服。
  • 例句：The soldiers surrendered to the enemy forces.

27. Turn to：

  • 表示转向或求助于某人或某事物。
  • 例句：When in trouble, he always turns to his family for support.

28. Yield to：

  • 表示屈服于或让步于某人或某事物。
  • 例句：The opposition party yielded to the majority vote.

  • 表示解释或说明某事的原因、情况或细节。

  • 表示请求某人提供信息、帮助或某物。
  • 例句：He asked for directions to the nearest store.

3. Act for/on：

  • 表示代表某人或某事物采取行动。
  • 例句：As the president's advisor, she acts on his behalf during negotiations.

4. Apologize for：

  • 表示为某事向某人道歉。
  • 例句：I apologize for the delay in responding to your email.

5. Blame for：

  • 表示因某事而责备某人。
  • 例句：The manager was blamed for the project's failure.

6. Beg for：

  • 表示恳求或乞求某物。
  • 例句：The homeless man begged for change on the street corner.

7. Call for：

  • 表示需要或呼吁采取某种行动或提供某物。
  • 例句：The new policy calls for stricter regulations on pollution.

8. Charge for：

  • 表示为某项服务或商品收费。
  • 例句：The restaurant charges for each dish on the menu.

9. Exchange for：

  • 表示交换某物以获得另一物。
  • 例句：She exchanged her old car for a new model.

10. Hope for：

  • 表示期待或希望某事发生。
  • 例句：We all hope for a peaceful resolution to the conflict.

11. Look for：

  • 表示寻找某人或某物。
  • 例句：I am looking for my lost keys.

12. Mistake for：

  • 表示错误地将某人或某物视为另一人或另一物。
  • 例句：In the dark, I mistook a bush for a person.

13. Mourn for：

  • 表示为某人的去世或某事的结束而哀悼。
  • 例句：The family mourned for their loved one who passed away.

14. Pay for：

  • 表示为购买的商品或服务付款。
  • 例句：You will have to pay for the damages you caused.

15. Prepare for：

  • 表示为即将到来的事件或情况做准备。
  • 例句：The athletes are preparing for the upcoming championship.

16. Provide for：

  • 表示供应或为某人提供所需的物品或支持。
  • 例句：Parents provide for their children's education and well-being.

17. Search for：

  • 表示寻找或搜索某人或某物。
  • 例句：The police searched for the missing child throughout the night.

18. Thank for：

  • 例句：I thank you for your help with my project.

19. Vote for/on：

  • 例句：We waited for the bus at the station.

与 "with" 连用的动词可以表达一系列动作和关系，通常涉及与某人或某物的互动。以下是您提供的动词短语的详细解释和例句：

1. Agree with：

  • 表示与某人意见一致或同意某事物。
  • 例句：I agree with your proposal for the new marketing strategy.

2. Begin with：

  • 表示以某事物作为开始。
  • 例句：The meeting will begin with a brief introduction.

3. Communicate with：

  • 表示与某人或某群体进行交流。
  • 例句：It's important to communicate with your team members regularly.

4. Compare with/to：

  • 表示将某人或某物与另一人或另一物进行比较。
  • 例句：You can compare the two models with each other to see which one is better.

5. Compete with/against：

  • 表示与某人或某团队竞争。
  • 例句：Our team will compete with the champions in the upcoming match.

6. Comply with：

  • 表示遵守规则、法律或要求。
  • 例句：All businesses must comply with the safety regulations.

7. Confuse with：

  • 表示将某人或某物错误地视为另一人或另一物。
  • 例句：I often confuse the two actors because they look alike.

8. Contrast with/to：

  • 表示与某人或某物形成对比。
  • 例句：The dark clothing contrasts with the bright background.

9. Cope with：

  • 表示应对或处理困难情况。
  • 例句：She is trying to cope with the loss of her job.

10. Correspond with：

  • 表示与某人进行书信往来或与某人的意见一致。
  • 例句：The two documents correspond with each other in terms of content.

11. Disgust(ed) with：

  • 表示对某人或某事感到厌恶或不满。
  • 例句：He was disgusted with the poor quality of the product.

12. Finish with：

  • 表示完成某事物或结束与某人的关系。
  • 例句：Are you finished with the report yet?

13. Help with/in：

  • 表示在某事上提供帮助。
  • 例句：Can you help me with this math problem?

14. Interfere with/in：

  • 表示干预或打扰某人或某事。
  • 例句：Please do not interfere with my work.

15. Mix with：

  • 表示与某物质混合或与某人交往。
  • 例句：You should not mix these chemicals, as it could be dangerous.

16. Occupy(ied) with：

  • 表示忙于某事或从事于某事。
  • 例句：She is occupied with preparing for her exams.

17. Part with：

  • 表示放弃、失去或与某人或某物分开。
  • 例句：He found it difficult to part with his old car.

18. Please(d) with：

  • 表示对某事物感到满意或高兴。
  • 例句：The customers were pleased with the service at the restaurant.

19. Quarrel with/about：

  • 表示与某人争吵或争论某事。
  • 例句：They quarreled about the distribution of tasks.

20. Reason with：

  • 表示试图通过逻辑和理由说服某人。
  • 例句：I tried to reason with him, but he wouldn't listen.

21. Satisfy (fied) with/by：

  • 表示对某事物感到满意或足够。
  • 例句：She is satisfied with her performance in the competition.

22. Threaten (ed) with：

  • 表示用某事物威胁某人。
  • 例句：He threatened me with a lawsuit if I didn't pay the debt.

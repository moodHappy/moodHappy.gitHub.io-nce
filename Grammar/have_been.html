“have been” 和 “had been” 的比较

用法 “have been/has been” “had been”
动词时态 现在完成时 过去完成时
含义 描述从过去某一时间点开始，持续到现在的动作或状态，或者在最近发生的动作或状态。 描述在过去某一时间点之前已经完成的动作或状态。
例句 “I have been to Paris twice.” (Indicates past experiences possibly influencing the present.) “By the time she arrived, we had already eaten dinner.” (We completed eating dinner before she arrived.)

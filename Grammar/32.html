比较和数量表达式

比较状语从句

结构 例句 意义
than She is taller than him. 比较两个事物，表明优势。
as...as This book is as interesting as that one. 显示相等或相似。
not so/as...as He is not as smart as his brother. 表达劣势。
less...than There are less people here than last year. 比较数量，表明较少。

数量表达式

表达式 用法 意义
little She has little patience. 否定，几乎没有。
a little We have a little time left. 肯定，一些。
few There are few apples left. 否定，数量少。
a few I have a few friends. 肯定，一些。
less Less noise is appreciated. 比较级形式，表示减少的数量。
fewer We have fewer tourists this year. 比较级形式，表示减少的数量。

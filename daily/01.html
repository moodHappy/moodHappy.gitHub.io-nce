Lesson 1: Where are you from?

Hello. 

Hi.

How are you? 

I’m good. How are you?

Good. Do you speak English? 

A little. Are you American?

Yes. 

Where are you from?

I’m from California. 

Nice to meet you.

Nice to meet you too. 

CONTINUE WRITING

What brings you to this part of town?

I'm just exploring. How about you?

I'm meeting a friend for lunch. Do you have any recommendations for good restaurants around here?

Yes, there's a great Italian place just down the street. Would you like me to show you?

That would be fantastic, thank you! By the way, what do you do for a living?

I'm a graphic designer. How about you?

I'm a student studying computer science. Do you enjoy your work as a graphic designer?

I love it. It's very creative and fulfilling. How about you, do you enjoy studying computer science?

Yes, I find it fascinating. There's always something new to learn.

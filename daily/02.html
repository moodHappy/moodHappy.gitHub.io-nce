Lesson 2: Do you speak English?

Excuse me, are you American? 

No.

Do you speak English?  

A little, but not very well. 

How long have you been here?  

2 months. 

What do you do for work?  

I’m a student. How about you? 

I’m a student too.  

CONTINUE WRITING

Where are you studying? 

At the University of Tokyo. 

What are you studying? 

I’m studying Japanese literature. 

That’s very interesting. 

Thank you. I like it very much. 

Do you have any hobbies? 

Yes, I like to play chess and listen to music. 

Me too. Do you want to play chess with me sometime? 

Sure, that would be nice. 

Great. Here is my phone number. Call me when you are free. 

OK. Thank you. Goodbye. 

Goodbye. See you soon.

Here is a concise summary of the article in simple English (B1 level): An Australian woman, Erin Patterson, is on trial for murdering her relatives with poisonous mushrooms. Prosecutors say she used kitchen scales to measure a deadly dose of the mushrooms. Patterson, 50, says it was an accident and she didn't mean to hurt anyone. Three people died and one person survived after eating a meal at Patterson's home in July 2023. Patterson admits to picking wild mushrooms before the incident, but says she didn't know they were poisonous. The trial is ongoing and is expected to last for at least two more weeks.

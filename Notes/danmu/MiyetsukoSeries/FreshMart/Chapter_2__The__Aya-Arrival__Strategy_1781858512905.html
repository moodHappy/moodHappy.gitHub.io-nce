Chapter 2: The "Aya-Arrival" Strategy

发布时间：2026/6/19 16:41:51

第二章：绫的接待作战计划

The breakfast table had transformed into a war room. With Aya’s response to the invitation—a simple, elegant "I’ll drop by this weekend"—confirmed, the family needed to prepare.

早餐桌瞬间变成了作战指挥部。绫回复了那份邀请函，只简单回了一句“周末路过”，这让全家都进入了备战状态。

Fujiwara cleared his throat, tapping his finger on the table like a CEO before a board meeting. "Alright, team. We have an VIP guest. We need to look normal, functional, and ideally, like we aren't a chaotic group of people who barely follow the rules of physics."

藤原清了清嗓子，像个准备董事会的 CEO 那样敲着桌子。“好了，各位。咱们要迎来一位 VIP。我们得表现得正常点、靠谱点，最起码看起来不像是一群连物理常识都不太在乎的混乱个体。”

Sayuri, busy folding laundry, looked up with a smirk. "’Normal’ is a strong word for this group, Fujiwara-san. But I’m willing to play along."

正在叠衣服的小百合抬头坏笑：“对我们这群人来说，‘正常’这个词是不是太奢侈了点？不过，我愿意配合演戏。”

Fujiwara held up a list he’d scribbled down. "Division of labor is key. Let’s make this efficient."

藤原举起一张写满了字的纸。“分工是关键，咱们得讲效率。”

[The Official "Aya-Welcome" Roster / 接待分工表]

Fujiwara (The "Host" / 门面担当): "My job is to keep things civil, handle the drinks, and ensure that I don't look too eager to see her. I need to balance 'welcoming brother-in-law' with 'mysterious, high-value landlord'."

藤原（门面担当）： “我的任务是维持体面，负责端茶送水，还得保证看她的时候别表现得太猴急。我得在‘热情的姐夫’和‘神秘的高价值房东’之间找到平衡。”

Rin (The "Social Lubricant" / 社交润滑剂): "Since she’s my sister, I’ll take the lead on the 'bonding' conversation. I'll make sure she feels like part of the family, and I’ll handle the seating arrangements so nobody gets too weird."

铃（社交润滑剂）： “毕竟是我妹妹，‘情感交流’由我主导。我会让她感到归属感，还会安排好座位，免得场面失控变得太奇怪。”

Sayuri (The "Domestic Chef" / 餐饮总指挥): "I’ll handle the menu. I’m thinking something sophisticated but not too intimidating. Something that says 'we are a healthy, stable family,' even if we're clearly not."

小百合（餐饮总指挥）： “菜单交给我。我想做点精致又不失亲和力的菜。那种看起来像‘健康稳定家庭’会吃的晚餐，尽管我们明摆着不是。”

Fujiwara paused, looking at his list. "Wait, where’s the cleaning crew?"

藤原停下来，看着清单。“等等，那清洁组呢？”

Miyetsuko, who had been lazily sharpening a nail file in the corner, looked up. "I’ll handle the 'visual appeal.' Which means I’ll make sure the apartment is clean enough to pass inspection, and I’ll ensure no one leaves any... intimate evidence... out in the open."

一直缩在角落里懒洋洋修指甲的美悦子抬头道：“‘视觉美化’我包了。意思就是，我会把公寓打扫得像模像样，还会确保没人把任何……私密证据……落在外面。”

"Excellent," Fujiwara grinned. "Team, we have 48 hours. Let's make Aya think we’re the most boring, normal people on the planet. If she suspects we’re weird, we lose the game."

“完美，”藤原咧嘴一笑。“各位，咱们还有48小时。让绫觉得我们是地球上最无聊、最正常的人。如果她怀疑我们这儿有什么古怪，我们就输了。”

Rin giggled. "Oh, Fujiwara-san, she already knows we’re weird. She just wants to see how weird."

铃咯咯直笑。“噢，藤原先生，她早就知道我们古怪了。她只是想看看我们到底能古怪到什么程度而已。”

[Learning Point]

War room: 指挥部/作战室。

Functional: 功能正常的（此处指看起来像正常人）。

Domestic: 家居的/家庭的。

Intimidating: 令人畏惧的（指菜式太复杂显得高不可攀）。

Pass inspection: 通过检查。

Chapter 85: The Ghost of the Past

发布时间：2026/6/15 00:27:31

第八十五章：罪与赎的重逢

The door to the room creaked open, and the fluorescent light spilled in, illuminating a familiar, trembling figure. It was Xiao Chen. He looked ragged, his skin sallow from the drugs that had become his only companion, but his eyes—those eyes that had once captivated her—were wide with shock.

隔间的门吱呀一声推开，日光灯惨白的光泻进来，照亮了一个熟悉而颤抖的身影。是小陈。他看起来落魄不堪，长期吸食毒品让他的皮肤呈现出一种病态的蜡黄，但他那双曾令她魂牵梦萦的眼睛，此刻正因为巨大的惊愕而瞪得滚圆。

He had come seeking the one thing he always sought: a thrill, a temporary fix. He hadn't expected to find his former supervisor, the woman whose life he had once been inextricably linked to, serving as a disposable tool in the very cesspool he now called home.

他来这里是为了寻找他一直渴求的东西：短暂的刺激，某种精神的慰藉。他做梦也没想到，会在这里遇见他曾经的主管——那个曾经与他命运紧密纠缠的女人，如今却沦为这处污水坑里的消耗品。

"Miyetsuko...?" he gasped, his voice cracking. "What... how are you...?"

“美悦子……？”他倒吸一口冷气，声音破碎，“怎么……你怎么会在这里……”

She didn't offer a polite smile. She didn't look at him with the softness of a lover or the authority of a supervisor. She looked at him with the cold, dead eyes of a survivor.

她没有给出礼貌的微笑，也没有用恋人的温存或主管的威严注视他，而是用一种幸存者才有的、死灰般的冷寂目光看着他。

In the stifling silence of the room, she spoke. She didn't hold back. She laid it all out: the arrest, the extortion, the three days of hell at the mountain retreat, and the six-month sentence she was currently serving—all to keep his record clean and pay for a mistake that wasn't hers to bear.

在这窒息的寂静中，她开口了。她没有保留，全盘托出：那场逮捕、勒索、山间别墅的三天炼狱，以及她正在服的这半年“刑期”——这一切，都只是为了抹去他的案底，为那个本不该由她背负的错误买单。

As she spoke, Xiao Chen crumbled. The drugs were forgotten. The arrogance of the "kept man" vanished, replaced by a raw, suffocating shame. He wept, his head bowing low until his forehead pressed against her knees—the same knees that had been bruised for his sake.

随着她的讲述，小陈彻底崩溃了。毒品带来的幻觉抛之脑后，那个“软饭男”的傲慢荡然无存，取而代之的是令人窒息的羞耻感。他放声痛哭，额头重重地抵在她的膝盖上——那双正是因为他而受尽屈辱的双腿。

"I didn't know," he sobbed, his voice raw. "I thought... I thought you let it go. I had no idea..."

“我不知道，”他哽咽着，嗓音嘶哑，“我以为……我以为你已经放下了。我根本没想到……”

He looked up at her, his face a mask of ruined resolve. "I'm still seeing her. The woman who got me out. She... she still controls everything. I'll go to her. I'll beg her to intervene. I'll tell her everything."

他抬头望向她，脸上写满了破碎的决心。“我还在见她。就是那个把我捞出来的女人。她……她依然掌控着一切。我会去找她。我会求她介入，我会把一切都告诉她。”

Miyetsuko looked down at him, neither forgiving nor condemning. She saw him not as the boy she loved, but as a lever.

美悦子俯视着他，既不宽恕，也不指责。她眼前的不再是曾经深爱的少年，而是一枚可以撬动局势的杠杆。

"Go," she commanded, her voice a cold whisper. "If you owe me anything, Xiao Chen, make it count. If you don't break this cycle now, you aren't just a junkie—you're the reason I will never be free."

“去吧，”她冷冷地命令道。“如果你还欠我什么，小陈，那就让它发挥点作用。如果你现在不能打破这个轮回，那你不仅是个瘾君子——你还是让我永远无法获得自由的罪魁祸首。”

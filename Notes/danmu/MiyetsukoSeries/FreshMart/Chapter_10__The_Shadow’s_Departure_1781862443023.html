Chapter 10: The Shadow’s Departure

第十章：暗影的撤离

The bathroom was still steaming from the "toilet-birth" commotion when the door clicked open with clinical precision. Aya walked in, her silhouette sharp against the fluorescent light, looking as if she had just stepped out of a high-stakes corporate boardroom.

浴室里还弥漫着刚才那场“马桶分娩”闹剧留下的蒸汽，门被极具职业素养地轻轻推开了。绫的身影在日光灯下显得格外犀利，仿佛她刚从一场高难度的商务谈判中抽身而出。

She looked at the soapy, wailing—but decidedly alive—bundle in Fujiwara’s arms, then at the toilet bowl. She didn't flinch. She simply adjusted her spectacles.

她看着藤原怀里那个浑身肥皂泡、哭闹着但生命力惊人的小东西，又看了看马桶，眼皮都没抬一下，只是扶了扶眼镜。

"A toilet," she mused, her voice devoid of emotion. "I suppose that’s one way to bypass the waiting room. However, the decibel level is already exceeding my tolerance threshold."

“马桶，”她沉吟道，语气平静得像在谈论行情，“跳过候诊室，这倒是个不落俗套的方案。不过，这噪音的分贝值已经超过了我的容忍限度。”

She turned to leave, grabbing a small duffel bag she had apparently packed in anticipation of the chaos.

她转过身准备离开，顺手拎起了一个小行李袋，显然她早就预料到了这场混乱，并做好了撤离准备。

"Where are you going?" Fujiwara asked, stunned. "The kid is family!"

“你去哪？”藤原愣住了，“这孩子可是咱们家人！”

"I’ve decided to enroll in a three-year intensive training course at a remote tractor repair facility in the mountains," Aya replied, not looking back. "It’s quiet, mechanical, and notably devoid of infants. I’ll stop by occasionally to see if you’ve managed to turn the kid into a productive member of society, or if you’ve just produced another toilet-dwelling hazard."

“我决定去山里的拖拉机修理厂深造三年，”绫头也不回地答道，“那里安静、机械，而且绝对没有婴儿。我会偶尔回来看看，确认一下你们是把他培养成了社会的栋梁，还是仅仅生产了另一个住在马桶里的生态危害。”

Fujiwara stepped forward, his heart sinking at the thought of her vanishing into the shadows. He grabbed her hand, and in a final, defiant gesture of intimacy, he shoved a small, buzzing device into her palm.

藤原走上前，想到她即将消失在阴影中，心中不禁一沉。他一把拽住她的手，在最后那一刻，带着一种挑衅意味的亲昵，将一个小巧的、嗡嗡作响的跳蛋塞进了她掌心。

"Use this," he whispered, his grin a mix of sadness and challenge. "When you're alone in that tractor shop, thinking about the chaos you left behind. Don't let yourself get too bored, Aya."

“用这个，”他低语，笑容里揉杂着一丝落寞与挑战，“当你在那拖拉机房里独处时，要是想起了你留下的这摊烂摊子，就用这个。别让自己太无聊了，绫。”

Aya paused. She looked down at the tiny device in her hand, then looked back at Fujiwara. A slow, predatory smile—the first genuine smile he had ever seen from her—curled on her lips.

绫停住了脚步。她看了看手心里那个小玩意儿，又抬眼看向藤原。那一刻，一抹缓慢的、带有掠食者气息的微笑——这是藤原第一次从她脸上看到的、发自真心的笑容——在她唇边绽放。

She reached out, running a hand over the front of her trousers. With a soft click, she pulled out a massive, industrial-grade remote-controlled vibrator, far larger and more sophisticated than the one Fujiwara had offered.

她伸出手，在裤前轻轻一划。伴随着“咔哒”一声轻响，她竟从裤兜里掏出了一个巨大的、工业级的遥控震动器，看起来比藤原给她的那个要精密且凶猛得多。

"You’re sweet, Fujiwara," she murmured, her eyes glinting with a dangerous, electric light. "But never underestimate your sister-in-law. I’m always prepared for the long haul."

“你真可爱，藤原，”她喃喃道，眼中闪烁着危险而诱人的电光，“但永远别小看你的小姨子。我可是随时准备好了，打持久战的。”

With a wink, she turned and vanished into the night, the faint bzzzz of her heavy-duty machinery echoing in the hallway like a farewell signal.

她眨了眨眼，转身没入夜色之中。走廊里隐约传来她那重型器械发出的细微嗡嗡声，像是某种独特的告别信号。

Mused: 沉思/喃喃自语。

Threshold: 门槛/阈值。

Defiant: 挑战的/反抗的。

Predatory: 掠食者的（形容眼神或神态）。

Long haul: 持久战/漫长的旅程。

Chapter 73: The Penalty of the Silent Game

发布时间：2026/6/18 18:10:30

第七十三章：沉默游戏的惩罚

Back on the sand, the adrenaline began to fade, replaced by the warm, intoxicating glow of the setting sun. Rin, having wiped the stranger’s sticky residue off her skin, was sitting on a towel, recounting the experience with a calm that was both unnerving and hilarious.

回到沙滩上，肾上腺素逐渐褪去，取而代之的是夕阳温热迷人的余晖。铃擦去了身上那陌生人留下的粘稠痕迹，坐在毛巾上，以一种既让人发毛又让人忍俊不禁的冷静语气，复盘刚才的遭遇。

"I really thought it was Fujiwara-san," she explained, her face perfectly serious. "But when I felt that... that small thing, I was confused. It felt like a little finger, not a man's pride. I thought maybe he was just shrinking from the cold water, so I tried my best to warm it up. But no matter how much I moved, it stayed like a soft, wet piece of dough."

“我真的以为那是藤原先生，”她一脸严肃地解释道，“但当我摸到那个……那个小东西的时候，我很困惑。感觉就像根小指头，一点也不像男人的雄风。我当时还以为他是被冷水冻缩了，所以竭尽全力想把它捂热。结果无论我怎么努力，它都像块又湿又软的面团。”

Fujiwara and Miyetsuko were already doubled over, gasping for breath. The image of the blind, highly skilled Rin putting her professional effort into "massaging" that pathetic, tiny organ was the pinnacle of their afternoon.

藤原和美悦子早已笑得直不起腰，喘不上气。那种画面感——盲眼且技术高超的铃，正对着那个可怜的小东西施展“专业按摩”——成了他们一下午的笑料巅峰。

"A soft piece of dough!" Miyetsuko choked out, slapping her knee. "Rin, you didn't just reject him, you insulted his entire existence!"

“一块软面团！”美悦子笑得直打嗝，猛拍大腿，“铃，你这哪是在拒绝他，你这是在侮辱他整个人生啊！”

Fujiwara wiped tears from his eyes, leaning back on his elbows. "Well, Rin, that was a hell of a story, but let's not forget the rules. We were playing the silence game. You screamed, didn't you? That means you lost."

藤原抹着眼泪，手肘撑在身后，“好吧，铃，这故事确实精彩，但别忘了规则。我们刚才在玩沉默游戏，你尖叫了，对吧？这意味着你输了。”

Rin tilted her head, a soft, mischievous smile blooming on her lips. "I did scream. And I suppose I must pay the price."

铃歪了歪头，嘴角泛起一抹柔软而狡黠的笑意。“我是尖叫了。我想，我确实得付出代价。”

"The penalty is a full-body massage for all of us," Fujiwara declared, watching the way the evening light caught her pale skin. "Starting with me."

“惩罚是为我们每一个人提供全身按摩，”藤原宣布道，目光追随着夕阳下她白皙的肌肤，“从我开始。”

Rin crawled over, her movements deliberate and sure. She didn't need to see where they were; she felt their presence like a map. She knelt beside Fujiwara and began to work her thumbs into his neck, her touch precise and heavy, turning his playful mood into a sudden, deep shiver of pleasure.

铃爬了过来，动作从容而笃定。她不需要看见他们的位置，她像阅读地图一样感知着他们的存在。她跪在藤原身边，拇指按入他的颈后，触感精准且厚重，让他那份戏谑的心情瞬间转为一阵深沉的愉悦颤栗。

"You’re quite tense today, Fujiwara-san," she murmured, her thumbs circling his tense traps. "Perhaps after I finish with you, I can attend to Sayuri and Miyetsuko. I know every muscle in your bodies, and I know exactly where the tension hides."

“你今天很紧张，藤原先生，”她低声呢喃，拇指揉捏着他僵硬的斜方肌，“也许等我弄完你，我可以照顾一下小百合和美悦子。我熟悉你们身体上的每一块肌肉，更知道压力藏在哪个角落。”

Sayuri looked on, feeling a strange mix of jealousy and anticipation, while Miyetsuko simply lay back, closing her eyes, ready to receive the penance. It was a perfect, bizarre, and utterly harmonious end to a day that had started with a near-assault and ended in a group-wide indulgence.

小百合坐在一旁，心中涌动着嫉妒与期待交织的复杂情绪，而美悦子只是躺下闭上眼，准备接受这场“惩罚”。这一天始于一场未遂的骚扰，最终却在这集体沉沦中完美落幕，既诡异又和谐。

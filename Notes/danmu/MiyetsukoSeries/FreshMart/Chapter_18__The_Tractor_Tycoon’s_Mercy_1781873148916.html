Chapter 18: The Tractor Tycoon’s Mercy

发布时间：2026/6/19 20:45:47

第十八章：拖拉机大亨的慈悲

Fujiwara, desperate and rapidly nearing the point where he’d have to start renting out his own kidneys to satisfy Rin’s digital habit, had resorted to the oldest communication method in the book: a letter in a bottle tossed into the nearest murky, trash-filled river.

藤原已经到了快要卖肾来填补铃那“数字无底洞”的边缘。他绝望地使出了最古老的通讯手段：把一封求救信塞进瓶子，扔进了那条浑浊不堪的河里。

Miraculously, the next morning, Aya was standing in the living room, smelling faintly of diesel fuel and mountain dust. She looked at the family—Fujiwara looking like a man who hadn’t slept since the invention of the internet, and Rin, frantically tapping her screen.

奇迹发生了，第二天一早，绫就站在客厅里，身上带着淡淡的柴油味和山里的尘土气息。她看着这一家子——藤原一脸“从互联网发明那天起就没合过眼”的憔悴，而铃还在疯狂地点屏幕。

"I’m here to save my sister," Aya announced, her voice as flat as a highway. "The addiction ends today."

“我是来拯救我姐姐的，”绫面无表情地宣布，“成瘾到此为止。”

She walked over to Rin and grabbed the phone. "Rin, listen. There was a guy, a total livestreaming fanatic. Really into the shopping channels. On his birthday, he collapsed and died. Stone cold."

她走到铃身边，一把夺过手机。“铃，听着。有个家伙，是个十足的直播购物狂，每天盯着直播买买买。结果在他生日那天，他倒下就死了，凉得透透的。”

Rin blinked, unbothered. "But I only watch the pretty boys, Aya. They’re too healthy to die. I’m fine."

铃眨了眨眼，一脸淡定。“但我只看小鲜肉直播啊，绫。他们健康着呢，不会死的。我没事。”

Aya’s face twitched. A single, dark vein pulsed on her forehead. She let out a strained, awkward cough. "Correction. My mistake. There was a guy, a fanatic watcher of pretty-boy livestreams. On his birthday, he died. Violently."

绫的脸颊抽动了一下，额头上爆出一条青筋。她尴尬地干咳一声，满头黑线地纠正道：“修正一下，我讲错了。有个家伙，也是个狂热的小鲜肉直播观众。结果在他生日那天，他死了。死状极惨。”

Rin froze. Her birthday was in three days. The fear of dying on one's birthday—a phobia she had harbored since she was six—hit her like a freight train. She threw the phone into the fish tank. "No more! I’m done! I’m going to spend my birthday eating vegetables and breathing fresh air!"

铃僵住了。她的生日就在三天后。那个“生日当天会死”的诅咒，像一列货运列车般撞碎了她的心理防线。她直接把手机丢进了鱼缸。“不看了！我戒了！我要去吃蔬菜、呼吸新鲜空气，安稳地过完生日！”

[The Beauty of the Beast / 圣子的美学]

Aya then turned her gaze toward the "Toilet Prodigy," who was currently gnawing on a coaster. She leaned in, squinted, and shrugged. "God, he’s ugly. Looks like a potato that was left in the microwave too long."

绫随即转向那个正在啃杯垫的“马桶圣子”。她凑近了打量半天，耸耸肩：“天哪，他长得真够丑的。像个在微波炉里热过头的土豆。”

The baby, offended, responded by burping directly into her face.

圣子大怒，直接对着她打了个响亮的嗝。

[The Tractor Fortune / 拖拉机财富]

Before they could even recover from the insult, Aya tossed a heavy, vibrating leather bag onto the dining table. It hit with a sound that suggested it was full of solid gold bricks.

还没等大家从这人身攻击中缓过神来，绫把一个沉甸甸的皮包往餐桌上一扔。那沉闷的撞击声，显然里面装满了金条。

"What is this?" Fujiwara whispered, picking it up.

“这是什么？”藤原颤声问。

"My earnings from the tractor factory," Aya said, turning toward the door. "It’s about a hundred times more than what Rin threw away on those virtual yachts. Consider it an investment in the family's basic sanity."

“我在拖拉机厂赚的钱，”绫转身走向大门，“大概比铃在那些虚拟游艇上败掉的钱多出一百倍。权当是对这个家庭基础智力水平的投资吧。”

"Wait, Aya! Stay for dinner!" Fujiwara shouted, already imagining how much high-grade beef they could buy.

“等等，绫！留下来吃饭呀！”藤原大喊，脑子里已经在盘算能买多少高级和牛了。

But when they looked up, the living room was empty. The door was still swinging in the breeze, and the smell of diesel was already fading. She was gone—back to her tractors, leaving them richer, saner, and deeply confused by her erratic kindness.

可当他们抬头看时，客厅早已空空如也。大门还在微风中吱呀作响，那股柴油味也已经散去。她又走了——回到她的拖拉机世界去了，给他们留下了一笔巨款，和一个关于她那反复无常的慈悲的深深谜团。

[Learning Point]

Diesel fuel: 柴油。

Apprenticeship: 学徒身份。

Phobia: 恐惧症。

Erratic: 反复无常的/古怪的。

Tycoon: 大亨。

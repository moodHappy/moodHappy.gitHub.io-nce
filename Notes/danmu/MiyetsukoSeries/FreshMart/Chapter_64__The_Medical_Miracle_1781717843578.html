Chapter 64: The Medical Miracle

发布时间：2026/6/18 01:37:21

第六十四章：医生的“反转”

The silence that had followed the laughter was still heavy when the doctor, face beet-red, suddenly let out a strangled cough. He frantically fumbled with the files on his desk, his eyes darting back and forth like a trapped bird.

之前的狂笑过后，空气中还残留着尴尬的余韵，医生突然面红耳赤地干咳了几声。他手忙脚乱地翻找着桌上的病历，眼神像只受惊的鸟一样乱窜。

"Wait! Wait, stop laughing!" he stammered, his voice rising an octave. "I… I am so incredibly sorry. I grabbed the wrong file! That last one was a… a research case study on extreme anomalies from 1995. This," he said, slapping a new sheet onto the light box, "is actually the correct scan for your pregnancy."

“等等！等等，别笑了！”他结结巴巴地喊道，声音尖了八度，“我……我太抱歉了！我拿错片子了！刚才那份是个……是个1995年的极端病例研究报告。这份，”他啪地一声把一张新单子拍在观片灯上，“这才是你们真实的产检结果。”

Fujiwara stopped mid-laugh, his chest still heaving. Miyetsuko froze, one tissue still halfway to the doctor’s sleeve.

藤原笑声戛然而止，胸口还在剧烈起伏。美悦子愣住了，手里的纸巾还悬在医生的袖口边。

The doctor wiped sweat from his brow, his hands trembling as he gestured to the clear, vibrant ultrasound on the screen. "The fetus is completely healthy. Perfect development, strong heartbeat, everything is normal. You… you’re going to have a perfectly normal baby. Please, forgive the… logistical error."

医生抹了一把额头的冷汗，手抖着指向屏幕上那张清晰、鲜活的超声波图。“胎儿非常健康。发育完美，心跳有力，一切正常。你们……你们会生下一个完全健康的孩子。请务必……务必原谅这次操作失误。”

He paused, looking at the trio, then added with a nervous twitch, "Wait, is it a grandson or… should I be congratulating you on a daughter? Oh, wait, the gender is… ah, let's just say 'congratulations on the baby'!"

他看着这三人，紧张地抽动了一下嘴角，又补充道：“等等，是该祝贺你们得个孙子，还是……该祝贺你们得个女儿？噢等等，这性别……啊，算了，总之‘恭喜你们当父母’吧！”

The absurdity of the situation hit them like a tidal wave. After the peak of hysterical dread, the sudden relief was too much. Fujiwara didn't just laugh this time; he leaned back and howled, the sound echoing off the sterile walls.

这种荒谬感像海啸一样冲垮了他们。在经历了刚才那场歇斯底里的恐惧巅峰后，这种突如其来的如释重负让人根本招架不住。藤原这次不只是笑，他仰起头嚎叫起来，声音在无菌诊室的墙壁间激烈回荡。

"A perfectly normal baby!" Miyetsuko cackled, clutching her sides. "God, I almost started planning a funeral for a genetic experiment!"

“一个完全正常的孩子！”美悦子笑得直不起腰，捂着肚子，“天哪，我刚才差点连这实验品畸形儿的葬礼都安排上了！”

Sayuri felt the tension drain out of her body, leaving her limp. She put a hand over her heart, closing her eyes as a genuine, overwhelming wave of relief washed over her. She was a mother, not a biological hazard.

小百合只觉得那股紧绷的神经瞬间断开了，整个人松懈下来。她捂着胸口闭上眼，一股真切、汹涌的如释重负感席卷全身。她是个母亲，而不是什么生物危害源。

Fujiwara stood up, walked over to the doctor, and shook his hand with mock-solemnity, his eyes still streaming with tears of laughter. "Doc, you’ve given us the best comedy show of the year. I think we’ll be seeing you for the delivery. Just, for the love of god, keep the files straight next time!"

藤原站起身，走过去，带着一种滑稽的庄重握住了医生的手，眼角还挂着笑出的泪花。“医生，你简直给了我们今年最棒的一场脱口秀。生的时候我们肯定还来找你。不过，看在上帝的份上，下次把病历摆摆齐啊！”

As they walked out of the clinic, they were still shaking with giggles. It was the most normal, most chaotic, and most beautiful news they could have asked for. The "stray" baby was safe, the genetics were clean, and the future—however messy and bizarre—was finally wide open.

当他们走出诊所时，依旧笑得浑身发抖。这是他们能祈求到的最正常、最混乱，也最美好的消息了。这个“路人”宝宝很安全，基因没有任何问题，而他们的未来——不管有多么杂乱荒诞——终于彻底敞开了。

[Learning Point]

Stammer: 结巴/结结巴巴地说。

Logistical: 后勤的/操作上的。

Cackle: 咯咯笑/狂笑。

Limp: 软弱无力。

Solemnity: 庄重。

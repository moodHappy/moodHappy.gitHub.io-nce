Chapter 98: The Malignant Cradle

发布时间：2026/6/16 04:47:47

第九十八章：恶毒的摇篮

The night was heavy, thick with an ominous chill that seemed to seep through the walls of the villa. Miyetsuko collapsed, shivering with violent, rhythmic tremors—malaria, or something just as foul, had taken hold of her, burning her alive from the inside out. Fujiwara, his composure cracking, scrambled to summon a doctor of mastery, dragging a renowned physician from his bed to treat her, his own hands stained with the sweat of uncharacteristic fear.

夜晚阴沉而厚重，那股不祥的寒意仿佛穿透了别墅的墙壁。美悦子瘫倒在地，剧烈地颤抖着——是疟疾，又或是某种更加肮脏的疫病正吞噬着她，将她由内而外焚烧殆尽。藤原原本镇定的神情瞬间崩裂，他疯了般去请一位技术精湛的名医，从睡梦中将那医师拽来救治，他自己的双手此刻也浸透了反常的恐惧之汗。

The diagnosis was not illness, but inception. She was pregnant. The fever was the body’s rebellion against the parasite growing within her. Fujiwara immediately demanded a midwife, a woman of shadowed expertise to manage the rapid, unnatural gestation.

诊断结果并非疾病，而是受孕。那高烧只是身体对体内寄生虫般存在的排异反应。藤原立即找来了一名助产士，一个拥有阴暗经验的女人，来处理这场迅速而诡异的孕育过程。

Fujiwara, in his frantic, delirious state, paced the city, covering excessive mileage as he searched for rare herbs to stabilize her. In his desperation, he was misled by the neon-lit desperation of the city, stumbling into a sleazy, dirty bubble-bath club. The girls inside were grotesque, their mascara running in oily, loathsome streaks down their faces, a caricature of the allure he was seeking.

藤原在绝望的狂乱中奔走，跑了数英里的里程，只为搜寻能稳住她身体的草药。因极度焦虑，他被这城市的霓虹迷雾所误导，误打误撞闯入了一家肮脏的泡泡浴俱乐部。里面的女郎面目可憎，她们脸上的睫毛膏如油腻而可憎的污痕般流淌，那是他此刻所求欲望的扭曲讽刺。

The newborn arrived in a rush of blood and bile. A servant, terrified and subservient, stood by with a bowl of oatmeal—not for the mother, but for the wretched creature that had just been expelled from her womb, a gruel intended to fatten the infant before it could even cry.

那个新生儿在血水与苦胆的冲刷下匆匆降临。仆人颤抖着侍立一旁，端着一碗燕麦粥——这不是给母亲的，而是给那个刚刚从她子宫里剥离出来的可怜生物，那是为了让婴儿在啼哭之前就赶紧增肥的糊状饲料。

But Miyetsuko looked at the squirming thing with curdled hatred. She refused to nurture it, terrified that the infant’s rooting mouth would defile her pure breasts. She would not sacrifice her form to feed the leech that had nearly killed her.

美悦子厌恶地看着那个蠕动的生物，眼中满是凝固的恨意。她拒绝哺育它，恐惧那婴儿找寻乳头的嘴会玷污她纯洁的奶子。她绝不牺牲自己的身体去喂养这个差点害死她的寄生虫。

The end came in the dead of night, an ominous silence descending upon the nursery. Left unattended, the infant struggled, its tiny throat clogged by the thick, lukewarm oatmeal the servant had forced down its gullet. It choked, its fragile existence snuffed out in the swaddling clothes, its face turning a bruised, mottled purple as it gasped for air that would never come. It died in the suffocating dark, a mistake of nature, unmourned and unloved.

终局在深夜降临，那间婴儿房笼罩在一种不祥的死寂中。无人看顾时，婴儿剧烈挣扎，它那细小的喉咙被仆人强行灌下的黏稠、温凉的燕麦粥堵塞。它呛住了，脆弱的生命在襁褓中被彻底掐灭，面孔因窒息而呈现出瘀青的斑点。它死在窒息的黑暗中，那是大自然的错误，无人哀悼，无人怜惜。

Chapter 66: The Road Not Taken

发布时间：2026/6/18 13:36:13

第六十六章：车窗外的世界

Fujiwara found Rin at the back of the parlor, her fingers rhythmically kneading the stiff shoulders of a regular client. When he proposed the move—the room at the "Fresh Mart," the steady salary, the role of primary caregiver for Sayuri—she listened intently, her head tilted like a curious bird.

藤原在按摩店的里间找到了铃，她的手指正有节奏地揉捏着一位老顾客僵硬的肩膀。当他提出那个搬家方案——“新鲜马特”的房间、稳定的薪水、作为小百合贴身护理师的职位——她听得很认真，脑袋像只好奇的鸟儿一样歪着。

But when he finished, she offered a gentle, hesitant smile.

但他话音刚落，她却露出了一个温柔而迟疑的微笑。

"It is a beautiful offer, Fujiwara-san," she said, her voice soft. "But I fear... my rhythm is set here. The darkness of my world is familiar, and the way your house breathes—it feels too heavy, too complex. I don’t think I could adapt to the way you three love each other."

“这是个美妙的提议，藤原先生，”她的声音轻柔，“但我恐怕……我的节奏已经定在这里了。我世界的黑暗是熟悉的，而你们家呼吸的方式——感觉太沉重，太复杂了。我想，我大概无法适应你们三个人那种爱彼此的方式。”

Fujiwara didn't press her. He respected the line she drew. "I understand. But if you ever feel that your rhythm is changing... the door is open."

藤原没有强求。他尊重她划下的界限。“我明白。但如果你哪天觉得自己的节奏变了……门随时为你敞开。”

As he turned to leave, Rin added, her voice suddenly wistful, "Actually, there is something I have always wanted. It isn't a family. It is a horizon."

就在他转身要走时，铃突然补充道，声音里透着一股突如其来的憧憬：“其实，一直有一件事是我想要的。不是家庭。而是地平线。”

Fujiwara paused. "A horizon?"

藤原顿住了。“地平线？”

"I want to drive," she said, her sightless eyes facing the window. "I want to feel the road beneath the tires, the wind against my face, and the freedom of going where no one expects me to be. My friends and I talk about road trips, about driving to the coast, but... I am blind. Who would let me take the wheel?"

“我想开车，”她说，盲目的双眼对着窗户，“我想感受轮胎下的路，感受吹过脸庞的风，感受那种去往无人预料之地的自由。我和朋友们常谈起自驾游，谈起开车去海边，但是……我是盲人。谁会让我握住方向盘呢？”

Fujiwara looked at her, seeing not a caregiver, but a woman with a desperate, quiet hunger for control. He thought of his own need to anchor his chaotic life.

藤原看着她，看到的不再是一个护理师，而是一个极度渴望掌控自己人生的女人。他想到了自己——那种迫切想要锚定动荡生活的渴望。

"I can teach you," Fujiwara said, the words slipping out before he could second-guess them. "Not on a public road, of course. But I have an old van, and a private patch of land behind the warehouse. If you want to learn how to drive—how to feel the machine, how to steer—I can be your eyes."

“我可以教你，”藤原脱口而出，还没来得及怀疑自己就说了出来，“当然，不是在公路上。但我有一辆旧货车，仓库后面有一块私人土地。如果你想学开车——想学着感受机器，学着掌控方向——我可以做你的眼睛。”

Rin’s smile widened, genuine and bright. "You would do that? For a woman who just refused your offer?"

铃的笑容更灿烂了，真诚而明亮。“你愿意为我做这个？为一个刚拒绝了你提议的女人？”

"You gave me a good massage," Fujiwara chuckled. "And besides... everyone should have the chance to chase their own horizon, even if they can't see it."

“你给我按得不错，”藤原轻笑，“再说……每个人都该有机会去追逐自己的地平线，哪怕看不见也一样。”

[Learning Point]

Wistful: 渴望的/憧憬的。

Horizon: 地平线/远方。

Second-guess: 事后怀疑/犹豫。

Steer: 驾驶/操纵。

Patch: 一小块（土地）。

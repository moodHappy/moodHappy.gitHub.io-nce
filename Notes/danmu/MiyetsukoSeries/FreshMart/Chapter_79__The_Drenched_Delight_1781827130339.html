Chapter 79: The Drenched Delight

发布时间：2026/6/19 07:58:48

第七十九章：暴雨中的傻姑娘

The humidity of the Japanese summer was soul-crushing, and Sayuri was paying the price. Her pregnancy had turned her world upside down; she was constantly nauseous, exhausted, and—perhaps most distractingly—increasingly ditzy. Rin, with her blind woman’s patience and her hyper-sensitive touch, had become Sayuri’s lifeline, bathing her, feeding her, and keeping her grounded when the morning sickness made the walls spin.

日本夏季那股让人窒息的湿热简直要人命，小百合正备受煎熬。怀孕彻底打乱了她的生活；她整天反胃、疲惫不堪，而且——最让人头疼的是——她变得越来越笨了。铃凭借着盲人独有的耐心和超灵敏的触觉，成了小百合的救命稻草，帮她洗澡、喂饭，并在她因孕吐而天旋地转时扶稳她。

That afternoon, the sky turned a bruised, ugly purple. Sayuri had insisted on walking to the local market for a specific craving, forgetting both her umbrella and her common sense. Ten minutes later, the sky opened up—a torrential, violent downpour that turned the streets into rivers.

那天下午，天空呈现出一副淤青般的诡异深紫色。小百合非要步行去超市买个什么馋嘴零食，结果既忘了带伞，也把脑子丢在了家里。十分钟后，暴雨倾盆而下，街道瞬间变成了河流。

When she finally stumbled back into the "Fresh Mart," she was a spectacle. Her thin, white sundress was soaked through, plastered to her skin like a second layer of flesh. Every curve, every dip, and the dark, flushed patch of her skin were starkly visible through the translucent fabric. She looked like a shivering, wide-eyed kitten that had fallen into a fountain.

当她最终踉跄着闯进“新鲜马特”时，那景象简直绝了。她那件单薄的白色夏裙早已湿透，像第二层皮肤一样紧紧贴在身上。每一处曲线、每一处凹陷，甚至是那深色而泛红的皮肤纹理，在半透明的布料下都一览无余。她看起来就像只掉进喷泉里、瑟瑟发抖且一脸茫然的小猫。

Fujiwara, Miyetsuko, and Rin were standing behind the counter, staring at the dripping wet, semi-nude woman standing in their lobby.

藤原、美悦子和铃站在柜台后，目瞪口呆地看着这个湿身、半裸的女人站在他们大厅里。

"You absolute idiot!" Miyetsuko erupted, though her laughter was immediate. "You forgot your umbrella? In this weather? We literally own a supermarket full of goods!"

“你这蠢货！”美悦子爆发出一阵大笑，“下这种鬼天气你竟然不带伞？我们自己家开超市的啊！”

Fujiwara stepped out from behind the counter, his eyes devouring the sight of her. The translucent dress clung to her, and the sight of her pregnant, drenched body was like a hit of adrenaline to his system. He didn't bother with words; he grabbed her wrist, his grip firm and impatient.

藤原从柜台后跨出，目光贪婪地扫过她。半透明的裙子勾勒出的孕体被雨水冲刷得轮廓毕现，这景象对他而言简直比肾上腺素还猛烈。他没废话，一把拽住她的手腕，力度坚定而迫切。

"You're a hazard to yourself," he growled, pulling her toward the back storage area. "I'm going to have to 'dry you off' properly."

“你真是个把自己搞成麻烦的笨蛋，”他低吼着，把她往后仓拽，“我得给你好好‘烘干’一下。”

The storage room was cool, smelling of cardboard and stale air. He didn't bother with the lights. He pushed her against a stack of crates, his hands already working at the sodden, clinging mess of her clothes.

后仓阴凉，弥漫着纸箱和陈旧空气的味道。他连灯都没开，直接把她推到一堆货箱上，双手迅速剥离那件湿透、黏腻的衣服。

"Fujiwara-san, wait!" she gasped, her hands trembling as she held onto his shoulders. "I'm... I'm a mess! I'm wet and cold!"

“藤原先生，等等！”她惊呼，双手颤抖着攀住他的肩膀，“我……我浑身都是水，又湿又冷！”

"Exactly," he whispered, his hands roaming over her slick, shivering skin. "Let me warm you up."

“正好，”他低语，手掌滑过她湿滑、颤栗的肌肤，“让我来给你暖暖身子。”

He didn't wait for her to adjust. He thrust into her with a sudden, wet intensity that made her head snap back. The environment was raw—the sound of rain drumming against the corrugated metal roof providing a frantic, chaotic beat to their rhythm.

他没给她任何适应的时间，猛地贯穿了她。那种湿润而猛烈的快感让她仰起头。环境原始而粗粝——雨点敲打在波纹铁皮顶棚上的声音，为他们的律动提供了一种疯狂、混乱的鼓点。

He pummeled her, his movements frantic and low-brow, treating her like the clumsy, beautiful, pregnant mess she was. She gripped the crates, her nails digging into the wood, sobbing and laughing as his relentless, thick strokes hammered into her.

他折磨着她，动作粗暴而下流，将她视为那个笨拙、美丽又带球的湿透尤物。她死死抓着货箱，指甲扣进木头里，在他那沉重、无休止的撞击下又哭又笑。

"You're a fool, Sayuri," he grunted, his pace building, "a total, beautiful fool. Getting yourself caught in the rain... just so I could see you like this."

“你真是个傻瓜，小百合，”他哼哧着加快速度，“一个彻底的、美丽的傻瓜。为了让我能看到你这副模样……竟然把自己淋成这样。”

"I didn't mean to!" she cried, but her hips met him halfway, greedy and hungry.

“我不是故意的！”她哭喊着，胯部却贪婪而饥渴地迎合着他的深浅。

In the dim, damp dark of the warehouse, amidst the smell of damp cotton and sweat, he filled her until she was shaking, her body a perfect, chaotic intersection of desire and motherhood.

在这昏暗潮湿的仓库里，在那股湿棉布与汗水交织的气味中，他将她填满，直到她全身颤抖——她的身体，此刻正是欲望与母性碰撞出的最完美、最混乱的杰作。

[Learning Point]

Torrential: 倾盆的。

Spectacle: 奇观/景象（此处指湿身后的狼狈模样）。

Translucent: 半透明的。

Corrugated: 波纹状的。

Intersection: 交叉点。

Chapter 110: The Inventory of Intimacy

发布时间：2026/6/16 06:52:48

第一百一十章：盘点温情

The morning light in the city was soft, filtering through the glass windows of "Fresh Mart" and dancing off the polished floor. It was Tuesday, and the shop was quiet, the rush hour lull leaving them alone in the aisles of colorful produce and neatly stacked boxes.

城市里的晨光柔和地洒进“新鲜玛特”的玻璃窗，在地板上跳动。周二的清晨，店里格外宁静，早高峰后的空档让他们能在这堆满新鲜蔬果和货物的过道里享受片刻安宁。

Miyetsuko was busy adjusting a row of organic oranges, her movements precise and leisurely. She wore a simple apron, her hair tied up with a sturdy clip. Fujiwara was behind the counter, auditing the stock list on a tablet, the harsh, unforgiving digital numbers of his old life replaced by the satisfyingly predictable count of beverage crates and snack bags.

美悦子正忙着整理一排有机橙子，动作优雅从容。她系着简单的围裙，头发用发夹随意地盘起。藤原正守在柜台后，用平板电脑核对库存清单。比起过去那些无情的数字，这些关于饮料箱和零食袋的盘点，显得既务实又让人感到踏实。

"Fujiwara," Miyetsuko called out, holding up a particularly round and shiny apple. "We’re running low on the Fuji variety. Should I order another crate from the wholesaler?"

“藤原，”美悦子唤了一声，举起一颗又圆又亮红的富士苹果，“富士品种快没了，要不要给批发商再订一箱？”

Fujiwara looked up, his eyes softening as they landed on her. He walked over, abandoning his tablet, and stood behind her, his arms wrapping around her waist from behind. He leaned down, his chin resting on her shoulder as he watched her hands work.

藤原抬起头，目光在触及她时变得柔和。他放下平板走过去，从背后环住她的腰，下巴轻轻抵在她的肩膀上，看着她忙碌的双手。

"You’re the manager today," he whispered into her ear, a playful smirk dancing on his lips. "Whatever you think is best, my Empress."

“今天你是店长，”他在她耳边低语，嘴角带着顽皮的坏笑，“无论你觉得怎么好，都听你的，我的女皇。”

Miyetsuko swatted his arm playfully, a bright, genuine laugh escaping her. "Stop calling me that! Here, I’m just 'Miyetsuko, the clerk.' And the clerk says we need more apples."

美悦子调皮地拍了一下他的胳膊，发出了一阵清脆且真实的笑声。“别这么叫我！在这里，我只是‘店员美悦子’。而店员觉得，我们需要更多苹果。”

Just then, a regular customer—an elderly neighbor who lived in the unit above theirs—toddled in, her cane clicking rhythmically against the tile. "Morning, you two! Such a lovely pair, always so busy."

这时，一位住在他们楼上的老邻居蹒跚着走进来，拐杖敲击瓷砖发出清脆的响声。“早啊，你们俩！真是恩爱的一对，每天都这么忙。”

Miyetsuko greeted her warmly, her eyes crinkling in that familiar, sweet way. She helped the grandmother pick out the freshest milk and a bunch of grapes, her movements natural and caring. Fujiwara handled the checkout, chatting easily about the weather. There was no grandeur, no violence, and certainly no cult-like devotion—just two people, a store, and the quiet, steady hum of a Tuesday morning.

美悦子热情地迎接她，眼角带着标志性的甜美笑意。她帮老奶奶挑选了最鲜的牛奶和一串葡萄，动作自然而贴心。藤原熟练地结账，随口聊着今天的天气。这里没有浮华，没有暴力，更没有那种狂热的膜拜——只有两个人，一家店，以及周二早晨那宁静而稳定的生活节奏。

[Learning Point]

Inventory: 库存。

Wholesaler: 批发商。

Regular customer: 常客。

Toddled in: 蹒跚着走进来。

Steady hum: 稳定的节奏/喧闹。

Chapter 84: The Investigation of Shadows

发布时间：2026/6/19 12:13:38

第八十四章：阴影下的真相

Rin spent the next few days in a fog of misplaced maternal anxiety. The image of Aya, that vibrant, sharp-tongued sister of hers, hanging onto the withered arm of an old man, wouldn't leave her mind.

接下来的几天，铃一直笼罩在某种错位的母性焦虑中。绫——那个充满活力又毒舌的妹妹，挽着一个枯萎老头手臂的画面，始终在她脑海里挥之不去。

Fujiwara, ever the loyal partner, decided to play the private investigator. He spent forty-eight hours lurking near the entrance of the public bath and prowling the neighborhood—a task he performed with a dedication usually reserved for calibrating Anki decks.

藤原一如既往地展现了他的忠诚，决定客串一把私家侦探。他花了四十八小时徘徊在浴场门口和附近的街区——这份执着，通常只在他校准 Anki 卡片组时才会出现。

On the third night, he returned to the hotel, pulling Rin into the quiet corner of the suite.

第三天晚上，他回到酒店，把铃拉到套房的角落。

"I found out the truth," he whispered.

“我查到真相了，”他低声说。

"Is he... is he some kind of predator? Does he have a yacht? A secret fortune?" Rin asked, her hands clenched nervously in her lap.

“他……他是什么捕猎者吗？他有游艇？还是什么隐形富豪？”铃紧张地攥着膝盖问。

Fujiwara let out a short, hollow laugh. "Hardly. His name is Sato. He works as a security guard at the apartment complex next door. He’s about as wealthy as the dust in his own guard booth. He’s been a widower for years—he’s just lonely, and the lack of intimacy has left him desperate."

藤原发出一声短促而空洞的轻笑。“哪有什么游艇。他叫佐藤，是隔壁小区的看门保安。他穷得跟保安亭里的灰尘差不多。他丧偶多年——纯粹是太孤独了，长期缺乏亲密关系让他变得很绝望。”

Rin’s shoulders dropped, but she still looked troubled. "And Aya? Is she... is she in love with him?"

铃的双肩塌了下来，但看起来依然忧心忡忡。“那绫呢？她是……她爱上他了吗？”

Fujiwara shook his head. "Not even close. It’s a business transaction. Aya is working as a casual escort. She’s just doing it for pocket money. It’s transactional, once-off, and strictly for the cash. They’re strangers who meet to scratch an itch, and that’s the end of it."

藤原摇了摇头。“差远了。这只是场生意。绫在做那种偶尔性的陪侍工作。她只是为了赚点零花钱。这是交易性质的，一次性的，纯粹为了钱。他们只是两个陌生人聚在一起解决生理需求，结束后就各奔东西。”

The tension that had been coiled in Rin’s stomach for days snapped. She let out a long, shuddering breath, her posture softening instantly.

这几天一直紧绷在铃胃里的那根弦，“啪”地断了。她长长地呼出一口气，紧绷的姿态瞬间变得柔软下来。

"Oh," she murmured, a genuine, relieved smile spreading across her face. "So she’s just... she’s just being a practical, modern girl. She isn't trapped in some tragic romance with a ghost."

“噢，”她轻声喃喃，脸上浮现出一个真诚而如释重负的微笑，“所以她只是……只是个现实的现代女孩。她并没有陷在什么与幽灵共舞的悲剧爱情里。”

She leaned against Fujiwara, feeling an odd, warped sense of comfort. Her sister wasn't losing her mind; she was just being as messy and transactional as the rest of their bizarre "family."

她靠在藤原身上，心中涌起一股奇怪而扭曲的慰藉感。妹妹并没有疯；她只是像他们这一家古怪的“家庭”成员一样，活得乱七八糟又充满交易气息。

"You know," Rin said, her head resting on his chest, "I think I prefer that. It’s so much more logical. I was worried she was becoming sentimental."

“你知道吗，”铃枕着他的胸膛说，“我反而更喜欢这样。这逻辑通顺多了。我还担心她变得多愁善感呢。”

[Learning Point]

Lurking: 潜伏/徘徊。

Widower: 鳏夫。

Escort: 陪侍/伴游。

Transactional: 交易性的。

Sentimental: 多愁善感的。

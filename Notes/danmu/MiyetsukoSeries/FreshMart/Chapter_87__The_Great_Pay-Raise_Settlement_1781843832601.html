Chapter 87: The Great Pay-Raise Settlement

发布时间：2026/6/19 12:37:11

第八十七章：520日元的“宏大”调薪

The sudden chill in the room was replaced by a collective, awkward cough. Miyetsuko blinked, her predatory glare evaporating into a mischievous, slightly sheepish grin.

房间里那阵突如其来的寒意被一阵尴尬的咳嗽声取代。美悦子眨了眨眼，那股捕食者的凶光瞬间消散，转而换上了一副顽皮又略带羞愧的笑容。

"Wow," Miyetsuko chuckled, waving a hand dismissively. "Tough crowd. I was just practicing my 'ruthless corporate consultant' routine. You guys need to lighten up!"

“哇哦，”美悦子轻笑一声，漫不经心地挥了挥手，“反应也太强烈了吧。我只是在练习我的‘无情企业顾问’路数。你们几个也太不经逗了！”

Sayuri, who had been on the verge of tears, let out a shaky, relieved giggle. The room’s tension snapped like a dry twig, dissolving back into the familiar, goofy chaos of their dysfunctional little clan.

小百合原本已经眼眶泛红，此时终于发出一声颤抖的、如释重负的轻笑。房间里的紧绷感像枯枝一样折断，重新溶解回他们那个不正常却又熟悉的、傻里傻气的家庭闹剧里。

Fujiwara stood up, walking over to Sayuri and wrapping his arms around her waist, giving her a reassuring squeeze. "Look, I’ve been thinking. Miyetsuko is right about one thing—inflation is real. And honestly, Sayuri, your performance as the 'Royal Overseer' is top-tier."

藤原站起身，走过去环住小百合的腰，给了她一个安抚性的拥抱。“听着，我想过了。美悦子说得对，通货膨胀可是实打实的。而且说真的，小百合，你作为‘皇家监工’的表现简直是顶级水准。”

He reached into his pocket, pulled out a stack of loose coins, and dramatically placed a single extra twenty-yen coin into her hand.

他从口袋里掏出一叠零钱，夸张地往她手里郑重其事地放了一枚二十日元的硬币。

"Effective immediately," Fujiwara announced with the gravity of a CEO signing a merger, "your annual support contract is being bumped from five hundred to five hundred and twenty yen per year. That’s a four-percent increase! You’re basically a high-yield asset now."

“即刻生效，”藤原用 CEO 签署合并协议般的严肃语气宣布，“你的年度包养合同从每年五百日元上调至五百二十日元。这可是百分之四的涨幅！你现在基本上已经是高收益资产了。”

Sayuri stared at the coin, then looked up at him, her eyes dancing with joy. "Five-twenty? Oh, Fujiwara-san! Does this mean I get to upgrade my tea allowance?"

小百合盯着那枚硬币，随即抬头看向他，双眼闪烁着喜悦。“五百二？噢，藤原先生！那是不是意味着我的茶叶补贴可以升级了？”

"Sky's the limit, darling," Fujiwara joked, kissing her forehead. "Tonight, we celebrate. A fancy dinner, and then—who knows?—maybe we’ll even splurge on a new set of towels."

“你想买什么都行，宝贝，”藤原开玩笑地吻了吻她的额头，“今晚我们要好好庆祝一下。吃顿大餐，然后——谁知道呢——说不定我们还能奢侈一把，换套新毛巾。”

Rin, who had been listening with a beaming, sightless smile, clapped her hands. "Can we celebrate with a group bath? I promise to keep count of the soap suds!"

一直听着他们对话、笑得一脸灿烂的铃拍了拍手。“我们可以集体泡澡庆祝吗？我保证这次会好好数清楚泡沫的数量！”

"Absolutely not," Miyetsuko laughed, tossing a cushion at Rin. "Last time you tried to count, the bathtub overflowed because you were too busy feeling the bubbles! But I’m in for the dinner. And Fujiwara, since you’re such a big spender now, you’re buying the drinks."

“想都别想，”美悦子笑着，随手扔了个靠垫过去，“上次你尝试数数的时候，浴缸都要溢出来了，因为你光顾着感受那些泡泡了！不过晚餐我参加。还有藤原，既然你现在成了大款，酒水你包了。”

Fujiwara laughed, the "tragedy" of the morning feeling like a million miles away. They were a mess—broke, weird, and mathematically hopeless—but sitting there, bickering over a twenty-yen raise, they felt exactly like the sitcom cast they were meant to be.

藤原笑了起来，早晨的那场“悲剧”仿佛已是万里之遥。他们一团糟——穷困、古怪，数学逻辑还烂得要命——但此刻，看着他们为了二十日元的涨薪而斗嘴的模样，他们就像极了那本该出现在情景喜剧里的主角团。

[Learning Point]

Sheepish: 羞愧的/局促的。

Dismissively: 不屑地/漫不经心地。

Dysfunctional: 功能失调的（此处指性格各异、古怪的）。

Top-tier: 顶级的。

Bickering: 争吵/斗嘴。

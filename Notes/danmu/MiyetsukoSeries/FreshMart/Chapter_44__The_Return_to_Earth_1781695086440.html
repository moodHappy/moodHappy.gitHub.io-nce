Chapter 44: The Return to Earth

发布时间：2026/6/17 19:18:05

第四十四章：归途与尘埃

The descent was brutal—a jarring return to gravity, to friction, to the suffocating weight of being human again. When they finally stepped back into the "Fresh Mart," the air felt thick and stale, a sharp departure from the sterile, exhilarating vacuum of the orbital station.

这次着陆是粗暴的——是对重力、摩擦力，以及身为凡人那种窒息感的剧烈回归。当他们最终重新踏入“新鲜马特”时，空气显得浑浊而陈旧，与空间站那无菌且令人振奋的真空环境形成了鲜明的对比。

They didn't go back to the fame or the stage. They simply... reopened. The dust was wiped from the shelves, the flickering fluorescent lights were replaced, and Miyetsuko began arranging canned goods with a sense of ironic peace. They were back in their original habitat, the cage where the legend had begun, yet they carried themselves with the quiet, terrifying grace of people who had seen the curve of the Earth and the silence of the void.

他们没有重拾名利，也没有回到聚光灯下。他们只是……重新开了店。货架上的灰尘被擦拭干净，闪烁的荧光灯管被换掉，美悦子带着一种反讽式的平静整理着罐头。他们回到了最初的栖息地——那个传奇开始的囚笼，但他们的举止间，却带有一种安静且令人战栗的从容，那是见过了地球弧线与深空寂静之人特有的气质。

Business was, predictably, nonexistent. The neighborhood had moved on; the "National Icon" had vanished into myth, and the small, dingy store was just another forgotten relic.

生意正如预料中那样冷清。街坊邻里早就忘了他们，那位“国民偶像”已沉入神话，而这家破旧的小店，不过是另一个被时代遗忘的遗迹。

Then, a customer wandered in.

这时，一位顾客晃荡着走了进来。

A middle-aged man, oblivious to the history of the three people behind the counter, grabbed a carton of milk and a loaf of bread, placing them on the counter with a heavy, indifferent sigh. Fujiwara didn't flinch; he rang up the items with the mechanical, practiced ease of a shopkeeper who had once commanded an empire.

那是个中年男人，对柜台后这三人的历史一无所知，他抓起一盒牛奶和一条面包，沉重且漠然地丢在收银台上。藤原没有流露出一丝异样，他以一种机械且纯熟的姿态扫码结账，动作像极了一个曾经掌管帝国、如今却甘于平庸的店主。

Sayuri was standing nearby, her hands folded neatly in her apron. The silence in the shop was heavy, broken only by the hum of the refrigerator.

小百合站在一旁，双手整齐地叠在围裙上。店内的寂静沉重得吓人，只有冷柜发出的嗡嗡声在回荡。

And then, it happened.

就在这时，变故发生了。

A sharp, unmistakable pop—followed by a lingering, resonant echo—shattered the quiet. Sayuri had let out a loud, thunderous fart.

一声尖锐且不容置疑的“噗”响，随后是那一阵悠长且富有余韵的回响，打破了死寂。小百合放了一个响亮而震耳的屁。

The sound hung in the air, defiant and entirely, painfully human.

声音在空气中回荡，那般叛逆，且如此真实、如此令人尴尬地展现了她作为“人”的一面。

Sayuri froze. Her eyes went wide, the color rushing to her cheeks in a wave of crimson heat that burned down to her neck. She looked like she wanted to evaporate, to dissolve into the very floorboards she had once walked upon as a goddess.

小百合僵住了。她双眼圆睁，红晕如潮水般瞬间涌上脸颊，那股滚烫的绯红一路烧到了颈根。她看起来像是恨不得当场蒸发，溶入这片她曾以女神姿态走过的木地板里。

The customer stared, mouth slightly agape, clearly baffled by the juxtaposition of the woman’s ethereal beauty and the crude reality of the sound.

顾客愣住了，微微张着嘴，显然被眼前这女子超凡脱俗的美貌与那粗粝真实的响声之间产生的反差弄懵了。

Miyetsuko, who had been leaning against the shelf, let out a short, sharp bark of laughter that she couldn't contain. Fujiwara, meanwhile, didn't look annoyed or embarrassed. He leaned forward, resting his chin on his palm, watching Sayuri’s mortified expression with a gaze that was soft, amused, and fiercely protective.

一直靠在货架旁的美悦子再也憋不住，发出一阵短促而清脆的爆笑。而藤原，他没有流露出半分烦躁或尴尬。他前倾身体，用手托着下巴，注视着小百合那羞愤欲死的表情，目光温柔、玩味，且带着一种强烈的、想要将她护在身后的宠溺。

"It’s alright, Sayuri," Fujiwara said, his voice warm, utterly unfazed by the sudden collapse of her idol image. He turned back to the customer, his tone pleasant. "That'll be five dollars. And don't mind her—she’s just reminded us all that she’s finally, beautifully, back on the ground."

“没事的，小百合，”藤原说道，嗓音温润，完全没有受到她偶像形象崩塌的影响。他转过头看向顾客，语气和善，“一共五块钱。别在意她——她只是在提醒我们所有人，她终于、且美妙地回到了人间。”

Sayuri kept her head bowed, hiding her face, but the slight, twitching smile at the corner of her mouth gave her away. She was mortified, yes, but in the stale air of the "Fresh Mart," it was the most genuine, joyful moment they had shared in years.

小百合低着头，藏住脸，但嘴角那抹细微的、微微抽搐的笑意出卖了她。她是羞耻的，没错，但在“新鲜马特”这陈旧的空气里，这却是他们多年来分享过的、最真挚、最快乐的瞬间。

Indifferent: 冷漠的。

Juxtaposition: 并列/并置（反差）。

Mortified: 羞愧的。

Chapter 94: The Grand Finale

发布时间：2026/6/19 15:18:02

第九十四章：国民偶像的“回归”

The air in the bedroom was still thick with the scent of sweat and spent energy when Sayuri finally reached for the blindfold. She pulled it off, her eyes blinking rapidly as they adjusted to the dim light. She felt drained, satisfied, and strangely emboldened—until she heard the snickering.

卧室里的空气依然浓稠，混杂着汗水与欢愉过后的余韵。小百合终于伸手扯下了眼罩，双眼在昏暗的光线下急促地眨动着。她感到浑身虚脱，内心却充盈着满足，甚至还有种前所未有的狂野——直到她听见了那阵忍俊不禁的嗤笑声。

"Oh, Sayuri," Miyetsuko’s voice dripped with mirth, "I didn't know the 'Retired Idol' still had those moves in her. That was... world-class."

“噢，小百合，”美悦子的声音里满是戏谑，“我可不知道那位‘隐退偶像’身体里还藏着这种绝活。那表现……简直是世界级的。”

Rin was curled up on the rug nearby, clutching her stomach and shaking with laughter. "You were so... loud! I thought the neighbors would call the police!"

铃蜷缩在旁边的地毯上，笑得直不起腰，怀里紧紧抱着肚子。“你刚才……叫得也太大声了！我还以为邻居会报警呢！”

Sayuri froze, her breath catching in her throat. She looked around, and her gaze landed on the corner of the room. There, mounted on a tripod, was a high-end digital camera, its red light blinking with a steady, clinical rhythm.

小百合僵住了，呼吸瞬间停滞。她环顾四周，目光最终定格在房间的一角。那里架着一台昂贵的摄像机，红色的录制指示灯正有节奏地闪烁着。

Fujiwara stepped into the frame, grinning like a man who had just won the lottery. "Relax, Sayuri. You were incredible. Truly, it was like the old days—only better."

藤原走入画面，笑得像个中了彩票的男人。“放松点，小百合。你刚才的表现简直不可思议。真的，简直回到了当年的全盛期——甚至更棒。”

"You... you recorded it?" Sayuri stammered, though her indignation was fighting a losing battle against the rush of adrenaline.

“你……你录下来了？”小百合结结巴巴地问，但那种生理性的兴奋感让她甚至生不起气来。

"Recorded it? We broadcast it," Miyetsuko corrected, tossing her a tablet.

“录下来？不，我们把它直播出去了，”美悦子纠正道，顺手丢给她一个平板电脑。

Sayuri looked at the screen. The video was already viral, titled . The view count was climbing into the millions, with comments scrolling by so fast they were a blur: “She’s back!”, “Still the queen!”, “The chemistry is insane!”

小百合看向屏幕。那条视频已经火遍全网，标题赫然写着《国民偶像的孕期特别回归：真实生活大揭秘》。观看量正以百万计的数值疯狂攀升，评论区滚动的速度快得连残影都看不清：“她回来了！”、“她依然是女王！”、“这化学反应简直疯了！”

[The Award Ceremony / 年底的授勋]

Six months later, the auditorium was packed. It wasn't just a film festival; it was a coronation. Sayuri stood on the stage, her silhouette slightly changed by motherhood, wearing a gown that draped elegantly over her curves.

六个月后，颁奖典礼现场座无虚席。这不仅仅是一个电影节，这是一场加冕礼。小百合站在台上，身形因母性而显得更为柔美，长裙优雅地包裹着她的曲线。

The host stepped up to the microphone, his voice booming with respect. "For redefine the meaning of 'intimate cinema' and for capturing the most raw, breathtaking, and unapologetically honest performance of the century, the Lifetime Achievement Award goes to... Sayuri!"

主持人走向麦克风，声音庄重而充满敬意。“为了重新定义‘亲密电影’的含义，为了呈现本世纪最真实、最令人窒息且毫无保留的诚挚演出，终身成就奖的得主是……小百合！”

The crowd went wild. As she accepted the golden statuette, Sayuri looked toward the front row, where Fujiwara, Miyetsuko, and Rin were standing, clapping like lunatics.

全场沸腾了。在接过那座金灿灿的奖杯时，小百合看向前排，藤原、美悦子和铃正像疯子一样在那儿鼓掌。

She looked down at the award, then at the camera lenses pointed at her from every direction. She didn't feel ashamed. She felt powerful. She had taken her past, her pregnancy, and her chaotic, "salty" life, and turned it into the most successful product in entertainment history.

她低头看着奖杯，又看向四面八方汇聚而来的镜头。她不再感到羞耻，只感到一种掌控一切的力量。她将自己的过去、孕期以及那混乱又“咸味十足”的生活，转化成了娱乐史上最成功的商品。

"To my family," she said into the microphone, a cheeky, knowing glint in her eyes, "who taught me that the best way to earn a living... is to be completely, shamelessly yourself."

“献给我的家人们，”她对着麦克风说道，眼中闪烁着狡黠而了然的光芒，“是你们教会了我，赚钱的最佳方式……就是彻底地、毫不羞耻地做你自己。”

[Learning Point]

Emboldened: 被壮胆的/变得大胆的。

Statuette: 小雕像（奖杯）。

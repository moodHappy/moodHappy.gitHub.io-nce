Chapter 107: The Ring Toss Gamble

发布时间：2026/6/16 06:23:50

第一百零七章：套圈的赌局

The night market was a riot of color and noise, the air thick with the irresistible aroma of grilled squid and caramelized sugar. Miyetsuko tugged on Fujiwara’s sleeve, her eyes sparkling like polished glass as she pointed at a stall laden with cheap, oversized plush toys. "Hey, Fujiwara," she chimed, her voice light and teasing. "I want that stuffed bear. Be a dear and win it for me, will you?"

夜市里色彩斑斓，人声鼎沸，空气中弥漫着烤鱿鱼和焦糖那令人无法抗拒的香气。美悦子拽了拽藤原的衣袖，双眼如打磨过的玻璃般闪烁，指向不远处摆满廉价大号毛绒玩具的摊位。“喂，藤原，”她轻声唤道，声音轻快而俏皮，“我想要那个玩偶。你去帮我赢回来，好吗？”

Fujiwara followed her gaze to the gaudy, prize-laden stall, then turned back to her with a roguish, playful smirk. He leaned in close, his voice dropping to a mischievous, private murmur that made her cheeks flush instantly. "That's a tall order," he hummed, his hand brushing against his groin in a light, cheeky gesture. "Tell you what: unless you promise to give my two 'big balls' some serious service tonight, I’m not sure I’ve got the motivation to win it."

藤原顺着她的目光看去，又转过头，嘴角挂着一抹玩世不恭的坏笑。他凑近她，声音压低，变成了一种只有两人能听见的恶作剧般的呢喃，瞬间让美悦子双颊通红。“这要求可不低啊，”他嘟囔着，手暧昧地拂过裆部，比划出一个调戏的动作，“这样吧，除非你答应今晚好好服侍我的那两颗‘大丸子’，否则我可没动力去赢它。”

Miyetsuko burst into a fit of giggles, swatting his shoulder with a playful glare. "You're incorrigible! We're in public!" she whispered, though she leaned into him, her smile wide and genuine. "Fine, fine. You win the bear, and... I’ll give you whatever 'service' you want."

美悦子忍不住咯咯笑了起来，娇嗔地瞪了他一眼，拍了拍他的肩膀。“你真是无可救药！这可是在公共场合！”她低声耳语，身体却顺势依偎向他，笑容灿烂而真诚，“行行行，只要你赢了那个玩偶，我就……给你你想要的任何‘服侍’。”

Fujiwara let out a triumphant laugh and strode toward the stall, picking up a handful of plastic rings. He tossed them with a casual grace, his eyes locked on the target. Clink! The ring landed perfectly over the neck of the bear. The vendor cheered, and Miyetsuko clapped her hands, her heart feeling light and bouncy, just like the bear she had just won. It wasn't about the prize anymore; it was about the silly, sweet rhythm of a life that felt, for the first time, truly their own.

藤原得意地大笑一声，大步走向摊位，抓起一把塑料圈。他漫不经心地随手一掷，目光紧锁目标。当啷！ 圈子精准地套在了玩偶的脖子上。摊主欢呼起来，美悦子开心地鼓起掌，心跳轻盈而雀跃，就像她刚刚赢得的那只小熊一样。这已经不再是奖品的问题了，而是那种傻里傻气又甜蜜的节奏——这是她第一次感到，生活终于真正属于了他们。

[Learning Point]

Roguish, playful smirk: 玩世不恭的坏笑。

Tall order: 苛刻的要求。

Groin: 裆部。

Incorrigible: 无可救药的。

Light and bouncy: 轻盈雀跃的。

Chapter 27: The Morning After the Dream

发布时间：2026/6/20 01:26:56

第二十七章：梦醒后的烂摊子

Fujiwara stared at the phone, his heart sinking with every mechanical tu-tu-tu that echoed through the small, cramped room like a digital heartbeat. The call remained unanswered. Exhaustion, having finally caught up with his erratic adrenaline, pulled him under. He crumpled sideways onto the mattress, instantly sinking into a heavy, coma-like slumber beside the still-posed Rin.

电话里传来“嘟、嘟、嘟”的空洞声，像是一颗毫无回应的电子心脏，在狭窄的房间里回荡。藤原那仅存的愤怒配额在刚才那一吼之后彻底耗尽。他那紧绷了一个小时的脊梁瞬间垮掉，整个人横着瘫倒在床垫上，紧挨着姿势还没调整过来的铃，沉入了一场类似昏迷的深沉睡眠。

Rin, still holding the phone in a death grip, eventually let her arms drop. The silence returned to the room, punctured only by the ragged, synchronized snoring of the pair.

铃死死攥着手机的手终于松开。房间里重归死寂，只剩下两人杂乱而同步的鼾声。

Miyetsuko was the first to rise, her biological clock as precise as a Swiss watch. She drifted through the hallway, robes flowing, looking for her morning coffee. She poked her head into Rin’s room, expecting to find the blind girl alone. Instead, she found a tangled, fleshy pile of limbs and sheets, with Fujiwara dead to the world, snoring like a lumberjack.

美悦子是全家起得最早的，她的生物钟精准得像瑞士表。她披着长袍穿过走廊，本想找早间咖啡，却在推开铃的房门时愣住了。她本以为能看到铃独自一人，没想到却看见了一团纠缠不清、肉感十足的肢体和床单，而藤原就像头累瘫的猪，正打着震天响的呼噜。

Miyetsuko blinked. She leaned against the doorframe, a single, eloquent question mark hovering over her head.

美悦子眨了眨眼，靠在门框上，脑门上仿佛挂着一个硕大的问号：“？？？”

"Seriously?" she muttered to the empty air. "The guy just finished his midnight shift with Sayuri, and he’s already back on the clock? The man is a physiological anomaly. A tireless, walking battery."

“认真的吗？”她对着虚空喃喃道，“这货半夜刚在小百合那儿下了班，这会儿又排上队了？这男人的生理结构简直是个异类。简直就是个不知疲倦的移动电池。”

She shook her head, not entirely surprised, but deeply impressed by the sheer, reckless audacity of his libido. She didn't wake them. She simply turned around and headed to the kitchen to start the breakfast that no one would likely eat.

她摇了摇头，虽然并不意外，但对这种肆无忌惮的旺盛精力还是深表“敬佩”。她没叫醒他们，只是转身走向厨房，开始准备那顿大概没人会吃的早餐。

By noon, the household was awake, but the mystery of the "Tractor Tycoon" remained. The phone calls to Aya continued to return nothing but the hollow, mocking silence of an empty inbox.

到了中午，全家人都醒了，但“拖拉机大亨”的下落依然是个谜。打给绫的电话依旧无人接听，只有那空洞的、嘲弄般的忙音在回荡。

"She’s ghosting us," Fujiwara sighed over a bowl of cold, congealed oatmeal. "Either she’s actually dead, or she’s so deep into the high-frequency trading abyss that she’s transcended the need for biological relatives."

“她把咱们拉黑了，”藤原看着碗里凉透的燕麦粥，叹了口气，“要么她真死了，要么她已经深陷那个高频交易的深渊，进化到不需要生物学亲戚的地步了。”

"Maybe she's really at the tractor factory," Rin whispered, still feeling a bit sore from the morning's confusion.

“也许她真的在拖拉机厂呢，”铃小声嘟囔，还没从早上的混乱中缓过劲来。

Sayuri shrugged, nursing the "Toilet Saint" who was currently trying to eat his own foot. "If she’s gone, she’s gone. She left us enough money to turn the 'Fresh Mart' into a palace of silicone. Who needs an aunt who speaks in metaphors anyway?"

小百合耸了耸肩，怀里的“马桶圣子”正试图把自己的脚趾头塞进嘴里。“走了就走了呗。她留下的钱足够把‘新鲜马特’改成硅胶宫殿了。谁稀罕一个满嘴比喻的小姨子啊？”

They settled back into their bizarre, rhythmic lives, the trauma of the night fading into the familiar, chaotic noise of their existence. Aya had vanished as quickly as she had appeared, leaving behind a fortune, a few lingering questions, and a house full of people who had long since stopped questioning why they were even together.

他们重新回到了那种奇葩而规律的生活轨道上，夜晚的创伤很快就消融在那种熟悉的、混乱的日常琐碎中。绫来得快，去得也快，留下了一笔巨款、一串没解开的疑问，和一屋子早就懒得去追究“为什么我们还凑在一起”的人。

Chapter 77: The Tears of the Cheetah

发布时间：2026/6/14 22:25:04

第七十七章：猎豹的泪水

The door to Miyetsuko’s apartment was unlocked, a testament to how little she cared about her own safety now. She was sitting in the dark, the silence of the room punctuated only by the distant, rhythmic ticking of a clock. She didn't turn when the door creaked open. She didn't need to. She knew the cadence of his footsteps.

美悦子家门没锁，这足以证明她现在有多么不在乎自己的安危。她坐在黑暗中，房间里的寂静只有远处钟摆规律的滴答声。门发出吱呀声时她没有回头，因为她太熟悉那个步伐的节奏了。

Fujiwara entered, his silhouette stark against the light of the hallway. When he saw her—disheveled, bruised, and radiating a cold, brittle emptiness—his posture faltered.

藤原走进来，身影在走廊的灯光下显得格外落寞。当他看清她——衣衫不整、满身淤青，浑身散发着一种冷冽而脆弱的空虚时，他的身形晃了晃。

It didn't take long for him to find out. Through the network of office rumors, through the digital footprints she hadn't scrubbed, and through the sheer, gut-wrenching intuition of a man who had obsessed over her for months. He had seen the clips. He had heard the stories.

没花太久他就查清了一切。通过办公室的流言、她没来得及清理的数字足迹，以及作为一个已对她痴迷数月的男人那精准得令人心碎的直觉。他看到了那些片段，听闻了那些恶言。

The "Logistics Cheetah," the man who played games of power and pride, fell to his knees in the center of her living room. He didn't look like a genius or a tormentor. He looked like a boy who had just lost the world.

那位曾在权力与自尊的游戏中运筹帷幄的“物流猎豹”，此时此刻却瘫跪在她客厅中央。他看起来不再像个天才或施虐者，而像个刚刚失去了全世界的男孩。

Tears, hot and unbidden, tracked through the dust on his cheeks. He was a poor man in a world of wolves; he had no money to buy justice, no influence to crush the man who had defiled her. He was, for all his sharpness, utterly, wretchedly powerless.

灼热而无法抑制的泪水划过他布满灰尘的脸颊。在这个弱肉强食的世界里，他只是个穷小子；他没有钱去购买正义，也没有权力去摧毁那个玷污了她的男人。尽管他那么聪明，此刻却显得如此无能且卑微。

"I'm sorry," he choked out, the words tearing through his throat. "I'm so... so sorry."

“对不起，”他哽咽着，每一个字都像在撕裂喉咙。“我真的……真的对不起。”

He crawled toward her, not with the arrogance of a lover, but with the desperation of a drowning man reaching for a shore. He rested his head against her knees, his sobs shaking his small, sturdy frame.

他向她爬去，不再带着恋人的傲慢，而像个溺水者拼命抓向岸边。他将头靠在她的膝盖上，啜泣声震动着他那窄小而结实的身体。

"He can take whatever he wants," he whispered, his voice shattered. "He can take your body, he can take your Fridays. But he can't take us. To me, you’re still... you’re still my little bird. My sweet Miyetsuko. Nothing has changed. Not to me."

“他可以拿走他想要的一切，”他低语，声音破碎。“他可以拿走你的身体，拿走你的周五。但他拿不走‘我们’。对我而言，你仍然是……你仍然是我那只小鸟依人的美悦子。什么都没变。对我来说，一切都未曾改变。”

Miyetsuko looked down at him. The coldness that had protected her for the last few hours began to dissolve, replaced by a searing, painful tenderness. He was a fool to forgive a woman who felt this stained, but in his madness, she found the only warmth she had felt in seventy hours.

美悦子低下头看着他。过去几小时里保护着她的那层寒冰开始消融，取而代之的是一阵灼热且酸涩的温柔。去原谅一个觉得自己早已遍体鳞伤的女人，他真是个傻瓜，但在这场疯狂中，她找到了这七十个小时里唯一的暖意。

[Learning Point]

Cadence: 节奏/韵律。

Stark: 鲜明的/明显的。

Wretchedly: 卑微地/可怜地。

Shattered: 破碎的（形容声音）。

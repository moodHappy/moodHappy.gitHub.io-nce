第三十四章：劫匪的头套困境

The "Fresh Mart" had risen from the ashes like a neon-lit phoenix, though the shelves were mostly stocked with cheap dreams and questionable inventory. Old Wang stood in front of the chilled goods section, staring at a stack of cotton underwear with the intensity of a philosopher contemplating the void. The mounting pressure of inflation was crushing his spirit; he needed a solution, and he needed it to be drastic.

全新的“新鲜玛特”像一只霓虹凤凰般在废墟上拔地而起，尽管货架上塞满了廉价的幻梦和来路不明的库存。老王站在冷柜前，盯着那一摞纯棉内裤陷入了沉思，眼神深邃得像是在思考宇宙的虚无。物价上涨的压力像磨盘一样碾压着他的神经；他需要一个方案，一个极其狂野的方案。

He ripped open a package, pulled out a pair featuring delicate lace trim, and jammed it onto his head. It was a makeshift mask, an attempt to hide his face—and his existential dread—from the world.

他撕开包装，掏出一条带蕾丝花边的内裤，生硬地套在头上。这是一个临时面具，试图以此遮住他的脸，也试图遮住他对这操蛋世界的极度焦虑。

Rin, the cashier, watched him with the emotional range of a stone gargoyle. Her voice, crisp and devoid of any human warmth, cut through the silence. "Sir, those are this season’s newest arrivals. They aren't meant to be used as headgear."

收银员铃面无表情地看着他，像尊石雕的怪兽。她的声音清脆且毫无温度，像刀锋一样割开了空气：“先生，这是当季新品，不建议作头饰。”

Wang grunted, muffled by the lace. "What if they weren't new? What if I wanted the ones that have been... 'seasoned' by use? Sell me what you're wearing right now."

老王嘟囔着，声音被蕾丝闷得瓮声瓮气。“那要是不是新品呢？要是我想买那种……被‘润色’过的旧货呢？把你现在穿的卖给我。”

Rin blinked, her blind eyes unreadable. "I am wearing the new arrivals, sir. Ask Miyetsuko."

铃眨了眨眼，那双盲眼看不出任何情绪。“我穿的是新品，先生。去问美悦子吧。”

Miyetsuko, who was currently reorganizing the adult magazine rack, didn't even turn around. "Don't look at me, Wang. I’m currently experimenting with 'no-panties' minimalism. It’s an efficiency thing."

美悦子正忙着整理成人杂志架，头都没回。“别看我，老王。我最近在尝试‘无底裤’极简主义。这叫提升效率。”

Desperate, Wang cornered Sayuri near the diaper aisle. She was rocking the "Toilet Saint" in a makeshift carrier. As she looked at Wang, she felt a sudden, familiar wave of warmth between her thighs—a moist, humid sensation of pure, spontaneous biology. She sighed, gently set the infant down on a pile of discounted tissues, and began to slowly, languidly peel off her own garment.

老王彻底绝望了，他在尿布区截住了小百合。她正摇晃着“马桶圣子”。当她看到老王时，突然感到一阵熟悉的湿热感——那是一种纯粹而自发的、生物性的潮湿。她叹了口气，把婴儿轻轻放在一堆打折的纸巾上，开始慢悠悠、慵懒地褪下自己的内裤。

It was a masterpiece of erotic engineering—a scrap of lace so minimal it weighed less than an ounce of silk. She handed it over, her expression as blank as a blank check.

那简直是情趣工艺的巅峰之作——一条丝线不足一两的极品情趣内裤。她递了过来，神情坦然得像是一张白纸。

Wang took the damp, warm scrap in his calloused hands. He stared at it, then at the security camera, then back at the tiny, translucent shred of fabric.

老王用那双长满老茧的手接过这丝带着温热潮湿的物件。他盯着看了一会儿，看看监控探头，又看看这块近乎透明的布条。

"What is this?" he shouted, throwing the lace onto the floor in disgust. "How am I supposed to rob a bank with this? It’s not a mask; it’s a dental floss! If I wear this, they’ll identify me by my pores! Can’t you people provide a proper, full-coverage piece of laundry?!"

“这算什么？”他怒吼道，嫌弃地把那条丝线丢在地上，“你让我怎么拿这玩意儿去抢银行？这不是面罩，这是牙线！我要是套上这个，连脸上的毛孔都遮不住，一眼就被认出来了！你们这儿就没点覆盖面积大的正经衣服吗？！”

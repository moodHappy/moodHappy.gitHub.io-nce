Chapter 29: The Signal from the Summit

发布时间：2026/6/20 01:42:26

第二十九章：来自巅峰的越洋通话

The "Fresh Mart" was in the middle of a post-vacuum-trauma recovery session when Rin’s phone, still wet from its accidental dip in the fish tank earlier that week, buzzed with the force of a tectonic shift. It wasn't just a ringtone; it was a screeching, digital distress signal.

“新鲜马特”正处于“吸尘器创伤”的余震恢复期，铃那台前几天刚掉进鱼缸里的手机，突然剧烈震动起来，力道大得像是一场地震。那不是普通的铃声，简直是一种刺耳的数字求救信号。

Rin lunged for it, nearly knocking over the "Toilet Saint" in the process. She tapped the screen, and there she was: Aya. But the connection was abysmal. The video flickered like a dying strobe light, showing Aya against a backdrop of jagged, snow-capped peaks.

铃扑向手机，差点把“马桶圣子”给撞翻。她点了接听，屏幕上出现了绫的身影。但信号烂得一塌糊涂，画面像坏掉的频闪灯一样抽动，背景里是参差不齐的雪山。

"Aya!" Rin yelled at the pixelated ghost. "You bitch, you're alive! Are you actually working at a tractor factory or are you—"

“绫！”铃对着那个像素化的人影吼道，“你这臭婆娘，你居然还活着！你到底是在拖拉机厂干活，还是……”

Aya’s voice chopped in and out, sounding like a demon speaking through a blender. "I... enough money... buy... New York... signal... cutting out!" She gestured vaguely at the horizon, where golden sunlight hit the peaks. "I saw your call... missed it... roaming charges are a bitch... didn't want to burn the profit."

绫的声音断断续续，听起来像个恶魔在搅拌机里说话。“我……钱够多……买下……纽约……信号……太烂了！”她随手指向地平线，金色的阳光洒在山巅。“我看到姐姐的……来电……没接……漫游费太坑了……我可不想烧掉那点利润。”

Fujiwara and Miyetsuko crowded around the screen, squinting at the frozen, jagged images of Aya’s smug, triumphant face.

藤原和美悦子挤在屏幕前，眯着眼盯着绫那张傲慢而得意的、时断时续的脸。

"Wait!" Fujiwara shouted, pushing his nose against the screen. "Buy New York? What are you—"

“等等！”藤原大喊，鼻子都快贴到屏幕上了，“买下纽约？你到底在——”

"Had a dream!" Aya cut him off, her image freezing in a particularly unflattering contortion. "Last night... felt like you were in trouble, sister. Something about a vacuum cleaner? Whatever. Just wanted to... check in. Don't... spend it all on silicone!"

“做了个梦！”绫打断他，画面恰好定格在一个极其扭曲的丑表情上。“昨晚……觉得你有难，姐姐。什么吸尘器之类的？管他呢。就是想……确认一下。别……全把钱花在硅胶上！”

The connection dropped. The screen went black, leaving the "Fresh Mart" crew staring at their own reflections, dumbfounded.

通话中断。屏幕彻底黑了，留下“新鲜马特”的一伙人对着自己的倒影面面相觑，一脸懵逼。

Miyetsuko rubbed her temples, looking at the dead phone. "She’s either the greatest visionary of our time or the most sophisticated con artist to ever come out of this gene pool."

美悦子揉着太阳穴，看着那台死掉的手机。“她要么是咱们这个时代最伟大的远见者，要么就是这个基因库里产出的、最精明的诈骗犯。”

Sayuri hugged the baby, looking genuinely touched. "But she cared! She had a dream about your hair getting stuck, Rin! That’s, like, telepathic sister-bonding."

小百合抱着孩子，一脸感动。“可她关心你呀！她居然梦到你头发被卷进去了，铃！这简直是心电感应式的姐妹羁绊。”

Rin sat on the floor, clutching the phone to her chest. She looked a bit less angry and a bit more confused. "She said she has enough money to buy New York. Do you think she meant, like, the whole city, or just the state?"

铃坐在地板上，把手机紧紧贴在胸口。她看起来没那么气了，反倒更加困惑。“她说她赚的钱够买下纽约。你们觉得她是说整个城市，还是指整个州？”

Fujiwara walked to the window and looked out at the street, which was still dotted with men walking around with their "Sayuri Juicers." He sighed deeply.

藤原走到窗边，看着外面的街道。街上依然到处是拿着“小百合榨汁机”游荡的男人们。他长叹一口气。

"It doesn't matter," he said, turning back to the room. "If she’s really that rich, she can buy us all out of this town. Until then, we’ve got a massive pile of silicone to sell and a town of idiots to paint white. Back to work, everyone."

“这不重要，”他转身对屋里的人说，“如果她真那么有钱，她能把咱们从这个镇子里赎出去。在那之前，咱们还有一堆硅胶没卖掉，还有一整镇的蠢货等着‘刷墙’呢。都干活去。”

Abysmal: 极坏的（信号）。

Strobe light: 频闪灯。

Roaming charges: 漫游费。

Visionary: 有远见的人。

Telepathic: 心灵感应的。

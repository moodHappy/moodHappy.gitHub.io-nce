第四十三章：排气管与钢铁之恋

The package from Aya arrived without a note, containing nothing but a jagged, heat-scored length of heavy-duty tractor exhaust pipe. Rin, in one of her habitual bouts of impulsive curiosity, decided it was a massage device. She tucked it away beneath her skirt, aiming for a moment of solitude, but gravity and anatomy conspired against her. The steel pipe wedged firmly against her pubic bone, a structural mismatch that refused to budge.

绫寄来的包裹里没有留言，只有一截火烧火燎、粗粝沉重的拖拉机排气管。铃照例脑回路错位，把它当成了按摩棒塞进了裙底，结果那是何等硬核的灾难——金属管道在耻骨处死死卡住，拔不出来。

For the next seven days, Rin became a walking, clanking percussion instrument. Every step she took in the "Fresh Mart" was announced by a harsh, grating screech of metal against floor tiles. She dragged the pipe everywhere, a rhythmic, abrasive soundtrack to the store’s descent into madness.

在接下来的七天里，铃变成了一个会走路的金属打击乐器。她在“新鲜玛特”里的每一步，都伴随着金属与瓷砖刺耳的摩擦声。她拖着这根铁管到处晃悠，那尖锐的金属剐蹭声，成了这家店迈向毁灭的背景音乐。

Fujiwara, already frayed by the aftermath of the Senbei Apocalypse, tried to initiate intimacy on the third night. He lasted five minutes before the metallic clink-clunk against his own pelvis broke his spirit. He sighed, defeated, and crawled out of bed.

藤原在“仙贝末日”的余波中本就心力交瘁，这晚试图行房，结果每动一下，那根排气管就发出“叮当”一声，砸在他胯骨上。他彻底泄了气，长叹一声爬下床去。

He sought refuge in Sayuri’s room, but she was busy cradling the "Toilet Saint." She didn't even look up. She tossed a small, synthetic object at him. "It’s a leftover from the promotional event, Fujiwara. My own custom-molded 'Sayuri-brand' masturbator. Same dimensions, same tactile experience. Go play with yourself."

他转头找小百合，小百合正哄着圣子，眼皮都没抬，甩手丢给他一个硅胶制品：“上次活动剩下的，小百合牌飞机杯。尺寸跟我一模一样，你自己玩去吧。”

Rejected, he slunk to Miyetsuko’s lab. She was neck-deep in a vat of human waste, meticulously calibrating an "Edible Excretion Standard." She didn't even turn around, her voice dripping with clinical boredom. "I’m busy, Fujiwara. I’m trying to turn this into a gourmet experience. Your carnal urges are such a pedestrian, vulgar hobby. Leave me to my science."

被拒后，他溜达到美悦子的实验室。她正埋头在一缸排泄物里，精细地校准着“食用级排泄标准”。她头也没回，声音里透着临床级的冷漠：“别烦我，我正忙着把这东西变成美食体验。你那点低俗的肉欲，简直是对我科学事业的亵渎。”

When Rin finally managed to extract the exhaust pipe—a gruesome ordeal involving a liter of industrial grease—Fujiwara had undergone a metamorphosis. He was no longer the man who wanted intimacy; he was a man possessed by the machine.

当铃终于历经磨难，用一升工业润滑油把那根铁管从身体里“卸载”下来时，藤原已经变了一个人。他不再渴求那种软绵绵的温存，而是彻底被机器本身给洗脑了。

He stood in the center of the store, the exhaust pipe clamped between his knees. He was scrubbing his own anatomy against the cold, charred surface of the pipe with a frantic, rhythmic intensity. The friction was raw, the metal was unforgiving, and the noise it made was a metallic hymn to his own decline. He didn't need the women anymore. He had found his soulmate in the tractor, and he was going to grind his humanity away until only the steel remained.

他站在店中央，双腿夹着那根排气管，正对着那冰冷、灼烧过的金属表面进行着疯狂而有节奏的摩擦。摩擦感生猛而无情，发出的阵阵金属回响，像是他走向毁灭的赞美诗。他不再需要女人了。他在拖拉机里找到了灵魂伴侣，他要用这种方式，把自己残余的人性一点点磨成齑粉，直到剩下那冰冷的钢铁。

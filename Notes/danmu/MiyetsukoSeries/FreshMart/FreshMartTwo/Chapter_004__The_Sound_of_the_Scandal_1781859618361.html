Chapter 4: The Sound of the Scandal

发布时间：2026/6/19 17:00:16

第四章：裂开的“正常人”

The room fell into a heavy, suffocating silence. Aya stood tall, adjusting her posture, trying to reclaim the shred of dignity she had arrived with. She looked at the four of them—the disheveled host, the tea-soaked blind girl, the meat-covered pregnant idol, and the embarrassed "visual expert"—with an expression of cold, icy judgment.

房间里陷入了一片压抑的死寂。绫挺直了腰杆，试图重拾她来时那仅存的一点尊严。她冷冷地扫视着这四个人——狼狈的房东、湿透的盲女、满身肉块的准妈妈，还有那个尴尬的“视觉专家”——眼神里写满了冷冰冰的审判。

"Yes," she said, her voice dripping with authority. "I am staying. And I suspect that for the next few hours, I will be the only sane, normal human being in this zip code."

“是的，”她高傲地吐出两个字，“我会留下。而且我怀疑，在接下来的几个小时里，我将是这一带唯一一个心智正常的正常人。”

She took a confident step toward the sofa, ready to assert her dominance over the chaos.

她自信地迈向沙发，准备在这一片混乱中建立自己的威信。

Rrrrriiiip.

嘶啦——

The sound was sharp, definitive, and echoed like a gunshot. The tight, professional fabric of her pencil skirt surrendered, splitting right down the seam between her legs. But the sound didn't stop there. As the skirt gaped open, a rhythmic, mechanical bzzzzzzz filled the room.

那声音清脆而果断，简直像枪声一样回荡。她那条昂贵、严谨的包臀裙彻底投降了，沿着两腿之间的接缝处裂成两半。但声音还没完，随着裙摆敞开，一种有节奏的机械嗡嗡声瞬间填满了整个客厅。

A small, neon-pink bullet vibrator was lodged firmly between her thighs, vibrating with the intensity of a trapped hornet.

一枚粉色的子弹跳蛋正牢牢卡在她双腿之间，嗡嗡作响的力度大得像只被困住的胡蜂。

For three seconds, no one moved.

三秒钟内，没人动弹。

Then, the "Fresh Mart" family erupted. Rin let out a high, melodic shriek of laughter, Sayuri folded over clutching her stomach, and Miyetsuko leaned against the wall, wheezing.

三秒后，“新鲜马特”一家彻底失控了。铃发出尖锐的笑声，小百合捂着肚子笑得直不起腰，美悦子更是直接瘫在墙上狂喘。

Aya, the paragon of sanity, froze. Her face shifted from icy-cool to a deep, agonizing shade of magenta.

绫，这位“理智的化身”，瞬间石化了。她的脸色从冰冷的酷劲儿，迅速转变成了一种痛不欲生的洋红色。

Fujiwara, seeing the shift in the power dynamic, felt his embarrassment vanish, replaced by a surge of opportunistic devilry. He straightened up, his previous "exposed" mishap now feeling like a deliberate opening gambit.

藤原察觉到了局势的逆转，刚才的尴尬瞬间烟消云散，取而代之的是一种投机取巧的坏心思。他挺直腰杆，刚才那次“不小心暴露”瞬间变成了一种精心设计的开场白。

He swaggered forward, closing the distance between them with a predator's grace. He pointed a dismissive thumb toward the vibrating device.

他大摇大摆地走上前，带着掠食者的优雅缩短了两人的距离。他用大拇指不屑地指了指那个还在跳动的玩意儿。

"Hey," he said, his voice dropping to a smooth, mocking rumble. "Ditch the toy, Aya. I told you earlier—this place specializes in raw, unfiltered quality. Why bother with batteries when you can try the real thing?"

“嘿，”他开口，声音低沉而戏谑。“把那玩意儿丢了，绫。我刚才就说过了——我们这儿专营纯粹、无添加的顶级服务。既然能试试真家伙，何必跟这种靠电池驱动的破烂过不去呢？”

He gave his anatomy a deliberate, rhythmic swing—a casual, brazen demonstration of his own "hardware."

他故意地、有节奏地甩了甩自己的那玩意儿——那是对他自己“硬件”的一次漫不经心却又极其放肆的展示。

Aya looked at the toy, then at Fujiwara’s shameless grin, then at the camera still recording the entire thing from the corner. She let out a long, heavy breath, her shoulders sagging. The mask of the "normal person" had officially dissolved into the family mess.

绫看着那个跳蛋，又看了看藤原那张不要脸的坏笑，最后看向角落里那台还在忠实记录一切的摄像机。她长叹一口气，肩膀彻底垮了下来。“正常人”的面具，终于被这群疯子彻底撕碎了。

[Learning Point]

Definitive: 明确的/决定性的。

Paragon: 典范/化身。

Magenta: 洋红色。

Opportunistic: 投机取巧的。

Brazen: 肆无忌惮的。

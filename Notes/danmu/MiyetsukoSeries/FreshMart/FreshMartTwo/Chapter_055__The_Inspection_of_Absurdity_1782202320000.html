Chapter 55: The Sanitary Audit

发布时间：2026/6/23 22:30:28

第五十五章：卫生大检查的荒诞午后

The "Fresh Mart" was braced for the most dreaded event in the corporate calendar: The Annual Sanitary Audit. Inspector Vane, a man whose soul had been desiccated by years of checking grease traps, stepped into the store. He was not looking for cleanliness; he was looking for compliance, and what he found instead was a fever dream.

“新鲜玛特”迎来了公司年度大戏：卫生大检查。督察官威恩——一个灵魂被油水分离器熬干的男人——踏入了店门。他不在乎什么卫生，他在乎的是“合规”，可他看到的，却是一场持续发酵的白日梦。

Miyetsuko stood by the entrance, draped in a silk robe that left very little to the imagination and absolutely nothing to interpretation. She held a clipboard, but instead of inventory logs, it was filled with sketches of anatomically impossible machines.

美悦子伫立在入口，丝绸长袍松垮地挂在身上，几乎等于没穿。她手里拿着块写字板，上面没记库存，全是些解剖学上不可能存在的机器草图。

"Welcome, Inspector," she purred, her voice dripping with enough insincerity to lubricate the entire store’s floor. "We’ve optimized our workflow. Please, take a seat. The chair is... biological."

“欢迎，督察官，”她柔声说，声音里的虚伪足够把整个店的地板都润滑一遍，“我们优化了工作流。请坐。这椅子……是生物质的。”

She gestured to Rin. The girl was on all fours, her back arched, her chest positioned perfectly to provide a soft, cushiony foundation. Inspector Vane, too stunned to protest, lowered himself onto Rin’s breasts, sinking into the soft, yielding flesh.

她指向铃。铃四肢着地，背部隆起，双乳正好成了那个软糯的底座。督察官威恩被震撼得失去了抗议能力，顺从地坐了下去，深深陷进那团柔软、温热的肉感里。

"Remarkable," Vane muttered, shifting his weight. "Ergonomic. Is this... memory foam?"

“真惊人，”威恩嘟囔着，调整了一下重心，“符合人体工学。这是……记忆海绵吗？”

"Something like that," Rin chirped, her voice muffled by the Inspector’s suit trousers.

“差不多吧。”铃闷闷地应道，声音被督察官的西装裤淹没。

Sayuri, meanwhile, decided that her contribution would be "prowling." She slinked toward Vane, trying to look like a predatory kitten, but her stomach betrayed her. She had spent the morning snacking on a crate of raw, fermenting daikon radishes—an ill-advised fuel for a seduction attempt.

小百合决定走“诱惑风”。她像只捕猎的猫咪一样贴向威恩，可肠胃却在这个节骨眼背叛了她。她早上刚嗑了一箱发酵中的生萝卜——这显然不是诱惑现场该有的燃料。

Pffft-bloop. A wet, explosive sound ripped through the tension, followed by the pungent, earthy aroma of fermented vegetable gas.

噗——嗵！ 一声湿润而剧烈的声响撕裂了空气，伴随着一股发酵萝卜的浓郁土腥气。

"My, what a fragrance," Vane said, lifting his nose. "Is this the store’s signature scent? It’s... aggressive."

“哎呀，这什么香气，”威恩耸动着鼻子，“这是店里的招牌味吗？真是……充满侵略性。”

Sayuri’s face turned beet red. "It’s a secret recipe, Inspector! It’s designed to... heighten the appetite."

小百合脸涨成了紫红色。“这是秘方，督察官！专门用来……提升食欲的。”

"It’s not helping, Sayuri," Miyetsuko snapped from the side, not breaking her cold stare. "You’re leaking agricultural waste all over the upholstery. If you’re going to compete with Rin’s chair-performance, at least have the decency to be aromatic in a way that doesn't imply a sewage backup."

“别丢人了，小百合，”美悦子冷冷地呵斥，连眼神都没移开，“你在往沙发上渗农业废弃物。如果你要跟铃的人体椅竞争，至少得优雅点，别搞得像化粪池倒灌一样。”

"I can't help it!" Sayuri shrieked, letting out another brrrraaaap that shook her own frame. "The daikon... it’s reacting with the bleach in my pores!"

“我忍不住啊！”小百合尖叫着，又连环释放了一个震颤全身的连环屁。“萝卜……正在跟我毛孔里的漂白水起反应！”

Vane stood up, the chair-Rin shivering as the pressure was removed. He looked at the clipboard, then at the girl-chair, then at the gassy seductress, and finally at Miyetsuko.

威恩站起身，铃如释重负地颤抖了一下。他看看写字板，看看人肉椅，又看看不断排气的诱惑者，最后看向美悦子。

"I've seen some things in this industry," he said, adjusting his tie. "But I think I'll just check the box for 'Exceeds Expectations' and leave before I pass out from the gas."

“我在这个行当见识过不少，”他整了整领带，“但我决定直接打个‘超出预期’，在被这股屁味熏晕前赶紧跑路。”

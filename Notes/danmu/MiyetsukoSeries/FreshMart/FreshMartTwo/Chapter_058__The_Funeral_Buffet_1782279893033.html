Chapter 58: The Funeral Buffet

发布时间：2026/6/24 13:44:48

第五十八章：黑崎家的豪华谢幕

The Kurosaki conglomerate was a titan of industry, and Soichiro Kurosaki, its patriarch, had died as he lived: surrounded by opulence and protected by an iron-clad legal team. His funeral was being held at the Kurosaki Mausoleum—a neo-Gothic monstrosity that smelled of expensive lilies and corporate tax loopholes.

黑崎集团是工业巨头，其当家人黑崎宗一郎死得和他活着时一样：极尽奢华，由顶级法律团队护航。葬礼在黑崎陵园举行——那是一座哥特式的庞然大物，空气中弥漫着昂贵的百合花香和避税天堂的味道。

Fujiwara and his troupe pulled up in a stolen hearse that still had a half-eaten burrito on the dashboard. They were dressed in thrift-store suits that were three sizes too large, aiming for "distraught billionaire bestie" but landing somewhere near "homeless funeral crasher."

藤原领着他的“慰问团”开着一辆偷来的灵车赶到，仪表盘上还放着半个没吃完的卷饼。他们穿着大上三个号的二手西装，试图装出“悲痛欲绝的亿万富翁好友”的气质，但看起来更像是“流浪汉灵堂诈骗小分队”。

"Remember," Fujiwara hissed, adjusting his clip-on tie. "We are Soichiro’s closest confidants. We shared cigars, we shared secrets, and most importantly, we shared a mutual love for top-tier catering. If anyone asks, I’m the one who taught him how to short-sell the housing market in 2008."

“记住了，”藤原拽着那个夹式领带低语，“我们是宗一郎最亲密的挚友。我们共享过雪茄，分享过秘密，最重要的是，我们同样热爱顶级的酒席。如果有人问起，我就说是我教会了他怎么在2008年做空房地产。”

Miyetsuko adjusted her fascinator—a fascinator that looked suspiciously like a dried piece of sea cucumber. "I’m his occult advisor," she deadpanned. "I’ll tell the widow that his soul is currently trapped in a high-interest savings account."

美悦子扶正了头上的饰品——那看起来像块风干的海参。“我是他的玄学顾问，”她面无表情地说，“我会告诉遗孀，他的灵魂现在正被困在一个高利息储蓄账户里。”

They waltzed into the reception hall, which was laid out like an all-you-can-eat palace. There were mountains of wagyu beef, towers of Beluga caviar, and a chocolate fountain that flowed with single-origin, artisanal molten gold.

他们大摇大摆地走进接待大厅，那里简直是一座自助餐宫殿。堆积如山的和牛、如塔般的黑鱼子酱，还有一台流淌着精品熔岩巧克力的喷泉。

Rin, acting as the "unstable, grieving ex-mistress," let out a high-pitched, performative wail. She collapsed onto a table of hors d'oeuvres, her skirt hiking up just enough to reveal that she wasn't wearing underwear—only a barcode sticker she’d peeled off a can of beans.

铃作为“精神不稳定的悲痛前情妇”，发出一声高亢、表演性质的哀嚎。她一头扑在开胃菜桌上，裙摆掀起的角度刚好露出她没穿内裤——胯部只贴了一张从豆子罐头上撕下来的条形码。

"Soichiro!" she shrieked, grabbing a handful of lobster rolls and shoving them into her dress. "You left me with nothing but this hunger!"

“宗一郎！”她尖叫着，抓起一把龙虾卷塞进裙子里，“你留给我的，只有这无尽的饥渴！”

Sayuri, dressed in a black lace veil that was effectively transparent, moved through the room like a siren. She targeted the Kurosaki heir, a man whose face was frozen in a permanent state of botoxed boredom.

小百合披着那条几乎透明的黑色蕾丝面纱，如塞壬般在厅内游走。她锁定了一位黑崎家的继承人——一个满脸肉毒杆菌，表情早已僵化的男人。

"Your father," she whispered, leaning in so close the heir could smell the garlic butter on her breath, "was a man of... hidden appetites. He once asked me if I could simulate the sensation of a bankrupt company during intimacy."

“你父亲，”她凑过去低语，甚至能让他闻到她吐息间的蒜香黄油味，“是一个……有着隐藏食欲的男人。他曾问我，能不能在欢愉中模拟出‘公司破产’的那种刺激感。”

The heir sputtered, his drink splashing onto his designer shoes. Sayuri used the distraction to swipe a gold-plated cake server, tucking it into her bodice.

继承人喷了一口酒，饮料溅在了他的定制皮鞋上。小百合趁他手忙脚乱，顺手摸走了一把镀金蛋糕铲，塞进了抹胸里。

In the corner, Fujiwara was in a food-induced fever dream. He was eating wagyu beef with both hands, tears streaming down his face as he toasted the coffin. "To the man who taught me that morality is just a hurdle for those who can't afford a good lawyer!" he bellowed, before tripping into the chocolate fountain.

角落里，藤原正陷入一种因暴食而引发的狂热幻觉。他双手齐上抓着和牛，满脸泪水地向灵柩举杯。“敬那个教导我‘道德只是穷人跨不过的门槛’的男人！”他怒吼着，随后一头栽进了巧克力喷泉里。

The guards began to move in, but Miyetsuko stood up, holding a tray of caviar. "If you eject us," she warned the security chief, "I will trigger the curse of the high-interest account. Is your pension worth a 20% annual return reduction?"

保安开始围上来，美悦子却端着一盘鱼子酱站了起来。“如果你想赶我们走，”她警告保安队长，“我就启动高利息账户的诅咒。你的养老金够不够扣那20%的年化收益？”

The security chief hesitated, looking at the chaos—Rin eating lobster rolls off the floor, Sayuri flirting with the corporate board, and Fujiwara drowning in chocolate. It was too absurd to be a crime. It was just... modern life.

保安队长犹豫了，看着这混乱的一幕——铃正从地上捡龙虾卷吃，小百合正调戏着董事会成员，藤原正被巧克力淹没。这太荒谬了，不像是犯罪，倒像是现代生活的常态。

"Leave them," he sighed to his subordinates. "They’re mourning. In their own... specific way."

“让他们吃吧，”他对着手下叹了口气，“他们是在吊唁，以一种……非常独特的、极其下流的方式。”

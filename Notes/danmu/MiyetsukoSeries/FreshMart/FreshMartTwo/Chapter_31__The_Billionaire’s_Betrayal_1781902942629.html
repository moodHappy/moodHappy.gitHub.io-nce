Chapter 31: The Billionaire’s Betrayal

发布时间：2026/6/20 05:02:20

第三十一章：马斯克的利息与人间蒸发

The "Fresh Mart" wreckage had barely been cleared when a notification buzzed on Miyetsuko’s phone—a transfer confirmation from Elon Musk. 1.4 trillion units of currency. The conditions were simple: keep it for a week, generate a massive return on interest, and use the profit to launch the production of The Queen's Protocol.

“新鲜马特”的瓦砾刚清理出个雏形，美悦子手机就弹出了一条通知——埃隆·马斯克转入了一笔1.4万亿的款项。条件很简单：代管一周，用利息作为电视剧《女王的法则》的启动资金。

But Miyetsuko, caught in the intoxicating whirlwind of "method acting" with her co-star Chen Mo, had different plans. The script of The Queen's Protocol bled into reality. Why act like a queen when you can spend like a deity?

然而，美悦子沉浸在与陈默的“沉浸式演技”漩涡中，早就把剧本抛到了九霄云外。既然能像神一样挥霍，谁还要在那儿演什么女王？

Within days, the "interest" was already being spent on private islands, sub-orbital jets, and champagne that cost more than the original Fresh Mart building. She and Chen Mo vanished into a blur of globetrotting opulence, leaving the drama production team—and the remnants of her family—to rot in the rubble of the convenience store.

几天之内，那笔“利息”就被烧在了私人海岛、亚轨道喷气机，以及价格贵过便利店大楼的香槟上。她和陈默消失在一片纸醉金迷的环球旅行中，留下了剧组和她那群家人，在便利店的废墟里发霉。

A week later, exhausted by the sheer physical tax of living like a billionaire, Miyetsuko decided she needed a "two-day break." She touched down in her private jet, strolled into the construction site of their new home, and expected a hero’s welcome.

一周后，穷奢极欲的节奏掏空了美悦子，她决定回老家“歇两天”。她乘坐私人飞机降落，溜进他们那还没建好的新家工地，满心期待着一场英雄般的欢迎仪式。

She found Fujiwara, Sayuri, and Rin busy laying bricks. They looked better fed, oddly peaceful, and—most disturbingly—busy.

她发现藤原、小百合和铃正忙着砌砖。他们看起来比以前吃得更好，神情异常安详，而且最让人不安的是——他们看上去忙得不亦乐乎。

"I'm back!" she announced, striking a pose.

“我回来啦！”她摆了个造型宣布。

Fujiwara stopped, squinting through a cloud of plaster dust. He looked at Miyetsuko, then at the brick in his hand, then back at Miyetsuko. "Do I know you? Are you here for the masonry supply delivery?"

藤原停下动作，眯着眼穿过石膏粉尘。他看看美悦子，看看手里的砖头，又看看美悦子。“我认识你吗？你是来送砌砖材料的？”

"It's me! Miyetsuko!" she gasped.

“是我啊！美悦子！”她惊呼。

Rin tilted her head, her blind eyes fixed on a point somewhere near Miyetsuko’s left ear. "Miyetsuko? Doesn't ring a bell. Is she the one who was supposed to pay for the new roof? Because if not, we’re busy."

铃歪了歪头，盲眼看向美悦子左耳旁的一点。“美悦子？没听说过。她是那个负责出钱修屋顶的人吗？如果不是，那我们很忙。”

Sayuri walked out, holding the "Toilet Saint." She looked at Miyetsuko with the polite, vacant expression one reserves for a stranger asking for directions at a bus stop. "I'm sorry, dear. You seem lost. This is a private construction site. Unless you have the permits, you really should leave. We’ve already moved on to a new management style."

小百合抱着“马桶圣子”走了出来，用那种招待在车站问路陌生人的礼貌且空洞的眼神看着她。“抱歉，亲爱的。你好像走错路了。这是私人施工现场。除非你有许可证，否则请离开。我们已经换了新的管理方式了。”

Miyetsuko stood frozen. The 1.4 trillion, the globe-trotting, the fake romance—it had all happened in a week, and in that same week, the family had simply… overwritten her. She was a deleted file, a ghost in the machine of their collective memory.

美悦子呆住了。那1.4万亿、那全球奢游、那虚假的恋情——这一切不过是一周之内的事，而在这一周里，家人竟然彻底……覆盖了她。她成了一个被删除的档案，成了他们集体记忆机器里的一个幽灵。

"You forgot me?" she whispered, the billionaire ego crumbling in the plaster dust. "I spent the interest! I lived the dream!"

“你们把我忘了？”她低语道，亿万富翁的自尊心在石膏灰中碎了一地。“我花了利息！我过了那种梦幻人生！”

"Interest?" Fujiwara scoffed, turning back to his wall. "We don't know what that is. We just know that if you’re not helping us lay bricks, you’re just another stranger blocking the sunlight."

“利息？”藤原嗤之以鼻，转过身继续砌他的墙，“我们不知道那是什么。我们只知道，如果你不来帮忙砌砖，你就是个挡着阳光的陌生人。”

Miyetsuko watched them work, the realization sinking in: she had left to play a queen, only to come home and find she was never even a pawn.

美悦子看着他们干活，终于意识到：她离开是为了演女王，结果回来才发现，她连个卒子都算不上。

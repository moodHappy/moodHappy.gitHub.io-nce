Chapter 1: The Invitation

发布时间：2026/6/19 16:37:58

第一章：一份特殊的邀请函

The sun streamed through the window of the small, cozy apartment, casting a warm glow over the breakfast table. Fujiwara was busy buttering his toast, his eyes occasionally darting toward Rin, who was skillfully pouring tea for everyone.

阳光穿透窗户，洒在这间温馨的小公寓里，给餐桌镀上了一层温暖的光。藤原正忙着涂抹吐司，目光不时飘向正熟练地为大家倒茶的铃。

"Rin," Fujiwara started, trying to keep his voice casual as he took a bite of his breakfast. "I’ve been thinking. Our house feels a bit... quiet lately. Wouldn't it be nice to have more family around?"

“铃，”藤原开口道，咬了一口早餐，尽量让语气听起来随意，“我在想，家里最近是不是有点太……安静了？如果能多点家人在一起，不是很好吗？”

Rin tilted her head, her blind eyes sparkling with curiosity. "You mean Aya? She’s been so busy with her own life since graduation."

铃歪了歪头，那双失明的眼睛里闪烁着好奇。“你是说绫吗？自从毕业后，她一直忙着自己的生活呢。”

Fujiwara paused. He had been eyeing Aya for a long time—her sharp wit and independent spirit were incredibly attractive—but the professional "escort" rates she commanded on the apps were simply way out of his league. A direct approach was financially impossible.

藤原顿了顿。他关注绫很久了——她那敏锐的才智和独立性格让他深深着迷——但她在社交平台上那高昂的“陪侍”费用实在是让他望尘莫及。直接下单？那对他来说简直是天方夜谭。

"Exactly!" Fujiwara said, a bit too loudly. "I think we should invite her over. For dinner, maybe? Or just a casual weekend visit. It would be great for the family dynamic."

“没错！”藤原激动地提高了声调。“我觉得我们应该邀请她过来。吃顿晚餐，或者周末来玩？这会对家庭氛围很有帮助。”

Sayuri, who was lounging on the sofa with a book, chuckled softly. "Oh, Fujiwara-san, you just want to see if she’s as witty in person as she is on the phone, right?"

正斜靠在沙发上看书的小百合轻笑出声。“噢，藤原先生，你只是想看看她本人是不是像在电话里那样机智，对吧？”

Fujiwara scratched his head, feeling slightly bashful. "Maybe a little. But mostly, I think she just needs a break from that busy world of hers."

藤原不好意思地挠了挠头。“可能有一点吧。但主要还是觉得，她或许该从那个忙碌的世界里抽身出来，休息一下。”

Rin smiled, pulling out her smartphone to type a quick message. "I agree. She’s my sister, after all. I’ll send her a formal invitation. Something sweet and welcoming."

铃笑了，拿出手机快速打着字。“我同意。毕竟她是我妹妹。我会发一份正式的邀请函给她。要写得甜美、热情一点。”

Fujiwara watched her fingers fly across the screen, a secret smile of anticipation playing on his lips. If he couldn't "hire" her, he’d just have to make her a part of the family instead.

藤原看着她在那屏幕上敲击的手指，嘴角不自觉地泛起一抹期待的暗笑。如果“聘请”不起她，那就干脆把她变成家人好了。

[Learning Point]

Cozy: 舒适的/温馨的。

Darting: 飞快地移动（眼神）。

Out of one's league: 超出某人能力/财力范围。

Dynamic: 动态/氛围（指家庭成员关系）。

Bashful: 害羞的。

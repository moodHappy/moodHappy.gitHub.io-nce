Chapter 65: The Gentle Touch

发布时间：2026/6/18 13:14:09

第六十五章：盲眼推拿师

The "Fresh Mart" had been running at a fever pitch. Between Sayuri’s pregnancy cravings and Miyetsuko’s increasingly sharp-eyed management of their domestic chaos, Fujiwara was feeling the toll. His back was a map of knots and stress, a direct result of being the "axis" of this bizarre family.

“新鲜马特”这段时间一直处于一种高度亢奋的状态。小百合孕期的嘴馋，加上美悦子对家庭琐事日益敏锐的管控，藤原感到有些吃不消了。他的后背成了压力与疲惫的集结地，这无疑是他作为这个怪诞家庭“轴心”的必然代价。

"You look like a crumpled sheet of paper," Miyetsuko noted one afternoon, watching him struggle to reach a high shelf. "Go to that massage parlor around the corner. The blind masseuse, Rin. Sayuri says she’s a miracle worker."

“你看起来像张被揉皱的纸，”美悦子下午注意到他，看着他吃力地去够高处的货架，“去转角那家按摩店吧。那个盲人按摩师，铃。小百合说她简直是个神医。”

Fujiwara sighed, surrendering. He walked down the street and ducked into the quiet, incense-filled room of the massage center.

藤原叹了口气，缴械投降。他沿着街道走过去，一头扎进那家点着熏香、安静的盲人按摩店。

Rin was waiting, her hands folded neatly in her lap. She had a delicate, pale face, and her eyes, though sightless, seemed to track his movement with an uncanny, rhythmic precision.

铃正在等候，双手整齐地交叠在膝头。她长着一张精致而苍白的脸，虽然双目失明，但她的眼神却似乎带着一种不可思议的、有节奏的精确，捕捉着他的一举一动。

"Fujiwara-san," she said, her voice soft and sweet like warm milk. "I can hear the tension in your stride. Please, lie down."

“藤原先生，”她的声音柔软甜美，像温牛奶一样，“我听得出你步子里的紧绷。请躺下吧。”

As Fujiwara laid his chest against the face cradle, Rin’s hands descended. They were firm, yet impossibly soft. She began to work on his shoulders, her fingers digging deep into the dense muscle.

当藤原将脸贴在按摩椅上时，铃的手落了下来。她的手法有力，却又柔软得不可思议。她开始揉捏他的肩膀，指尖深深嵌入那些紧绷的肌肉里。

"Your deltoids are so incredibly tight," she murmured, her face inches from his back. She didn’t hesitate. She kneaded a spot near his shoulder blade and leaned in, her voice earnest. "Do you have frequent erections during the day? Or is this just common male blood pressure?"

“你的三角肌紧得简直不可思议，”她轻声呢喃，脸庞离他的背只有几英寸。她没有丝毫犹豫，按压着他肩胛骨附近的一块肌肉，神情认真地凑近问道：“你白天会频繁勃起吗？还是说这只是常见的男性血压问题？”

Fujiwara choked on his own breath, his face flushing deep red. "I… what? I don’t think—"

藤原被自己的呼吸呛了一下，脸憋得通红。“我……什么？我不觉得……”

Rin didn't even blink. Her hands moved lower, tracing the line of his spine with clinical detachment. "The structure of your lumbar region is very curious. It suggests you perform... strenuous activities quite often. Do you have a lot of partners? Your stamina is excellent, but your body is begging for recovery."

铃连眼都没眨一下。她的手向下移，以一种临床般的疏离感顺着他的脊椎线条滑动。“你腰椎的结构非常奇特。这说明你经常进行……剧烈的活动。你有好几个伴侣吗？你的耐力非常出色，但你的身体已经在乞求修复了。”

Fujiwara felt exposed, yet oddly comfortable. She saw nothing, yet she understood everything about him that was hidden beneath the skin.

藤原感到一种被剥离的赤裸感，却又莫名地感到舒适。她什么也看不见，却又看穿了他皮囊下隐藏的一切。

A few days later, back at the "Fresh Mart," the conversation turned to Sayuri’s upcoming needs.

几天后，回到“新鲜马特”，话题转到了小百合即将到来的需求上。

"She needs help," Fujiwara said, looking at Sayuri, who was gently rubbing her belly. "Someone to watch over her, someone who understands anatomy, and… someone who won't judge us."

“她需要照顾，”藤原看着正轻轻抚摸肚皮的小百合说，“一个能照料她的人，一个精通人体构造的人，而且……一个不会用异样眼光审视我们的人。”

Miyetsuko looked at the store entrance, then back at Fujiwara. She smiled—that sharp, knowing smile. "You mean Rin. You’ve been thinking about her ever since that massage, haven't you?"

美悦子看向店门口，又转回藤原身上。她笑了——那种锐利、了然的笑。“你是说铃。自从上次按摩回来，你就一直在想她，对吧？”

"She has nowhere else to go," Fujiwara reasoned, his voice calm. "And she needs a stable home. We have space. It’s practical."

“她没别的地方可去了，”藤原语气平静地分析，“而且她需要一个稳定的家。我们有地方。这很实际。”

Sayuri looked up, intrigued. "A blind live-in caregiver? She’d be perfect. She could feel exactly how the baby is growing without needing fancy machines all the time."

小百合抬头，很感兴趣。“一个盲人住家护理师？这太完美了。她可以通过触觉感受宝宝的成长，甚至不需要时刻依赖那些冰冷的精密仪器。”

Fujiwara nodded, his plan already forming. "I’ll go talk to her. We’ll offer her a room, a job, and… a part of this family. A life where she doesn't have to guess what people are thinking—she can just feel it."

藤原点点头，计划已经在脑中成型。“我去和她谈。我们给她一个房间，一份工作，还有……这个家的一席之地。在这里，她不需要去猜别人在想什么——她只需要去感受就好了。”

[Learning Point]

Incredible: 不可思议的。

Detached: 疏离的/客观的。

Strenuous: 剧烈的/费力的。

Anatomy: 人体构造/解剖。

Intrigued: 感兴趣的。

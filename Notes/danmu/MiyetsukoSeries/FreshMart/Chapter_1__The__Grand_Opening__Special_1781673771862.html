Chapter 1: The "Grand Opening" Special

发布时间：2026/6/17 13:22:50

第一章：开业“特别优惠”

The morning in the new city was cold and damp. The sign for "Fresh Mart" flickered weakly at the street corner. Inside, the shelves were barely stocked. Miyetsuko stood behind the register, wearing a tight-fitting uniform apron, her brow furrowed as she double-checked the ledger.

在这座新城市的清晨，空气中带着湿冷。街角的“新鲜马特”招牌闪烁着微弱的光。超市里，货架刚摆满。美悦子站在收银台后，穿着一件贴身的围裙，眉头紧锁地核对着账本。

"Fujiwara!" Miyetsuko snapped, not looking up, waving the pathetic sales report. "Is this what you called a 'prime location'? Yesterday, the only people who came in were three guys asking for the bathroom and two teenagers trying to steal our AC. We aren't even covering the electricity bill!"

“藤原！”美悦子头也不抬，晃着那张少得可怜的营业报表，“这就是你说的‘黄金地段’？昨天整整一天，进门的除了三个问厕所在哪儿的，就是两个蹭空调的小屁孩。咱们连电费都挣不回来！”

Fujiwara was crouched under an aisle, rearranging a display of discount cans. He peeked out from behind the shelf, his stubble-covered face showing zero concern. "It's the beginning, honey. Did you think a major chain store is built in a day?"

藤原正蹲在货架底下，重新摆放那一排特价罐头。他从缝隙里探出头，胡子拉碴的脸上是一副死猪不怕开水烫的表情：“这叫刚开始，宝贝。你以为那些大超市是一天盖起来的？”

"Beginning?" Miyetsuko slammed the ledger onto the counter with a loud crack. "If we don't pay the rent in a week, we'll be sleeping on the sidewalk! And you're busy playing with cans?"

“刚开始？”美悦子把账本往柜台上一拍，发出一声脆响，“再过一周交不上租金，咱们就得卷铺盖去睡大街了！你还有心情玩这些罐头？”

Fujiwara dusted off his hands and stood up slowly. He walked straight behind Miyetsuko, looking at her back as she fumed. Without warning, he reached out and untied her apron strings.

藤原拍了拍手上的灰，慢悠悠地站起来，径直走到美悦子身后。他看着她那因为生气而起伏的背影，突然伸手，毫无预兆地解开了她的围裙系带。

"What are you doing!" she yelped, spinning around to slap him, but he caught her waist and pulled her into his arms, pinning her against the register.

“你干什么！”她惊叫着转过身就要去拍他，却被藤原一把揽住腰，顺势带进了怀里，把她抵在收银台上。

"Why so tense?" Fujiwara whispered near her ear, his voice low and raspy. "If customers won't come to us, we’ll make our own entertainment. If people hear some noise coming from the store, maybe it’ll attract the neighbors. That’s what I call 'foot traffic generation.'"

“急什么？”藤原的呼吸就在她耳边，声音低沉沙哑，“既然顾客不来，那咱们就自己找点乐子。要是街坊们听到咱们店里传出点动静，说不定还能吸引人围观。这就叫——人流带动。”

Miyetsuko flushed red, her breathing hitching. Just as she was about to call him crazy, the automatic door chimed: Ding-dong, welcome.

美悦子脸上一红，呼吸也乱了。她刚想骂他疯了，超市门口就传来了自动门的电子音：叮咚，欢迎光临。

[Learning Point]

Prime location: 黄金地段。

Ledger: 账本。

Stubble-covered: 胡子拉碴的。

Foot traffic: 人流量。

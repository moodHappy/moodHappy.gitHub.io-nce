Chapter 24: The World Cup

发布时间：2026/6/17 16:01:17

第二十四章：世界杯之夜

The back room of "Fresh Mart" was cluttered with empty beer cans and the lingering scent of charred meat from the night before. On a small, flickering television perched on a stack of cardboard boxes, the World Cup final was underway. The crowd’s roar from the screen filled the cramped space, mixing with the frantic, irregular heartbeat of the small room.

“新鲜马特”的后仓里散乱地堆着几个空啤酒罐，空气中还残留着昨晚烧烤留下的焦香。堆成一摞的纸箱上，放着一台闪烁着雪花点的小电视，正直播着世界杯决赛。屏幕里传出的巨大欢呼声填满了这间逼仄的小屋，与房间里那几个人紊乱且急促的心跳声交织在一起。

Fujiwara was slouched on a wooden crate, his eyes fixed on the screen, though he wasn't really watching the game. His gaze kept drifting to Sayuri, who sat between him and Miyetsuko. The nurse was dressed in her pale uniform, her knees pulled up to her chest, her eyes wide as she tracked the players moving across the pitch. She looked fragile, a stark contrast to the rough, gray atmosphere of the store.

藤原瘫在木箱上，双眼死死盯着屏幕，但心思根本不在球赛上。他的视线总是不受控制地飘向坐在他和美悦子中间的小百合。护士穿着那身浅色的制服，双膝蜷在胸前，眼神随着球场上奔跑的球员闪动。她看起来脆弱极了，与这间粗粝、阴沉的超市形成了鲜明对比。

"Goal!" the announcer screamed, but no one in the room cheered.

“球进了！”解说员嘶吼着，可这屋子里却没人喝彩。

Miyetsuko didn't care about football. She was tracing the line of Sayuri’s collarbone with a manicured nail, watching the girl flinch slightly at the sudden, sharp volume from the TV. "It’s so loud, isn't it, Sayuri? Does it make you nervous?"

美悦子对足球毫无兴趣。她正用修长的指甲沿着小百合的锁骨线条缓慢游走，看着女孩因为电视里突然拔高的音量而轻微战栗。“太吵了，对吧，小百合？这让你紧张吗？”

Sayuri bit her lip, her chest rising and falling rapidly. "I... I'm just waiting to see who wins."

小百合咬住嘴唇，胸口剧烈起伏。“我……我只是在等谁会赢。”

Fujiwara stood up, the chair scraping harshly against the floor. He leaned over, effectively blocking the TV screen, casting a shadow over Sayuri. "Nobody wins tonight," he murmured, his hand gripping the back of her neck. He pulled her hair back, exposing her throat, his thumb pressing into the soft skin. "We’re just here to watch you."

藤原站起身，椅子在地面上刮出刺耳的声响。他俯下身，挡住了电视画面，将阴影笼罩在小百合身上。“今晚没人会赢，”他低语，手扣住她的后颈，将她的头发向后捋去，露出脆弱的咽喉，大拇指重重按在上面。

Sayuri’s eyes blurred with a mixture of terror and an electric, base-level arousal—the hallmark of her nature. She didn't struggle. Instead, she leaned into the pressure, her pupils dilating as she stared up at the man towering over her.

小百合的眼神变得朦胧，混杂着恐惧与某种原始的、过电般的快感——那是她与生俱来的印记。她没有挣扎，反而顺从地贴上那股压力，瞳孔涣散，直勾勾地盯着头顶这个庞大的男人。

Miyetsuko watched the display with a cold, appreciative look. She reached out and turned the volume on the TV down, silencing the crowd’s roar. "Better," she whispered. "Now we can hear ourselves."

美悦子冷眼旁观，露出赞许的神色。她伸手将电视音量调小，压住了那阵狂热的欢呼。“这样好多了，”她轻声说，“现在，我们能听见彼此的声音了。”

The match on the screen turned into a silent, colorful blur. The only thing that mattered was the tension in the room, the way Sayuri’s breathing stuttered, and the way the two of them—the hunter and his partner—closed the distance around their newest acquisition.

屏幕上的赛事变成了一团无声的、五彩斑斓的乱影。此时此刻，唯一重要的只有屋子里那股紧绷的空气，小百合那破碎的喘息，以及他们两人——猎手与他的同伙——正一步步收紧对这名新猎物的包围。

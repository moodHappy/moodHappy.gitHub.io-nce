Chapter 103: A Fresh Start in the Sun-Drenched City

发布时间：2026/6/16 06:06:19

第一百零三章：阳光下的重启

The move to the new city was, in every sense, a breath of fresh air. They had left the suffocating shadows and the heavy, metallic taste of the past far behind. Here, the sky was a deeper, more optimistic blue, and the air didn't smell of secrets; it smelled of salt, blooming jasmines, and the promise of a quiet life.

搬到新城市是一个全新的开始，每一寸空气都显得格外清新。他们将过去那些令人窒息的阴影和钢铁般的沉重抛在脑后。在这里，天空蔚蓝得更加乐观，空气中不再弥漫着秘密的味道，而是充满了海盐、茉莉花香，以及一种平淡生活的希望。

They had franchised a "Fresh Mart" in a vibrant, leafy neighborhood. It wasn't a temple of power, just a clean, well-lit store where the produce was always crisp and the customers were friendly neighbors who greeted them by name. Miyetsuko found a peculiar joy in the simplicity of it—stocking shelves with colorful bell peppers, organizing the fruit displays, and helping a grandmother pick out the perfect, ripened cantaloupe.

他们在繁华且绿树成荫的街区加盟了一家“新鲜玛特”。这不再是权力的殿堂，只是一家干净、明亮的超市，农产品总是新鲜翠绿，顾客们都是会亲切地叫出他们名字的友善邻居。美悦子在这一切简单中找到了一种奇特的乐趣——往货架上摆放彩色的甜椒，精心整理水果陈列，或者帮一位老奶奶挑选最成熟的哈密瓜。

Fujiwara, surprisingly, took to the life of a small-business owner with a gentle focus. He spent his mornings checking inventory and his afternoons chatting with the local delivery drivers. He had traded his sharp suits for simple linen shirts, and for the first time in years, the tension that once held his shoulders tight had melted away.

藤原竟然非常适应小店主的生活，专注且温和。他每天早晨检查库存，下午则与当地的送货员闲聊。他换下了那身棱角分明的西装，穿上简单的亚麻衬衫。多年来，他肩膀上那股紧绷的弦，终于彻底松弛了下来。

In the evenings, they would close the shop together, the chime of the door announcing their "freedom" for the day. They’d walk home hand-in-hand, picking up a baguette or some fresh pasta from the local deli. Their apartment was cozy—not a fortress, but a home filled with sunlight and the occasional mess of a life being lived for the first time.

傍晚时分，他们一起关掉店门，那声清脆的门铃声宣告了属于他们的“下班时间”。他们手牵手走在回家的路上，顺路去熟食店买根法棍或者新鲜意面。他们的公寓温馨舒适，不再是冰冷的堡垒，而是一个洒满阳光、偶尔会有生活琐碎杂乱的家，这是他们第一次真正地去“生活”。

There was no grand plot, no shadowy deal—just the small, wonderful certainties of a life reclaimed. Miyetsuko learned to bake cookies that were a little too sweet, and Fujiwara learned that he was actually quite good at fixing a leaky faucet. They were just a young couple, starting over, finding their "small happiness" in the quiet hum of a sun-drenched city.

没有庞大的阴谋，没有阴暗的交易——只有重获新生后那些细小而美好的确定性。美悦子学会了烤那种稍微有点太甜的饼干，藤原则发现自己居然很擅长修理漏水的水龙头。他们只是一对重新开始的情侣，在这座阳光普照的城市里，在那种宁静的生活节奏中，寻找着属于他们的“小确幸”。

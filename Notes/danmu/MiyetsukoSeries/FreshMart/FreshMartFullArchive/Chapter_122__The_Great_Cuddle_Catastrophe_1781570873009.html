Chapter 122: The Great Cuddle Catastrophe

发布时间：2026/6/16 08:47:51

第一百二十二章：枕头“掉包”记

The ride home was a blur of frantic energy. Fujiwara, driven by a primal, irresistible magnetism, couldn't stop eyeing Miyetsuko as if she were the only sustenance left on earth. As soon as they burst through the apartment door, Miyetsuko—still fueled by that rush of post-labor adrenaline—clutched the bundle in her arms, rushing into the bedroom. In the heat of the moment, she deposited the "bundle" onto the sofa and dove straight into his arms.

回家的路途充满了火急火燎的冲动。藤原被一种原始而无法抗拒的吸引力所驱动，一路上紧紧盯着美悦子，仿佛她是这世上唯一的慰藉。当他们冲进公寓大门时，美悦子还沉浸在产后的肾上腺素中，紧紧抱着襁褓冲进了卧室。在急不可耐的驱使下，她将“包裹”往旁边的沙发上一放，便直接扑进了他的怀里。

The world narrowed down to just the two of them. Clothes were scattered like confetti, and the bed became a sanctuary of unbridled, desperate hunger. They moved with the frantic pace of a couple who had been deprived of their rhythm for far too long, their bodies finding each other with practiced, electric precision.

世界在这一刻缩小到只剩下他们两人。衣物如纸屑般散落，床铺成了他们肆意宣泄的乐园。他们以一种久违的急促步调交缠在一起，身体在那熟悉的、电光火石般的默契中疯狂碰撞。

It wasn't until the sweat began to cool and the heavy, hazy fog of ecstasy started to lift that they remembered their newest family member.

直到汗水渐冷，那股浓重且迷离的欢愉感退去，他们才猛然想起家中那名新成员。

"The baby..." Miyetsuko gasped, pushing her hair back, her voice tinged with a sudden, sharp alarm. "Fujiwara, check on the baby!"

“宝宝……”美悦子惊呼出声，撩开乱发，声音里带上了一丝急促的警觉，“藤原，快去看看宝宝！”

Fujiwara scrambled up, stumbling as he pulled on his trousers, and rushed into the living room. Miyetsuko followed, heart pounding. They scanned the sofa where they had tossed their precious bundle.

藤原手忙脚乱地爬起身，连滚带爬地穿上裤子，冲进了客厅。美悦子紧随其后，心脏狂跳。他们扫视着刚才随手放下那个“宝贵包裹”的沙发。

Sitting there, plump and stationary, was a single, silk-covered throw pillow.

只见那里躺着的，是一只圆滚滚、一动不动的丝绸靠枕。

Silence descended upon the room, heavy and absolute.

房间里陷入了一片沉重而绝对的死寂。

"A pillow," Fujiwara whispered, his jaw dropping. "We… we just left our child in the hospital? And brought home a pillow?"

“枕头……”藤原低语，下巴几乎要掉到地上，“我们……我们竟然把孩子落在了医院？然后带回来了一个枕头？”

Miyetsuko stared at the pillow, then at Fujiwara, her face cycling from horror to hysterical laughter. She pointed a trembling finger at him. "It’s your fault! You were like a dog on a leash, dragging me home! I was so busy trying to keep up with your ‘hunger’ that I grabbed the wrong thing!"

美悦子盯着那只枕头，又看向藤原，脸色从惊恐转为歇斯底里的爆笑。她颤抖着手指指向他，“都怪你！你当时像条被牵住的狗一样把我拖回来！我忙着应付你那份‘饥渴’，才顺手抓错了东西！”

"My fault?" Fujiwara countered, pacing back and forth with a look of mock outrage. "I wouldn't have been in such a rush if you hadn't looked so incredibly, maddeningly beautiful! It’s your own fault for being so damn stunning that I completely lost my mind!"

“怪我？”藤原反驳道，装出一副义愤填膺的样子来回踱步，“要不是你美得让人发疯，我至于那么着急吗！都怪你太美艳，弄得我彻底乱了方寸！”

They looked at each other, the absurdity of the situation finally winning over their panic. They dissolved into laughter, collapsing back onto the sofa—this time, carefully avoiding the pillow.

两人面面相觑，荒诞的现实终于压倒了惊慌。他们大笑起来，瘫倒在沙发上——这次，他们小心翼翼地避开了那个枕头。

第六十二章：意料之外的“种子”

The laughter in the "Fresh Mart" was absolute bedlam. Miyetsuko, still clutching her stomach, suddenly let out a strangled, high-pitched gasp. The sheer, overwhelming absurdity of the last hour had finally done her in—her bowels simply gave up the ghost. A heavy, warm weight shifted in her trousers, and the unmistakable, pungent aroma of a fresh mess began to seep through her designer clothes.

“新鲜马特”里的笑声简直像疯了一样。美悦子正捂着肚子，突然发出一声像是被掐住脖子的尖叫。这一小时里发生的荒唐事让她彻底失控了——她的括约肌直接罢工。一股沉重、温热的感觉在她裤子里散开，那股不可名状、浓烈的味道开始从她的名牌裤子里渗透出来。

Fujiwara, eyes streaming with tears, fell against the shelves, gasping for air. "Oh my god, Miyetsuko! You actually shit yourself?"

藤原笑得眼泪鼻涕横流，整个人瘫在货架上喘气：“我的天，美悦子！你竟然笑拉了？”

Miyetsuko didn't even try to hide it. She just sat there, legs splayed on the floor, laughing until she wheezed. Sayuri, meanwhile, stood by the shutter, still half-naked, her cheeks a deep, burning crimson. She was mortified—the image of her own public defilement still fresh in her mind—but looking at the mess on the floor and the look on her two favorite people's faces, she couldn't help it. She let out a giggle, then a full, bubbly laugh that filled the dusty store.

美悦子甚至懒得遮掩。她就那么大喇喇地摊开腿坐在地板上，笑得直喘气。小百合还站在卷帘门边，身上半裸，脸颊烧得通红。她本该羞愤欲绝——那当街被骑的羞耻画面还在脑中萦绕——可看着地板上的那摊“杰作”，又看看这两个她最爱的人此刻狼狈又滑稽的表情，她再也绷不住了。她先是咯咯轻笑，接着爆发出一阵清脆欢快的笑声，填满了整间积灰的小店。

[The Revelation / 几个月后]

Months melted away. The store had returned to its sleepy, quiet rhythm, but the air felt different—fuller, somehow.

几个月的时间如流水般过去。小店又恢复了那种慵懒安静的节奏，但空气却似乎变了——沉甸甸的，仿佛塞满了什么东西。

One morning, Sayuri stood in the bathroom, staring at a small, plastic stick. Two distinct, unshakeable lines stared back. She walked out to the kitchen, where Fujiwara was brewing coffee, and handed him the test.

一天早晨，小百合站在浴室里，盯着手里的小塑料棒。上面那两条清晰、不可撼动的红线死死地回望着她。她走进厨房，藤原正在煮咖啡，她默默把验孕棒递给了他。

Fujiwara studied the result, his brow furrowing, then clearing into a look of sheer, perplexed amazement. He looked at Sayuri, then toward the back room where Miyetsuko was resting. He let out a low, dry whistle.

藤原端详着结果，眉头先是皱起，随后舒展成了一种极度困惑的震惊。他看向小百合，又望向美悦子所在的后仓。他发出一声低沉、干涩的感叹。

"Well, I’ll be damned," he said, shaking his head. "That random guy in the street... he really had a one-shot-kill trigger, didn't he?"

“行啊，真有你的，”他摇摇头，“那个路人甲……真是一发入魂啊，不是吗？”

Sayuri bit her lip, feeling a strange mix of nerves and quiet joy. "I think so, Fujiwara-san."

小百合咬着嘴唇，心中交织着紧张与一种宁静的喜悦。“我想是吧，藤原先生。”

Fujiwara walked over, pulling her into a gentle, protective embrace, his hand resting on the tiny, invisible curve of her belly. "We’ve been raw-dogging for months, and nothing. That guy walks by for ten seconds, and boom. Life finds a way, I guess."

藤原走过去，将她温柔地护入怀中，手掌轻轻覆在她尚未显怀的小腹上。“我们无套折腾了这么久，一点动静都没有。那家伙路过十秒钟，砰，就中了。看来生命这东西，确实有点玄学。”

He leaned down, kissing her forehead, his eyes sparkling with a mix of cynical humor and genuine, doting affection. "Don't worry about it. It’s ours now. And honestly? It’s probably the most hilarious thing that's ever happened to us."

他低下头，吻了吻她的额头，眼神里既有嘲弄的幽默，又有发自内心的宠溺。“别担心。现在它是咱们的了。说实话？这大概是我们这辈子发生过最搞怪的一件事儿了。”

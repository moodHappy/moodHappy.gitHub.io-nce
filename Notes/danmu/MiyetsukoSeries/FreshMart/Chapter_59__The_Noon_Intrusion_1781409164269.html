Chapter 59: The Noon Intrusion

发布时间：2026/6/14 11:52:41

第五十九章：午间的“空降兵”

The recovery period had passed in a blur of late-night "audits," competitive coffee brewing, and a tension so thick it practically had its own job description. With Miyetsuko back on her feet, the temporary "home office" arrangement had been dissolved—much to the silent disappointment of both parties—and they were back to their routine at the main office.

康复期在深夜“审计”、咖啡冲煮竞赛以及那几乎要把人压垮的紧绷气氛中匆匆而过。随着美悦子伤愈复出，那种临时的“居家办公”安排也宣告结束——尽管双方内心深处都有些许沉默的失落——他们回到了总部的日常轨道上。

It was a lazy, humid Tuesday afternoon. Miyetsuko was buried behind a wall of invoices, trying to focus on a shipment manifest, when a sudden ripple of whispers swept through the office like a wave.

那是一个懒散潮湿的周二午后。美悦子正埋在一堆发票墙后，试图核对一份出货清单，这时办公室里突然掀起一阵窃窃私语，像潮水般蔓延开来。

She looked up, expecting a manager, but what she saw instead was a vision of refined, polished perfection. A woman, draped in a designer silk dress that looked like it cost more than Miyetsuko’s entire annual wardrobe, was weaving through the desks with a confident, effortless grace. She carried a sleek, thermal lunch carrier as if it were a high-fashion accessory.

她抬起头，本以为是哪位经理，却撞见了一抹精致而考究的身影。那女人穿着一件剪裁考究的丝绸长裙，那价格看上去恐怕比美悦子一整年的衣橱加起来还要贵，她穿梭在办公桌间，步履间透着一种自信且毫不费力的优雅。她手里拎着一个时髦的保温饭盒，拎得像是在拿一件高级时装配件。

"Fujiwara!" the woman chirped, her voice clear and melodious, cutting through the hum of the office.

“藤原！”那女人轻快地唤道，声音清脆悦耳，穿透了办公室里的嘈杂声。

Fujiwara, who had been lazily swiveling in his chair to check a query, stood up with a speed that was almost jarring. His usual mask of cool, detached professionalism didn't just slip—it shattered. He looked genuinely surprised, his eyes softening as the woman reached his desk and leaned in, pressing a light, affectionate kiss to his cheek.

藤原本来正懒洋洋地转着椅子核对指令，此刻却以一种近乎惊人的速度站起身。他平日里那副冷淡、疏离的职场面具不仅是滑落，简直是瞬间粉碎了。他看起来真的非常惊讶，当女人走到他桌前并倾身在他脸颊上落下温柔一吻时，他的眼神竟然瞬间柔和了下来。

"I thought I’d surprise you," she said, her smile radiant and completely, devastatingly secure. "The bistro made that carbonara you like, and they were finally doing the special dessert you wanted."

“我心想给你个惊喜，”她说道，笑容明媚且带着一种让人绝望的、绝对的安全感。“那家小酒馆做了你喜欢的奶油培根面，而且他们终于做了你想吃的那款特制甜点。”

Miyetsuko felt a cold, jagged knot tighten in her chest. She gripped her pen so hard the plastic cracked under her thumb. She had spent the last two weeks thinking she understood the man—that she knew the rhythm of his dark, possessive games. But watching him now, so genuinely at ease, so taken, she felt like an outsider staring at a page of a book she’d never been allowed to read.

美悦子感到心口一阵冷锐的紧缩，仿佛打了个死结。她死死攥住手中的笔，直到塑料笔杆在指下发出破碎的脆响。过去两周，她一直以为自己读懂了这个男人——以为自己掌握了他那些阴暗占有欲游戏的节奏。可看着现在的他，如此真诚地放松，如此地“名花有主”，她觉得自己像个局外人，正盯着一本她从未被允许翻阅的书页。

Fujiwara glanced over, his eyes briefly meeting Miyetsuko’s. For a split second, the playful arrogance was gone, replaced by a flicker of... guilt? But then, the mask was back. He turned away, laughing at something his visitor said, and Miyetsuko lowered her head, suddenly very interested in the contents of her invoices.

藤原扫了一眼，目光与美悦子短暂交汇。那一瞬间，那股顽皮的傲慢消失了，取而代之的是一丝……愧疚？但转瞬即逝，面具又戴回了他的脸上。他转过头，对来访者的谈笑做出回应，而美悦子低下头，突然对发票上的内容产生了浓厚的兴趣。

Jarring: 刺耳的 / 不和谐的。

Polished: 优雅 / 完美。

Woven through: 穿梭。

Devastatingly secure: 让人绝望般笃定的。

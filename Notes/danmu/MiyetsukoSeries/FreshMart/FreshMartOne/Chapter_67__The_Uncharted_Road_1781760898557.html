Chapter 67: The Uncharted Road

发布时间：2026/6/18 13:34:56

第六十七章：方向盘下的悸动

The private lot behind the warehouse was a dusty, sun-drenched expanse, perfect for a beginner. Rin was a natural. Her blindness seemed to heighten her other senses; she felt the vibration of the engine in her heels, the subtle shifting of the chassis through the steering wheel, and the way the van swayed on the loose gravel.

仓库后的私人空地在烈日下尘土飞扬，是新手的绝佳练习场。铃简直是个天才。她的失明似乎强化了其他感官；她能用脚后跟感受引擎的震动，通过方向盘感知底盘的细微位移，甚至能察觉货车在碎石路上晃动的节奏。

"A little more gas, Rin. Steady," Fujiwara coached from the passenger seat.

“再给点油，铃。稳住，”藤原在副驾驶座上指导。

She turned the wheel with a sharp, graceful motion, drifting the van around a pile of tires with a grin that could light up the gloomiest night. She pulled to a stop, the engine purring softly. She was breathless, her cheeks flushed with the thrill of control.

她猛打方向盘，动作优雅又精准，驾驶着货车绕过一堆废旧轮胎，笑容灿烂得能照亮最阴暗的夜晚。车稳稳停下，引擎发出温柔的轰鸣。她气息微喘，脸颊因掌控一切的快感而红润。

Fujiwara watched her, the raw intensity of the moment spiking his own pulse. The thrill of teaching her to navigate the physical world made him crave the same intimacy with her own body.

藤原看着她，这一刻原始的张力让他的心跳加速。教她如何驾驭这金属机械的快感，让他同时也渴望着去“驾驭”她那副身体。

"You drive like a pro, Rin," he murmured, his voice dipping into a low, dangerous register. "I’ve been teaching you how to steer this machine all afternoon. Now, I find myself really wanting to... steer you, too."

“你开得像个专业车手，铃，”他低语道，声音沉入危险的低音区，“整个下午我都在教你操控这台机器。现在，我发现自己真的很想……也来操控一下你。”

Rin’s breath hitched. She didn't strike him, didn't pull away. Instead, she turned her face toward him, her lips parting slightly. Her skin bloomed with a deep, rosy heat, and she leaned into his space, her silence a loud, ringing consent.

铃的呼吸一滞。她没有打他，也没有退缩。相反，她将脸转向他，双唇微启。她的肌肤泛起阵阵潮红，身体向他靠去，那份沉默是一种清晰而响亮的应允。

Fujiwara reached over, his hands sliding over her frame. She was soft, trembling like a leaf. He lifted her onto his lap, her movements clumsy and naive. It was only when he tried to move inside her that he felt the resistance—the tight, unmistakable barricade.

藤原伸出手，掌心滑过她的身体。她很柔软，颤抖得像片叶子。他将她抱到腿上，她的动作笨拙而青涩。直到他试图进入她时，才感受到了那股阻力——那是紧致的、无可辩驳的处女防线。

He froze, stunned. A blind, beautiful woman, living in this city, and she was untouched? The realization hit him with a wave of protective, overwhelming tenderness. He wanted to break her, but he wanted to treasure her even more.

他愣住了，满心震惊。一个漂亮盲女，生活在这座城市，居然还是个处女？这一认知如潮水般带来一股强烈的保护欲与怜爱。他想占有她，但更想把她揉化在掌心，珍之重之。

"Rin..." he groaned, kissing her eyelids. "You're... you're still a virgin?"

“铃……”他呻吟着，亲吻她的眼睑，“你……你还是处女？”

"I was waiting for someone who... who wasn't looking at my eyes," she whispered, her hands finding his chest, gripping his shirt.

“我一直在等一个……一个不盯着我眼睛看的人，”她低语，双手摸索到他的胸膛，抓紧了他的衬衫。

He moved slowly, agonizingly so, breaking through that seal with the care of a surgeon. She let out a sharp, surprised cry, her back arching, her hands clutching him as if he were her only anchor in the darkness. The car rocked gently under the rhythm of their heat, the dusty sun filtering through the windows as they became one.

他动作缓慢，小心翼翼得近乎折磨，极其温柔地撕开了那层禁锢。她发出一声尖锐的惊呼，背部弓起，双手死死抓着他，仿佛他是她在黑暗中唯一的浮木。汽车在他们律动的热量下轻轻摇晃，尘土飞扬的阳光透过车窗洒进来，将他们合二为一。

He didn't treat her like a conquest; he treated her like a fragile, hidden treasure finally brought into the light. He worshipped her, every moan, every touch, until they were both left panting, tangled in the backseat of a van that had become their private world.

他没有把她当作战利品，而是当作一件终于重见天日的易碎珍宝。他虔诚地对待她，每一个呻吟，每一次触碰，直到两人气喘吁吁地瘫软在一起，缠绕在那辆成为他们私人领地的货车后座上。

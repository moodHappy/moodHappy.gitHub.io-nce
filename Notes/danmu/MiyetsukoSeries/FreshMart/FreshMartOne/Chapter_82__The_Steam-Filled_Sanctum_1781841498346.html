Chapter 82: The Steam-Filled Sanctum

发布时间：2026/6/19 11:58:16

第八十二章：氤氲中的“特殊待遇”

The "Grand Public Bath" was a labyrinth of steam, tiles, and muffled echoes. By the time they entered, the weekend crowd had thinned, leaving the spacious, cedar-lined bathing area to the four of them—Fujiwara, Miyetsuko, Rin, and the increasingly pregnant Sayuri.

“大浴场”是一座由水汽、瓷砖和闷声回音构成的迷宫。当他们走进去时，周末的人潮已经散去，宽敞的雪松木浴区成了他们四个人的私人领地——藤原、美悦子、铃，以及孕味渐浓的小百合。

The heat was heavy, clinging to their skin like a damp blanket. They settled into the main pool, the water lapping gently against the stone edges.

湿热感如同一层潮湿的毛毯，紧紧贴在他们的皮肤上。他们滑入主池，水波在石质池边轻柔地荡漾。

"Alright," Miyetsuko declared, wringing out a hot towel. "Let’s establish the labor hierarchy for the evening. Since Sayuri is currently incubating a human, she is exempt from all manual labor. She is the 'Royal Overseer'—she directs, she relaxes, and she stays put."

“好了，”美悦子宣布着，拧干一条热毛巾，“我们来排一下今晚的劳动阶级。鉴于小百合正在孵化人类，她被豁免一切体力劳动。她是‘皇家监工’——她负责指挥，负责放松，还得负责别乱动。”

Sayuri let out a soft, pleased hum, leaning her head back against the rim of the pool. "I accept this title. You three are my designated scrubbing squad."

小百合发出一声轻柔而满足的哼声，将头靠在池沿上。“我接受这个头衔。你们三个，就是我的指定搓澡小队。”

Fujiwara smirked, his eyes trailing over the three women—each one a different texture, a different mystery. "I’ll handle Rin. She needs someone to navigate the tricky spots, and I think I’ve become quite familiar with her anatomy."

藤原嘴角上扬，视线扫过这三个女人——每一个都有着不同的触感，不同的谜团。“我负责铃。她需要个能搞定难缠部位的领航员，而且我想，我对她的身体构造已经相当熟悉了。”

Rin giggled, leaning into Fujiwara’s shoulder. "I don’t mind, as long as he doesn’t try to count anything while he’s scrubbing."

铃咯咯笑着，倚在藤原肩头。“我不介意，只要他在搓澡的时候别试图数什么数字就行。”

"That leaves me and the pregnant lady," Miyetsuko laughed, grabbing a loofah. "Sayuri, lean forward. I’ll make sure your back is clean enough to host a state dinner."

“那剩下我和这位准妈妈了，”美悦子大笑着，抓起搓澡巾，“小百合，往前靠。我会把你背搓得干干净净，干净到能举办一场国宴。”

The bathing area dissolved into a symphony of splashes, soapy lathers, and the rhythmic shhh-shhh of towels against skin. Fujiwara took a stiff brush to Rin’s back, his hands firm and deliberate, exploring the muscles that had carried them across the state for that coastal "educational" trip.

浴场里很快变成了一首由水花、肥皂泡沫和搓澡巾在皮肤上摩挲出的节奏交响曲。藤原拿着硬毛刷在铃的背上来回推磨，动作坚定而从容，感受着那块带他们横跨州界进行海边“教学”的肌肉，正变得舒缓。

"You’re missing a spot near the shoulder blade, Fujiwara-san," Rin murmured, her voice drowsy from the heat.

“肩胛骨那儿漏了一块，藤原先生，”铃咕哝着，声音因高温而变得慵懒。

"I know," he replied, sliding his hand down to her waist, his touch lingering in places a scrub brush shouldn't go. "I'm just appreciating the geography."

“我知道，”他回答着，手掌下滑至她的腰间，触碰着那些搓澡刷不该去的地方。“我只是在欣赏一下地理地貌。”

Across the pool, Sayuri closed her eyes, letting Miyetsuko’s vigorous scrubbing turn her skin a healthy, glowing pink. It was a perfect, bizarre little ecosystem—a family built on rules, arguments, and a deep, unspoken indulgence.

池子对面，小百合闭上眼，任由美悦子那充满活力的搓澡手法将她的皮肤搓得泛起健康的粉红光泽。这是一个完美、荒诞的小生态系统——一个建立在规章、争吵和那份深沉而心照不宣的放纵之上的家庭。

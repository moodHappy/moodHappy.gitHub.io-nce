第五十三章：彻底的沦陷

Fujiwara and Sayuri realized that talking was useless. Words were just air, and Miyetsuko was drowning in a silence deeper than the ocean. So, they did something drastic. They brought in a sledgehammer.

藤原和小百合意识到讲道理没用。话语不过是空气，而美悦子正淹没在比海洋更深邃的死寂里。于是，他们做了件决绝的事。他们抡起了大锤。

With a few thunderous crashes, the wall of the dim, dusty back storage room—the room that had been her tomb of depression—crumbled. They replaced the rotting boards with a massive, floor-to-ceiling glass window. Suddenly, the outside world—the brutal, indifferent, blinding sunlight—poured in, washing away the shadows.

随着几声轰然巨响，那面昏暗、布满灰尘的后仓墙壁——她抑郁的坟墓——倒塌了。他们用一面巨大的落地窗取代了朽烂的木板。刹那间，外面的世界——那残酷、冷漠、耀眼的阳光——倾泻而入，冲刷走了所有的阴霾。

Fujiwara and Sayuri didn’t care about shame or privacy. They stripped right there in the harsh, revealing light, turning the room into a theater of raw, grinding flesh.

藤原和小百合不在乎羞耻或隐私。他们在刺眼的光线下直接剥光了彼此，把这间屋子变成了一场原始、粗暴的肉体博弈场。

藤原压在小百合身上，皮肤被汗水浸透，胯下有节奏地、猛烈地撞击着她。小百合的叫声高亢、狂乱，让整间屋子都充斥着一个女人被彻底蹂躏的声音。

Miyetsuko sat in the corner, her eyes fixed on them. She watched the way Fujiwara’s back muscles knotted and strained, the way Sayuri’s thighs wrapped tightly around his waist. Something sparked in her dead eyes. It wasn't love, and it wasn't just lust—it was the savage, undeniable proof that they were alive.

美悦子缩在角落里，双眼死死盯着他们。她看着藤原背部绷紧的肌肉，看着小百合双腿死死缠住他的腰。她死寂的眼中闪过一丝火花。那不是爱，也不仅仅是性欲——那是野蛮的、不可辩驳的生命铁证。

Finally, she couldn't take it anymore. The primal heat pulled her up. She staggered over, her legs shaking from months of disuse, and threw herself onto the pile of tangled limbs.

终于，她再也按捺不住。那种原始的热度将她拽了起来。她摇摇晃晃地走过去，因数月没怎么走动而发颤的双腿险些跪倒，她直接把自己抛向了那团纠缠的躯体。

She grabbed Fujiwara’s hair and shoved her hand between them, feeling the wet, hot friction of their union. She was hungry, desperate to be part of the heat. But just as she began to press her own body against them, Fujiwara went soft.

她抓着藤原的头发，将手插进他们之间，感受着那种潮湿、灼热的摩擦。她饥渴万分，疯狂地想要融入这份滚烫中。但就在她刚将身体贴上去时，藤原那玩意儿却瞬间软了。

He fell back, panting, looking absolutely drained.

他向后瘫倒，大口喘气，看起来彻底被掏空了。

"Damn it," he hissed, grabbing a small, crumpled pill bottle from the floor.

“妈的，”他骂道，从地板上捡起一个被揉皱的小药瓶。

He had been popping potent blue pills like candies for days, drugging himself into a rigid, artificial performance just to bait Miyetsuko back to life. He had been playing the role of a tireless bull, all while his real heart was near failing from the stress.

这几天他像吃糖一样吞着强效壮阳药，强行让自己维持在一个虚假的硬挺状态，只是为了诱使美悦子对生活产生一点渴望。他在扮演一头不知疲倦的种马，可他真实的身体早已因为极度焦虑而到了崩溃边缘。

"I... I can't," he groaned, throwing the empty bottle aside.

“我……我不行了，”他呻吟着，将空药瓶甩到一边。

Miyetsuko looked at the pills, then at his defeated, limp member, then back at his exhausted face. She finally laughed—a genuine, dark, raspy laugh. She realized what he had been doing. She didn't feel pity; she felt a weird, twisted sense of connection.

美悦子看了看药瓶，又看了看他那萎靡不振的货，最后看向他那张疲惫不堪的脸。她终于笑了——那是真切的、阴郁且沙哑的笑。她看穿了他的把戏。她没有同情，反而感到了一种诡异、扭曲的联结。

"You're a pathetic bastard, Fujiwara," she whispered, her eyes finally alight with a cold, sharp hunger as she crawled on top of him. "But at least you're trying."

“你真是个可怜的混蛋，藤原，”她低语道，眼中终于燃起了一抹冰冷、锐利的欲望，她缓缓爬到他身上，“但至少，你还在努力。”

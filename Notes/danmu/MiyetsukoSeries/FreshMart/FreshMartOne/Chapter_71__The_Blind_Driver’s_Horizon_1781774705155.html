Chapter 71: The Blind Driver’s Horizon

发布时间：2026/6/18 17:25:03

第七十一章：驶向海平线

The "Fresh Mart" was locked up tighter than a tomb. A sign hung on the door: “Closed for Scientific Discovery and Coastal Research.”

“新鲜马特”大门紧闭，封得像个坟墓。门上挂着块牌子：“闭店进行科学探索与海洋考察。”

The old van was packed to the gills. Fujiwara sat in the passenger seat, his heart hammering against his ribs like a trapped bird. Miyetsuko was sprawled in the back, tossing popcorn into her mouth, while Sayuri sat beside her, her pregnant belly a soft, burgeoning mound beneath her sundress, her face glowing with the kind of peace only a woman who has survived a "public defilement" and a "genetics scare" could possess.

旧货车里塞得满满当当。藤原坐在副驾驶位上，心脏像只被困的鸟一样在肋骨下乱撞。美悦子在后座瘫着，往嘴里抛爆米花；小百合坐在她身旁，夏日长裙下隆起的孕肚像一个小巧、蓬勃的山丘，脸上带着那种唯有经历过“当街乱搞”和“基因乌龙”后才能拥有的宁静与光彩。

And at the helm? Rin.

而坐在驾驶位上的？是铃。

"Hands at ten and two, Rin," Fujiwara coached, his voice tight. "The road is wide open. Just… keep it between the lines. If you feel the vibration change, that’s the gravel. Don’t jerk the wheel."

“手放在十点和两点钟方向，铃，”藤原声音紧绷地指导，“路面很宽阔。只要……保持在车道中间就行。如果感觉到震动变了，那是碎石路。别猛打方向盘。”

Rin sat with her back ramrod straight, her sightless eyes fixed on the windshield—or rather, on the feeling of the space ahead. She didn't need to see the road; she felt the pitch of the engine, the shift of the wind against the metal frame, and the rhythmic thump-thump of the tires hitting the pavement joints.

铃坐得笔直，那双失明的眼睛死死盯着挡风玻璃——或者说，盯着前方空间的触感。她不需要看路；她能感知引擎的音调、风撞击金属车身的细微改变，以及轮胎碾过路面接缝时那有节奏的“哐当”声。

"I have it, Fujiwara-san," she whispered, her lips curled into a serene, terrifyingly confident smile. "I can feel the horizon."

“我掌控得住，藤原先生，”她低语，嘴角勾起一抹宁静而又令人胆寒的自信微笑。“我能感觉到地平线了。”

She pressed the pedal. The van lurched forward, gaining speed with a groaning roar.

她踩下油门。货车猛地向前冲去，发出沉重的轰鸣，速度逐渐提升。

"This is insane!" Miyetsuko shrieked from the back, laughing as she was pinned against the seat. "We’re being driven by a blind girl toward the ocean! If we die, I’m haunting your ass, Fujiwara!"

“这简直疯了！”美悦子在后座尖叫，被惯性压在座位上大笑，“我们正在被一个盲眼姑娘载着冲向大海！要是我们死了，我做鬼也不会放过你，藤原！”

Sayuri just giggled, her hand resting on her belly. "I think the baby likes the speed. He’s kicking!"

小百合只是咯咯笑着，手抚着肚子。“我觉得宝宝喜欢这速度。他在踢我呢！”

Rin drove with a bizarre, instinctual grace. She drifted slightly onto the shoulder, felt the rumble, and corrected with a smooth, fluid motion that put professional drivers to shame. She was driving by pure vibration, a symphony of metal and nerves.

铃开得有种古怪而本能的优雅。她微微偏离了路肩，感受到震动后，又以一种流畅得让职业司机汗颜的动作稳稳修正了回来。她是在靠纯粹的震动驾驶，那是一场金属与神经的交响乐。

As the van crested the final hill, the blue, endless expanse of the ocean exploded into view—even for Rin, who felt the sudden, expansive shift in the air pressure and the salty tang of the sea breeze filling the cabin.

当货车翻过最后一道山坡，蔚蓝、无尽的海洋轰然映入眼帘——即便对铃来说，她也能感受到空气压力的骤然释放，以及那股涌入车厢的、带着咸味的清爽海风。

"We're here," Rin announced, slamming on the brakes with perfect, heart-stopping timing. The van skidded to a halt, centimeters from the beach cliff.

“到了，”铃宣布道，在心脏停跳的精准时刻猛地踩下刹车。货车滑行着停了下来，距离海边悬崖只有几厘米。

They sat in the ringing silence of the stopped engine, the sound of crashing waves rolling in like thunder. Fujiwara looked at Rin, who was beaming, her hair windswept and wild.

引擎熄灭后，车内陷入了一阵嗡嗡的静谧，滚滚的海浪声如雷鸣般涌来。藤原看着铃，她正笑得灿烂，乱发在风中狂舞。

"Well," Fujiwara exhaled, his heart finally slowing down. "That was the most terrifying, glorious ride of my life."

“好吧，”藤原呼出一口长气，心脏终于慢了下来，“这是我这辈子坐过最惊悚、也最辉煌的一程。”

"Shall we go for a swim?" Rin asked, unbuttoning her shirt with a playful, teasing glint in her lacklustre eyes. "I hear the water is perfect for... anatomy studies."

“要下水游个泳吗？”铃问，带着玩味的神色解开衬衫扣子，她那双失神的眼睛里闪烁着一丝调皮。“我听说海水最适合用来……进行‘人体构造’研究了。”

[Learning Point]

Packed to the gills: (习语) 塞得满满的。

Burgeoning: 迅速生长的/萌芽的。

Ramrod straight: 笔直的。

Tang: 强烈的味道。

Lacklustre: 无光泽的（形容铃的眼睛）。

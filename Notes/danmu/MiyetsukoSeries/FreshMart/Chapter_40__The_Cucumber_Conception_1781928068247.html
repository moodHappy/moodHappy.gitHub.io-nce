Chapter 40: The Cucumber Conception

发布时间：2026/6/20 12:01:07

第四十章：黄瓜引起的“怀孕”恐慌

The "Fresh Mart" was in the middle of a particularly surreal promotion event. To boost sales of produce, someone—presumably Fujiwara in a fit of market-driven madness—had propped up a row of long, crisp cucumbers right next to the checkout counter. Sayuri, while reorganizing a display, had tripped, landing with a precision that bordered on the divine onto one of the more rigid, promotional specimens.

“新鲜玛特”正处于一场极其魔幻的促销活动中。为了推销农产品，不知是谁——多半是藤原在那阵发了疯的市场营销冲动下——在收银台旁边摆了一排又长又脆的黄瓜。小百合在整理货架时，脚下一滑，以一种近乎神迹的精准角度，“坐”进了其中那根最为坚挺的促销样本里。

She didn't immediately move. Instead, a strange, rhythmic sway took over her hips, her eyes glazing over with a sudden, dazed tranquility. A wave of warmth bloomed within her—a soft, internal glow that had nothing to do with physics and everything to do with the bizarre nature of their household.

她没有立刻起身，反而摇晃起了屁股，动作竟然带着某种富有韵律的节拍。她的双眼失焦，笼罩在一阵奇异的、恍惚的宁静中。一股温热在她体内绽放——那是一种柔和的、内在的辉光，与物理法则无关，却与这个家庭诡异的本质息息相关。

Old Wang, lurking near the frozen foods like a vulture, saw the performance and didn't miss his cue. He sidled up, his grin greasy and predatory. "That looks like a tight fit, sweetheart. How about I step in and show you what 'comfortable' really feels like?"

一直像秃鹫一样盘踞在冷冻区旁的老王，看到了这一幕，立刻捕捉到了机会。他凑了过来，脸上挂着油腻而贪婪的狞笑：“看起来挺紧啊，美人。要不要我来搭把手，让你知道什么才叫真正的‘舒服’？”

Sayuri snapped out of her stupor, her face twisting into a mask of righteous fury. "You disgusting, low-life creep!" she shrieked, grabbing a nearby bottle of industrial-strength cleaner to brandish as a weapon. "Get back before I melt your skin off!"

小百合从恍惚中惊醒，脸上瞬间布满了正义的愤怒。“你这恶心的下流胚子！”她尖叫着，抓起旁边一瓶工业级清洁剂挥舞起来，“滚远点，不然我把你皮都给化了！”

The incident should have ended there, but in the "Fresh Mart," normalcy is a virus that gets eradicated immediately. By the next day, Sayuri was convinced. She walked around with a hand resting protectively over her lower belly, her expression haunted by the specter of biological destiny.

这事儿本来该到此为止，但在“新鲜玛特”，正常逻辑是会被立刻清除的病毒。到了第二天，小百合彻底魔怔了。她双手护着小腹，走起路来小心翼翼，脸上写满了对“生命奇迹”的恐惧。

"Fujiwara," she whispered, pulling him into the back room. "I think... I think I'm pregnant. From the cucumber."

“藤原，”她把他拽进后仓，压低声音说，“我觉得……我觉得我怀孕了。就是那根黄瓜。”

Fujiwara stared at her, his jaw dropping. "You're what? That's not how biology works. That's not how anything works."

藤原盯着她，下巴都要掉下来了。“你什么？生物学不是这么运作的。这世界上根本没这种运作方式。”

"Don't you dare use science on me!" she snapped, her voice trembling. "I can feel it. The energy is different. My cycle hasn't arrived, and I'm craving pickles at three in the morning. If I'm not pregnant, then what is this internal temperature?"

“少拿科学那套来压我！”她急了，声音发抖，“我感觉得到。能量场都不一样了。我的姨妈没来，而且我大半夜地疯狂想吃酸黄瓜。如果不是怀孕，那这体内的温热又是怎么回事？”

She spent the rest of the week hiding in the bathroom, checking for her period with a desperation usually reserved for gambling addicts waiting for the results of a high-stakes match. To Sayuri, the cucumber wasn't just a vegetable anymore—it was a ticking biological bomb that threatened to turn her life into a botanical nightmare.

她此后的一周都躲在厕所里，在那儿焦急地等待姨妈造访，那种绝望感简直像是个等着翻盘的赌徒。在小百合眼里，那根黄瓜不再只是蔬菜，它是一枚正在倒计时的生物炸弹，随时可能把她的生活炸成一场植物学的梦魇。

[Learning Point]

Stupor: 恍惚/迟钝。

Lurking: 潜伏/徘徊。

Righteous fury: 正义的愤怒。

Specter: 幽灵/恐慌（此处指对怀孕的恐惧）。

Botanical: 植物学的。

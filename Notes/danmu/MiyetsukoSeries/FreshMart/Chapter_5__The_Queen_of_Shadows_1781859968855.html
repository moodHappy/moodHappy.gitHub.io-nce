Chapter 5: The Queen of Shadows

发布时间：2026/6/19 17:06:06

第五章：游离的归宿

Aya didn't just fit in; she became the missing piece of a puzzle no one knew was broken. Her humor was dry, her antics were bizarrely timed, and her ability to vanish from a room only to reappear five minutes later in a completely different outfit—or with a completely different drink—kept the entire "Fresh Mart" crew on their toes.

绫不仅融入得极快，甚至成了这个拼图中本不存在的一块。她的幽默感冷得掉渣，行为举止诡异且神出鬼没。她能前一秒还在客厅，五分钟后就消失得无影无踪，再出现时却换了一身衣服，甚至手里还端着杯全新的饮料——这搞得“新鲜马特”全员时刻保持着战备状态。

The "Dinner" was, predictably, a total failure. Or rather, it was abandoned before the first plate even touched the table.

那顿“晚餐”不出所料地成了彻头彻尾的败笔。或者说，还没等第一道菜上桌，就被所有人抛诸脑后了。

Fujiwara and Aya didn't even bother with the living room furniture. As soon as the laughter from the vibrator incident died down, they exchanged a look—a silent, electric agreement—and disappeared into the back warehouse.

藤原和绫甚至没打算在客厅里凑合。在跳蛋闹剧带来的笑声平息后，两人交换了一个眼神——那是种无声却充满电荷的默契——转瞬间就钻进了后仓。

Miyetsuko, sitting alone on the sofa, simply sighed and opened a book. "Well," she muttered to the empty room. "At least the storage area has good acoustics. They’ll have a symphony of crates to moan against."

美悦子独自坐在沙发上，无奈地叹了口气，翻开了一本书。“好吧，”她对着空荡荡的房间嘟囔，“至少仓库的共鸣效果不错。他们在那儿搞出的动静，足以让一堆货架为之伴奏了。”

[The Terms of Engagement / 留下的契约]

An hour later, they emerged. Aya looked like she had just conquered a small nation, her hair slightly messy but her expression as composed as ever. Fujiwara followed, looking like he’d been put through a professional blender, but with a grin that could light up a city block.

一小时后，两人走了出来。绫看起来像是刚征服了一个小国，发丝微乱，但神情依旧淡定如初。藤原紧随其后，样子活像刚从专业的果汁机里被榨过一遍，但那嘴角的笑意足以点亮整个街区。

"I’m staying," Aya announced, leaning against the counter. "But let’s be clear. I don’t ‘live’ here. I inhabit the shadows. If I want to leave for a week to go to Paris, I leave. If I want to show up in the middle of the night and make you all miserable, I do that too."

“我留下来，”绫靠在柜台上宣布，“但把话说清楚。我不是在这‘居住’，我是在这里‘游离’。如果我想去巴黎待一周，我就走；如果我想在大半夜冒出来，把你们折腾得鸡飞狗跳，我也照做不误。”

Fujiwara shrugged, holding out his hand. "Sounds like standard operating procedure for this family. We don't do 'normal,' Aya. We do 'chaotic'."

藤原耸耸肩，伸出了手。“听起来简直是我们这个家的标准作业程序。我们这儿不搞‘正常’那一套，绫。我们专攻‘混乱’。”

Aya shook his hand, her grip surprisingly firm. "Good. Because the moment you all start acting like a regular, boring suburban couple, I’m gone. I need the madness to keep my edge."

绫握住他的手，力度出奇地坚定。“很好。因为只要你们表现得像那种平庸无趣的郊区夫妇，我就立刻走人。我需要这种疯狂来保持我的锋芒。”

Rin and Sayuri scurried over, eager to welcome their new "shadow" sister. The warehouse was still humming with the aftershocks of their encounter, but the vibe in the store had completely stabilized.

铃和小百合兴奋地凑了过来，热烈欢迎这位新的“暗影”姐妹。后仓里还回荡着他们刚才那场相遇的余韵，但店里的氛围却前所未有地稳定了下来。

They weren't just a shop anymore. They were a collective of misfits, bound together by sex, grocery store debt, and the mutual understanding that the most "normal" thing they could do was to be as delightfully dysfunctional as possible.

他们不再只是一间便利店。他们是一群格格不入的怪胎，被肉欲、商店债务，以及那份“只有维持绝对的混乱才算正常”的共识紧紧绑在了一起。

[Learning Point]

Flux: 变动/流转。

Acoustics: 声学/共鸣效果。

Symphony: 交响乐（此处形容他们的动静）。

Inhabit: 栖息/居住。

Dysfunctional: 功能失调的/不正常的。

第三章：秘制咖啡

The morning shift at Fresh Mart began with a frantic energy. Fujiwara burst into the staff restroom, slamming the door shut with his heel. He didn't even bother to turn on the light. Driven by a sudden, manic surge of morning impulse, he unzipped his pants and took care of himself right there, his hands moving with hurried, practiced intensity until he climaxed directly into the paper cup of black coffee he’d been holding. He swirled the viscous fluid into the dark roast, a perverse, silent apology brewing in the steam.

“新鲜玛特”的早班伴随着一阵疯狂的活力开启。藤原冲进员工厕所，用脚后跟用力关上门，连灯都没开。在那股突如其来的晨间冲动驱使下，他解开裤子，手指带着急促而熟练的节奏，直到将精液直接射进了手中的黑咖啡里。他将这粘稠的液体搅进深烘咖啡中，一杯充满了扭曲歉意的“特调”在热气中氤氲。

Ten minutes later, he walked out to the checkout counter, holding the cup out with a charming, apologetic smile. "Miyetsuko, I’m sorry about yesterday. I was a jerk. Please, take this as a peace offering."

十分钟后，他走到收银台，带着迷人而诚恳的笑容将咖啡递了过去。“美悦子，关于昨天的事我很抱歉。我当时是个混蛋。请收下这个，算作我的道歉。”

Miyetsuko looked at him, her eyes still holding a trace of yesterday’s coldness, but she couldn't resist the smell of the fresh brew. She took a tentative sip. Her brows furrowed slightly; it was thicker, saltier, and creamier than the usual bitter bean water. She looked up at him, surprised. "It tastes... different," she murmured, a faint, shy blush creeping up her neck as she finished half the cup in one go. "But it’s actually kind of sweet."

美悦子看着他，眼里还带着昨天的寒意，但她无法抗拒那杯现煮咖啡的香气。她试探性地喝了一口。眉头微微蹙起；这咖啡比平时苦涩的豆浆要浓稠、咸鲜且滑顺得多。她惊讶地抬头看着他。“味道……有点不一样，”她低语道，当她一口气喝掉半杯时，一阵淡淡的、羞涩的红晕爬上了她的脖颈。“但意外地有点甜。”

Fujiwara’s grin widened, his eyes dancing with secret triumph as he watched her swallow. "I'm glad you like it. I put a lot of effort into making it extra special just for you."

藤原的笑容更灿烂了，看着她咽下去，眼里闪烁着胜利的暗芒。“很高兴你喜欢。为了让你喝到，我可是下了很大功夫，特地加了点‘私房秘方’。”

Miyetsuko chuckled, the tension of the previous night evaporating into the morning air. She reached out, playfully bumping her hip against his. "Well, you're forgiven, Fujiwara. But don't think this makes up for the bento yesterday. You still owe me."

美悦子轻笑出声，昨晚的紧绷感在晨光中消散无踪。她伸出手，俏皮地用胯部撞了撞他。“好吧，藤原，我原谅你了。但别以为这就能抵消昨天的便当，你还欠着我呢。”

Chapter 33: The Bento Ransom

发布时间：2026/6/14 09:11:59

第三十三章：炸鸡赎金

Miyetsuko lunged, her fingers brushing the plastic lid, but Fujiwara was faster. With a smooth, practiced motion, he pivoted, pinning her against the cold glass of the display case. He held the bento high above his head, safely out of reach, while his other hand settled firmly on her waist, anchoring her in place.

美悦子猛地扑过去，指尖堪堪擦过塑料盖，但藤原更快。他动作娴熟地一转，将她抵在冰冷的展示柜玻璃上。他高高举起那份便当，让她够不着，另一只手则稳稳扣住她的腰，将她禁锢在原地。

The smell of the freshly fried karaage was intoxicating, but the scent of Fujiwara—sharp, clean, and dangerously close—was stronger. She was trapped in the narrow space between the shelf and his hard, muscular frame.

刚出锅的炸鸡香气诱人，但藤原身上那股干净、凌厉且危险的气息更加浓烈。她被困在货架和他强壮的躯体之间。

"That’s cheating!" she hissed, though her breath caught in her throat. She tried to squirm away, but he pressed closer, his body heat radiating through the thin fabric of her blouse.

“这可是耍赖！”她低斥道，喉咙却微微发紧。她试图挣扎，但他压得更近，滚烫的体温穿透她薄薄的衬衫，烫得她心尖发颤。

Fujiwara leaned down, his lips ghosting against the shell of her ear. The thrill of having her pinned like this, completely at his mercy, sent a surge of pure adrenaline through him.

藤原低下头，嘴唇若有若无地蹭过她的耳廓。这种将她彻底掌控、让她任凭摆布的快感，让他全身的血液都沸腾起来。

"Wants vs. needs, Princess," he murmured, his voice a low, gravelly vibration that rattled her nerves. "You need to eat, and I want a little reward. A kiss, right here, right now, and the bento is yours. Take it or starve."

“想和需要，小公主，”他低语道，那沙哑而低沉的嗓音震得她心跳乱了节奏，“你需要吃饭，而我想要一点报酬。亲我一下，就在这儿，现在，这便当就是你的。选吧，是亲我还是挨饿？”

Miyetsuko stared up at him, her eyes bright and defiant, though her lower lip trembled ever so slightly. The store was empty, the fluorescent lights humming overhead. She knew he was playing with her, pushing his luck—and she loved every second of it.

美悦子抬头盯着他，眼中闪烁着倔强与挑衅，下唇却止不住地微微轻颤。商店里空无一人，头顶的日光灯发出嗡嗡声。她知道他在玩火，在试探底线——而她正沉迷于这其中的每一秒。

She didn't back down. Instead, she slid her hand up his chest, her fingers curling into his collar to pull him just a fraction closer. "You drive a hard bargain, Fujiwara," she whispered, her gaze dropping to his mouth. "Let’s see if the chicken is even worth it."

她没有退缩，反而将手覆上他的胸口，指尖揪住他的领口，用力将他向下拉近了一分。“你可真会做生意啊，藤原，”她低语，目光落在他的唇上，“让我看看这份炸鸡，到底值不值得。”

[Learning Point]

Pivoted: 旋转 / 转身。

At someone's mercy: 任凭某人摆布。

Gravelly: 沙哑的（形容嗓音）。

Drive a hard bargain: 极力讨价还价 / 开出苛刻条件。

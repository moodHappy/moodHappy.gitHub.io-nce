Chapter 92: The Resurrection of the Succubus

发布时间：2026/6/15 22:39:21

第九十二章：魅魔的复生

Miyetsuko found a perverse, crystalline clarity in the anguish of others. She watched from her balcony as the powerful knelt in the dirt, their desperation a slow-motion collapse she savored like vintage wine. But the cruelty she dealt out eventually circled back to her own body. A simple aspirin taken for a persistent, throbbing headache triggered a catastrophic, violent allergic reaction. Her entire system went into anaphylactic shock; her asshole and throat swelled to a grotesque, burning red, closing off her airway. She was clinically dead. The morgue prepared for an autopsy, her skin cooling on the steel slab.

美悦子在众人的痛苦中找到了一种病态的、如同结晶般的清醒。她从阳台上俯瞰那些跪在泥土中的权贵，享受着他们绝望中那场慢动作般的崩塌，如同品尝陈年美酒。然而，她散播的残忍终究反噬到了自己身上。为了缓解持续的剧烈头痛，她服下了一片阿司匹林，却引发了灾难性的剧烈过敏。她的全身陷入过敏性休克，肛门与喉咙肿胀成狰狞的紫红色，彻底封死了呼吸道。她在临床医学上已经死亡。停尸房甚至准备好了尸检，她的体温在金属托盘上渐渐流逝。

In the hollowed-out silence of the morgue, Fujiwara refused the finality of it. With a manic, desperate devotion, he began feeding her avocados—the high-fat, nutrient-dense pulp mashed between his lips and forced into her cold mouth, a frantic gamble against the dark. It was a bizarre, ritualistic alchemy. As the cream-like substance slipped down her throat, her heart stuttered, then sparked. She rose from the slab, a miraculous reclamation of life that defied every law of biology. It was, in every sense, awesome.

在停尸房空洞的死寂中，藤原拒绝接受这终局。他带着一种疯狂而绝望的虔诚，开始用嘴喂她吃牛油果——那种富含高油脂、营养密集的果肉在他唇间捣碎，强行渡入她冰冷的口中，这是一场对抗黑暗的孤注一掷。那是一场诡异的仪式化炼金术。随着那乳脂般的物质滑入喉咙，她的心脏抽动了一下，随即迸发出火花。她从托盘上坐起，那是一场违背所有生物学规律的奇迹。那画面，从任何层面看，都令人敬畏。

Upon awakening, Miyetsuko’s descent into madness took a decorative turn. She transformed the villa into a gaudy, gold-drenched Baroque nightmare, heavy with velvet and gilded excess. She commanded the sentry to stand his post while brandishing a jagged, rusted bayonet, a silent, lethal sentinel meant to "exorcise" the demons she no longer feared but welcomed.

苏醒后，美悦子的疯狂转向了装饰艺术。她将别墅改造成了一场浮夸、镀金的巴洛克式噩梦，充满了天鹅绒与镀金的奢靡。她命令哨兵在执勤时挥舞着一把锈迹斑斑、锯齿状的刺刀，那是一个沉默而致命的守卫，旨在“驱魔”——驱逐那些她已不再恐惧、反而引以为友的恶魔。

Their residence became a vortex—a gravitational center of depravity. Rumors began to infect the city, spawning vulgar jokes about the "Fresh Mart Witch." The common folk whispered she was a wallow-loving bitch, a creature whose very anatomy was cursed; they claimed her ass-scent was like a weasel—musky and pungent—and that she was nothing more than a vessel of lust conjured by the dark arts of Fujiwara the Wizard. She walked the halls with a bloated, heavy abdomen, perpetually filled with the sticky, drying semen of a dozen masters, a walking testament to the demon she had become.

他们的居所成了漩涡——一个堕落的重力中心。流言开始在城中蔓延，催生出无数关于“新鲜玛特女巫”的粗俗笑话。平民们窃窃私语，说她是只沉溺于烂泥坑的母狗，躯体本身就受过诅咒；他们声称她的屁股散发着黄鼠狼般的臭气——那种麝香且刺鼻的味道——说她不过是“巫师”藤原以黑魔法召唤出的欲望化身。她在长廊间行走，腹部高高隆起，里面装满了那些主人们粘稠且正在干涸的精液，那是她已化身为魔的活证据。

Chapter 21: The Unraveling

发布时间：2026/6/17 15:27:47

第二十一章：失控

The atmosphere in the shop shifted, turning heavy and humid, saturated with the scent of lavender oil and something more primal. Under the expert, rhythmic pressure of the professional's hands, the wall Miyetsuko had built against the world—and against Fujiwara—finally crumbled. Her body, starved for a release that wasn't laden with tension or hostility, began to respond with a mind of its own.

店内的气氛骤然生变，变得闷热潮湿，空气中充斥着薰衣草精油的味道，以及某种更原始的气息。在专业按摩师老练而有节奏的按压下，美悦子为自己筑起的那道屏障——那道用来隔绝世界，也用来隔绝藤原的防线——终于彻底坍塌了。她的身体早已渴求一种不带任何紧绷或敌意的释放，开始不由自主地做出反应。

She moved with a sudden, fluid grace, her legs snapping upward like a spring. With a desperate, hungry strength, she hooked her ankles around the masseur’s waist, pulling him into her space. Her eyes were glazed, locked onto the ceiling as she gasped, "Come inside... just come inside."

她动作流畅得近乎本能，双腿如弹簧般猛地向上勾起。她带着一种近乎绝望的渴望，用双脚死死扣住按摩师的腰，强行将他拉入自己的领地。她的眼神有些失焦，直勾勾地盯着天花板，喘息着低吟道：“进来……进来吧。”

The professional paused for only a fraction of a second, his calm, clinical mask slipping. He discarded the oil bottle, the glass clattering on the counter, and followed her invitation, thrusting forward into her hot, welcoming depths.

专业按摩师的动作只停顿了不到一秒，他那副冷静、职业的面具瞬间碎裂。他一把推开精油瓶，瓶子撞击收银台发出脆响，随即顺从了她的邀请，猛地挺入她火热而包容的深处。

In the shadows of the back room, Fujiwara’s breath hitched. He had wanted her to relax, but not like this. Seeing her completely lost—her body arching, her nails digging into the stranger’s shoulders, her voice filled with a needy, raw intensity he hadn't heard in years—felt like a physical blow. He wasn't the one soothing her; he was the one being replaced.

在后仓的阴影里，藤原倒吸了一口凉气。他要的是她放松，但决不是这样。看着她彻底沦陷——身体弓起，指甲陷入陌生人的肩膀，那嗓音里满是一种他多年未闻的、充满索求的原始渴望——这感觉简直像挨了一记重击。不再是他抚慰她，他成了那个被彻底取代的人。

The store was silent, save for the wet, rhythmic slapping of skin against skin and Miyetsuko’s incoherent, pleasure-filled cries. She was a different woman—open, desperate, and entirely surrendered.

店里静得可怕，只剩下皮肉碰撞时那湿润而有节奏的声响，以及美悦子语无伦次、充斥着快感的呻吟。此刻的她判若两人——毫无保留、绝望而彻底地臣服着。

When it was over, the silence that followed was suffocating. The professional adjusted his uniform, his breathing steadying, while Miyetsuko lay splayed on the counter, still flushed and trembling. He looked at Fujiwara in the shadows, then back at her.

当一切归于平静，接踵而至的死寂令人窒息。按摩师整理好制服，呼吸渐渐平复，而美悦子仍瘫软在收银台上，面色潮红，身体还在微微颤抖。他看了看阴影里的藤原，又转头看向美悦子。

"The massage..." the man said, his voice quiet, almost respectful. "Consider it free. You've given me an experience I’ll never forget. Thank you for your time." He gathered his kit and walked out, leaving the door slightly ajar.

“这次按摩……”那男人轻声说道，语气里竟带着几分莫名的恭敬，“就当是免费的吧。你给了我一段终生难忘的体验。感谢你们。”他收好工具箱，径直离去，只留下一扇半掩的门。

Fujiwara stepped out from the darkness, his face a mask of cold, controlled fury. He looked at Miyetsuko, who was still staring at the ceiling, utterly spent.

藤原从黑暗中走出来，脸上戴着一张由冰冷与克制组成的怒火面具。他注视着仍瘫在那儿、望着天花板精疲力竭的美悦子。

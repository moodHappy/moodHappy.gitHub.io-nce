第八十八章：正妻的秘密武器

Fujiwara was out—a rare, silent afternoon at the supermarket leaving the three women alone in the suite. The air felt lighter without his constant, bumbling presence. Sayuri, still glowing from her "high-end" treatment the night before, and Rin, ever the curious apprentice, found themselves cornered in the living room by Miyetsuko’s languid, queenly aura.

藤原不在——难得的静谧午后，超市那边暂时不需要他，这让三人在套房里拥有了一段难得的独处时间。少了他在场那种笨拙的忙乱，空气似乎都轻盈了些。小百合昨晚刚享受完“高端服务”，正散发着温润的余韵，而铃，那个永远好奇的学徒，和她一起被美悦子那慵懒的女王气场逼进了客厅的角落。

"Fujiwara is simple," Miyetsuko began, lighting a cigarette and exhaling a plume of smoke. "You two treat him like a king, and he gets bored. You’re too eager to please. You want to know why I’m the one he trusts most? It’s because I don’t just serve him. I ruin him."

“藤原这人很简单，”美悦子点燃一支烟，缓缓吐出一口烟圈，“你们俩把他当国王供着，他迟早会腻。你们太想讨好他了。想知道为什么我才是那个让他最离不开的人？因为我不仅仅是在伺候他，我是在‘毁’了他。”

Sayuri blinked, her hand resting instinctively on her belly. "Ruin him? But he seems... so happy with us."

小百合眨了眨眼，本能地抚摸着孕肚。“毁了他？可他跟我们在一起时……看起来很幸福啊。”

Miyetsuko scoffed. "Happy is fine, but it’s not addictive. Listen, when he’s in bed with you, you’re trying to be 'good.' You’re worried about whether you’re too loud, or if you’re doing it right. With me? I make him forget he’s a man. I make him feel like he’s losing his grip on reality."

美悦子嗤笑一声。“幸福是不够的，那不叫上瘾。听好了，当他跟你们上床时，你们总想着要表现得‘好’。你们担心自己是不是叫得太大声，或者姿势是不是正确。而我呢？我让他忘记自己是个男人，我让他感觉自己正在失去对现实的掌控力。”

She leaned forward, her eyes narrowing. "Let me be graphic. When I take him, I don’t wait for his permission. I ride him with such a violent, suffocating intensity that he has to beg for air. I use my muscles to milk every drop of energy out of him, squeezing him until his eyes roll back and he starts babbling nonsense like a child."

她向前倾身，双眼微眯。“说得直白点。我搞他的时候，从不等待他的许可。我骑在他身上，那种暴力而窒息的律动，会让他不得不开口乞求喘息的机会。我用肌肉榨干他每一滴精力，直到他翻白眼，像个孩子一样只会说胡话。”

Rin gasped, her face flushing. "I... I usually try to follow his rhythm. I thought that was the correct way to handle his anatomy."

铃倒吸一口凉气，脸涨得通红。“我……我通常尽量跟上他的节奏。我以为那是应对他生理构造的正确方式。”

"That’s your mistake," Miyetsuko chuckled. "You treat his dick like a precious artifact. I treat it like a weapon I’m stealing. I play with his balls with a sharp, flicking pressure that makes him jump, and I use my tongue to tease him until he’s begging me to just finish him off. I bite his shoulders until I leave bruises, and I moan—not because it feels good, but because I’m mocking how pathetic he sounds when he loses control."

“这就是你们的错，”美悦子轻笑，“你们把他那玩意儿当成传世珍宝供着。而我把它当成我抢夺来的武器。我用指尖带着那种尖锐的、弹拨般的压力去玩弄他的要害，让他受惊跳起来；我用舌头一点点撩拨，直到他求着我赶快让他泄出来。我会在他肩膀上咬出淤青，我会呻吟——不是因为舒服，而是为了嘲笑他在失控时发出的那种可怜声音。”

Sayuri looked shocked, but there was a flicker of realization in her eyes. "So... it's about being more aggressive? Taking control?"

小百合一脸震惊，但眼神中却闪过一丝了悟。“所以……关键在于更主动？掌握主权？”

"It's about being dangerous," Miyetsuko corrected. "You two are 'safe.' I’m the adrenaline hit he needs to remember he’s alive. You want the secret? Stop worrying about his pleasure and start focusing on your own domination. When you stop trying to keep him satisfied and start trying to make him fear your intensity—that’s when you become irreplaceable."

“关键在于‘危险’，”美悦子纠正道，“你们俩太‘安全’了。我是那种能让他意识到自己还活着的肾上腺素。想要秘诀吗？别再操心怎么让他爽，去专注于你们自己的支配欲。当你不再试图满足他，而是开始让他敬畏你们的狂野时——那才是你们真正变得不可替代的时候。”

The room was silent, the smoke from Miyetsuko's cigarette curling toward the ceiling. Rin looked lost in thought, and Sayuri looked at her own hands, suddenly wondering if she was ready to be a little more... dangerous.

房间里一片死寂，美悦子指尖的烟雾蜿蜒升向天花板。铃陷入了沉思，小百合看着自己的双手，突然好奇自己是否也准备好变得稍微……危险一点了。

Chapter 143: The New Partnership

发布时间：2026/6/17 10:11:10

第一百四十三章：三人行，必有“新秩序”

The office felt like an interrogation room, the air stagnant and heavy. Chen Mo stood in the center, his hands trembling at his sides. He looked like a man expecting a firing squad, not a promotion.

办公室里压抑得像间审讯室，空气死气沉沉。陈默局促地站在中央，双手不安地垂在身侧，看起来倒像是准备去领死，而非来升职。

Fujiwara sat behind the desk, his gaze locked on the clerk with the intensity of a predator who had already decided on the meal. "You’ve got guts, kid," he began, his voice dropping into a low, menacing growl. "’Hole-in-one,’ huh? You really thought you were that slick?"

藤原坐在办公桌后，眼神像是一头已经选好猎物的野兽，死死盯着对方。“胆子不小啊，小子，”他开口了，声音低沉而充满威胁，“‘一发入魂’，是吧？你真以为自己那么精明？”

Chen Mo swallowed hard, his face pale. "I… I used protection, Boss! I swear! But Miyetsuko… she said it didn’t feel right. When we were… when she was on top, she slipped it off when I wasn't looking. I was so damn gone, Boss. Her ass is just so… so fucking plump, and that hole—it just gripped me, ring after ring of muscle, swallowing me whole. I lost my mind."

陈默艰难地咽了口唾沫，脸色惨白。“我……我带套了，老板！我发誓！但是美悦子说……她说那样不舒服。当时是女上位……她趁我没注意偷偷拿下来了。那时候我真的爽昏头了，老板。她的屁股太肥美了，那个穴……紧紧地一圈圈裹着我，像是要把我整个人都吸进去。我当时根本没法思考。”

Fujiwara stared at him for a long, torturous silence. Then, his face did a complete 180. He leaned back in his leather chair, a slow, bizarrely satisfied grin spreading across his lips.

藤原沉默地盯着他，那段寂静漫长得近乎折磨。随后，他的脸色来了个一百八十度大转弯。他靠在皮椅背上，嘴角勾起一抹缓慢而诡异的、心满意足的坏笑。

"Well, now that we’ve confirmed you’re capable of... results," Fujiwara chuckled, opening a drawer and tossing a thick contract onto the desk. "Let’s get down to business. Since you’ve already had a taste of the manager’s 'hospitality' and provided us with a new team member, we’re keeping things professional."

“行吧，既然确认了你小子确实有‘产出’的能力，”藤原轻笑一声，从抽屉里甩出一份厚厚的合同，“咱们来谈谈生意。既然你都已经领教过店长的‘热情’了，还给我们增添了新成员，那我们接下来就得公事公办。”

He pointed at the contract. "Effective immediately, you’re our new Vice Manager. I’m splitting one-third of the store’s shares in your name. We’re going to run this place as a trio."

他指了指合同，“即刻生效，你是我们的新任副店长。我分给你三分之一的股份。咱们以后就以三人组的模式经营这家店。”

Chen Mo blinked, his confusion absolute. "A… a trio?"

陈默眨了眨眼，一脸懵逼。“三人组？”

"Exactly," Fujiwara said, his eyes gleaming with a manic, shared-wealth logic. "We’re establishing a rotation. You take her on Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays. Tuesdays, Thursdays, and Saturdays are my territory. Sundays? Well, Sunday is a group project. We keep this business growing, we keep the family expanding, and most importantly, we keep life good. What do you think?"

“没错，”藤原眼中闪烁着一种近乎病态的“共享经济”逻辑，“我们实行轮班制。周一、三、五你陪她。周二、四、六归我。至于周日？那是团队建设日。咱们齐心协力把店做大，把家扩大，最重要的是，把日子过好。你觉得呢？”

Chen Mo looked at the contract, then at Fujiwara’s unshakable, predatory smile. The fear was replaced by a slow, dawning realization of the paradise he had been handed.

陈默看着合同，又看了看藤原那副不可动摇、如野兽般的微笑。恐惧瞬间烟消云散，取而代之的是一种狂喜——他终于意识到，自己掉进了一个怎样的温柔乡。

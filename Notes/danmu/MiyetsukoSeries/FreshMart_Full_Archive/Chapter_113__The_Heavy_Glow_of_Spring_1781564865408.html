Chapter 113: The Heavy Glow of Spring

发布时间：2026/6/16 07:07:43

第一百一十三章：春日的甜蜜负担

The second year in the city brought a new rhythm to their lives. The snow had long melted, replaced by the lush, vibrant greens of spring, and with it, a profound change: Miyetsuko was now six months pregnant, her silhouette rounded and soft, glowing with the heavy, serene radiance of motherhood.

在这座城市的第二年，生活换上了新的节奏。积雪早已融化，取而代之的是葱郁的春意。随之而来的，是一个重大的变化：美悦子怀有六个月的身孕，身形变得圆润丰盈，散发着一种母性那沉静而厚重的光辉。

However, domestic life was not without its "complications." Fujiwara, having traded his past brutality for a relentless, affectionate obsession, seemed to view her pregnancy as a challenge to his stamina. He was like a fervent, energetic hound, unable to keep his hands—or his desires—to himself, regardless of the time or place.

然而，平淡的生活并非没有“小插曲”。藤原虽然褪去了昔日的残暴，却变得如同一只热情如火的公狗，对她的爱意与欲念到了近乎执迷的地步。他似乎将她的孕期视作一场对他精力的考验，无论何时何地，总想着要亲近她。

Miyetsuko was often found bustling about the store, her hand resting protectively on her belly. "Fujiwara!" she would whisper, her voice a mix of exasperation and endearment as he slipped an arm around her waist while she was arranging jars of jam. "People are coming! Can't you be a little more... restrained?"

美悦子常在店里忙碌，手总是温柔地护着肚皮。“藤原！”每当她正在摆放果酱罐，他却从身后环住她的腰时，她总会嗔怪地低语，语气里既有无奈，又藏着宠溺，“客人们要来了！你就不能稍微……克制一点吗？”

"Restrained?" Fujiwara would chuckle, his lips grazing the nape of her neck. "With you looking like this? Impossible. You’re more tempting than ever, carrying our little one like that."

“克制？”藤原轻笑着，嘴唇摩挲着她的后颈，“看着你现在这个样子？那是不可能的。现在的你，怀着我们的孩子，比以往任何时候都更让人心动。”

The shop was their sanctuary, but also their playground. On a quiet Tuesday afternoon, as she sat on the counter, checking the ledger, he would lean in, his hand tracing the curve of her hip, his eyes dark with a hunger that made her pulse race.

超市成了他们的避风港，也是他们的游乐场。在一个宁静的周二午后，当她坐在柜台前核对账目时，他会凑过来，手掌沿着她圆润的臀线游走，眼底那抹饥渴让她心跳加速。

"I need you," he would murmur, a direct, unfiltered declaration that made her blush fiercely, even after all this time. "Just a quick one. Please?"

“我想要你，”他低喃着，这种直白且毫无修饰的表白，即便过了这么久，依然让她羞红了脸，“就一会儿。好吗？”

"You're an animal," she would sigh, though her hands were already pulling him closer, her dignity bowing to the sheer, overwhelming love she felt for him. She worried about the baby, about the decorum of a shopkeeper, but the heat between them was a flame that refused to be extinguished. It was a sweet, slightly frantic sort of happiness—the kind that turned their mundane days into a private, never-ending romance.

“你真是个野兽，”她叹了口气，但双手却早已将他拉近。骨子里的端庄虽在挣扎，却最终败给了对他那份排山倒海的爱意。她偶尔会担心肚子里的孩子，也会顾虑店主的身份，但这两人之间燃烧的热度是不容抗拒的。这是一种甜蜜却又略带疯狂的幸福——将他们琐碎的日常变成了永不落幕的私人罗曼史。

[Learning Point]

Six months pregnant (身怀六甲): 怀有身孕。

Fervent, energetic hound: 热情的公狗/充满活力的猎犬。

Exasperation and endearment: 无奈与宠溺。

Unfiltered declaration: 直白的表白。

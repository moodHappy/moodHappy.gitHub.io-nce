Chapter 18: The Architect of Shadows

发布时间：2026/6/23 19:49:58

第十八章：暗影中的操盘手

The alliance between Sato Mina and Takagi Ryuji was not built on trust, but on the cold, mutually beneficial intersection of ambition. While Mina provided the leverage—the digital chains binding Miyetsuko—Ryuji provided the infrastructure to ensure those chains never broke.

佐藤美奈与高木龙司之间的盟约并非建立在信任之上，而是建立在野心那冰冷且互利的交汇点。美奈提供了筹码——束缚美悦子的数字枷锁，而龙司则提供了确保枷锁永不断裂的后台基础设施。

Inside the penthouse of the Takagi estate, the city lights flickered far below like dying embers. Ryuji swirled a glass of vintage scotch, his eyes fixed on a monitor displaying a live, encrypted feed of Miyetsuko’s social media metrics.

在高木府邸的顶层公寓内，远方的城市灯火如将熄的余烬般闪烁。龙司晃动着手中的陈年威士忌，目光紧盯着监视器——屏幕上实时滚动着美悦子社交媒体数据的加密反馈。

"She’s trending higher than ever," Ryuji remarked, his voice devoid of emotion. "The 'Cold Goddess' persona is working. The students are obsessive, and the donors are salivating. You’ve done well, Mina."

“她的热度比以往任何时候都要高，”龙司评价道，声音里没有任何情感。“‘冷艳女神’的人设很成功。学生们为之疯狂，赞助商们也垂涎欲滴。你做得很好，美奈。”

Mina sat on the velvet sofa, looking less like a partner and more like a high-stakes employee. "She’s starting to push back. She and that boy, Kenta, have figured out that I need her image intact. They’re becoming... volatile."

美奈坐在天鹅绒沙发上，与其说她是合伙人，看起来倒更像是一名高风险的职员。“她开始反抗了。她和那个叫健太的男生已经发现，我需要她保持完美的形象。他们变得……难以控制了。”

Ryuji chuckled, a sound like glass grinding against stone. He walked over and placed a hand on Mina’s shoulder, his grip possessive and heavy. "Let them play. The more volatile they are, the more raw emotion she displays on stage. It makes for better content. But remember, Mina—if you lose control, I don't need a middleman. I have ways to make a 'Miracle' disappear without the need for digital blackmail."

龙司轻笑一声，那声音如同玻璃在岩石上摩擦。他走过去，将手搭在美奈肩上，力道既有占有欲又显得沉重。“让他们玩吧。他们越不稳定，她在台上展现出的原始情感就越丰富。这会让演出效果更好。但记住，美奈——如果你失去控制，我不需要中间人。我有的是办法让一个‘奇迹’消失，而无需依靠什么数字勒索。”

Mina shivered, feeling the chilling reality of her position. She was the one holding the leash, but Ryuji was the one who owned the kennel.

美奈打了个冷战，感受到了自身处境那令人战栗的现实。她是握着绳索的人，但龙司才是这间狗舍的主人。

"What about Kenta?" Mina asked, her throat dry.

“那健太呢？”美奈问道，喉咙发干。

"Kenta is a variable that needs to be factored out," Ryuji replied, turning back to the window. "A minor annoyance. I’ve already authorized a scholarship transfer to a technical academy in a distant prefecture. By the time the school festival concludes, he’ll be a ghost. And our 'goddess'? She’ll be exactly where I need her to be—on a pedestal, entirely under my patronage."

“健太是个需要被剔除的变量，”龙司转身看向窗外，回应道。“一个微不足道的麻烦。我已经授权将他的奖学金转往远县的一所技术学院。等校庆结束，他就会人间蒸发。至于我们的‘女神’？她会出现在我需要的地方——被供奉在神坛上，彻底成为我的私有物。”

Mina nodded, though her heart pounded with a mix of jealousy and terror. She realized too late that she wasn't just playing a game with a common boy; she was playing with a predator who saw the entire academy as his private game preserve.

美奈点头应下，内心却充斥着嫉妒与恐惧。她太晚才意识到，自己不仅仅是在和一个普通的男孩博弈，而是在与一个将整所学园视为私家猎场的捕食者周旋。

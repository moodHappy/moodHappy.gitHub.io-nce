Chapter 4: Rooftop Confession

发布时间：2026/6/21 19:58:50

第四章：天台告白

The rooftop of Sakura Academy was bathed in a deep, bleeding orange as the sun began its slow descent behind the distant mountains.

随着太阳开始缓慢向远山下沉，樱花学园的天台沐浴在深邃而浓烈的橘色霞光中。

Miyetsuko stood by the chain-link fence, her white dress catching the evening breeze, making the fabric cling to her generous thighs and heavy hips.

美悦子站在铁丝网边，白裙随晚风起舞，布料紧紧贴在她丰腴的大腿和宽阔的臀部上。

Kenta stood a few paces behind her, his heart hammering so hard he feared it might shatter his ribs.

健太站在她身后几步远的地方，心跳得如此剧烈，他真怕那跳动会撞碎自己的肋骨。

"Miyetsuko-chan," he called out, his voice barely a whisper against the whistling wind.

“美悦子酱，”他轻声唤道，声音在呼啸的风中微弱得近乎耳语。

She turned around, and the golden light hit her face, making her eyes shimmer like liquid honey.

她转过身，金色的光芒映照在她的脸上，让她的双眼像流动的蜂蜜一样闪烁。

Kenta reached into his pocket and pulled out a pale blue envelope, his fingers trembling uncontrollably.

健太伸手进兜里，掏出一个淡蓝色的信封，他的手指不可抑制地颤抖着。

"I... I can't keep this inside anymore. I know I'm just an ordinary guy, but being with you this past month... it’s all I think about."

“我……我再也无法隐藏这份感情了。我知道我只是个平凡的男生，但过去这一个月和你在一起的时间……是我脑海中唯一的念头。”

Miyetsuko looked at the letter, then up at Kenta, her breathing becoming shallow and fast, causing her ample chest to heave rhythmically.

美悦子看着那封信，又抬头看向健太，她的呼吸变得短促而急促，胸口也随之富有节奏地起伏着。

"Kenta-kun..." she breathed, a faint blush spreading across her porcelain cheeks like ink in water.

“健太君……”她轻声呼唤，淡淡的红晕像水中的墨迹般在她瓷白的脸颊上散开。

Kenta stepped forward and placed the letter in her soft, small hands. "I love you, Miyetsuko. Not as the school's goddess, but as the girl who cries for birds."

健太走上前，将信放在她那双柔软的小手里。“我喜欢你，美悦子。不是把你当成学园的女神，而是那个会为小鸟流泪的女孩。”

There was a long silence, only the sound of the wind and the distant chime of the school bell marking the end of the day.

长时间的沉默中，只有风声和远处预示着一天结束的校钟残响。

Slowly, Miyetsuko clutched the letter to her heart, her head dipping low as she gave a small, shy nod that sent Kenta’s spirit soaring.

缓缓地，美悦子将信紧紧贴在心口，微微垂下头，含羞地轻轻点了点头，这动作让健太的灵魂仿佛飞上了云端。

"I... I feel the same way," she whispered, her voice so sweet it felt like a physical caress. "I’ve been waiting for you to say that."

“我……我也是一样的心情，”她低声呢喃，那声音甜美得仿佛一种实质性的抚摸，“我一直都在等你开口。”

Kenta reached out and gently tucked a stray lock of hair behind her ear, feeling the incredible warmth radiating from her skin.

健太伸手，温柔地将她耳边的一缕碎发别到耳后，感受着从她皮肤上散发出来的惊人热量。

Under the dying light of the sunset, the goddess and the boy shared a secret that the rest of the world wasn't yet allowed to know.

在日落的余晖中，女神与少年共享了一个这个世界暂时还不被允许知晓的秘密。

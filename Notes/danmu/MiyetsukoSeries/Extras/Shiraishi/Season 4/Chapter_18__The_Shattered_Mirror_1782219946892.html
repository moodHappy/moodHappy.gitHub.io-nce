Chapter 18: The Shattered Mirror

发布时间：2026/6/23 21:05:45

第 18 章：破碎的镜子

The silence that had haunted the apartment since Shiraishi’s return was finally broken, not by reconciliation, but by a jagged, furious eruption. Word of Miyetsuko’s presence at the host club had traveled fast—like a poison seeping through the cracks of her social circle—and reached Shiraishi before the sun had even fully set on his return.

自白石回来后，公寓里那令人心悸的沉默终于被打破了，但带来的不是和解，而是一场狂暴的冲突。美悦子现身牛郎店的消息像毒液般渗透进她的社交圈，在白石回来的当天夕阳尚未落下时，就已经传到了他的耳朵里。

He confronted her the moment he walked through the door, his eyes cold, stripped of the raw, protective warmth she had once cherished. "A host club, Miyetsuko? Is this the company you keep when I’m gone? Am I not enough to satisfy your craving for attention?" His voice was low, a dangerous rumble that vibrated through the floorboards.

他一踏进家门便质问她，眼神冰冷，剥离了她曾珍视的那种原始而炽热的保护感。“牛郎店？美悦子？这就是我不在时你相处的人吗？难道我对你渴求的关注还不够吗？”他的声音低沉，像一阵危险的轰鸣，震动着脚下的地板。

Miyetsuko didn't shrink away. The lingering sting of the condom and the membership card—the secrets that had been festering in her mind—pushed her past the point of fear. She threw his accusation back at him, her voice trembling but sharp as a blade. "And what about you, Shiraishi? You talk to me of loyalty while keeping your pockets lined with 'bubble bath' memberships and condoms we have no use for. How was Tokyo? How was the 'business' in the city of Mizuki Nemuru?"

美悦子没有退缩。口袋里避孕套和会员卡的刺痛——那些在她脑海中发酵已久的秘密——让她超越了恐惧的界限。她将指责原封不动地抛回给他，声音颤抖却如刀锋般锐利。“那你呢，白石？你跟我谈忠诚，却在口袋里装着‘泡泡浴’会员卡和我们根本用不到的避孕套。东京之行如何？在那个属于美月眠的城市里，你的‘公事’办得顺利吗？”

The air in the room seemed to ignite. Shiraishi’s face darkened, his composure shattering as he realized his own secrets had been unearthed. They fell into a violent, chaotic argument—a collision of accusations, broken trust, and the agonizing truth that their perfect sanctuary was merely built on layers of deception. They were no longer lovers in a private sanctum; they were two strangers caught in a storm of their own making, tearing each other apart with the very words that once carried their love.

空气仿佛瞬间被点燃。白石脸色阴沉，意识到自己的秘密已被曝光，他的冷静瞬间崩塌。他们陷入了一场剧烈而混乱的争吵——那是责备、信任崩塌以及那令人痛苦的真相的碰撞：他们曾经完美的圣殿，不过是建立在层层欺瞒之上。他们不再是私密圣所里的爱人；他们是两名在自造风暴中挣扎的陌生人，用曾经承载过爱意的言语，撕裂着彼此。

📝 Vocabulary Highlight (学习笔记)

Jagged, furious eruption: 狂暴的爆发（jagged形容冲突的尖锐与不规则）。

Seeping through the cracks: 渗透进缝隙（比喻消息传播）。

Striped of: 被剥夺/剥离（指失去了某种特质）。

Dangerous rumble: 危险的轰鸣（形容低沉且充满压迫感的语气）。

Festering: 发酵/溃烂（比喻心事或怀疑在心中滋长）。

Collision of accusations: 责备的碰撞。

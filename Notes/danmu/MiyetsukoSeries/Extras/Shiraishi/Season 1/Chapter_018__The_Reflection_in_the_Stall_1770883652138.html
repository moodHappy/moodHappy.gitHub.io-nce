Chapter 18: The Reflection in the Stall

第 18 章：隔间里的倒影

Miyetsuko fumbled with the lock of the restroom stall, her breath coming in shallow hitches. She quickly discarded her school blouse and skirt, her skin still feeling the cool humidity of the morning air. With trembling fingers, she slid the sheer black lace over her hips and adjusted the delicate straps across her shoulders.

美悦子胡乱扣上厕所隔间的门锁，呼吸变得浅快而急促。她迅速脱掉校服衬衫和短裙，皮肤依然能感受到清晨空气中那股凉丝丝的潮气。她颤抖着指尖，将那件半透明的黑色蕾丝拉过臀部，并调整好搭在肩膀上的纤细肩带。

The sensation was unlike anything she had ever felt. The lace was impossibly soft yet felt like a thousand tiny electric sparks against her skin. She stood on the closed toilet lid to see herself in the small, flickering light of the stall. The contrast was breathtaking—the midnight-black patterns framed her snowy skin, and right there, peeking out from beneath a delicate lace edge, was the deep purple crescent of the bite mark Shiraishi had left.

这种感觉与她以往经历过的任何事都不同。蕾丝柔软得不可思议，却又像成千上万个微小的电火花在刺激着她的皮肤。她站在合上的马桶盖上，借着隔间里微弱闪烁的灯光打量着自己。那种对比惊心动魄——深夜般的黑色纹路勾勒着她雪白的肌肤，而就在那儿，从精致的蕾丝边缘下探出头的，正是白石留下的那道深紫色月牙齿痕。

She looked foreign to herself—no longer the "good student" her parents saw, but someone's specific, cherished obsession. A wave of heat surged through her, a mixture of intense shame and a terrifyingly sharp excitement. Just as she traced the curve of her hip through the transparent fabric, her phone vibrated in her discarded blazer.

她觉得自己变得陌生了——不再是父母眼中的那个“好学生”，而成了某个人的专属、被珍视的痴迷对象。一阵热浪席卷全身，其中夹杂着剧烈的羞耻感和一种可怕而尖锐的兴奋。正当她隔着透明的布料轻抚臀部曲线时，被丢在一旁的西装校服里的手机震动了起来。

It was a text from Shiraishi. She opened it with a pounding heart: "Send me a photo. Now."

是白石发的简讯。她心跳如雷地打开它：“拍张照给我看。现在。”

The command felt like a physical touch. Miyetsuko looked back at the mirror, her face flushed a deep, feverish red. Her first instinct was to refuse, to hide this secret version of herself. But then she remembered the weight of his hands in the storage room and the promise of "success" he had made.

这个命令感就像一次真实的触碰。美悦子重新看向镜子，脸红得像在发烧。她的第一直觉是拒绝，想把这个秘密的自己藏起来。但随后她想起了在器材室里他双手的重量，以及他许下的关于“成功”的承诺。

Trembling, she positioned the phone, capturing the way the black lace strained across her chest and the defiant purple mark on her shoulder. She didn't include her face, but the raw vulnerability of her pose said everything. With a shaky thumb, she hit send.

颤抖着，她调好手机位置，拍下了黑色蕾丝在她胸口紧绷的样子，以及肩膀上那道挑衅的紫色印记。镜头里没有拍到脸，但她那毫无防备的姿态已经说明了一切。她用颤抖的大拇指按下了发送键。

The silence that followed was agonizing. She stood there, half-naked in a public restroom, waiting for the judgment of the boy who owned her heart and her body.

随之而来的沉默让人备受煎熬。她就这样半裸着站在公共洗手间里，等待着那个掌控了她身心的男孩的审判。

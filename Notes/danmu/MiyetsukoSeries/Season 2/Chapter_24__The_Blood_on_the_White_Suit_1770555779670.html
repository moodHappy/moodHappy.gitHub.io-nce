Chapter 24: The Blood on the White Suit

第二十四章：白西装上的血

Silence returned to the warehouse. Dust settled on the bodies.

寂静回到了仓库。灰尘落在尸体上。

Fantasma crawled out from under a table, shaking. "Madre de Dios... you people are insane."

幽灵从桌子底下爬出来，发抖。“上帝啊……你们这群人是疯子。”

Miyetsuko jumped down from the forklift. Her white suit was ruined—torn at the shoulder, stained black with grease and red with blood splatter.

美悦子从叉车上跳下来。她的白西装毁了——肩膀撕裂，沾满了黑色的油污和红色的血点。

She walked over to Chen Mo.

她走向陈默。

He was leaning against the container, breathing heavily. A bullet had grazed his arm; blood was soaking his black shirt.

他靠在集装箱上，呼吸沉重。一颗子弹擦伤了他的手臂；血浸透了他的黑衬衫。

"You're bleeding," she touched his arm. Her fingers came away red.

“你流血了，”她碰了碰他的手臂。手指染红了。

"Just a scratch," Chen Mo shrugged. "But your suit is ruined."

“擦伤而已，”陈默耸耸肩。“但你的西装毁了。”

"I don't care about the suit," she grabbed his collar and pulled him down.

“我不在乎西装，”她抓住他的领子把他拉下来。

She kissed him. Hard. It was a kiss of possession, of relief, of adrenaline. She tasted the dust on his lips and the iron in the air.

她吻了他。狠狠地。这是一个占有的吻，解脱的吻，肾上腺素的吻。她尝到了他嘴唇上的灰尘和空气中的铁锈味。

Fantasma watched them, dumbfounded. Two killers, covered in filth and blood, making out in a graveyard of mercenaries.

幽灵目瞪口呆地看着他们。两个杀手，满身污垢和鲜血，在雇佣兵的坟场里激吻。

"You are my shield, Chen," she whispered against his lips, her hand sliding down to cup him through his pants. "And my sword. And my..."

“你是我的盾，陈，”她在他唇边低语，手向下滑，隔着裤子握住了他。“也是我的剑。还有我的……”

"Your partner," Chen Mo finished for her, kissing the blood on her cheek. "Always."

“你的搭档，”陈默替她说完，吻掉她脸颊上的血。“永远。”

Miyetsuko turned to Fantasma, her arm still around Chen Mo's waist, supporting him—and claiming him.

美悦子转向幽灵，手臂依然搂着陈默的腰，支撑着他——也宣示着主权。

"Clean this up, Fantasma. And double the security. The war has just begun."

“把这儿清理干净，幽灵。安保加倍。战争才刚刚开始。”

She looked at Chen Mo.

她看着陈默。

"Let's go home. I need to lick your wounds."

“我们回家。我要去舔你的伤口。”

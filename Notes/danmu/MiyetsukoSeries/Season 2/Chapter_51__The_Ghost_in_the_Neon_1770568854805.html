Chapter 51: The Ghost in the Neon

第五十一章：霓虹灯下的幽灵

"The Devil's Throat" club was celebrating. Fantasma was drinking champagne, laughing with his whores. He thought the explosion in the jungle meant his problem was solved.

“魔鬼喉咙”俱乐部正在庆祝。幽灵喝着香槟，和妓女们调笑。他以为丛林里的爆炸意味着他的麻烦解决了。

BOOM.

轰。

The front doors didn't open. They were blown off their hinges.

前门没有打开。它们被直接炸飞了。

Smoke filled the entrance. The music died instantly.

烟雾充满了入口。音乐瞬间死亡。

Out of the smoke walked a woman.

一个女人从烟雾中走出。

Miyetsuko.

美悦子。

She wasn't wearing the red dress anymore. She was back in the black tactical bodysuit, ripped at the shoulder, stained with mud and blood. She didn't look like a lady. She looked like a soldier who had crawled out of hell.

她不再穿那条红裙了。她换回了黑色战术紧身衣，肩膀处撕裂，沾满泥土和血迹。她看起来不像个淑女。她看起来像个刚从地狱爬出来的士兵。

In her left hand, she dragged a heavy assault rifle by the strap. In her right hand, she held The Butcher's gold-plated phone.

她左手拖着一把沉重的突击步枪的背带。右手拿着屠夫那部镀金手机。

Behind her, a wall of armed men—The Butcher's men—marched in, guns raised. But they weren't aiming at her. They were aiming at Fantasma.

在她身后，一堵武装人员的人墙——屠夫的人——走了进来，举着枪。但他们没有瞄准她。他们瞄准的是幽灵。

"You..." Fantasma dropped his glass. It shattered. "You're dead!"

“你……”幽灵掉了杯子。杯子碎了。“你死了！”

"I was," Miyetsuko walked onto the dance floor. The crowd parted like the Red Sea. "But hell spit me back out. It said I had unfinished business."

“我是死过，”美悦子走进舞池。人群像红海一样分开。“但地狱把我吐出来了。它说我还有未了的生意。”

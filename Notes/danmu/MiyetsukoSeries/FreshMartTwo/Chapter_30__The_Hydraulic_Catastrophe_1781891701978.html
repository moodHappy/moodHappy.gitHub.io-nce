Chapter 30: The Hydraulic Catastrophe

发布时间：2026/6/20 01:54:54

第三十章：液压千斤顶的暴力美学

The morning air was thick with the scent of cheap lubricant and industrial-grade disappointment. Just as the "Fresh Mart" crew was settling into a rhythm of despair, the earth began to tremble. A low, guttural rumble—the unmistakable sound of a high-torque diesel engine—ripped through the town’s quietude.

清晨的空气里弥漫着廉价润滑油和工业级失望的味道。“新鲜马特”的成员们正陷入绝望的日常节奏中，地面突然震动起来。一阵低沉、浑厚的轰鸣——那是高扭矩柴油发动机发出的、极具辨识度的声浪——撕碎了小镇的宁静。

Aya arrived. She wasn't driving a tractor in the traditional sense; she was piloting a custom-built, monster-sized rig that looked like it had been salvaged from the set of a post-apocalyptic thriller. She killed the engine in front of the convenience store, hopped down, and dragged a massive, industrial-grade hydraulic jack from the flatbed.

绫到了。她开的并不是传统意义上的拖拉机；她驾驭着一台定制的巨型怪兽，看起来像是从末日惊悚片的片场直接抢来的。她在便利店门口熄了火，跳下车，从拖斗里拖出了一个巨大的、工业级的液压千斤顶。

"Fujiwara," she barked, her face deadpan. "I’ve seen your 'maintenance' logs. Your structural integrity is failing. Demonstrate the lifting power of this unit. Now."

“藤原，”她吼道，一脸死板，“我看了你的‘维护日志’。你们的结构完整性已经崩了。给我演示一下这玩意儿的起重能力。现在。”

Fujiwara, still reeling from the shock of seeing her in the flesh, didn't argue. He positioned the jack against the central support beam of the store. He cranked the handle. Click. Clack. Hiss. The metal groaned, then shrieked. With a sound like a giant snapping a dry twig, the jack didn't just lift the store—it punched through the ceiling.

藤原还在震惊于她的真身出现，根本没法反驳。他把千斤顶顶在便利店的中心支撑梁下，用力转动把手。咔哒。咯吱。嘶。金属发出痛苦的呻吟，随后爆发出一声尖叫。随着一声巨响，仿佛巨人折断了一根枯枝，千斤顶不仅抬起了店面，还直接捅穿了天花板。

The roof folded like a wet cardboard box.

屋顶像个潮湿的纸盒子一样瞬间坍塌。

Rin, huddled under a shelf, felt a rain of dust and plaster. "Aya!" she shrieked over the sound of falling debris. "Are you really playing with tractors?! Or are you just trying to kill us with heavy machinery?!"

铃缩在货架下，被落下的灰尘和石膏劈头盖脸砸了一身。“绫！”她冲着坍塌声尖叫，“你到底是在玩拖拉机，还是单纯想用重型机械弄死我们？！”

Sayuri, miraculously unscathed, emerged from the wreckage of the back office. She was cradling the "Toilet Saint" like a football, her hair covered in drywall dust, looking like a war-torn refugee from a very strange apocalypse.

小百合居然奇迹般地毫发无伤，从后仓的废墟中爬了出来。她像抱橄榄球一样抱着“马桶圣子”，头发上全是石膏灰，看起来活像个从诡异末世里走出的受灾难民。

She blinked, seeing the sky through the hole where their ceiling used to be. "Well," she whispered, handing the baby to Aya. "At least we have an open-air floor plan now. Very trendy."

她眨了眨眼，透过天花板上的大洞看着天空。“好吧，”她低语道，把孩子递给绫，“至少我们现在是开放式户型了。挺时髦的。”

Aya took the infant, inspected his face with cold disdain, and sighed. "He’s still ugly. And you lot are still idiots."

绫接过婴儿，冷漠地打量了一番他的脸，叹了口气。“还是那么丑。而你们这群人，还是那么蠢。”

Fujiwara stood amidst the ruin of his empire, his shirt torn, holding the handle of the jack. "Miyetsuko isn't here to bail us out, Aya. She’s filming 'The Queen's Protocol' with Chen Mo. We’re on our own."

藤原站在帝国的废墟中，衬衫破破烂烂，手里还握着千斤顶的把手。“美悦子不在，没人帮咱们捞人了，绫。她去跟陈默搭档拍《女王的法则》了。咱们得靠自己了。”

The family stood in the rubble, looking at the sky where their roof had once been. The "Fresh Mart" was gone. The reconstruction would be a nightmare of zoning permits, lack of funds, and the sheer absurdity of their collective existence.

全家人站在瓦砾中，看着曾经是屋顶的地方。现在只剩下蓝天。“新鲜马特”完了。重建工作将是一场关于规划许可、资金短缺和集体存在荒谬性的噩梦。

Aya looked at the chaos, then climbed back into her monster rig. "I'll go find a lawyer. You lot start sweeping. And Fujiwara—stop playing with high-pressure hydraulics. You aren't built for it."

绫看着这片狼藉，爬回了她的怪兽车里。“我去找个律师。你们先把地扫了。还有，藤原——别再玩高压液压设备了。你那身板驾驭不了。”

She drove off, leaving them in the ruins of their convenience store, staring at the debris of a life they barely understood.

她开车走了，把他们丢在便利店的残骸中，对着那段连他们自己都搞不懂的生活碎片面面相觑。

[Learning Point]

Torque: 扭矩。

Salvaged: 打捞/回收（此处指从废品中淘出）。

Structural integrity: 结构完整性。

Drywall: 干墙/石膏板。

Zoning permits: 土地规划许可。

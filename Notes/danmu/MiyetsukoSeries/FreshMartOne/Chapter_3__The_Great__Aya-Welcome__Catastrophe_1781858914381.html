Chapter 3: The Great "Aya-Welcome" Catastrophe

发布时间：2026/6/19 16:48:33

第三章：灾难现场直播

The clock struck six. Aya stood in the doorway, looking elegant, sharp, and entirely unimpressed by the frantic atmosphere inside the apartment.

六点整，门铃响了。绫站在门口，神情冷峻而优雅，对公寓里那股诡异的紧张气氛视而不见。

Fujiwara, tasked with being the "polished host," felt his brain short-circuit the moment he made eye contact with Aya. Instead of a polite, "Welcome, please come in," he felt a surge of unmitigated, idiotic adrenaline. With the speed of a master gunslinger, he unzipped his trousers and brandished his anatomy like a party favor, his face twisted in a look of pure, moronic exhilaration: "Aya, would you like to... try this first?"

藤原，这位被委以“优雅接待者”重任的男人，在与绫目光交汇的瞬间，大脑仿佛短路了。他本该说出一句礼貌的“欢迎光临，请进”，却被一股无法控制的力量驱使着，以神枪手般的速度拉开裤链，像展示派对礼品一样亮出了自己的器官。他的脸上扭曲着一种纯粹而愚蠢的狂喜，说道：“绫，你要不要……先尝尝这个？”

Aya didn't scream. She didn't faint. She simply pulled out her phone and started taking a slow, steady panoramic video.

绫没尖叫，也没晕倒。她只是淡定地掏出手机，慢条斯理地录起了全景视频。

"Wow," she said, her voice flat. "I thought the invitation said 'Dinner,' not 'Free Sample'."

“哇哦，”她语气毫无波澜，“邀请函上写的是‘晚餐’，我以为不是‘免费试吃’。”

[The Cascade of Chaos / 连锁崩溃]

The disaster only spiraled from there.

灾难随即全面升级。

Rin (The "Social Lubricant"): Trying to recover the mood, Rin stumbled forward with the tea set, but tripped over the rug Fujiwara had neglected to fix. She didn't just spill the tea; she managed to launch the entire tray directly into the air. The teapot performed a perfect, graceful arc and landed squarely on Aya’s handbag.

铃（社交润滑剂）： 为了挽救气氛，铃端着茶具冲了上来，却被那块藤原忘了铺平的地毯绊了个狗吃屎。她不仅洒了茶，还把整盘茶具像导弹一样发射到了空中。茶壶划出一道完美的弧线，精准地扣在了绫的名牌手袋上。

Sayuri (The "Domestic Chef"): In the kitchen, Sayuri heard the crash and panicked. She rushed out, carrying her signature Nikujaga—but forgot she had placed the salt shaker inside the pot to "save time." She stumbled in, tripped over the tea-soaked rug, and dumped the entire pot of brine-soaked meat onto the floor, right at Aya’s feet.

小百合（餐饮总指挥）： 厨房里的小百合听到动静慌了神。她端着拿手的“炖肉”冲出来，却忘了自己为了“省事”把盐罐子直接扔进了锅里。她一脚踩在铃洒下的茶渍上，一个滑步，把那锅腌渍肉直接扣在了绫的脚边。

Miyetsuko (The "Visual Appeal"): Miyetsuko, meanwhile, had been trying to hide the "intimate evidence." As she scrambled to shove a stack of X-rated polaroids under the sofa, she slipped on a piece of tea-soaked meat, lost her balance, and slid across the floor like a penguin, colliding with Fujiwara—who was still standing there, unzipped and exposed.

美悦子（视觉美化）： 与此同时，美悦子正忙着藏匿那些“私密证据”。她在往沙发下塞色情宝丽来时，脚下一滑踩到了块炖肉，整个人像企鹅一样在地上滑了出去，一头撞在了还没来得及提裤子的藤原身上。

Aya stood in the center of the pile: tea-drenched handbag, salted meat, polaroids scattered like confetti, and Fujiwara pinned to the floor by an unzipped Miyetsuko.

绫站在这一地狼藉的中心：浸透茶水的手袋、咸肉块、像纸屑一样散落的照片，以及被美悦子撞翻、还露着那玩意儿的藤原。

She stood there for a long, agonizing minute. Then, she walked over to the fridge, grabbed a chilled beer, popped it open, and took a long sip.

她在那儿站了漫长而煎熬的一分钟。接着，她走到冰箱旁，拿出一罐冰啤酒，打开，喝了一大口。

"You know," Aya sighed, looking at the carnage, "I’ve seen some weird stuff in my line of work. But you people? You’re not weird. You’re a fucking biological hazard."

“你知道吗，”绫叹了口气，看着这幅惨状，“我这行见过的怪胎多了去了。但你们……你们不是怪胎，你们简直是生物危害。”

Fujiwara, still on the floor, offered a weak, thumbs-up. "So... does this mean you're staying for dessert?"

趴在地上的藤原虚弱地竖起大拇指。“所以……这意思是，你留下来吃甜点吗？”

[Learning Point]

Short-circuit: 短路（此处形容脑子乱了）。

Gunslinger: 枪手（形容动作快）。

Panoramic: 全景的。

Carnage: 大屠杀/大乱子。

Biological hazard: 生物危害。

Chapter 6: The Eternal Pregnancy

发布时间：2026/6/19 17:16:22

第六章：超长待机的“宿主”

The "Fresh Mart" had become a theater of the absurd, but even by their standards, Sayuri’s pregnancy was pushing the boundaries of biological reality. Eighteen months. A year and a half of "impending" motherhood.

“新鲜马特”已经成了荒诞剧的剧场，但即便以他们的标准来看，小百合的孕期也已经突破了生物学的底线。十八个月。一年半的“待产”期。

Sayuri sat on a crate, her belly a massive, round dome that seemed to have its own gravitational pull. She felt fine—if a bit like she was carrying a bowling ball fused to her spine.

小百合坐在一只板条箱上，那个巨大的圆滚滚的肚子仿佛自带引力。她感觉挺好——除了有点像脊柱上焊了一颗保龄球。

They had finally dragged her to a clinic. The doctor—a man whose glasses were perpetually perched on the tip of his nose and whose medical license was likely laminated from a cereal box—had barely glanced at the charts.

他们终于把她拖到了诊所。那个医生——眼镜永远挂在鼻尖上，医生执照看起来像是从麦片盒里剪出来的——压根没怎么看检查报告。

"Eighteen months?" the doctor had hummed, scratching his chin with a tongue depressor. "Well, everything seems 'normal' enough. Just keep waiting. Nature has a funny way of taking its time. It’ll come out when it’s good and ready."

“十八个月？”医生沉吟着，用压舌板挠了挠下巴。“嗯，一切看起来都‘正常’得很。安心等着吧。大自然嘛，总喜欢慢工出细活。它想出来的时候自然就出来了。”

[The Family Forum / 家庭研讨会]

Back at the store, the family gathered around the "resident expert."

回到店里，全家人围坐在“驻店专家”美悦子身边。

Miyetsuko sipped her wine, leaning against the counter. "Honestly? This kid is a perfect fit for us. It’s got the family brand of weirdness baked into its DNA. It’s been in there for eighteen months, probably because the rent is free and the view is spectacular."

美悦子抿了口酒，靠在柜台上。“说真的？这孩子简直是我们家的完美模板。它DNA里都刻着我们家的怪胎基因。在里面待了一年半，估计是因为房租免费，而且视线风景绝佳。”

Sayuri patted her stomach with a beatific smile. "It’s very cozy in there. I think it’s just waiting for the store to turn a profit again."

小百合面带圣洁的微笑，拍了拍肚子。“里面很舒服。我想它只是在等店里重新盈利。”

Fujiwara, who was busy scrubbing an imaginary stain off the floor, chimed in. "Maybe it’s just a strategic delay. It’s analyzing the market. It’ll be born a CEO."

正在卖力擦拭并不存在的地板污渍的藤原插话道：“或许这是一种战略性拖延。它在分析市场。出生就是个CEO的料。”

Then, Aya, who was lounging on the roof, dropped down—literally—through the skylight, landing silently on the floor. She dusted off her coat, her expression grim.

这时，一直窝在屋顶上的绫，直接从天窗跳了下来，无声地落地。她掸了掸大衣，一脸阴郁。

"I’ve been thinking," Aya said, her voice sharp. "I hate the sound of babies. The wailing, the squalling, the complete lack of professional boundaries—it’s like a living, breathing noise violation."

“我考虑过了，”绫声音尖锐。“我讨厌孩子的哭声。那种号啕大哭、尖叫，完全没有职业操守——简直就是个活着、会呼吸的噪音污染。”

She crossed her arms, looking at Sayuri’s belly as if it were a suspicious package. "We should just ship it to an orphanage the moment it pops out. Let someone else deal with the 'I’m hungry at 3 AM' protocol."

她交叉双臂，盯着小百合的肚子，仿佛那是个可疑包裹。“等它钻出来，我们直接把它送到孤儿院。让别人去处理那个‘凌晨三点饿得要命’的垃圾协议吧。”

The group looked at Aya, then at the "eternal" bump.

大家看着绫，又看了看那个“永恒”的孕肚。

"Aya," Sayuri said, laughing, "that’s a bit extreme, don't you think? It’s your niece or nephew."

“绫，”小百合笑着说，“这有点太极端了吧？那是你的侄女或侄子。”

"It’s a biological risk factor," Aya corrected, grabbing a snack from the shelf. "But I suppose I’ll allow it to stay... as long as it has a mute button."

“这是生物风险因子，”绫纠正道，从货架上顺手拿了袋零食，“不过，我姑且准许它留下来……只要它能有个静音键。”

[Learning Point]

Absurd: 荒诞的。

Gravitational pull: 引力（此处比喻肚子的沉重）。

Beatific: 圣洁的/幸福的。

Protocol: 协议/规程。

Biological risk factor: 生物风险因子。

Chapter 41: The Plastic Revolution

发布时间：2026/6/20 14:00:13

第四十一章：保鲜膜革命

Fujiwara’s brain chose that specific moment to short-circuit. Standing in the back room, surrounded by household essentials, he declared to the room at large that he was tired of "traditional" methods. He didn't want the standard; he wanted an upgrade. With the focus of a mad scientist, he grabbed a roll of heavy-duty, industrial-grade plastic wrap. He wrapped it layer upon layer around his phallus, creating a structure that was, by all accounts, absurdly thick and impossibly rigid.

藤原的大脑在这一刻彻底短路了。他站在后仓，四周环绕着各种生活必需品，宣布他厌倦了“传统”方式。他不要标准，他要升级。带着疯狂科学家的专注，他抓起一卷工业级厚保鲜膜，一层又一层地裹了上去，打造出一个粗得离谱、硬得骇人的结构。

Sayuri was the first test subject. As he pressed forward, the sheer circumference of the plastic-armored member elicited a reaction that transcended pleasure—it was a sensory overload. She sobbed, wailing in a mix of agony and ecstasy, her voice echoing through the walls.

小百合成了第一个测试对象。当他推入时，那层保鲜膜包裹下骇人的围度引发了一种超越愉悦的反应——那是一种感官的彻底过载。她哭天抹泪，哀号声与欢愉声交织，穿透了墙壁。

Miyetsuko and Rin were ripped from their sleep, their faces twisted with irritation. They stormed into the room, fueled by the primal jealousy of being left out of a breakthrough.

美悦子和铃被吵醒，脸上满是恼火。她们冲进房间，被那种没被带上“科技创新”的原始嫉妒心驱使着。

"Unfair!" Miyetsuko snapped, her eyes burning. "You’re testing new hardware without a full quality control audit!"

“不公平！”美悦子怒斥，眼中燃着火，“不经过全面质量控制审计就测试新硬件，你这算什么！”

Fujiwara let out a weary, triumphant laugh. "Relax. I’ll make sure you all get a trial run."

藤原发出了一声疲惫却得意的笑。“别急，一会儿给你们都试试。”

The next morning, Miyetsuko emerged from the bedroom looking unnervingly refreshed, her skin glowing with a post-experimental hum. "The tensile strength of that plastic," she mused, sipping her coffee with a predatory smile, "is genius. Fujiwara, we aren't just selling groceries anymore. We’re filing for a patent."

第二天早晨，美悦子从卧室走出来，整个人神清气爽，皮肤散发着一种实验后的迷人光泽。“那层保鲜膜的拉伸强度，”她抿着咖啡，露出掠食者般的笑容，“简直是天才。藤原，我们不再是卖杂货的了，我们得去申请专利。”

To overcome customer hesitation, they launched an aggressive marketing campaign: free, live demonstrations. To prove the product’s effectiveness, they designated Rin as the full-time test pilot. The results were immediate. Customers lined up by the dozens, and Rin became the centerpiece of the store’s most popular service. By sunset, she was utterly spent, a pale, trembling wreck literally swimming in a puddle of customer-donated DNA. She had bathed in the essence of the city’s population, and the store had never looked more profitable.

为了消除顾客疑虑，他们发动了激进的营销攻势：现场免费测试。他们指定铃担任全职测试员。结果立竿见影。顾客排起长龙，铃成了店里最受欢迎的服务项目。到日落时分，她已经彻底虚脱，瘫软成一团，在顾客留下的精液池里洗了个名副其实的澡。她浸泡在整座城市男性的精华中，而商店的利润也达到了巅峰。

The success was cataclysmic. The city was in a frenzy; pre-orders for the "Fujiwara Plastic Wrap Kit" were backed up until the winter of next year.

这场成功是毁灭性的。全城陷入狂热；“藤原保鲜膜套装”的预购订单已经排到了明年冬天。

That night, feeling a surge of performative gratitude, Fujiwara approached Rin. She was still shivering, her eyes unfocused, exhausted by the day’s labor.

那天晚上，藤原感到一股表演性的感激之情油然而生，他走到铃身边。她还在发抖，眼神涣散，整个人被一天的“劳动”彻底掏空。

"Rin," he murmured, leaning in. "I think you’ve earned a private session tonight."

“铃，”他低声靠近，“我想今晚你有权享受一场私人的临幸。”

Rin didn't even look up. She let out a dry, rattling laugh. "No time, Fujiwara. The order list is full until next year. I’m booked solid—don't wait up."

铃甚至没抬头。她发出一声干涩的笑声。“没时间，藤原。订单已经排到明年了。我已经被包圆了——别等我。”

Chapter 28: The Vacuum Virgin

发布时间：2026/6/20 01:35:33

第二十八章：真空吸尘器的初恋

Rin had developed a new, deeply unsettling obsession. With Aya gone and the "Tractor Tycoon" myth lingering like a bad smell, Rin had anchored her missing affection onto the most inanimate object in the house: the old, industrial-grade vacuum cleaner. She had draped one of Aya’s discarded skirts over the plastic housing, tying it with a bit of twine. To Rin, it was the closest thing to a sister she had left.

铃产生了一种极其诡异的新执念。绫消失了，“拖拉机大亨”的传说像残留的异味一样挥之不去，铃把满腔的思念全都寄托在家里那个最不起眼的物件上：那台老式工业吸尘器。她给吸尘器的外壳套上了绫穿过的一条旧裙子，还用绳子系紧。在铃眼里，这就是她身边最像绫的东西。

Every evening, Rin would curl up on the floor, whispering the day’s gossip into the nozzle. "You look so busy today, Aya," she’d murmur, stroking the cold, vibrating plastic. "Did you close any good trades?"

每天傍晚，铃都会蜷缩在地板上，对着吸尘器的喷嘴絮絮叨叨。“绫，你今天看起来真忙，”她抚摸着那冰冷、震动的塑料外壳，喃喃自语，“今天做了几笔好交易吗？”

One afternoon, disaster struck with the suddenness of a lightning bolt. Rin was leaning in for a particularly intimate confession when her foot brushed the power toggle. The machine roared to life with the fury of a jet engine. Before she could scream, the suction—designed to inhale gravel and dust—snatched a thick, luscious lock of her hair.

某天下午，灾难像闪电般降临。铃正凑过去准备进行一场深情告白，脚却不小心碰到了电源开关。机器像喷气式发动机般狂暴地咆哮起来。还没等她尖叫出声，那原本用来吸碎石和灰尘的吸力，瞬间就把她那撮浓密的秀发给拽了进去。

"Fujiwara! Miyetsuko! Help!" she shrieked, the vacuum dragging her head closer to its voracious, whirring heart. "It’s eating me! Aya is trying to consume my scalp!"

“藤原！美悦子！救命！”她尖叫道，吸尘器正把她的脑袋一点点往那个贪婪旋转的喉咙里拖，“它在吃我！绫想把我的头皮都吸走！”

Fujiwara and Miyetsuko sprinted into the room, their eyes widening at the sight: Rin was pinned to the floor, locked in a violent, one-sided wrestling match with a household appliance.

藤原和美悦子狂奔进屋，眼珠子差点瞪出来：铃被死死按在地板上，正跟一台家电进行着一场激烈、且单方面的扭打。

"Don't pull!" Fujiwara yelled, sliding across the linoleum. "You’ll pull her hair out by the roots!"

“别硬拽！”藤原在地板上滑跪过来，大吼，“头发会被连根拔出来的！”

Miyetsuko grabbed a kitchen knife, her eyes gleaming with terrifying intensity. "No, we have to perform an emergency extraction. Rin, hold still! I’m going to lobotomize this thing!"

美悦子抄起菜刀，眼神狂暴而犀利。“不行，得紧急破拆。铃，别乱动！老娘要给这破玩意儿做开颅手术！”

The vacuum was fighting back, rattling and screeching, seemingly determined to devour Rin’s entire head. Sayuri burst into the room, tripped over a loose cord, and landed squarely on the machine’s "Reverse" button. The vacuum let out a strangled cough, sputtered, and abruptly spat Rin’s hair back out, tangled, oily, and covered in a fine layer of gray dust.

吸尘器仿佛也学精了，剧烈地颤抖、尖叫，非要把铃的脑袋彻底吞下去不可。小百合这时冲进房间，被散乱的电线绊了一下，正好摔在了机器的“反转”按钮上。吸尘器发出一声像被扼住喉咙的干咳，喷出一阵灰尘，终于把铃的头发给吐了出来——那头发乱得像团鸡窝，又油又脏，还裹着厚厚一层灰。

Rin sat up, gasping, looking like a disheveled ghost. She hugged her hair to her chest, glaring at the vacuum. "You bitch," she hissed at the machine. "I told you I was sorry about the chores!"

铃坐起身，喘着粗气，看起来像个衣衫不整的冤魂。她护住自己的头发，恶狠狠地瞪着吸尘器：“你这骚货，我刚才不是说了我不小心吗？”

Fujiwara flopped back onto the floor, wiping sweat from his forehead. "Rin, I’m putting a lock on that machine. And if I see you whispering to it again, I’m donating you both to the local scrap yard."

藤原瘫倒在地，抹了把额头的汗。“铃，我以后得给这玩意儿上把锁。要是再看见你跟它说悄悄话，我就把你们俩一起扔进废品回收站。”

Sayuri looked at the dust-covered girl and the silent, skirt-wearing machine, and started to laugh. "Maybe we should just buy a real sister for her, Fujiwara. This is getting way too expensive on our blood pressure."

小百合看着那灰头土脸的女孩和那台依然穿着裙子的机器，忍不住大笑。“藤原，也许我们该干脆给她买个真正的姐妹。再这么玩下去，咱俩的血压怕是要先炸了。”

[Learning Point]

Inanimate: 无生命的。

Voracious: 贪婪的/渴求的。

Lobotomize: 切除额叶（此处幽默地指破拆机器）。

Disheveled: 乱蓬蓬的。

Scrap yard: 废品站。

The shopping mall in Tijuana was a cathedral of glass and excess, a place where the scent of expensive leather fought with the aroma of roasted agave.

蒂华纳的购物商场是一座由玻璃与过度消费构成的教堂，昂贵皮革的气味与烤龙舌兰的香气在此搏杀。

Miyetsuko moved through the boutiques like a predatory ghost, her shopping bags carried by a stoic Chen Mo.

美悦子像幽灵般穿梭于各家精品店，陈默面无表情地拎着购物袋跟在身后。

She stopped at a high-end lingerie shop, picking out a set of emerald green silk that matched the dark fire in her eyes.

她在一家高端内衣店停下，挑了一套翠绿色的丝绸内衣，那颜色正配她眼中暗藏的火光。

"Try it on," she whispered to Chen Mo, though the command was for him to follow her into the oversized fitting room.

“试试看，”她对陈默低声说道，但这命令其实是让他跟着她走进那间宽大的试衣间。

Inside, the air was cool and smelled of jasmine. The heavy velvet curtain muffled the sounds of the outside world.

试衣间内空气凉爽，透着茉莉香。沉重的丝绒帘幕隔绝了外界的喧嚣。

Miyetsuko stepped out of her sundress, her skin glowing like moonlight against the dark interior.

美悦子脱掉太阳裙，她的肌肤在暗沉的室内散发着如月光般的光泽。

She leaned back against the cold mirror, her large, heavy breasts swaying slightly as she guided Chen Mo’s hands to her waist.

The contrast was striking: his scarred, calloused palms against the impossible softness of her silk-clad curves.

对比极具视觉冲击力：他那满是伤痕、布满老茧的手掌，正贴在她那覆着丝绸、软到不可思议的曲线上。

Chen Mo breathed in the scent of her hair, his body a hard, grounding weight against hers.

陈默吸入她发间的香气，身体化作沉稳而坚硬的重量抵着她。

He lifted her, her legs wrapping around his hips, the green silk shifting and stretching as the tension in the room reached a breaking point.

他将她抱起，她的双腿缠上他的胯骨，随着室内的张力达到临界点，翠绿色的丝绸在滑动中被撑紧。

Just as the world narrowed down to the heat of their shared breath, and Chen was a heartbeat away from entering her—

就在世界缩小到只剩下两人交叠的呼吸，陈默离进入她仅剩一个心跳的瞬间——

STACCATO GUNFIRE.

连串的枪声响起。

The heavy plate glass of the storefront shattered, the sound of a thousand crystal bells screaming in the hallway outside.

店面的厚平板玻璃碎裂开来，数千只水晶铃铛般的尖啸声在门外的走廊回荡。

Screams replaced the ambient music, followed by the rhythmic thud-thud-thud of submachine guns.

尖叫声取代了背景音乐，紧接着是冲锋枪有节奏的“哒哒哒”声。

Chen Mo reacted instantly, shielding Miyetsuko’s body with his own and dropping to the floor, his hand already reaching for the concealed pistol in his waistband.

陈默反应迅速，用身体护住美悦子顺势伏倒在地，手已伸向腰间隐藏的手枪。

Miyetsuko lay beneath him, her emerald silk torn, her heart hammering not with fear, but with a cold, jagged fury.

美悦子躺在他身下，翠绿丝绸被扯坏了，她的心脏剧烈跳动，那不是因为恐惧，而是一种冰冷而参差的愤怒。

"In the middle of my climax?" she hissed, her eyes turning into two shards of black glass.

“在我高潮的关头？”她嘶声说道，双眼变成了两枚黑色玻璃碎片。

She reached for her white lace sundress, pulling the Golden Derringer from its hidden pocket before she even bothered to fix her bra.

她够向那件白色蕾丝裙，甚至懒得去整理胸罩，就先从隐蔽口袋里拔出了那把金色德林加。

"This isn't a gang war, Chen," she spat, wiping a stray smudge of lipstick from her chin.

“这不只是一场帮派枪战，陈，”她啐了一口，擦掉下巴上的一抹残红。

"This is an interruption. And I don't like being interrupted."

“这是在扫兴。而我不喜欢扫兴。”

She stood up, ignoring the chaos outside the curtain, the small gilded gun looking like a toy in her hand—until she clicked the safety off.

她站起身，无视帘外的混乱，那把精巧的镀金手枪握在手里像个玩具——直到她拨开了保险。

"Let's go kill whoever thinks their petty turf war is more important than my afternoon."

“走，去把那些觉得地盘争夺比我的下午更重要的家伙全杀了。”

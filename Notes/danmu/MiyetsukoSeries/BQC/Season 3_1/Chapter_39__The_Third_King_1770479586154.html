Chapter 39: The Third King

第三十九章：第三位国王

Silence. Not Mishima’s shot.

寂静。 枪声并非三岛所发。

The rolling door rose slowly— flooded by blinding light.

卷帘门缓缓升起—— 刺眼灯光倾泻而入。

Black armored SUVs. Engines idling like beasts.

黑色装甲SUV列阵。 引擎低吼如兽。

An old man stepped forward, silver wolf-headed cane in hand.

老人现身， 手握银色狼头手杖。

Kurosaki Soichiro. 黑崎宗一郎。

Rage burned in his eyes— the fury of a humiliated emperor.

他眼中燃起 被羞辱帝王的怒火。

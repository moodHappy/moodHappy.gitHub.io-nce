Chapter 49: The Prayer of the Wicked

第四十九章：恶人的祈祷

Chen Mo rappelled down from the tower. He walked into the ruins.

陈默从塔楼索降下来。走进废墟。

The smell of cordite and roast pork was overwhelming.

柯达炸药和烤猪肉的味道令人窒息。

Miyetsuko was sitting on the altar, next to the corpse. She was shaking. Not from fear, but from the massive adrenaline dump.

美悦子坐在祭坛上，就在尸体旁边。她在发抖。不是因为恐惧，而是因为巨大的肾上腺素释放。

"We killed him," she said, staring at her red hands. "The Butcher is meat."

“我们杀了他，”她说，盯着自己红色的双手。“屠夫变成了肉。”

Chen Mo holstered his rifle. He walked up to the altar. He didn't look at the corpse. He looked at her.

陈默收起步枪。走到祭坛前。他没看尸体。他看着她。

Her dress was ruined. Her hair was wild. She looked terrifying. She looked magnificent.

她的裙子毁了。头发狂乱。她看起来很可怕。她看起来很壮丽。

"You are a mess," he said softly, taking a handkerchief and wiping a spot of blood from her forehead.

“你一团糟，”他轻声说，掏出手帕擦掉她额头的一点血迹。

"I'm alive," she grabbed his wrist. Her grip was iron. "Take me out of here, Chen. I want to wash this off. In the ocean."

“我活着，”她抓住他的手腕。抓得死紧。“带我离开这儿，陈。我要洗掉这些。在海里。”

"The port is still closed," Chen Mo reminded her. "Fantasma betrayed us. We have no way out."

“港口还关着，”陈默提醒她。“幽灵背叛了我们。我们没有出路。”

Miyetsuko looked down at The Butcher's body. She saw his phone sticking out of his pocket.

美悦子低头看屠夫的尸体。她看到他的手机从口袋里露出来。

She smiled. A wicked, sharp smile.

她笑了。一个邪恶、锋利的笑。

"We have his phone. We have his accounts. We have his army's contacts," she picked up the phone. "The Butcher is dead. But his money isn't."

“我们有他的手机。有他的账户。有他军队的联系方式，”她拿起手机。“屠夫死了。但他的钱没死。”

She looked at Chen Mo, her eyes gleaming with a new plan.

她看着陈默，眼里闪烁着新计划的光芒。

"We don't need Fantasma's port. We are going to buy our own army. And we are going to march back into Rio not as refugees... but as the new Cartel."

“我们不需要幽灵的港口。我们要买下我们自己的军队。我们要杀回里约，不是作为难民……而是作为新的卡特尔。”

She pulled Chen Mo close, smearing blood on his tactical vest.

她把陈默拉近，血蹭在他的战术背心上。

"But first... kiss me. In front of him."

“但首先……吻我。在他面前。”

Chen Mo leaned in. He kissed her over the body of their enemy, amidst the burning ruins of the church. It was a blasphemous, holy moment.

陈默俯身。在敌人尸体的上方，在燃烧的教堂废墟中，吻了她。那是一个亵渎的、神圣的时刻。

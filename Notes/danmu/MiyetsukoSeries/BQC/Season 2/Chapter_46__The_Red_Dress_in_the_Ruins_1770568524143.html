Chapter 46: The Red Dress in the Ruins

第四十六章：废墟中的红裙

The Old Jesuit Ruins. A roofless stone church swallowed by the jungle. Vines strangled the altar, and sunlight pierced through the broken arches like spears.

旧耶稣会遗址。一座被丛林吞噬的无顶石教堂。藤蔓勒死了祭坛，阳光像长矛一样穿过破碎的拱门。

Miyetsuko stood in the center of the nave.

美悦子站在中殿中央。

She wasn't wearing tactical gear anymore. She had salvaged a dress from her emergency bag—a simple, blood-red slip dress. It was torn at the hem, stained with mud, and clung to her body with sweat.

她不再穿战术装备了。她从应急包里翻出一条裙子——一条简单的、血红色的吊带裙。裙摆撕裂，沾满泥土，被汗水粘在身上。

She looked like a sacrificial lamb. Or a fallen angel.

她看起来像只待宰的羔羊。或者一个堕落天使。

"How do I look?" she asked into the hidden earpiece.

“我看起来怎么样？”她对着隐藏耳机问。

"Like a trap," Chen Mo's voice came from above. He was perched high in the bell tower, concealed by ivy, looking through the scope of a sniper rifle.

“像个陷阱，”陈默的声音从上方传来。他栖身在高高的钟楼里，被常春藤遮蔽，通过狙击步枪的瞄准镜看着。

"Good," she smiled, running her hands over her hips. Under the thin red silk, taped to her inner thigh, was a ceramic knife. "Keep your eyes on me, Chen. Don't look at the jungle. Look at me."

“很好，”她笑了，双手抚过臀部。在薄薄的红丝绸下，大腿内侧贴着一把陶瓷刀。“看着我，陈。别看丛林。看着我。”

"I never look anywhere else," Chen Mo adjusted his scope. The crosshairs settled on the curve of her neck, then moved to the entrance of the ruins.

“我从不看别处，”陈默调整瞄准镜。十字准星停在她脖颈的弧线上，然后移向废墟入口。

The ground began to vibrate. Dust fell from the arches.

地面开始震动。灰尘从拱门落下。

They were here.

他们来了。

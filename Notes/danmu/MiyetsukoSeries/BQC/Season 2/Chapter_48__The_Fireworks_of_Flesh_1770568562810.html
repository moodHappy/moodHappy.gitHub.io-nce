Chapter 48: The Fireworks of Flesh

第四十八章：血肉烟花

"NOW!"

“现在！”

CLICK.

咔哒。

Chen Mo pressed the detonator.

陈默按下了引爆器。

The ground beneath the trucks exploded. The C4 charges buried in the ruins' foundation detonated simultaneously.

卡车下方的地面炸开了。埋在废墟地基里的C4炸药同时引爆。

BOOM!

轰！

A wall of fire and stone erupted. The shockwave knocked the guards off their feet. The roofless church became a cauldron of dust and shrapnel.

一堵火墙和石块喷发而出。冲击波把卫兵掀翻在地。无顶教堂变成了灰尘和弹片的熔炉。

In the chaos, Miyetsuko moved.

在一片混乱中，美悦子动了。

She didn't run away. She lunged at The Butcher.

她没有逃跑。她扑向了屠夫。

Before he could draw his gun, she ripped the ceramic knife from her thigh tape.

在他拔枪之前，她撕下大腿胶带上的陶瓷刀。

SHUCK.

噗嗤。

She drove the knife into his neck. Right into the jugular.

她把刀扎进他的脖子。正中颈静脉。

The Butcher gurgled, blood spraying onto her red dress, making it glisten. He fell back onto the stone altar.

屠夫咯咯作响，血喷在她红裙上，让裙子闪闪发光。他向后倒在石祭坛上。

"My cage is bigger," she hissed into his dying face.

“我的笼子更大，”她对着他垂死的脸嘶声说。

From the bell tower, a rhythmic crack-crack-crack began. Chen Mo was picking off the confused survivors one by one. Every shot was a precise period at the end of a sentence.

从钟楼上，有节奏的啪-啪-啪声开始了。陈默正在一个个点名那些困惑的幸存者。每一枪都是句子末尾精准的句号。

Miyetsuko stood over the body of The Butcher. She was bathed in blood and dust. The explosions continued around her, but she was the eye of the storm.

美悦子站在屠夫的尸体上。她沐浴在鲜血和灰尘中。爆炸声在她周围持续，但她是风暴眼。

She looked up at the bell tower. She couldn't see him, but she knew he was watching.

她抬头看钟楼。她看不见他，但她知道他在看。

She lifted her blood-soaked hem and wiped the knife clean on her thigh, deliberately exposing her leg to the scope.

她撩起浸透鲜血的裙摆，在大腿上擦干净刀，刻意向瞄准镜暴露着她的腿。

"Clear," Chen Mo's voice came through the earpiece. "Area secured."

“清除，”陈默的声音从耳机传来。“区域安全。”

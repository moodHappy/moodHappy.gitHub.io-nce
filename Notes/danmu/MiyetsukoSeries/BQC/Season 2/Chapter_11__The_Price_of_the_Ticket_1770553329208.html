Chapter 11: The Price of the Ticket

第十一章：门票的价格

Fantasma’s private dock. A 50-meter superyacht, The White Powder, was gleaming in the twilight.

幽灵的私人码头。一艘50米长的超级游艇“白粉号”在暮色中闪闪发光。

"The target is Chief of Police, Rodriguez," Fantasma handed a dossier to Chen Mo. "A pig. A greedy, perverted pig. He holds the encryption key to the port authority's server on a bio-locked tablet. I need his fingerprint. And I need his eye scan."

“目标是警察局长罗德里格斯，”幽灵递给陈默一份档案。“一头猪。一头贪婪、变态的猪。他手里掌握着港务局服务器的加密密钥，在那台生物锁定的平板电脑上。我需要他的指纹。还需要他的视网膜扫描。”

"So you want me to cut his hand and eye out?" Miyetsuko asked, sipping champagne.

“所以你想让我把他的手和眼睛挖出来？”美悦子抿着香槟问。

"Too messy. He needs to disappear after I transfer the cargo," Fantasma grinned. "He likes... 'exotic' women. Especially wives. He likes to... borrow them."

“太脏了。我需要在转移货物之后让他消失，”幽灵咧嘴笑。“他喜欢……‘异国情调’的女人。特别是人妻。他喜欢……借用她们。”

He looked at Miyetsuko’s body, currently wrapped in a silk robe.

他看着此刻裹在丝绸长袍里的美悦子。

"You are the gift. The bait. get him to his private cabin. Get him... distracted. Get the tablet unlocked."

“你是礼物。是诱饵。把他引到私人船舱。让他……分心。解锁平板。”

"And me?" Chen Mo asked coldly.

“那我呢？”陈默冷冷地问。

"You are the waiter. You pour the wine. You watch the door," Fantasma threw a waiter’s vest at Chen Mo. "And you listen. Just in case she needs... help."

“你是侍应生。你倒酒。你看门，”幽灵扔给陈默一件侍应生的马甲。“还有你负责监听。以防她需要……帮助。”

Chen Mo looked at Miyetsuko. He wanted to refuse. But Miyetsuko’s eyes were burning with a twisted excitement.

陈默看着美悦子。他想拒绝。但美悦子的眼里燃烧着扭曲的兴奋。

"Deal," she said, dropping the robe.

“成交，”她说着，让长袍滑落。

Underneath, she wore nothing but a dress made of sheer black mesh and strategically placed velvet ribbons. It was less of a dress and more of a suggestion.

在那下面，她只穿了一件由透明黑网和几条精心布置的天鹅绒丝带组成的裙子。这根本不算裙子，更像是一种暗示。

Chapter 42: The 800-Horsepower Coffin

第四十二章：八百马力的棺材

Rain and seawater slicked the dock. A black cigarette boat waited— sleek as a resting missile.

雨水与海水浸透码头。 一艘黑色快艇静伏—— 如停在水面的导弹。

"Get in!"

“上船！”

Keys already in the ignition. Mishima was always prepared.

钥匙早在点火孔中。 三岛从不失手。

"There!" A tactical beam slashed through darkness.

“在那儿！” 战术手电撕开黑夜。

Bang!

砰！

A spark burst from the railing beside her hand.

火花在她手边炸开。

"Jump!"

“跳！”

Twin V8 engines roared awake— thunder rolling across the water.

双V8怒吼苏醒—— 雷声震动海面。

She leaped aboard. The throttle slammed forward.

她跃上甲板。 油门被猛推到底。

The bow reared high. Propellers tore white scars through the bay.

船首扬起。 螺旋桨撕开白色浪痕。

Gunfire chased the wake— already too late.

子弹追逐尾迹—— 却终究落后。

They vanished into the vast darkness of Tokyo Bay.

他们消失在东京湾的无边黑暗中。

Chapter 36: The Bridge to Hell

第三十六章：通往地狱的桥

A modified Nissan GT-R roared onto the Shuto Expressway. The rain had stopped, but the asphalt still glistened.

一辆改装的日产GT-R咆哮着冲上首都高速。 雨停了，路面仍在发亮。

Chen Mo drove. Miyetsuko checked her magazine in the passenger seat. City lights streaked past like warp-speed stars.

陈默驾驶。 副驾的美悦子检查弹夹。 城市灯光如曲速星流般后退。

"Yokohama Bay Bridge ahead. No turning back after this."

“前面是横滨湾大桥。 过桥就无法回头。”

"Scared?"

“怕了？”

"No. Just calculating the odds."

“不。 只是计算胜率。”

"Odds are for gamblers. We're collecting a debt."

“胜率属于赌徒。 我们是去讨债。”

She placed her gloved hand over his on the gear stick— hot through the leather.

她戴着皮手套的手覆上他的手， 热度透过皮革传来。

"If we die tonight… at least we crash a billion-dollar empire."

“如果今晚死去…… 至少是为了撞碎一个十亿美元帝国。”

"We won't die." He downshifted. The engine roared.

“我们不会死。” 他降挡。 引擎咆哮。

"I haven't had enough of you yet."

“我还没要够你。”

Miyetsuko laughed— wild, real, alive.

美悦子笑了—— 狂野而真实。

"Then drive faster, my king. Don't keep Mishima waiting."

“那就更快点，我的王。 别让三岛久等。”

The GT-R tore through the night, heading straight toward the dark heart of Yokohama Port.

GT-R撕裂夜色， 直冲横滨港的黑暗心脏。

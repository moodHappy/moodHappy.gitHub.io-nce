Chapter 17: The 30-Second Counterattack

第十七章：三十秒的反击

The heavy metal door was closing, only a palm's width remaining. The red light of the alarm bathed Chen Mo's face in blood-like color.

厚重的金属门正在合拢，只剩下一掌宽的缝隙。警报的红光把陈默的脸映得像血一样红。

Most people would choose to run at this moment. But Chen Mo didn't move. He stood in front of the console, his fingers moving so fast they turned into shadows.

绝大多数人在这一刻会选择逃跑。但陈默没动。他站在控制台前，手指快得在键盘上敲出了残影。

"You think I'm just a gigolo, Miyetsuko?" He gritted his teeth, a crazy smile appearing on his sweaty face. "I'm a damn Senior Data Analyst."

“你以为我只是个小白脸吗，美悦子？”他咬着牙，满是冷汗的脸上浮现出一丝疯狂的笑意。“老子他妈的是高级数据分析师。”

She used the "Affair Photos" to trigger the system's defensive purge. The system would identify these files as "foreign viruses" and automatically initiate a disk formatting procedure to protect the core secrets—this was Kurosaki's "Scorched Earth Policy." She wanted to use Chen Mo's life to force the machine to burn the "Arms Deal List" along with him.

她利用“偷情照片”触发了系统的防御性清洗。系统会将这些文件识别为“外来病毒”，自动启动磁盘格式化程序以保护核心机密——这是黑崎家的“焦土政策”。她想用陈默的命，逼迫这台机器把那份“军火名单”连同他一起烧毁。

But she underestimated one thing: Data transfer is bidirectional.

但她低估了一件事：数据传输是双向的。

While the system was frantically "quarantining" the photo folder, the firewall's attention was fully diverted.

趁着系统在疯狂“隔离”那个照片文件夹的时候，防火墙的注意力被完全转移了。

Command: Mirror Copy. Target: Root Directory/Hidden Partition/Log_Black.

指令：镜像复制。目标：根目录/隐藏分区/Log_Black。

"Come on... Come on..." Chen Mo stared at the progress bar.

“快点……快点……”陈默死死盯着进度条。

98%... 99%... 100%.

98%……99%……100%。

Copy Complete.

复制完成。

He violently yanked out the USB drive and shoved it into his mouth, biting down hard to hold it.

他猛地拔下U盘，塞进嘴里死死咬住。

Then, he smashed the "Emergency Cooling" button on the wall with his fist.

然后，他一拳砸碎了墙上的“紧急冷却”按钮。

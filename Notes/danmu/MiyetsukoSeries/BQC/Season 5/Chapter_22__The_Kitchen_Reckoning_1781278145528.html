Chapter 22: The Kitchen Reckoning

发布时间：2026/6/12 23:29:02

第二十二章：厨房的清算

The kitchen was narrow, lit only by a flickering neon sign outside.

厨房狭窄，只有窗外闪烁的霓虹灯提供照明。

Miyetsuko stepped over the threshold, drenched in blood.

美悦子跨过门槛，满身血污。

She held the brass key tight, but her eyes weren't on the door.

她紧握着黄铜钥匙，但目光并不在门上。

Victor was standing by the stove, holding a sharpened kitchen knife.

维克托站在炉灶旁，手里拿着一把磨得锋利的菜刀。

His eyes were not afraid; they were shimmering with twisted excitement.

他的眼神中没有恐惧，只有扭曲的兴奋在闪烁。

"You look beautiful like that, Lin," he whispered, licking his lip.

“你这样真美，林。”他低语，舔了舔嘴唇。

"A goddess painted in red."

“一尊红色的女神。”

He didn't wait. He lunged, the knife arcing through the air.

他没等她反应，猛地扑来，刀锋划破空气。

Miyetsuko side-stepped, her movements fueled by pure, cold adrenaline.

美悦子侧身躲过，冷冽的肾上腺素支撑着她的动作。

The knife sliced her sleeve, baring her shoulder.

刀刃割开了她的袖子，露出了肩膀。

She didn't retreat; she slammed her palm into his throat.

她没有退缩，一掌猛击他的喉咙。

He choked, stumbling back into the counter, plates shattering.

他窒息着，撞向柜台，餐盘碎裂一地。

She followed instantly, grabbing his wrist and twisting it until the bone groaned.

她立刻跟上，攥住他的手腕猛转，直到骨头发出痛苦的哀鸣。

"This isn't a game, Victor," she hissed, her face inches from his.

“这不是游戏，维克托。”她在他耳边嘶吼，脸庞紧贴。

She kneed him hard in the stomach, his breath fleeing his lungs.

她重重膝顶他的腹部，将他肺里的空气挤个精光。

He collapsed, gasping for air, but his hand scrabbled for her hair again.

他瘫倒在地，大口喘气，手却依然在抓挠她的头发。

She grabbed his own knife from the floor, her expression stone-cold.

她从地上捡起他掉落的刀，表情坚若磐石。

The hunt was over.

狩猎结束了。

Threshold

Meaning: 门槛，门口。

Context: “Miyetsuko stepped over the threshold...”

Note: 标志着她从地窖的死亡地带进入了最终决战区。

Arcing

Meaning: 划出弧线。

Context: “...the knife arcing through the air.”

Note: 捕捉动作瞬间的动词，增加打斗的画面感。

Adrenaline

Meaning: 肾上腺素。

Context: “...fueled by pure, cold adrenaline.”

Note: 解释了美悦子为何在受重创后依然能保持高强度战斗力。

Stumbling

Meaning: 踉跄，跌跌撞撞。

Context: “...stumbling back into the counter...”

Note: 表现出维克托在遭到重击后的失控感。

Scrabbled

Meaning: 乱抓，抓挠。

Context: “...but his hand scrabbled for her hair again.”

Note: 体现出维克托即便濒死，依然保持着他那种疯狂、病态的占有欲。

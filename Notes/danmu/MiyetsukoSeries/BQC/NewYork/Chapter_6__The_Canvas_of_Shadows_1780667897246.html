Chapter 6: The Canvas of Shadows

发布时间：2026/6/5 21:58:09

第六章：阴影的画布

The gallery in Chelsea was stark white and suffocatingly quiet. Abstract sculptures cast long, twisted shadows under the minimalist spotlights. Miyetsuko stood before a massive canvas of chaotic black splatters, her crimson nails resting lightly against her dark coat.

切尔西区的这家画廊呈现出一种极简的纯白，安静得让人窒息。在极简主义的聚光灯下，抽象雕塑投射出长长而扭曲的阴影。美悦子站在一幅布满凌乱黑色泼墨的巨大画布前，深红色的指甲轻轻搭在深色大衣上。

Chen Mo stood a few steps behind her, holding a long black umbrella with a heavy silver handle. He wasn't looking at the art. Within thirty seconds, his eyes had already mapped the blind spots of the four thermal cameras and the patrol rhythm of the two armed guards near the back door.

陈默站在她身后几步远的地方，手里拿着一把带有沉重银色握柄的长黑伞。他没有在看艺术品。在三十秒内，他的双眼已经记录下了四个热成像摄像头的盲区，以及后门附近两名武装安保的巡视节奏。

A gallery manager in a tailored suit approached them, his smile polite but carrying the subtle arrogance native to Manhattan. "Exquisite taste, madam. That piece explores the void of modern existence. Are you interested in acquiring it?"

一名穿着定制西装的画廊经理走了过来，笑容礼貌却带着曼哈顿特有的那种微妙傲慢。“夫人品味真好。这件作品探讨了现代生存的虚无。您有兴趣收藏吗？”

Miyetsuko didn't even glance at him. "I'm more interested in how you move it," she said softly, her eyes tracking a freight elevator slowly descending in the restricted area behind the glass. "A piece this large... ordinary shipping wouldn't guarantee its safety, would it?"

美悦子连看都没看他一眼。“我更感兴趣的是你们怎么运输它，”她轻声说道，目光穿透玻璃，追踪着限制区内正在缓缓下降的货运电梯。“这么大的作品……普通的托运可无法保证它的安全吧？”

The manager’s smile stiffened for a fraction of a second. "We use an exclusive logistics partner for our high-net-worth clients. Absolute discretion is guaranteed." Across the room, Chen Mo caught a glimpse of a wooden crate being loaded into the elevator. Stamped into the pale wood was a single, elegant letter: V.

经理的笑容在瞬间微微一僵。“我们为高净值客户提供专属的物流合作伙伴。保证绝对的保密。”在大厅另一侧，陈默瞥见一个木箱正被装进电梯。在苍白的木板上，深深地烙印着一个优雅的孤零零的字母：V。

"Discretion," Miyetsuko repeated the word, a faint, cold smile finally appearing on her lips. She turned and walked toward the exit, her heels clicking rhythmically on the polished concrete. "Let's go, Mo. The art here is entirely fake."

“保密，”美悦子重复着这个词，一抹微弱而冰冷的微笑终于浮现在唇边。她转身走向出口，高跟鞋在抛光的水泥地上踩出有节奏的声响。“走吧，默。这里的艺术品全都是赝品。”

📝 Vocabulary Highlight

Suffocatingly

Meaning: 令人窒息地。

Context: “...stark white and suffocatingly quiet.”

Note: 形容画廊里那种为了强调高级感而刻意营造的极端安静，同时也暗示了这里隐藏的危险气息让人感到压抑。

Thermal cameras

Meaning: 热成像摄像头。

Context: “...the blind spots of the four thermal cameras...”

Note: 细节描写。一家普通的画廊不需要用到热成像级别的安保，这个词间接向读者和陈默暴露了这家画廊背后见不得光的秘密。

Acquiring

Meaning: 获得，购得（在艺术界常指“收藏”）。

Context: “Are you interested in acquiring it?”

Note: 画廊经理常用的装腔作势的高级词汇，比单纯的 "buying" 听起来更符合曼哈顿上流社会的语境。

Stiffened

Meaning: 变得僵硬。

Context: “The manager’s smile stiffened for a fraction of a second.”

Note: 细微的肢体语言描写，暴露出经理被美悦子一针见血的提问戳到了痛处。

Discretion

Meaning: 谨慎，保密。

Context: “Absolute discretion is guaranteed.”

Note: 犯罪和黑色电影里的高频词。表面上说的是对客户隐私的保护，实际上往往掩盖着非法的走私或洗钱活动。

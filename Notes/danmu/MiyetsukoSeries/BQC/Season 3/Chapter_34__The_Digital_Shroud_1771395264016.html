Chapter 34: The Digital Shroud

第三十四章：数字裹尸布

Chen Mo placed the briefcase on the table and opened it, a holographic display flickering to life.

陈默将公文包放在桌上打开，一个全息显示屏闪烁着开启。

It wasn't a map of territory; it was a map of Khun’s entire life, laid bare in cold, blue light.

那不是领土图，那是坤老整个人生的地图，在冰冷的蓝光下暴露无遗。

"Your accounts in Switzerland, your villas in Nice, the encrypted messages to the Thai General," Chen Mo said.

“你在瑞士的账户，你在尼斯的别墅，还有你发给泰国将军的加密信息，”陈默说道。

"Everything you think is private now belongs to the Shadow Network."

“你认为隐秘的一切，现在都属于暗影网络。”

Khun leaned forward, his ancient hands trembling as he saw his secret wealth flickering on the screen.

坤老凑上前，看着自己隐秘的财富在屏幕上闪烁，他那双苍老的手在颤抖。

"I have spent fifty years building this empire," Khun hissed, his eyes reflecting the data.

“我花了五十年的时间建立这个帝国，”坤老嘶声说道，双眼倒映着数据。

"You can't just delete a man's history with a machine."

“你不能只靠一台机器就删掉一个人的历史。”

Miyetsuko leaned back, her gaze cutting through the old man's pride like a diamond through glass.

美悦子向后靠去，她的目光穿透了老人的骄傲，如钻石划破玻璃。

"I’m not deleting it, Khun. I’m inheriting it," she whispered, the power in her voice undeniable.

“我不是在删掉它，坤老。我是在继承它，”她低声呢喃，声音中的权力感不容置疑。

"Your soldiers were paid this morning from a ghost account in Myanmar. They aren't yours anymore."

“你的士兵今天早上已经收到了从缅甸幽灵账户发出的薪水。他们不再属于你了。”

"They are waiting for my signal to either protect this garden or burn it to the ground."

“他们在等待我的信号，要么保护这个花园，要么把它夷为平地。”

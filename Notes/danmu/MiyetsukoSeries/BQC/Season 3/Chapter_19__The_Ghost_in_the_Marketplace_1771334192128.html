Chapter 19: The Ghost in the Marketplace

第十九章：市集中的幽灵

Mae Sai was no longer a town of chaotic noise; it was a town of held breaths.

夜赛镇不再是喧闹的混乱之地，而是一个屏息待变的小镇。

The sudden silence of the General’s compound and the blood-stained office of the Captain had sent shockwaves through the Mekong’s veins.

将军营地的突然沉寂和上尉满是鲜血的办公室，在湄公河的血脉中激起了阵阵冲击波。

Rumors traveled faster than the river silt, whispering of a "Black Widow" who traded her body for the souls of men.

流言传播得比河床的泥沙还快，低语着一个用身体交易男人灵魂的“黑寡妇”。

Miyetsuko sat on the veranda of the villa, a map of the region’s poppy fields spread before her like a skin.

美悦子坐在别墅的走廊上，一张该地区的罂粟田地图像皮肤一样铺在她面前。

She wore a cheongsam of pale gold, the silk catching the fading light of a bruised sky.

她穿着一件淡金色的旗袍，丝绸捕捉着如淤青般天色残存的余晖。

"The balance is broken, Chen," she said, her fingers tracing the mountain ridges.

“平衡被打破了，陈，”她说道，手指勾勒着山脉的轮廓。

"The smaller wolves are coming out of the forest to see who is the new Alpha."

“小狼们正从森林里钻出来，想看看谁是新的领头狼。”

Chen Mo stood in the doorway, his silhouette a sharp intrusion against the tropical dusk.

陈默站在门口，他的剪影在热带的黄昏中显得格外锐利。

He had spent the morning monitoring the dark web and the local radio frequencies, listening to the panic.

他花了一个上午的时间监控暗网和当地的无线电频率，倾听着那里的恐慌。

"Vang’s lieutenants are fighting over the armories, and Sawang’s men are looking for someone to pay their salaries," Chen Mo reported.

“旺将军的副手们正在争夺军火库，萨旺的人则在找人给他们发薪水，”陈默报告道。

"If we don't act, the town will burn before we can rule it."

“如果我们不采取行动，这座小镇会在我们统治之前就被付之一炬。”

Miyetsuko looked up, her eyes cold and calculating, the fire of revenge tempered by the ice of strategy.

美悦子抬起头，眼神冷酷而充满算计，复仇的火焰已被策略的冰冷所淬炼。

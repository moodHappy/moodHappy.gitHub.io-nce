Chapter 33: The Scent of Old Wood

第三十三章：旧木的气息

Chiang Rai. Elder Khun’s Compound. Twilight.

清莱。坤老的私宅。黄昏。

The garden was quiet, but it was the silence of a tomb, not a sanctuary.

花园里很安静，但那是坟墓般的死寂，而非避风港。

Elder Khun sat in his usual chair, but his pipe had gone cold, the embers dead.

坤老坐在他惯常坐的椅子上，但他的烟斗已经凉了，余烬已灭。

He looked at the gate as the black SUV pulled in, silent as a shark in dark water.

他盯着大门，那辆黑色SUV驶了进来，像深水中的鲨鱼一样无声无息。

Miyetsuko stepped out, wearing a sharp, tailored suit of charcoal grey—no silk, no skin.

美悦子走了出来，穿着一套剪裁锐利的深灰色西装——没有丝绸，没有裸露。

She looked like a CEO of a dying nation, her eyes two pieces of unyielding flint.

她看起来像是一个垂死国家的CEO，双眼犹如两块坚硬的燧石。

Chen Mo followed, carrying only a slim titanium briefcase, his presence a cold shadow.

陈默跟在后面，只提着一个纤薄的钛合金公文包，他的存在如同一抹冷酷的阴影。

"You’ve returned from the abyss, Miyetsuko," Khun said, his voice a dry rattle.

“你从深渊回来了，美悦子，”坤老说道，声音沙哑干涩。

"I expected you to come with an army. Or at least a gun in your hand."

“我以为你会带着一支军队来。或者至少手里拿着枪。”

Miyetsuko walked to the stone table and sat down, uninvited, her movements precise and heavy.

美悦子走到石桌前，不请自坐，她的动作精准而沉重。

"Guns are for people who still believe they have a choice, Khun," she said calmly.

“枪是给那些还觉得自己有选择余地的人用的，坤老，”她平静地说道。

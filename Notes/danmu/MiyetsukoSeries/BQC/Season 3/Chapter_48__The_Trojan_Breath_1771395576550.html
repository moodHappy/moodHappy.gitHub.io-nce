Chapter 48: The Trojan Breath

第四十八章：特洛伊的呼吸

Sato entered the grand hall, bowing low to Miyetsuko as if she were already on the throne of Kurosaki.

佐藤步入大厅，向美悦子深深鞠躬，仿佛她已然坐在黑崎的王座上。

"Mishima-san offers an olive branch," Sato said, placing a black lacquered box on the table.

“三岛先生递来了橄榄枝，”佐藤说道，将一个黑色漆器盒子放在桌上。

"He acknowledges your sovereignty over the river. He wants a partnership, not a war."

“他承认你对大河的主权。他想要的是合作，而非战争。”

Miyetsuko leaned forward, her scent filling the space between them, a distraction of jasmine and danger.

美悦子倾身向前，她的香气充盈在两人之间，那是茉莉与危险交织的诱惑。

Suddenly, Chen Mo’s earpiece let out a high-pitched whine—the radiation and chemical sensor in the walls had tripped.

突然，陈默的耳机里传出一声尖锐的鸣响——墙壁内的辐射与化学传感器被触发了。

"Miyetsuko, get back!" Chen Mo shouted, his hand already moving toward the emergency vent controls.

“美悦子，后退！”陈默大喊道，手已经伸向了应急通风控制开关。

"The box is a lead-lined housing for a micro-aerosol dispenser. It’s not a gift; it’s a death sentence."

“那个盒子是微型气溶胶喷雾器的铅衬外壳。这不是礼物，而是死刑判决书。”

The diplomat’s eyes widened, not with fear, but with the cold zeal of a suicide bomber.

外交官瞪大了眼睛，眼神中没有恐惧，只有自杀式袭击者那冷酷的狂热。

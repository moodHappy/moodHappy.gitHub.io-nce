Chapter 47: The White Flag

第四十七章：白旗

The silver helicopter descended into the jungle clearing like a dragon fly made of chrome.

银色的直升机降落在丛林空地，宛如一只用铬合金制成的蜻蜓。

It bore the seal of the Mishima Group, but it flew a white flag from its landing gear.

它带着三岛集团的印章，但起落架上却飘着一面白旗。

Miyetsuko stood on the balcony of the fortress, her charcoal suit replaced by a gown of white silk—the color of mourning and purity.

美悦子站在堡垒的阳台上，脱下了深灰色西装，换上了一袭白丝长裙——那是哀悼与纯洁的颜色。

"He sends an envoy to a Queen he tried to kill yesterday," she said, her voice like ice sliding over velvet.

“他向一个昨天还想杀掉的女王派来了使者，”她说道，声音如同滑过天鹅绒的冰块。

Chen Mo stood behind her, his thermal goggles scanning the diplomat’s vitals from a hundred yards away.

陈默站在她身后，热成像护目镜正在百码开外扫描着外交官的生命体征。

"His name is Kenjiro Sato. A senior negotiator, a man who prides himself on peace," Chen Mo reported.

“他叫佐藤健次郎。资深谈判专家，一个以和平为傲的人，”陈默报告道。

"But his pulse is too steady. He’s either the bravest man in Tokyo, or he knows something we don't."

“但他的脉搏太稳了。他要么是东京最勇敢的人，要么他知道一些我们不知道的事。”

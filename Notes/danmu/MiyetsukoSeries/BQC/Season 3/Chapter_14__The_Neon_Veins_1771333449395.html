Chapter 14: The Neon Veins

第十四章：霓虹脉络

The border town of Mae Sai was a jagged scar of neon and noise on the edge of the wilderness.

位于边界的夜赛镇是荒野边缘一道由霓虹与噪音组成的锯齿状伤痕。

It was a place where human desires were harvested like crops, raw and unapologetic.

这是一个人类欲望被如庄稼般收割的地方，原始而毫无廉耻。

Miyetsuko stood on a balcony overlooking the "Red District," where the scent of cheap perfume fought the heavy aroma of street food.

美悦子站在阳台上俯瞰着“红灯区”，廉价香水的味道正与街头小吃浓烈的香气搏斗。

She wore a cheongsam of blood-red silk, the fabric a liquid flame against the humid night.

她穿着一件血红色的丝绸旗袍，布料在潮湿的黑夜中如同一团流动的火焰。

"Rio was a playground, Chen," she said, her eyes reflecting the flickering signs of brothels and dens.

“里约只是个游乐场，陈，”她说道，双眼倒映着妓院与烟馆闪烁的招牌。

"But this place... it is a gold mine of filth waiting for a Queen to refine it."

“但这里……是一座等待着女王去提炼的污秽金矿。”

Chen Mo leaned against the doorframe, his shadow long and sharp as a blade.

陈默倚在门框上，他的影子如刀锋般修长而锐利。

"The local syndicates are disorganized, fighting over scraps of the poppy and the flesh trade."

“当地的辛迪加组织涣散，正为了罂粟与肉体生意的碎屑而互相残杀。”

"They need a central nervous system. They need an iron hand in a velvet glove."

“他们需要一个中枢神经系统。他们需要丝绒手套里的铁腕。”

Miyetsuko turned, the high slit of her dress revealing the golden derringer, its metal shimmering like a predator's eye.

美悦子转身，裙子的高开叉露出了那把金色德林加，它的金属光泽如捕食者的眼睛般闪烁。

"We start with the three crowns: the red light, the golden chips, and the white powder."

“我们从三重王冠开始：红灯区的色欲、金色的筹码，以及白色的粉末。”

"We will turn their chaotic lust into our structured empire."

“我们要将他们混乱的欲望转化为我们结构严整的帝国。”

She walked to the center of the room, where a map of the Mekong trade routes was spread across a table.

她走到房间中央，桌上铺着一张湄公河贸易路线图。

She placed a heavy bag of seized gold coins onto the map, the sound of wealth hitting the table like a hammer.

她将一袋缴获的沉甸甸的金币掷在地图上，财富撞击桌面的声音犹如重锤落下。

"Buy the police, blackmail the monks, and execution the rivals," she commanded.

“买通警察，勒索僧侣，处决对手，”她命令道。

"I want the river to bleed our color by the end of the month."

“我要在月底前，让整条大河都流淌着属于我们的颜色。”

Chen Mo stepped into the light, his gaze locking onto hers with a terrifying, silent devotion.

陈默步入光影之中，他的目光带着一种令人恐惧且无声的奉献感与她交锁。

"The foundations are already crumbling, my Queen. I will ensure they fall exactly where you want them."

“地基已经开始崩塌了，我的女王。我会确保它们精准地倒在你想让它们倒下的地方。”

Miyetsuko reached out, her fingers tracing the line of his jaw, the power between them thick as the tropical air.

美悦子伸出手，手指勾勒着他的下颚线，两人之间的权力感如热带空气般浓稠。

"Then let the expansion begin," she whispered, her smile a beautiful, dark omen.

“那么，让扩张开始吧，”她低声呢喃，笑意如同一抹华丽而黑暗的预兆。

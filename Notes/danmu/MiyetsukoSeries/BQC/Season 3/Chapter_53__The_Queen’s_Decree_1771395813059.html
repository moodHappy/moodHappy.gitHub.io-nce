Chapter 53: The Queen’s Decree

第五十三章：女王的法令

Miyetsuko grabbed a heavy steel lever, her muscles straining beneath her soaked silk. She looked at the drowning slaves in the nearby cell blocks—thousands of lives she had claimed to own.

美悦子抓起一根沉重的钢杆，肌肉在湿透的丝绸下紧绷。她看着附近牢房里溺水的奴隶——那些她声称拥有的数千条生命。

She realized that if they died here, she was no better than Kurosaki. She would be a Queen of corpses.

她意识到，如果他们死在这里，她并不比黑崎高尚。她将成为尸体之王。

"Open the floodgates to the secondary spillway!" she commanded, her voice cutting through the roar of the deluge. "Divert the pressure! Save the people, then the machines!"

“打开通往二次溢洪道的闸门！”她命令道，声音穿透了洪流的咆哮。“分流压力！先救人，再救机器！”

"It'll blow the power grid!" Chen Mo warned. "We'll be blind and deaf for days!"

“这会烧毁电网！”陈默警告道。“我们会变成好几天的瞎子和聋子！”

"DO IT!" Miyetsuko roared, her eyes burning with a ferocious, primal light.

“动手！”美悦子吼道，双眼燃烧着凶猛而原始的光芒。

Chen Mo slammed the manual override. With a sound like a dying titan, the pressure shifted. The water slowed its rise in the server room, roaring instead into the empty tunnels below.

陈默猛地按下手动覆盖开关。伴随着一声如泰坦陨落般的巨响，压力偏移了。机房的水位停止上涨，转而咆哮着冲入下方的空隧道。

Miyetsuko collapsed against the wet stone, her chest heaving, her large breasts straining against the ruined fabric of her dress.

美悦子瘫倒在湿冷的石头上，胸口剧烈起伏，巨大的乳房在破烂的裙装下紧绷。

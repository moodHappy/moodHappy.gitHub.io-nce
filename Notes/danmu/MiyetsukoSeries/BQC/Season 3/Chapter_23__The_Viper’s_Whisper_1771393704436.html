Chapter 23: The Viper’s Whisper

第二十三章：毒蛇的低语

Chiang Rai. Elder Khun’s Private Compound.

清莱。坤老的私宅。

Elder Khun sat in the shadows of his garden, the smoke from his pipe coiling like a dying snake.

坤老坐在花园的阴影里，烟斗里冒出的烟像一条垂死的蛇一样盘旋。

He looked at the digital transfer Miyetsuko had sent him—his 30% cut—but his eyes held no gratitude.

他看着美悦子发给他的数额——他那30%的抽成——但他的眼中没有感激。

"She is a storm that will drown us all," he muttered to the man standing in the shadows beside him.

“她是一场会把我们淹没的暴风雨，”他对着站在身边阴影里的男人低语。

The man was General Prayut of the Royal Thai Army, his uniform crisp and devoid of any emotion.

那人是泰国皇家陆军的巴育将军，制服笔挺，面无表情。

"She has decapitated the river's leadership," Prayut observed, his voice a dry rasp.

“她砍掉了这条大河的首脑，”巴育观察道，声音沙哑。

"And now she is bringing the filth of the flesh trade into the light. The government cannot ignore this."

“现在她又把人口贩运的污秽带到了明处。政府不能对此坐视不管。”

Khun leaned forward, his face a landscape of ancient, bitter wrinkles.

坤老探过身，脸上布满了古老而苦涩的皱纹。

"They are in Phnom Penh for the 'Golden Silk' summit," Khun whispered.

“他们正在金边参加‘金丝’峰会，”坤老低声说。

"When they return across the border into my territory, I will lead them into your 'extermination' zone. I want the girl silenced, and the boy’s brain picked clean."

“当他们跨越边界回到我的领地时，我会引导他们进入你的‘除妖’区域。我要让那个女孩闭嘴，把那个男孩的大脑挖空。”

The General nodded slowly, the trap beginning to close in the dark.

将军缓缓点头，陷阱开始在黑暗中闭合。

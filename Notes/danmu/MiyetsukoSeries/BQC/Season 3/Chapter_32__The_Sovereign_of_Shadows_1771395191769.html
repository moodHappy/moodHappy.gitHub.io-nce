Chapter 32: The Sovereign of Shadows

第三十二章：暗影元首

By dawn, the jungle was silent once again, the mud decorated with the brass of spent shells and the bodies of the greedy.

拂晓时分，丛林再次陷入寂静，泥地里点缀着废弃的黄铜弹壳和贪婪者的尸体。

The surviving warlords had retreated into the emerald shadows, broken by the ferocity of the "White Widow."

幸存的军阀们已退缩进翠绿的阴影中，被“白寡妇”的凶残所击溃。

Miyetsuko walked through the compound, her tactical suit torn, her skin smudged with soot and blood.

美悦子走过园区，战术服已经破损，皮肤上沾满了烟灰和鲜血。

She found Chen Mo in the server room, his face pale in the flickering blue light.

她在机房找到了陈默，他的脸在闪烁的蓝光下显得苍白。

He stood up and walked to her, his hands checking her for wounds with a desperate, silent intensity.

他站起身走向她，双手带着一种绝望而无声的强度检查着她身上的伤口。

"You are whole," he whispered, his voice a ragged thread of relief.

“你完好无损，”他低声呢喃，声音里透着一丝破碎的慰藉。

Miyetsuko leaned against him, the cold metal of the server racks behind her.

美悦子倚靠在他身上，身后是机柜冰冷的金属。

"We don't just own the river anymore, Chen," she said, looking at the screens that now displayed Mishima’s collapsing stock prices.

“陈，我们拥有的不再仅仅是那条河了，”她看着现在显示着三岛暴跌股价的屏幕说道。

"We own the data. We own the shadows."

“我们拥有了数据。我们拥有了暗影。”

She pulled him close, her lips brushing his ear, the power between them thick as the morning mist.

她将他拉近，嘴唇掠过他的耳廓，两人之间的权力感如晨雾般浓稠。

"Mishima will see us now," she whispered.

“三岛现在会看到我们了，”她低声说道。

"Not as fugitives, but as the ghost that will haunt his every transaction."

“不再是以逃犯的身份，而是以那个会纠缠他每一笔交易的幽灵身份。”

Chen Mo looked into her eyes, the loyalty in his gaze a terrifying, eternal flame.

陈默注视着她的双眼，他目光中的忠诚是一团令人恐惧的永恒火焰。

"Then let the world burn in our shadow, my Queen."

“那么，我的女王，就让世界在我们的阴影中燃烧吧。”

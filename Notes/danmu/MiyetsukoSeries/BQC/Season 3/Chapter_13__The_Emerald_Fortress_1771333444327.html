Chapter 13: The Emerald Fortress

第十三章：翡翠堡垒

The safehouse was an old French colonial villa, half-swallowed by the relentless emerald tide of the jungle.

安全屋是一座古老的法属殖民时期别墅，已被丛林那无情的翡翠浪潮吞噬了一半。

Moss grew like velvet over the scarred stone walls, a green shroud for a decaying empire.

苔藓像天鹅绒般在满是伤痕的石墙上蔓延，为腐朽的帝国披上了一层绿色的裹尸布。

Miyetsuko stood on the balcony, watching the orange sun drown in the mud of the Mekong.

美悦子站在阳台上，看着橘色的夕阳溺亡在湄公河的泥浆中。

She wore a thin white silk robe, a ghostly contrast to the dark, primitive heat of the mountains.

她穿着一件薄薄的白色丝绸睡袍，与大山那黑暗、原始的热浪形成了幽灵般的对比。

"Vang gave us the keys to the river," she whispered, the humidity curling her hair.

“旺将军给了我们开启河流的钥匙，”她低声呢喃，湿气卷曲了她的发丝。

"But a key is only useful if you know which throat to cut with it."

“但只有知道该用钥匙割开谁的喉咙时，它才有用。”

Chen Mo was in the shadows behind her, his fingers dancing across a tactical tablet.

陈默在她身后的阴影中，手指在战术平板上跳动。

The blue light of the screen carved sharp lines into his face, cold and calculating.

屏幕的蓝光在他脸上刻出锐利的线条，冷酷且充满算计。

"Mishima is quiet for now, like a spider waiting for the vibration of the web," he said.

“三岛目前很安静，像一只等待蛛网震动的蜘蛛，”他说道。

"He thinks we are rotting in the mud of Southeast Asia, buried by his influence."

“他以为我们正烂在东南亚的泥潭里，被他的势力所掩埋。”

Miyetsuko turned, the silk falling open to reveal the golden derringer strapped to her inner thigh.

美悦子转身，丝绸滑开，露出了大腿内侧绑着的金色德林加。

"Let him enjoy his silence," she smiled, a slow, venomous curve of her lips.

“让他享受这份寂静吧，”她笑了，唇角勾起一抹缓慢且恶毒的弧度。

"While we turn this emerald purgatory into our own private throne room."

“我们要趁机把这片翡翠炼狱变成我们自己的私人王座大厅。”

She walked to Chen Mo, placing her palm over the glowing screen, silencing the data.

她走到陈默身边，把手掌按在发光的屏幕上，切断了数据。

The power dynamic between them shifted, a heavy, silent hunger flaring in the humid air.

他们之间的权力动态发生了偏移，沉重而无声的饥渴在湿热的空气中燃起。

"Forget the ghost of Tokyo tonight, Chen," she commanded, her voice a low vibration.

“今晚忘掉东京的幽灵吧，陈，”她命令道，声音低沉而颤动。

"Tonight, help me plant the seeds of our new empire in this dark soil."

“今晚，帮我在这片黑暗的土地上播下我们新帝国的种子。”

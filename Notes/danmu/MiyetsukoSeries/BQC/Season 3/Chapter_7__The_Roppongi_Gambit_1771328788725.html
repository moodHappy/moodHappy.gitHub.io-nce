Chapter 7: The Roppongi Gambit

第七章：六本木的博弈

The abandoned warehouse smelled of rust, grease, and the lingering ghost of industrial power.

废弃的仓库里弥漫着铁锈、油垢和工业权力残存的幽灵味。

Mishima’s elite extraction team moved through the shadows like ink in water, their movements silent and professional.

三岛的精英提取小组在阴影中移动，如水中的墨汁一般，动作无声而专业。

"Room is secure. Server located," the leader whispered into his comms.

“房间安全。服务器已定位，”领队对着通讯器低声报告。

At the Conrad Hotel, Mishima watched the live helmet-cam feed, his fingers tapping the arm of his leather chair.

在康莱德酒店，三岛注视着头盔摄像机的实时画面，手指敲击着皮椅的扶手。

"Don't touch the physical drive," Mishima commanded. "Bridge the connection. Let my hackers do the rest."

“不要碰物理驱动器，”三岛命令道。“建立桥接连接。剩下的交给我的黑客。”

He was too smart to fall for a physical ambush; he fought his wars in the digital ether.

他太聪明了，不会落入物理埋伏；他在数字以太中进行他的战争。

In the Aman suite, Chen Mo watched the connection light up on his monitor.

在安缦套房，陈默注视着监控器上亮起的连接信号。

"He’s in," Chen Mo said, a ghost of a smile appearing on his lips. "He’s cautious. He’s using a triple-proxy tunnel."

“他进来了，”陈默说，唇边浮现出一抹若有若无的笑。“他很谨慎，使用了三重代理隧道。”

"Let him feel the power, Chen," Miyetsuko murmured. She was sitting on the floor, cleaning the Golden Derringer with a silk cloth.

“让他感受到那股力量，陈，”美悦子呢喃道。她坐在地板上，用丝绸布擦拭着那把金色德林加。

"Give him access to the first level of the satellite grid. Let him think he can see the whole world."

“给他第一层卫星网的访问权限。让他以为他能俯瞰全世界。”

As the progress bar reached 100%, Mishima’s screen displayed a high-resolution satellite image of Tokyo.

当进度条达到100%时，三岛的屏幕上显示出一张东京的高分辨率卫星图像。

He zoomed in on the Aman Hotel. He could see the very suite where Miyetsuko was hiding.

他将镜头放大，锁定了安缦酒店。他甚至能看到美悦子藏身的那间套房。

"I see you, my Queen," Mishima whispered to the screen, his eyes burning with greed and triumph.

“我看到你了，我的女王，”三岛对着屏幕低语，眼中燃烧着贪婪与胜利的光芒。

He didn't notice the tiny, sub-zero packet of data traveling back up the connection, embedding itself into his personal terminal.

他没有注意到，一个微小的、低于零度的数码包正沿着连接反向传输，嵌入了他的私人终端。

"He’s looking at us right now," Chen Mo observed, watching Mishima’s terminal activity.

“他现在正盯着我们看，”陈默观察着三岛的终端活动。

Miyetsuko stood up, the black silk of her robe falling perfectly around her curves.

美悦子站起身，黑色丝绸睡袍完美地垂落在她的曲线周围。

She walked to the window and looked up at the sky, knowing a satellite was watching her from the cold vacuum of space.

她走向窗户，仰望天空，深知一颗卫星正从冰冷的太空真空中俯视着她。

She didn't hide. She blew a kiss to the empty air, her eyes cold and inviting.

她没有躲避。她对着虚空飞吻，眼神冰冷而诱人。

"Do you like what you see, Mishima-san?" she whispered to the glass.

“你喜欢你所看到的吗，三岛先生？”她对着玻璃低声说道。

"Because this is the last time you will see me as a target. Next time, I am the hunter."

“因为这是你最后一次把我当作目标。下一次，我就是猎人。”

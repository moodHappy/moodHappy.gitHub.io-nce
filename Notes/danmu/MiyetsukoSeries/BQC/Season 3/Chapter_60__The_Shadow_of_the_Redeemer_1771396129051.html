Chapter 60: The Shadow of the Redeemer

第六十章：救世主的阴影

Rio de Janeiro. 02:00 AM.

里约热内卢。凌晨 02:00。

The plane landed on a private airstrip hidden in the salt marshes outside the city.

飞机降落在城市外盐碱滩中的一条私人跑道上。

The humid air of Brazil was different from the Mekong—it tasted of salt, old money, and impending violence.

巴西潮湿的空气与湄公河不同——它尝起来有盐、旧钱和即将发生的暴力的味道。

Miyetsuko stepped out, the Golden Derringer a warm weight against her thigh, hidden by the dark silk of her dress.

美悦子走下飞机，金色德林加手枪在大腿上散发着温暖的重量，被她深色的丝绸裙摆掩盖。

"Welcome home, Queen," a voice boomed from the darkness.

“欢迎回家，女王，”一个声音从黑暗中轰隆而至。

Ricardo, the former lieutenant of the Butcher and now the regent of Miyetsuko’s Rio empire, stepped into the light.

理查多，屠夫的前副官，现在是美悦子里约帝国的摄政王，走进了光亮处。

He looked at her tattered grace, her skin smudged with the echoes of the jungle, and he bowed his head in genuine fear.

他看着她那破碎的优雅，看着她那沾染了丛林回响的皮肤，在真正的恐惧中低下了头。

"I heard the river had claimed you," Ricardo whispered, his hand trembling as he reached for her luggage.

“我听说大河把你带走了，”理查多低声说道，伸手去拿她的行李时，手在颤抖。

"The river tried," Miyetsuko said, her smile a beautiful, dark omen. "But I decided I wasn't finished with the world yet."

“大河试过了，”美悦子笑了，笑容如同一抹华丽而黑暗的预兆。“但我决定还没和这个世界玩够。”

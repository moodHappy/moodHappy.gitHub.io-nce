Chapter 49: The Vault of the Dead

第四十九章：死者之窖

Before Sato could trigger the device, Chen Mo slammed a button on his console.

在佐藤触发装置之前，陈默猛地按下了控制台上的一个按钮。

Thick, transparent blast shields slammed down from the ceiling, sealing Sato in a vacuum-tight glass box in the center of the hall.

沉重且透明的防爆盾从天而降，将佐藤封死在大厅中央一个真空密封的玻璃箱内。

Miyetsuko stood just inches from the glass, watching the diplomat as he fumbled with the lacquer box.

美悦子站在离玻璃仅几英寸的地方，看着外交官笨拙地摆弄着那个漆盒。

A faint, yellowish gas began to hiss from the box, filling the small enclosure.

一股淡淡的黄色气体开始从盒子里嘶嘶冒出，充满了那个狭小的空间。

Sato clawed at the glass, his face contorting as the neurotoxin began to dissolve his nervous system from the inside out.

佐藤抓挠着玻璃，随着神经毒素开始从内部瓦解他的神经系统，他的脸变得扭曲狰狞。

"Mishima sent you to die for a truce that never existed," Miyetsuko whispered, her voice amplified through the internal speakers.

“三岛派你来，是为一个从未存在过的停战协定去死，”美悦子低声说道，声音通过内部扬声器被放大。

"Tell the devil that the Queen doesn't breathe the air of traitors."

“去告诉撒旦，女王从不呼吸叛徒的空气。”

The diplomat collapsed into a heap of twitching limbs, his eyes turning to milk as the poison finished its work.

外交官瘫倒成一堆抽搐的肢体，随着毒素完成使命，他的双眼变得如牛奶般惨白。

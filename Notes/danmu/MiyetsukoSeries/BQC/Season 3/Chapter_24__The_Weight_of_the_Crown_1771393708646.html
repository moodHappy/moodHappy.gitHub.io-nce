Chapter 24: The Weight of the Crown

第二十四章：王冠之重

Phnom Penh. Midnight.

金边。午夜。

Miyetsuko felt a sudden chill, a vibration in the air that had nothing to do with the humidity.

美悦子感到一阵突如其来的寒意，那是空气中的一种震颤，与湿度无关。

She turned as Chen Mo walked onto the balcony, his expression unreadable.

陈默走到阳台上时，她转过身，他的表情难以捉摸。

He pulled her into his arms, his hands sliding over the silver silk, claiming her in the quiet of the night.

他将她拉入怀中，双手滑过银色丝绸，在寂静的夜色中占有着她。

"The river feels different tonight, Chen," she murmured, leaning her head against his shoulder.

“今晚的河水感觉不一样，陈，”她喃喃道，把头靠在他的肩上。

"Like something is watching us from the mud."

“就像泥潭里有什么东西在盯着我们。”

Chen Mo tightened his grip, his presence a dark, protective fortress around her.

陈默收紧了双臂，他的存在如同她周围一座黑暗而坚固的防护堡垒。

"Khun is too quiet," Chen Mo whispered into her hair.

“坤老太安静了，”陈默在她发间低语。

"A man like that doesn't accept a 30% tax; he accepts an opportunity to kill the tax collector."

“像那样的男人不会接受30%的税；他只会接受一个杀掉征税人的机会。”

Miyetsuko looked up, her lips inches from his, the power and the danger swirling between them.

美悦子抬起头，嘴唇离他只有几英寸，权力和危险在两人之间盘旋。

"Then we let him try," she said, her smile a beautiful, lethal curve.

“那就让他试试吧，”她说道，笑容带着华丽而致命的弧度。

"I’ve survived the white kimono, the jungle, and the General. If Khun wants to play with the army, I’ll show him how a Queen handles a coup."

“我从白无垢、丛林和将军的手中活了下来。如果坤老想和军队玩玩，我就让他看看女王是如何处理政变的。”

She pulled him closer, the silk dress sliding against his skin, a moment of high-stakes intimacy before the storm broke.

她将他拉得更近，丝绸长裙在他的肌肤上滑动，这是风暴来临前极具张力的亲昵。

"But tonight, Chen... tonight, just be my knight. Tomorrow, we go back to being monsters."

“但今晚，陈……今晚，只做我的骑士。明天，我们再变回怪物。”

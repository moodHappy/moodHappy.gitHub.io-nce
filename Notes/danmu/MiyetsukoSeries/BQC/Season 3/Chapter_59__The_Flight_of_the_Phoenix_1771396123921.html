Chapter 59: The Flight of the Phoenix

第五十九章：凤凰的飞行

The "Iron Hive" provided more than just guns; it provided a long-range transport plane hidden in a mountain hangar.

“钢铁蜂巢”提供的不仅仅是枪支；它还提供了一架隐藏在山间机库中的远程运输机。

Miyetsuko sat in the dimly lit cabin, the hum of the engines a steady vibration against her bruised spine.

美悦子坐在昏暗的船舱里，引擎的轰鸣声在她受伤的脊椎上稳稳地跳动。

She had discarded her white rags for a simple, tight black dress that felt like armor.

她脱下了那身白色的破烂衣裳，换上了一件简单的紧身黑裙，感觉就像穿上了盔甲。

"Tokyo is our destination, but Rio is our foundation," she said, looking at the glowing map on the console.

“东京是我们的目的地，但里约是我们的根基，”她看着控制台上发光的地图说道。

"Mishima has cut our digital veins, so we must return to the source of our physical strength."

“三岛切断了我们的数字命脉，所以我们必须回到我们肉体力量的源泉。”

Chen Mo adjusted the autopilot, his face pale and sharp in the cockpit’s blue light.

陈默调整了自动驾驶仪，他的脸在驾驶舱的蓝光中显得苍白而锐利。

"The Fantasma cartel still owes you their lives," he reminded her, his voice a low rasp of fatigue.

“极道幻影依然欠你一条命，”他提醒她，声音因疲惫而沙哑。

"But seven months in the jungle is a lifetime in the underworld. They might have forgotten the taste of your whip."

“但在丛林里待七个月相当于在地下世界待一辈子。他们可能已经忘记了你鞭子的滋味。”

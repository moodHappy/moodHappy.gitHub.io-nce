Chapter 35: The King’s Retirement

第三十五章：国王的退位

The realization hit Khun like a physical blow, stripping away the mask of the untouchable patriarch.

这个认知像重击一样击中了坤老，剥落了他那不可一世的家长面具。

He looked around his compound, realizing the guards in the shadows were no longer looking at him for orders.

他环视着自己的宅邸，意识到阴影中的警卫不再指望从他那里得到指令。

They were looking at the woman in the grey suit.

他们在看着那个穿灰色西装的女人。

"What do you want?" Khun asked, his voice suddenly small, the sound of a man who had lost his horizon.

“你想要什么？”坤老问道，声音突然变得渺小，那是一个失去了地平线的男人的声音。

"I want you to retire, Khun," Miyetsuko said, her tone almost gentle, which was more terrifying than rage.

“我要你退休，坤老，”美悦子说道，语气近乎温柔，这比愤怒更令人恐惧。

"Take your remaining gold, take your youngest mistress, and disappear to your villa in Nice."

“带上你剩下的黄金，带上你最年轻的情妇，消失在你尼斯的别墅里。”

"If you ever step foot near the Mekong again, the Shadow Network will erase you from the banking system and the physical world simultaneously."

“如果你再踏进湄公河一步，暗影网络会将你从银行系统和物理世界中同时抹除。”

Khun looked at her for a long minute, searching for a spark of the victim he once thought he could control.

坤老注视了她良久，试图寻找那个他曾以为可以掌控的受害者的影子。

He found only the Queen.

但他只找到了女皇。

"The river has changed its master," he muttered, standing up with agonizing slowness.

“这条大河换了主人，”他喃喃自语，极其缓慢地站了起来。

He didn't look back as he walked toward the house, his silhouette a fading grey ghost in the growing dark.

他走向屋子时没有回头，他的背影在渐深的暮色中是一抹正在消散的灰色幽灵。

Chapter 57: The Iron Hive

第五十七章：钢铁蜂巢

By the third day, the "Iron Hive"—Mishima’s hidden supply hub—appeared through the mist like a metallic cancer.

到了第三天，被称为“钢铁蜂巢”的三岛隐秘供应站，像一块金属肿瘤般在薄雾中现身。

It was a fortress of concrete and steel, guarded by high-voltage fences and men who thought they were safe.

那是一座由混凝土和钢铁构成的堡垒，由高压电网和自以为安全的守卫看管着。

Miyetsuko stood on a ridge, the Golden Derringer in her hand, the sunlight catching the gilded metal.

美悦子站在山脊上，手里握着金色德林加，阳光捕捉到了那镀金金属的光泽。

"They have the guns, the fuel, and the medicine," she commanded, her voice an iron decree.

“他们有枪、有燃料、有药品，”她下达命令，声音如钢铁般的法令。

"Take everything. Leave nothing but ashes and the memory of who destroyed them."

“夺走一切。除了灰烬和毁灭者的记忆，什么都不要留下。”

Chen Mo led the first wave, a ghost in the shadows who disabled the perimeter sensors with a single, elegant virus.

陈默率领第一波攻击，他如同暗影中的幽灵，用一段优雅的病毒程序禁用了外围传感器。

The assault was not a battle; it was a harvest.

这场突袭不是战斗，而是一场收割。

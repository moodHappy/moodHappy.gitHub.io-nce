Chapter 10: The Emerald Purgatory

第十章：翡翠炼狱

The Gulfstream touched down on a cracked, overgrown runway in the heart of the Golden Triangle.

湾流客机降落在金三角腹地一条布满裂缝、杂草丛生的跑道上。

The humidity hit them like a wet shroud as the cabin door hissed open.

当舱门嘶嘶开启时，潮湿的空气如同一件湿重的裹尸布般扑面而来。

The scent of blooming jasmine and rotting vegetation was a sharp contrast to Tokyo's sterile steel.

盛开的茉莉花香与腐烂的植被气息交织，与东京冰冷的钢铁气息形成了鲜明对比。

Miyetsuko stepped onto the tarmac, the tropical heat instantly clinging to her black tactical suit.

美悦子踏上柏油路面，热带的高温瞬间粘附在她黑色的战术服上。

"Tokyo was a cage of glass," she said, looking at the dense emerald wall of the jungle.

“东京是一座玻璃牢笼，”她看着丛林那堵浓绿的翡翠之墙说道。

"This place... it is a green ocean of chaos."

“而这里……是一片混沌的绿色海洋。”

Chen Mo stood beside her, his hand resting on the hilt of his combat knife.

陈默站在她身边，手按在格斗刀的刀柄上。

His eyes scanned the treeline, searching for the shadows of local warlords and ghost soldiers.

他的目光扫视着林木线，寻找着当地军阀和幽灵士兵的影子。

"In the jungle, there are no algorithms," Chen Mo observed, wiping sweat from his brow.

“在丛林里，没有算法可言，”陈默抹掉额头的汗珠观察道。

"Only the hunger of the predator and the silence of the prey."

“只有捕食者的饥饿和猎物的沉默。”

Miyetsuko reached down and checked the Golden Derringer strapped to her inner thigh.

美悦子伸手检查了缚在大腿内侧的金色德林加手枪。

The metal was hot against her skin, a burning reminder of the law she carried with her.

金属紧贴着她的皮肤微微发烫，时刻提醒着她随身携带的生存法则。

"Mishima thinks he exiled us to the edge of the world," she whispered.

“三岛以为他把我们流放到了世界的尽头，”她低声呢喃。

"He doesn't realize he just gave us the perfect soil to grow a deadlier poison."

“他没意识到，他只是给了我们最完美的土壤，来培育更致命的毒药。”

Chen Mo looked at her, his expression a mask of absolute, unwavering loyalty.

陈默看着她，表情是一副绝对且动摇不得的忠诚面具。

"What is the first move, my Queen?"

“第一步该怎么走，我的女王？”

Miyetsuko looked toward the flickering lights of a distant village, her eyes cold.

美悦子望向远处村庄闪烁的灯火，眼神冰冷。

"We find the man who controls the river," she commanded.

“我们要找到控制这条河流的人，”她命令道。

"And we show him that the Widow has arrived to claim her tribute."

“我们要让他看到，寡妇已至，来索取她的祭品。”

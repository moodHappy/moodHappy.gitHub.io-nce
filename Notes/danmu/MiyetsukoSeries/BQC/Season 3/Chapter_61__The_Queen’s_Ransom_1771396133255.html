Chapter 61: The Queen’s Ransom

第六十一章：女王的赎金

The Favela Fortress. Midnight.

贫民窟堡垒。午夜。

Miyetsuko sat on a throne of reclaimed mahogany, overlooking the glittering lights of Copacabana.

美悦子坐在红木宝座上，俯瞰着科帕卡巴纳闪烁的灯火。

She had summoned the leaders of the five major favelas, men who ruled with iron and blood.

她召集了五个主要贫民窟的首领，那些用钢铁和鲜血进行统治的男人们。

"I need three things," she commanded, her voice cutting through the smell of cigars and expensive rum.

“我需要三样东西，”她下达指令，声音穿透了雪茄和昂贵朗姆酒的味道。

"A fleet of submersible drones, a hundred silent killers who fear nothing, and ten tons of raw industrial diamonds."

“一队潜水无人机，一百名无所畏惧的无声杀手，以及十吨未经加工的工业钻石。”

The men looked at each other, the cost of her request staggering even for them.

男人们面面相觑，即使对他们来说，她的要求也是惊人的。

"And what do we get in return, Empress?" one of the leaders asked, his eyes lingering on her curves.

“那我们能得到什么回报，女皇？”其中一个首领问道，目光停留在她的曲线上。

Miyetsuko stood up, the high slit of her dress revealing the Golden Derringer as she walked toward him.

美悦子站了起来，她走向他时，长裙的高开叉露出了那把金色德林加。

She didn't use the gun. She leaned down and whispered into his ear, her breath a lethal caress.

她没有动枪。她俯下身在他耳边低语，呼吸如同一抹致命的爱抚。

"You get to live to see the dawn. And you get a seat at the table when Tokyo becomes a colony of Rio."

“你能活到看见黎明。而且当东京成为里约的殖民地时，你能在桌旁占有一席之地。”

She pulled back, her eyes burning with a cold, absolute authority.

她退后一步，双眼燃烧着冰冷而绝对的权威。

"The Shadow Network is being reborn. Choose your side now, or find yourself buried in the sand."

“暗影网络正在重生。现在就选边站，或者等着被埋在沙子里。”

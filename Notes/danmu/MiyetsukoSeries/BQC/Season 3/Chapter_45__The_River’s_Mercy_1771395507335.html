Chapter 45: The River’s Mercy

第四十五章：河流的慈悲

The Mekong Gorge.

湄公河峡谷。

The Blackwood mercenaries attempted to cross the river in tactical rafts, confident in their superior tech.

黑木雇佣兵试图乘坐战术突击筏过河，对自己的尖端技术充满信心。

Suddenly, the water erupted.

突然间，水面炸裂。

Miyetsuko had repurposed old river mines with digital triggers; she didn't just want to kill them, she wanted to erase their arrogance.

美悦子将旧的水雷改装成了数字触发式；她不仅要杀了他们，更要抹除他们的傲慢。

From the dense treeline, her new private militia—recruited from the ruins of the General's army—opened fire.

从浓密的林木线后，她那支从将军残部中招募的私人士兵开始交火。

Miyetsuko stood on a jagged rock overlooking the carnage, the Golden Derringer in her hand reflecting the muzzle flashes.

美悦子站在一块俯瞰屠杀的嶙峋怪石上，手中的金色德林加映射着枪口的火光。

She looked like a war goddess of the new age, indifferent to the screams rising from the red-stained water.

她看起来像是一位新时代的战争女神，对从染红的水面传来的尖叫声无动于衷。

"Mishima sent boys to do a Queen's job," she whispered, pulling the trigger as a survivor reached the bank.

“三岛派了群男孩来做女王的工作，”她低声呢喃，在一名幸存者爬上岸时扣动了扳机。

The bullet found its mark, and the river claimed the rest.

子弹正中红心，剩下的全交给了大河。

Chapter 63: The Steel Leviathan

第六十三章：钢铁利维坦

Mid-Atlantic. Onboard the 'Black Queen' Command Ship.

大西洋中部。“黑皇后”号指挥舰。

The Atlantic was a vast, churning desert of indigo, hidden beneath a sky of bruising grey.

大西洋是一片辽阔、翻腾的靛青色沙漠，隐藏在如淤青般的灰色天空下。

Below the surface, the "Ghost Fleet"—a dozen autonomous submersible pods carrying Rio’s elite mercenaries and Myanmar’s silicon weapons—slid through the depths like silent sharks.

在水面之下，“幽灵舰队”——十几艘载着里约精英雇佣兵和缅北硅基武器的自主潜航舱，像沉默的鲨鱼一样穿梭在深海中。

Miyetsuko stood on the observation deck, her silhouette framed by the spray of salt and the cold wind.

美悦子站在观察台上，她的身影被盐雾和冷风勾勒得清晰可见。

She had traded her silk for a heavy, dark trench coat of Italian leather, but beneath it, the Golden Derringer remained her most loyal companion.

她脱下了丝绸，换上了一件沉重的深色意大利皮革风衣，但在大衣之下，金色德林加手枪依然是她最忠诚的伴侣。

"The first pods will breach the Japanese maritime border in forty-eight hours," Chen Mo said, stepping up behind her.

“第一批潜航舱将在四十八小时内突破日本领海边界，”陈默走到她身后说道。

"Mishima’s satellites are looking for a storm, but they won't see the ghosts under the waves."

“三岛的卫星正在搜寻风暴，但他们看不到海浪下的幽灵。”

Miyetsuko turned, her face pale and sharp, her eyes reflecting the cold hunger of the sea.

美悦子转过身，脸色苍白而锐利，双眼倒映着大海那冰冷的饥渴。

"He thinks he can keep me at the edge of the world, Chen," she whispered, her voice a low vibration.

“他以为能把我困在世界的边缘，陈，”她低声呢喃，声音带着低沉的震颤。

"But I am the world now. And I am coming to reclaim my inheritance."

“但现在，我就是世界。我正回来收回我的遗产。”

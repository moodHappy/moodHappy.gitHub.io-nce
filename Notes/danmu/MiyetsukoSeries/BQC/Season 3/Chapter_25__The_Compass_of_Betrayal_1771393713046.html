Chapter 25: The Compass of Betrayal

第二十五章：背叛的罗盘

The Gulfstream veered away from the Thai border, banking sharply toward the rugged mountains of Shan State.

湾流客机偏离了泰国边境，朝掸邦崎岖的山脉急转弯。

"Khun's trap is snapping shut on an empty forest," Chen Mo said, his eyes fixed on the radar.

“坤老的陷阱正对着一片空荡荡的森林收拢，”陈默盯着雷达说道。

"He notified the Thai military, but he didn't count on us smelling the rot in his smile."

“他通知了泰国军方，但他没料到我们从他的笑容里闻到了腐臭味。”

Miyetsuko sat in the leather chair, the silver silk of her gown reflecting the cockpit’s red emergency lights.

美悦子坐在真皮座椅上，银色丝绸长裙映照着驾驶舱红色的应急灯光。

She traced the line of the Golden Derringer on her thigh, her touch a cold, steady caress.

她轻抚着大腿上那把金色德林加的轮廓，指尖的触碰冰冷而稳健。

"Thailand is too polite for what I need to do next," she whispered, her voice a low vibration of intent.

“对于我接下来的计划，泰国显得过于礼貌了，”她低声呢喃，声音中带着低沉的决绝。

"In Northern Myanmar, the air is thick with the scent of unpunished sins. It’s the perfect place for a Queen to build a fortress."

“在缅北，空气中弥漫着未受惩罚的罪恶气息。那是女王建立堡垒的完美之地。”

Chen Mo adjusted the controls, his presence a silent anchor in the turbulent air.

陈默调整着控制杆，他在动荡的气流中如同一座沉静的锚。

"The telecom fraud compounds are ruled by Chairman Lin," Chen Mo reported.

“那些电诈园区由林主席统治，”陈默报告道。

"He has ten thousand digital slaves and a private army that answers to no one but gold."

“他拥有一万名数字奴隶，还有一支只听命于黄金的私人士兵。”

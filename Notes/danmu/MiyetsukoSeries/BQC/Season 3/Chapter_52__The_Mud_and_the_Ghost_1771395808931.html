Chapter 52: The Mud and the Ghost

第五十二章：泥与幽灵

The Subterranean Tunnels.

地下隧道。

The elegant high-tech sanctuary was now a claustrophobic tomb. Red emergency lights pulsed through the rising brown water, illuminating the panic of the guards and the silent terror of the captives.

原本优雅的高科技避难所如今成了幽闭的坟墓。红色的应急灯光在不断上涨的棕色水中搏动，照亮了警卫的恐慌和俘虏们无声的恐惧。

Miyetsuko descended into the chaos, her white gown clinging to her skin, transparent and stained with the filth of the river.

美悦子冲入混乱之中，白裙紧贴着她的肌肤，透明且沾满了河水的污秽。

She didn't run for the helicopters. She ran toward the server core.

她没有跑向直升机，而是跑向了服务器核心。

"Chen! If we lose the data, we lose our eyes in Tokyo!" she screamed, wading through waist-deep sludge.

“陈！如果我们丢了数据，我们在东京就没眼线了！”她在没过腰际的淤泥中跋涉着大喊。

Chen Mo was already neck-deep in the cooling vats, his hands bleeding as he ripped hard drives from their mounts. He looked up, his face a mask of grease and grit.

陈默已经没入冷却池至颈部，他在从支架上扯下硬盘时双手鲜血淋漓。他抬起头，脸上满是油脂和沙砾。

"I can't save them all, Miyetsuko!" he gasped, the water rising to his chin. "Choose: the empire or our lives!"

“我救不了全部，美悦子！”他喘息着，水已涨到他的下巴。“选吧：是要帝国，还是要命！”

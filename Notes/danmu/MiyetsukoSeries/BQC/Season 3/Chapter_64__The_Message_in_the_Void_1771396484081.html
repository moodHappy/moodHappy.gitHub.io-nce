Chapter 64: The Message in the Void

第六十四章：虚空信息

Tokyo. Mishima Group Headquarters. CEO’s Office.

东京。三岛集团总部。CEO办公室。

Mishima sat in his high-backed chair, the red emergency lights of the stock market ticker reflecting in his glasses.

三岛坐在高背椅上，股市行情自动收录器的红色应急灯光映照在他的眼镜上。

His empire was bleeding, but he was still a king, a man who believed in the absolute power of his wealth.

他的帝国正在流血，但他依然是个国王，一个相信财富拥有绝对权力的男人。

Suddenly, his private terminal hummed, a single, encrypted file appearing on the screen.

突然，他的私人终端发出了嗡鸣声，一个加密文件出现在屏幕上。

It was an image: a handful of black Mekong mud, resting in the center of a white silk kimono, stained with the diplomat’s blood.

那是一张图片：一把来自湄公河的黑泥，放在一件沾满外交官鲜血的白色丝绸和服中央。

And beneath the image, a single line of text in English and Japanese:

图片下方，有一行用英文和日文写成的文字：

"THE WIDOW HAS NO MERCY. THE QUEEN HAS NO HOME. I AM COMING."

“寡妇绝无怜悯。女王居无定所。我来了。”

Mishima’s hand trembled as he reached for his cognac, the ice clicking against the glass like bone.

三岛伸手去拿干邑时，手颤抖着，冰块撞击玻璃的声音清脆如骨。

"She survived the river," he whispered, the realization hitting him with the weight of a mountain.

“她从大河中活了下来，”他低声自语，这个认知如泰山压顶般袭来。

"She didn’t just survive. She became the river."

“她不仅活了下来。她变成了那条河。”

He picked up his phone and dialed his chief of security. "Prepare the bunkers. Double the guard. And find me every asset we have in the Yakuza. This is no longer a business deal. This is a war of extermination."

他拿起电话拨给安保主管。“准备地堡。守卫翻倍。帮我找遍我们在极道的所有资源。这不再是一场生意。这是一场灭绝战争。”

Chapter 27: The Chairman’s Price

第二十七章：主席的代价

Inside the compound’s central tower, the hum of thousands of computers vibrated through the floor.

在园区的中央大楼内，数千台电脑的轰鸣声透过地板震动着。

Chairman Lin sat behind a desk of black marble, pouring two glasses of expensive cognac.

林主席坐在黑大理石桌后，倒了两杯昂贵的干邑。

"Your man stays outside," Lin commanded, gesturing toward Chen Mo.

“你的人留在外面，”林主席指着陈默命令道。

Miyetsuko glanced at Chen Mo, a silent command passing between them, a shared secret of lethal intent.

美悦子瞥了陈默一眼，一种无声的指令在两人间传递，那是致命意图的共有秘密。

Chen Mo stepped back into the shadows, his hand resting invisibly on the grip of his blade.

陈默退入阴影中，手无声地搭在刀柄上。

Lin walked around the desk, his gaze lingering on the swell of Miyetsuko's breasts beneath the silver silk.

林主席绕过办公桌，目光停留在美悦子银色丝绸下起伏的胸部。

"The military in Thailand is looking for you," Lin whispered, leaning close enough to smell her jasmine perfume.

“泰国的军方正在找你，”林主席低声说，凑得近到能闻到她茉莉花的香水味。

"But here, I am the law. If you want my protection, you must show me that the 'Blood Queen' is as compliant as they say."

“但在这一带，我就是法律。如果你想要我的庇护，你必须向我展示，‘血色女王’是否如传闻中那样顺从。”

He reached out, his hand sliding over the silk of her shoulder, seeking to pull the gown down.

他伸出手，手掌滑过她肩头的丝绸，试图将长裙拉下。

Miyetsuko didn't move, her smile a beautiful, frozen mask that concealed the roaring engine of her rage.

美悦子纹丝不动，她的微笑是一副华丽而冰冷的面具，掩盖了内心咆哮的愤怒。

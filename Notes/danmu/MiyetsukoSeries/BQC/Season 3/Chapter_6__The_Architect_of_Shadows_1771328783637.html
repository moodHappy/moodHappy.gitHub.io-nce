Chapter 6: The Architect of Shadows

第六章：阴影的建筑师

Mishima sat in the center of his glass hive, watching the red numbers on the monitor fall like fresh blood.

三岛坐在他的玻璃蜂巢中央，看着显示器上的红色数字像鲜血一样跌落。

"She thinks she can bankrupt me with Rio’s dirty money," he murmured, swirling a glass of 30-year-old Yamazaki.

“她以为用里约的脏钱就能让我破产，”他低声呢喃，摇晃着一杯30年的山崎威士忌。

He wasn't panicked; he was mesmerized by the sheer audacity of Miyetsuko’s return.

他并没有恐慌；他被美悦子回归后的那份狂妄彻底迷住了。

"A woman who survives the jungle doesn't come back to play by the rules," Mishima smiled, his scar twitching in the dim light.

“在丛林里活下来的女人，回来绝不是为了遵守规则的，”三岛笑了，他的伤疤在微光中抽动。

He knew the "hostile takeover" was a feint, a smoke screen for something far more lethal.

他知道这场“恶意收购”只是个佯攻，是为某种更为致命的目标而打出的烟幕弹。

Meanwhile, at the Aman suite, Chen Mo was feeding the first strand of the spider's web into the dark web.

与此同时，在安缦套房里，陈默正将蜘蛛网的第一根丝线喂入深网。

"I've leaked the 'Omega Files' location," Chen Mo said, his fingers dancing over the mechanical keyboard.

“我已经泄露了‘奥米加文件’的存放位置，”陈默说着，手指在机械键盘上飞舞。

"Mishima's intelligence team will find a hidden server in an abandoned Kurosaki warehouse in Roppongi."

“三岛的情报团队会在六本木一座废弃的黑崎仓库里，发现一台隐藏的服务器。”

Miyetsuko walked up behind him, her silk robe rustling like a warning against the quiet.

美悦子走到他身后，丝绸睡袍发出如警告般的沙沙声。

She leaned over, her breath hot against Chen Mo's neck, watching the data packets transfer.

她俯下身，呼吸灼热地喷在陈默的颈间，注视着数据包的传输。

"He will think it’s the key to Kurosaki’s satellite laser grid," she whispered, her eyes glowing with a predatory light.

“他会以为那是开启黑崎卫星激光网的钥匙，”她低语，眼中闪烁着掠食者的光芒。

"In reality, it’s a logic bomb that will map every private node in his corporate empire."

“而实际上，那是一个逻辑炸弹，会测绘出他企业帝国里的每一个私密节点。”

Chen Mo turned his head, his lips inches from hers, the blue light of the screens reflecting in his cold eyes.

陈默转过头，嘴唇离她仅有几英寸，屏幕的蓝光映照在他冰冷的双眸中。

"He is a strategist, Miyetsuko. He will send a decoy team first. He won't walk in until he thinks it’s safe."

“他是个战略家，美悦子。他会先派一支诱饵小组过去。在他认为安全之前，他绝不会踏入其中。”

"Then we make it look dangerous enough to be real, but accessible enough to be tempting," she replied.

“那我们就得把它做得足够危险，好显得真实；又要足够易得，好显得诱人，”她回答道。

She reached out, her fingers tracing the line of Chen Mo’s collarbone, a silent acknowledgment of their shared darkness.

她伸出手，手指勾勒着陈默锁骨的线条，无声地确认着他们共有的黑暗。

"He wants to own me, Chen. He wants to see the Queen on her knees."

“他想占有我，陈。他想看到女王跪下的样子。”

"He will die seeing that image," Chen Mo pulled her closer, his hand firm on her waist. "But the knees won't be yours."

“他会死在这个幻象里，”陈默将她拉近，手紧紧扣住她的腰。“但跪下的绝不会是你。”

The rain outside intensified, blurring the neon lights into a chaotic mosaic of red and black.

窗外的雨势加剧，将霓虹灯火模糊成一片红黑交错的混沌马赛克。

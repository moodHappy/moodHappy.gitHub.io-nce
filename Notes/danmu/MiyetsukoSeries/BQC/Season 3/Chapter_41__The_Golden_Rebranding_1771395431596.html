Chapter 41: The Golden Rebranding

第四十一章：金色的重塑

Miyetsuko turned in his arms, her eyes reflecting the cold blue light of the status monitors.

美悦子在他怀中转过身，双眼倒映着状态监控器冰冷的蓝光。

"Let them come. We aren't selling flesh or powder anymore, Chen."

“让他们来吧。陈，我们不再贩卖肉体或粉末了。”

She gestured to the screens, which showed global data traffic being rerouted through their private servers.

她指向屏幕，上面显示着全球数据流量正通过他们的私人服务器重新路由。

"We are selling the one thing everyone in the world is desperate for: Total Anonymity."

“我们在贩卖全世界都渴求的东西：绝对匿名。”

"Every politician in Bangkok, every Yakuza in Tokyo, every hacker in Rio—they will pay their tithe to the Black Lotus."

“曼谷的每个政客，东京的每个极道，里约的每个黑客——他们都要向‘黑莲花’缴纳十一税。”

Chen Mo pulled her closer, the tension of the past weeks melting into a slow, pulsating heat between them.

陈默将她拉得更近，过去几周的紧张感在两人之间融化成一股缓慢搏动的热浪。

"You’ve built a ghost nation in the mud," he whispered, his lips brushing hers.

“你在泥潭里建立了一个幽灵国度，”他低声呢喃，嘴唇擦过她的唇。

"And a Queen needs a King to hold the shadows while she rules the light."

“而女王需要一位国王在她统治光明时守住阴影。”

Miyetsuko smiled, her hands tracing the hard lines of his chest beneath his tactical vest.

美悦子笑了，双手勾勒着他战术背心下坚硬的胸部轮廓。

"I don't need a King, Chen. I need my Exception. The only man who can touch the crown and live."

“我不需要国王，陈。我需要我的‘豁免者’。唯一能触碰王冠并活下来的男人。”

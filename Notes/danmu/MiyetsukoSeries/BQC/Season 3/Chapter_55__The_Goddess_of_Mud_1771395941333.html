Chapter 55: The Goddess of Mud

第五十五章：泥潭女神

The march began in the predawn mist, a column of ghosts rising from the ruins of the Black Lotus.

长征在黎明前的薄雾中拉开序幕，一队幽灵从“黑莲花”的废墟中缓缓升起。

Miyetsuko walked at the head, her white silk gown now a tattered, blood-stained banner of war.

美悦子走在最前方，她那身白色丝绸长裙如今已成了破烂、沾满鲜血的战旗。

The mud of the Mekong caked her bare legs, but she moved with the grace of a predator that had found its purpose.

湄公河的泥浆糊满了她赤裸的双腿，但她的动作依然带着一种寻得目标后掠食者特有的优雅。

Behind her followed three thousand survivors—slaves who were now her disciples, and soldiers who were now her zealots.

在她身后跟随着三千名幸存者——曾经的奴隶如今成了她的信徒，曾经的士兵如今成了她的狂热者。

"They aren't following a CEO anymore, Chen," she whispered, the humidity curling her damp hair.

“陈，他们不再是跟随着一位CEO了，”她低声呢喃，湿气卷曲了她潮湿的发丝。

"They are following a miracle. And a miracle needs to feed on the blood of its enemies."

“他们是在跟随一个奇迹。而奇迹需要以敌人的鲜血为食。”

Chen Mo walked beside her, his rifle slung low, his eyes scanning the dense emerald canopy for Mishima's scouts.

陈默走在她身边，步枪低低地挂着，双眼扫视着浓密的翠绿树冠，搜寻三岛的侦察兵。

Chapter 38: The Bribed Shadows

第三十八章：受贿的阴影

Yuki wiped her mouth and looked at the man who had ignored her beauty for the sake of data.

由纪擦了擦嘴，看着这个为了数据而忽视她美貌的男人。

"Your friends are waiting in the next room," she whispered, her voice trembling.

“你的朋友们在隔壁房间等着，”她低声说道，声音在颤抖。

Chen Mo stood up and put on a black silk robe, leaving Yuki on the floor like a discarded toy.

陈默站起身，披上一件黑色丝绸长袍，像丢弃一件玩具般把由纪留在地板上。

He walked into the conference room where three former executives of Kurosaki Heavy Industries were waiting.

他走进会议室，三名黑崎重工的前高管正在那里等候。

They were men who had been pushed aside by Mishima, men who hungered for their lost power and status.

他们是被三岛排挤的人，是渴望夺回失去权力和地位的人。

"The tsunami has started," Chen Mo said, sitting at the head of the table.

“海啸已经开始了，”陈默坐在长桌的首位说道。

"Mishima's assets are frozen. His creditors are panicking."

“三岛的资产已被冻结。他的债权人正陷入恐慌。”

He tossed three encrypted cold-wallets onto the table, each glowing with the promise of millions.

他将三个加密冷钱包扔在桌上，每一个都闪烁着数百万资产的诱惑。

"These contain the keys to the Shadow Network’s offshore accounts," Chen Mo explained.

“这里面有暗影网络海外账户的密钥，”陈默解释道。

"Use them to buy back the distressed shares. We are going to swallow Mishima from the inside out."

“用它们回购那些崩盘的股票。我们要从内部彻底吞噬三岛。”

The men reached for the wallets with shaking hands, their greed overriding their fear of the woman who sent them.

男人们颤抖着手伸向钱包，贪婪压倒了他们对那位派出陈默的女人的恐惧。

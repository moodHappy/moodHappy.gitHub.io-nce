Chapter 62: The Sacred Reunion

第六十二章：神圣的重逢

Later that night, the villa was silent except for the crashing of the Atlantic waves.

当晚深夜，除了大西洋波浪的撞击声外，别墅里一片寂静。

Miyetsuko stood by the window, the sea breeze whipping her black dress against her pale skin.

美悦子站在窗边，海风吹拂着她的黑裙，紧贴着她苍白的肌肤。

Chen Mo walked up behind her, his hands sliding over her shoulders, grounding her in the quiet darkness.

陈默走到她身后，双手滑过她的肩膀，在寂静的黑暗中让她感到踏实。

The preparations in Rio were moving faster than they had in Myanmar. The old world was falling into place.

里约的准备工作进展得比缅甸快。旧世界正在步入正轨。

"You are stronger here, Miyetsuko," Chen Mo whispered, his lips brushing the curve of her neck.

“美悦子，你在这里更强大了，”陈默低声呢喃，嘴唇掠过她颈部的曲线。

"The jungle gave you teeth, but this city... it gives you a crown."

“丛林给了你牙齿，但这座城市……它给了你王冠。”

Miyetsuko turned in his arms, her hands grabbing his lapels, her hunger for vengeance and power manifesting as a slow, pulsing desire.

美悦子在他怀中转过身，双手抓住他的翻领，她对复仇和权力的渴望表现为一种缓慢、搏动的欲望。

"I don't want the crown yet, Chen," she murmured, pulling him toward the bed.

“我还不要王冠，陈，”她低声说道，将他拉向床边。

"I want to feel the weight of what we’ve built. I want to know that when we burn Tokyo, we have a home to return to."

“我想感受我们所建立的一切的重量。我想知道当我们烧毁东京时，我们还有一个家可以回。”

Chapter 36: The Silent Coronation

第三十六章：无声的加冕

Miyetsuko stood up and walked to the edge of the veranda, looking out at the river.

美悦子站起身走到走廊边缘，望着大河。

The Mekong was a silver thread in the moonlight, now entirely within her grasp.

湄公河在月光下像一根银色的丝线，现在已完全在她的掌控之中。

Chen Mo closed the briefcase and stood beside her, his hand finding hers in the dark.

陈默关上公文包，站在她身边，在黑暗中找到了她的手。

"He’s gone," Chen Mo said, the tension finally leaving his shoulders.

“他走了，”陈默说道，肩膀上的紧张感终于消散。

"He was the last of the old world. Now there is only Mishima."

“他是旧世界的最后一人。现在只剩下三岛了。”

Miyetsuko squeezed his hand, the coldness of the night air failing to touch the fire in her blood.

美悦子紧握住他的手，夜晚空气的寒冷无法触及她血液中的火焰。

"Mishima thinks he’s playing a game of chess," she whispered, her eyes fixed on the northern horizon.

“三岛以为他在玩一盘国际象棋，”她低声呢喃，目光锁住北方的地平线。

"He doesn't realize I’ve already burned the board."

“他没意识到，我已经把棋盘烧了。”

"Prepare the flight, Chen. We aren't going back to Tokyo as fugitives."

“准备飞行，陈。我们不再以逃犯的身份回东京了。”

"We are going back as the reckoning."

“我们要以清算者的身份回去。”

Chapter 54: The Aftermath of Emerald

第五十四章：翡翠余烬

The Ridge overlooking the Mekong. Dusk.

俯瞰湄公河的山脊。黄昏。

The river was unrecognizable. It was a vast, scarred plain of grey mud and broken timber. The "Black Lotus" fortress sat like a broken tooth in the center of the destruction.

大河已变得满目疮痍。那是一片由灰泥和断木组成的广阔而满是伤痕的平原。“黑莲花”堡垒像一颗断牙般立在毁灭的中心。

Miyetsuko sat on a rock, wrapped in a rough wool blanket Chen Mo had found. Her feet were bare and cut, the Golden Derringer lying beside her, covered in silt.

美悦子坐在岩石上，裹着陈默找到的粗羊毛毯。她赤着脚，上面布满伤口，金色德林加手枪沾满泥沙，躺在她身边。

Chen Mo sat behind her, cleaning a portable satellite uplink. They were isolated. The network was dark. The army they had built was scattered.

陈默坐在她身后，清理着便携式卫星上行链路。他们被隔离了。网络已断。他们建立的军队也溃散了。

"We've lost 70% of the infrastructure," Chen Mo said quietly. "Mishima didn't just try to kill us. He erased our base of operations."

“我们损失了70%的基础设施，”陈默轻声说道。“三岛不仅仅想杀了我们。他抹除了我们的行动基地。”

Miyetsuko looked at the survivors huddling by small fires below—the slaves and soldiers who now looked at her not with fear, but with something new.

美悦子看着下方围在小火堆旁缩成一团的幸存者——那些现在不再用恐惧、而是用某种全新的眼神看着她的奴隶和士兵。

"No," she said, her voice raspy but iron-strong. "He gave me exactly what I needed. A legend."

“不，”她说道，声音沙哑却坚如钢铁。“他恰恰给了我想要的东西。一个传说。”

"The girl in the white kimono is gone. The Widow of the Mekong is gone."

“穿白无垢的女孩消失了。湄公河的寡妇也消失了。”

"From the mud, the Goddess of the Damned has risen."

“从泥潭中，被诅咒者的女神已经升起。”

She turned to Chen Mo, the blanket falling away to reveal the raw, bruised power of her body.

她转向陈默，毛毯滑落，露出她那充满原始、淤青力量的身体。

"He thought the river would drown me. Now, I'm going to turn the river into a flood that burns down Tokyo."

“他以为大河会淹没我。现在，我要把大河变成一场烧毁东京的洪灾。”

Chapter 51: The Roar of the Naga

第五十一章：纳迦的怒吼

The Mekong Gorge. 05:30 AM.

湄公河峡谷。凌晨 05:30。

The earth didn't just shake; it screamed. Upstream, the massive "Golden Shield" dam, a project funded by Mishima’s offshore silent partners, buckled under a series of coordinated deep-crust thermite explosions.

大地不仅在震动，它在尖叫。在上游，由三岛海外隐名合伙人出资建设的巨大“金盾”大坝，在系列协调一致的深层铝热剂爆炸中崩塌。

A wall of black water and mountain silt, two hundred feet high, tore through the gorge like the fist of an angry god.

一道两百英尺高的黑水与山泥巨墙，如愤怒之神的拳头般冲破峡谷。

Miyetsuko stood on the highest observation deck of the Black Lotus fortress, feeling the vibration in her marrow. The sunrise was blotted out by a spray of liquid mud.

美悦子站在“黑莲花”堡垒最高的观察台上，感受着骨髓里的震颤。日出被喷涌的泥浆彻底遮蔽。

"It wasn't just a truce," she whispered, her white silk gown whipping around her legs like a frantic ghost. "The diplomat was the distraction. The river is the weapon."

“这不仅仅是停战协定，”她低声呢喃，白丝长裙像疯狂的幽灵般在腿间抽动。“外交官只是幌子。这条大河才是武器。”

Chen Mo appeared at her side, his tactical suit drenched in the sudden, violent spray. He didn't look at the water; he looked at his screens.

陈默出现在她身边，战术服被突如其来的猛烈水雾打湿。他没看水，而是盯着屏幕。

"The lower levels are flooding," Chen Mo shouted over the thunder of the collapse. "Our server farms, the digital slaves... they’re being buried alive in the mud."

“低层正在被淹没，”陈默在崩塌的雷鸣声中大喊。“我们的服务器机群，那些数字奴隶……他们正被活埋在泥浆里。”

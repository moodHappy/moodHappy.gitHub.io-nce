Chapter 31: The Siege of Red Mud

第三十一章：红泥围城

The first mortar shell hit the compound’s outer wall as the monsoon rain began to fall.

当季风雨开始落下时，第一枚迫击炮弹击中了园区的外墙。

The world turned into a chaos of red mud, grey smoke, and the staccato rhythm of machine guns.

世界陷入了红泥、灰烟和机关枪断续节奏交织的混乱之中。

Miyetsuko stood behind a concrete pillar, the heat of the tropical air making her tactical suit cling to her curves.

美悦子站在混凝土柱后，热带空气的高温让战术服紧贴着她的曲线。

She fired with calculated precision, each burst of her rifle a punctuation mark in the symphony of violence.

她以精确计算的准度开火，步枪的每一次点射都是这场暴力交响乐中的标点符号。

Chen Mo was in her earpiece, a calm conductor in the middle of the storm.

陈默的声音出现在她的耳机里，他是这场风暴中冷静的指挥家。

"Warlord Saw’s infantry is breaching the north gate," he said.

“苏将军的步兵正在突破北门，”他说道。

"I'm venting the server room’s Halon gas into the lower tunnels. It will buy us ten minutes."

“我正将机房的哈龙气体排入下方隧道。这能为我们争取十分钟。”

Miyetsuko felt the adrenaline coursing through her veins, a dark nectar that heightened her senses.

美悦子感觉到肾上腺素在血管中流淌，这种黑暗的琼浆提升了她的感官。

She saw a commander in the distance, a man in a camouflage beret shouting orders.

她看到远处的一名指挥官，一个戴着迷彩贝雷帽正在大声下令的男人。

She didn't use the rifle; she pulled the Golden Derringer from her thigh.

她没有动用步枪，而是从大腿处拔出了那把金色德林加。

She took a breath, the scent of ozone and gunpowder filling her lungs.

她深吸一口气，臭氧和火药的味道充满了肺部。

BANG.

砰。

The commander fell, a small, gilded hole in his throat, a silent tribute to the Law.

指挥官应声倒地，喉咙上多了一个精巧、镀金的洞，这是对法则的无声祭献。

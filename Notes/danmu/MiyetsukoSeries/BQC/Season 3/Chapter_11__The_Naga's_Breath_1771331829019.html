Chapter 11: The Naga's Breath

第十一章：纳迦之息

The Naga's Breath was a rusted palace of sin, drifting slowly through the brown arteries of the Mekong.

“纳迦之息”号是一座生锈的罪恶宫殿，在湄公河棕色的动脉中缓缓漂流。

Neon signs flickered like dying stars against the humid velvet of the tropical night.

霓虹招牌在热带夜晚潮湿的天鹅绒衬托下，像即将熄灭的恒星般闪烁。

Miyetsuko walked across the creaking deck, her movements a fluid dance of silk and shadow.

美悦子穿过嘎吱作响的甲板，她的动作是丝绸与阴影交织而成的流畅舞蹈。

She wore a traditional sarong of deep emerald, the fabric clinging to her curves like a lover’s memory.

她穿着一件深翡翠色的传统纱笼，布料像情人的回忆一般紧贴着她的曲线。

The golden derringer remained a secret weight against her thigh, a silent guardian in the den of wolves.

金色德林加手枪依旧是大腿上的一份隐秘重量，是狼窝里的无声守卫。

Chen Mo followed behind, his presence a silent shield that absorbed the predatory gazes of the room.

陈默紧随其后，他的存在犹如一面无声的盾牌，吸收了屋内所有充满掠夺性的目光。

Inside, the casino was a hive of desperate men and drifting smoke, where fortunes were sacrificed on the altar of luck.

赌场内部是亡命徒与缭绕烟雾的蜂巢，无数财富在运气的祭坛上沦为祭品。

The local kingpin, General Vang, sat at the center of the madness, his eyes like two polished obsidian stones.

当地枭雄旺将军坐在这场疯狂的中心，他的眼睛如同两颗打磨过的黑曜石。

He watched Miyetsuko approach, his gaze a heavy pressure that sought to weigh the worth of her soul.

他注视着美悦子走近，那目光如同一股沉重的压力，试图衡量她灵魂的价值。

"I heard a ghost from Tokyo had drifted into my waters," Vang said, his voice a low growl like shifting river silt.

“我听说有个来自东京的幽灵漂到了我的水域，”旺将军开口道，声音低沉得如同河底移动的淤泥。

Miyetsuko leaned over the mahogany table, her perfume cutting through the thick scent of opium.

美悦子俯身靠在红木桌上，她的香水味穿透了浓烈的鸦片气息。

"I am no ghost, General," she whispered, her smile a silver blade flashing in the dim light.

“我不是幽灵，将军，”她低声呢喃，在昏暗灯光下的微笑犹如一闪而过的银色刀刃。

"I am the architect of a new order, and I’ve come to build my foundation on your river."

“我是新秩序的建筑师，而我打算在你的河流上奠定我的基石。”

Vang laughed, the sound a dry rattle of dry bones, but his eyes never left the dangerous curve of her lips.

旺将军笑了起来，那声音像是枯骨的摩擦，但他的眼睛从未离开过她唇角危险的弧度。

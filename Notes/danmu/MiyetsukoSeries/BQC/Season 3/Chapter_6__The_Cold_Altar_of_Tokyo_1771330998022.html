第六章：东京的冷冽祭坛

Aman Tokyo. The Corner Suite. Midnight.

东京安缦。角房套房。午夜。

The skyline of Tokyo bled neon through the floor-to-ceiling windows.

落地窗外，东京的天际线渗出霓虹的血色。

Below, the city was a sprawling circuit board of millions of lives.

下方，这座城市是一块由数百万生命组成的巨大电路板。

All were blissfully unaware of the two predators nesting in the dark above them.

所有人都幸福地对头顶黑暗中筑巢的两头掠食者一无所知。

Miyetsuko stood by the glass, her reflection a ghost of silk and lethal grace.

美悦子站在窗前，她的倒影是一抹由丝绸与致命优雅组成的幽灵。

She wore a midnight-blue silk robe that slid over her skin like cool water.

她穿着一件午夜蓝的丝绸睡袍，绸缎像凉水般滑过肌肤。

In her hand, she swirled a glass of vintage Hibiki, the ice clicking like a countdown.

她轻轻摇晃着手中的陈年响牌威士忌，冰块撞击声如倒计时般清脆。

"The security logs from Fuchu Prison are updated," Chen Mo’s voice floated from the shadows.

“府中监狱的安保日志更新了，”陈默的声音从阴影中飘来。

He sat at a low table, his face illuminated by the cold blue glow of holographic screens.

他坐在矮桌旁，全息屏幕的冷蓝光映照着他的脸。

Lines of green code cascaded down his glasses, reflecting the blueprint of a fortress.

绿色的代码流顺着他的镜片倾泻而下，折射出一座堡垒的蓝图。

"Mishima’s money is flowing into the pockets of the guards," Chen Mo said coldly.

“三岛的钱正流进警卫的口袋，”陈默冷冷地说道。

"He thinks he’s building a wall to keep Kurosaki in."

“他以为自己在筑墙困住黑崎。”

"He doesn't realize he's just building a coffin for himself."

“他没意识到，他只是在给自己造棺材。”

Miyetsuko turned, the high slit of her robe revealing her long, pale thigh.

美悦子转身，睡袍的高开叉露出她修长苍白的大腿。

There, strapped against her skin, sat the Golden Derringer.

在那儿，紧贴着她皮肤缚着的，正是那把金色德林加。

It caught the moonlight, a beautiful, gilded promise of death.

它捕捉着月光，是一份华丽、镀金的死亡诺言。

"Mishima is getting greedy, Chen," she murmured, walking toward him.

“三岛变得贪婪了，陈，”她呢喃着走向他。

"He wants to be the new owner of the Kurosaki throne."

“他想成为黑崎王座的新主人。”

"And he wants me as the crown jewel."

“而他想要我成为那颗皇冠上的明珠。”

She stopped behind him, resting her hands on his shoulders.

她在陈默身后停下，双手搭在他的肩头。

Her scent—jasmine and gunpowder—enveloped him.

她的气息——茉莉花与火药味——笼罩了他。

"He doesn't know the Law," she whispered into his ear.

“他不知道那条法则，”她贴着他的耳朵低语。

"Whoever tries to own the Widow... pays with his blood."

“凡试图占有寡妇者……必以鲜血偿还。”

Chen Mo reached up, his hand covering hers with a firm, grounding grip.

陈默伸手覆住了她的手，那抓握有力且让她感到踏实。

"The 'Trojan Horse' plan is ready," he said, his fingers tapping the keys.

“‘特洛伊木马’计划准备就绪，”他说道，手指敲击着键盘。

"He will think he’s rescuing Kurosaki, but he's actually walking into our theater."

“他会以为自己在营救黑崎，但实际上，他是在走进我们的剧场。”

Miyetsuko leaned down, her heavy breasts pressing against his back through the silk.

美悦子俯下身，沉甸甸的胸部隔着丝绸紧贴他的后背。

"I want the execution to be poetic, Chen."

“我要这场处决富有诗意，陈。”

"I want to burn the memory out of his eyes."

“我要从他的眼睛里烧掉那段记忆。”

Chen Mo turned his head, looking at her with a chilling intensity.

陈默转过头，用一种令人胆寒的强烈眼神看着她。

"The only man I can't kill," she whispered with dark affection.

“我唯一杀不掉的男人，”她带着黑暗的柔情呢喃。

"My Knight. My Brain. My only exception."

“我的骑士。我的大脑。我唯一的豁免。”

Chapter 21: The Silk Web

第二十一章：丝绸之网

The brokers left with a new master, leaving the villa in a heavy, humid silence.

中间人们带着一位新主人离去了，别墅陷入了沉重而潮湿的宁静。

Miyetsuko stood by the open balcony, the first drops of the monsoon beginning to tap against the stone.

美悦子站在敞开的阳台旁，季风的第一滴雨开始敲击着石面。

She felt a hand on her waist, the touch familiar and grounded.

她感觉到腰间有一只手，触感熟悉且让她感到踏实。

Chen Mo pulled her back against his chest, his breath hot against her neck.

陈默将她拉回自己的怀里，呼吸热辣地喷在她的颈间。

The tension of the negotiations was replaced by a slow, pulsing intimacy.

谈判的紧张感被一种缓慢而搏动的亲昵所取代。

"You played them like a violin, Miyetsuko," he murmured, his hands sliding over the silk of her cheongsam.

“美悦子，你像拉小提琴一样玩弄了他们，”他低声呢喃，双手滑过她旗袍的丝绸。

"They fear you more than they feared the General, because they can't predict you."

“他们怕你胜过怕将军，因为他们无法预判你。”

Miyetsuko turned in his arms, her hands finding the buttons of his shirt.

美悦子在他怀中转过身，双手找到了他衬衫的纽扣。

She didn't want to talk about power; she wanted to feel the only person who truly knew her.

她不想谈论权力，她只想感受这个世界上唯一真正了解她的人。

"Fear is a fragile tool, Chen," she whispered, pulling him closer.

“恐惧是一个脆弱的工具，陈，”她低声说着，将他拉得更近。

"But what we have... it is the only thing that is real in this green hell."

“但我们所拥有的……是这片绿色地狱里唯一真实的东西。”

Outside, the storm broke, a wall of water erasing the world beyond the villa.

窗外，暴雨倾盆而下，水墙抹去了别墅以外的世界。

In the dim light, they moved together, a quiet dance of recovery and planning.

在昏暗的光线下，他们依偎在一起，这是一场关于恢复与谋划的静谧之舞。

The "Tokyo Ghost" was becoming the "Empress of the Mekong," and the web was finally starting to hold.

“东京幽灵”正在蜕变为“湄公河女皇”，这张网终于开始稳固了。

第五十八章：新军火库

The hub fell within an hour, the "Blackwood" guards dying in the mud before they could even radio for help.

供应站在一小时内陷落，“黑木”警卫甚至还没来得及无线电求援，就已成片死在泥地里。

Miyetsuko walked through the smoking ruins of the warehouse, her bare feet stepping over the discarded rifles of her enemies.

美悦子走过仓库冒烟的废墟，赤脚踩过敌人丢弃的步枪。

She picked up a black tactical jacket and threw it over her shoulders, the heavy fabric a contrast to her tattered silk.

她捡起一件黑色战术夹克披在肩上，沉重的布料与她破烂的丝绸形成了鲜明对比。

"We are re-armed, my Queen," Chen Mo said, presenting her with a crate of high-grade plastic explosives and encrypted satellite phones.

“我们重整武装了，我的女王，”陈默说道，为她呈上一箱高等级塑胶炸药和加密卫星电话。

Miyetsuko looked at her reflection in a shattered window—a woman of mud, blood, and cold, hard steel.

美悦子在破碎的窗户中看着自己的倒影——一个由泥土、鲜血和冰冷钢铁铸就的女人。

"Mishima wanted to drown the Queen in the mud," she said, her smile a beautiful, dark promise.

“三岛想把女王淹死在泥潭里，”她说道，笑容如同一抹华丽而黑暗的诺言。

"But all he did was give the mud a soul. And now, the mud is coming for Tokyo."

“但他所做的一切，只是给泥潭注入了灵魂。而现在，泥潭正向东京逼近。”

She turned to her followers, who were now loading trucks with stolen weapons.

她转向正在往卡车上装载缴获武器的部下。

"We don't return as fugitives," she shouted, her voice echoing across the mountains.

“我们不再以逃犯的身份回归，”她大声喊道，声音在大山间回荡。

"We return as the storm."

“我们要以风暴的姿态归来。”

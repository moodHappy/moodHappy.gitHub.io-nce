Chapter 28: The Blood Ransom

第二十八章：鲜血赎金

Lin’s hand moved lower, his breath hot and smelling of greed against her neck.

林主席的手向下移，呼吸热辣且带着贪婪的味道，喷在她的颈间。

"The price for your life is one night, Miyetsuko," he growled, his fingers tightening on her waist.

“救你命的代价就是一个夜晚，美悦子，”他低吼道，手指在她的腰间收紧。

Miyetsuko leaned back against the marble desk, her eyes locking onto his with a terrifying, calm clarity.

美悦子向后靠在黑大理石桌上，眼神带着一种令人恐惧、冷静而清澈的光芒锁住了他。

"I have paid many prices to reach this river, Chairman," she whispered, her voice a silk cord.

“为了来到这条大河，我已经付出了许多代价，主席，”她低声呢喃，声音如同一根丝弦。

"But there is a law that governs my body. A law you are about to learn."

“但我的身体有一条法则。一条你即将领教的法则。”

As Lin pulled her closer, his lips seeking her throat, Miyetsuko reached down to the garter on her inner thigh.

当林主席将她拉近，嘴唇寻找着她的喉咙时，美悦子伸手摸向了大腿内侧的枪带。

The Golden Derringer felt hot in her hand, a small, gilded instrument of finality.

金色德林加在手中感觉发烫，那是一件精巧、镀金的终结工具。

She didn't hesitate. She pressed the muzzle against his groin and pulled the trigger.

她没有犹豫。她将枪口顶在他的裆部，扣动了扳机。

BANG.

砰。

The sound was muffled by his own scream of agony as he collapsed to the floor, clutching his ruined manhood.

闷响被他痛苦的尖叫声淹没，他瘫倒在地，紧紧捂住被废掉的命根子。

Chen Mo burst through the door, his silenced pistol coughing twice, dropping the guards in the hallway before they could speak.

陈默冲进门，加装消音器的手枪轻咳了两声，在走廊的警卫还没来得及开口前就将他们击倒。

Miyetsuko stood over the writhing Chairman, the silver silk of her dress stained with his panicked blood.

美悦子站在翻滚的主席身旁，银色丝绸长裙沾染了他惊恐的鲜血。

"I don't need your protection, Lin," she whispered, looking down at him with pure indifference.

“我不需要你的庇护，林，”她低声说道，极其冷漠地俯视着他。

"I need your servers. I need your slaves. And now, I own them all."

“我要你的服务器。我要你的奴隶。而现在，他们全部归我了。”

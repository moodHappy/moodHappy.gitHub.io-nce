Chapter 29: The Vacuum of Power

第二十九章：权力的真空

The death of Chairman Lin was a stone thrown into a pool of piranhas.

林主席的死犹如投向食人鱼池的一块巨石。

Within hours, the digital hum of the "Garden of Prosperity" was drowned out by the distant rumble of convoys.

数小时内，“繁荣园区”的电子轰鸣声就被远处车队的隆隆声所掩盖。

"The hyenas have smelled the carcass, Chen," Miyetsuko said, standing on the observation deck.

“鬣狗们闻到了尸体的味道，陈，”美悦子站在观察台上说道。

She had traded her silver gown for a tactical suit of matte black, her skin pale against the shadows.

她换下了银色长裙，穿上了一身哑光黑的战术服，苍白的肌肤在阴影中格外显眼。

The Golden Derringer was back in its place, a heavy, golden secret against her thigh.

金色德林加手枪回到了原位，那是她大腿上一个沉重且金色的秘密。

Chen Mo was hunched over a bank of monitors, his fingers moving like spiders across the keys.

陈默躬身在一排显示器前，手指如蜘蛛般在按键上飞掠。

"Three local warlords are converging on our coordinates," he reported, his voice devoid of emotion.

“三方当地军阀正向我们的坐标汇合，”他语气平静地报告道。

"They don't want to liberate the slaves; they want to inherit the counting machines."

“他们不是想解放奴隶，他们是想继承这些点钞机。”

Miyetsuko looked at the thousands of digital slaves below, their faces illuminated by the flickering light of their screens.

美悦子俯视着下方数以千计的数字奴隶，他们的脸被闪烁的屏幕光映照着。

"They are looking for a new master," she whispered, a cold light in her eyes.

“他们在寻找一位新主人，”她低声呢喃，眼中闪烁着冷光。

"And I will show them that a Queen is far more terrifying than a Chairman."

“而我会让他们看到，女王比主席要恐怖得多。”

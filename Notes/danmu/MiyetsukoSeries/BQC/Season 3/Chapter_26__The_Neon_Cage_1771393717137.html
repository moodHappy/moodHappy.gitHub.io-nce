第二十六章：霓虹笼牢

The plane landed on a private strip surrounded by watchtowers and rolls of razor wire.

飞机降落在一个被岗楼和铁丝网环绕的私人跑道上。

The heat of Northern Myanmar was dry and metallic, tasting of dust and high-voltage servers.

缅北的热浪干燥且带着金属味，尝起来有尘土和高压服务器的味道。

Miyetsuko stepped out, the silver silk of her dress a blinding intrusion in the grey, industrial hellscape.

美悦子走出机舱，银色丝绸长裙在灰色工业地狱般的景象中显得格外刺眼。

Armed men in mismatched uniforms watched her with eyes that had forgotten how to blink.

穿着杂牌军服的武装人员注视着她，眼神中充满了早已忘却如何眨眼的死寂。

"Welcome to the 'Garden of Prosperity'," a man said, walking forward with a hungry smile.

“欢迎来到‘繁荣园区’，”一个男人走上前来，带着贪婪的微笑说道。

Chairman Lin was a man of soft silk and hard eyes, a spider sitting in the center of a web of digital glass.

林主席是一个身着柔滑丝绸却眼神狠戾的人，他是坐在数字玻璃网中央的一只蜘蛛。

He looked at Miyetsuko’s curves with a slow, offensive greed, ignoring the shadow that was Chen Mo.

他带着缓慢而冒犯的贪婪扫视着美悦子的曲线，完全无视了身为影子的陈默。

"I heard you have the 'Golden Silk' from Cambodia," Lin said, his voice a oily rasp.

“我听说你带来了柬埔寨的‘金丝’，”林主席说道，声音油腻而沙哑。

"I have the infrastructure to turn those souls into pure, untraceable data. But the price of entry is... personal."

“我有基础设施把那些灵魂变成纯粹且无法追踪的数据。但准入门票是……私人性质的。”

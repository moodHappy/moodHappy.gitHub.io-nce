Chapter 30: The Digital Barricade

第三十章：数字堡垒

Chen Mo bypassed the compound’s central firewall, turning the surveillance grid into a weapon.

陈默绕过了园区的中央防火墙，将监控网络转化为了武器。

"I've locked the gates and activated the automated turrets," he said, his eyes reflecting a waterfall of data.

“我已经锁死大门并激活了自动防御炮塔，”他说道，双眼倒映着数据瀑布。

"But steel and code won't stop a hungry army. They need to see blood on the walls."

“但钢铁和代码挡不住饥饿的军队。他们需要在墙上看到鲜血。”

Miyetsuko walked to the weapon rack and picked up a customized assault rifle.

美悦子走向武器架，拿起一把定制的突击步枪。

She felt the weight of the metal, a grounding reality in this digital purgatory.

她感受着金属的重量，这是在这片数字炼狱中脚踏实地的现实感。

She turned to the remnants of Lin’s private guard, men who were now adrift in the wake of their master’s death.

她转向林主席私兵的残部，这些男人在主人死后正处于迷茫之中。

"Your Chairman is dead because he was weak," she shouted, her voice echoing through the steel halls.

“你们的主席死了，因为他很虚弱，”她大声喊道，声音在钢铁走廊间回荡。

"I am the Widow, and I offer you two choices: die for a ghost, or live for a Queen."

“我是那名寡妇，我给你们两个选择：为幽灵而死，或者为女王而活。”

The soldiers looked at her, at the lethal elegance of her form and the cold fire in her gaze.

士兵们看着她，看着她那致命的优雅姿态和目光中冰冷的火焰。

One by one, they lowered their heads in a silent, desperate surrender.

一个接一个，他们在沉默而绝望的臣服中低下了头。

"Good," Miyetsuko smiled, a beautiful, dark omen.

“很好，”美悦子笑了，这是一个华丽而黑暗的预兆。

"Now, man the perimeter. The vultures are here."

“现在，守住外围。秃鹫们到了。”

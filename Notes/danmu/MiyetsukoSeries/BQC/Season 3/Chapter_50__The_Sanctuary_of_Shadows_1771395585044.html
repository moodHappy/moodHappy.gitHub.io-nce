Chapter 50: The Sanctuary of Shadows

第五十章：暗影避难所

The hall was silent again, the body in the glass box a grim monument to Mishima's desperation.

大厅再次陷入寂静，玻璃箱里的尸体成了三岛垂死挣扎的阴冷纪念碑。

Miyetsuko turned to Chen Mo, who was already initiating the decontamination sequence.

美悦子转向陈默，他已经开始启动去污程序。

She walked to him, the white silk of her dress stained with the imaginary blood of the encounter.

她走向他，白丝长裙仿佛沾染了这场邂逅中臆想出来的鲜血。

She pulled him close, her hands finding the warmth of his neck, her body trembling with a mix of fury and relief.

她将他拉近，双手寻找着他颈部的温度，身体因为愤怒与宽慰的交织而颤抖。

"He tried to take the air from my lungs, Chen," she murmured, her face buried in his chest.

“陈，他想夺走我肺里的空气，”她在他怀里低声呢喃。

"He thinks he can reach me anywhere. He thinks I am still that girl in the white kimono."

“他以为他在任何地方都能抓到我。他以为我还是那个穿白无垢的女孩。”

Chen Mo held her with a fierce, protective grip, his gaze fixed on the dead man in the box.

陈默以一种强烈的保护姿态抱着她，目光死死盯着箱里的死人。

"He just signed his own death warrant," Chen Mo growled, his voice a dark promise.

“他刚刚签下了他自己的死亡证明，”陈默低吼道，声音里带着黑暗的诺言。

"We aren't waiting for him to come to us anymore. We are going to Tokyo, and we are going to tear his world apart."

“我们不再等他上门了。我们要回东京，我们要把他的世界撕成碎片。”

Miyetsuko looked up, the white silk of her gown reflecting the red emergency lights, turning her into a vision of blood and snow.

美悦子抬起头，白丝长裙映照着红色的应急灯光，将她映衬成一幅血与雪交织的幻象。

"Yes," she whispered, her smile a beautiful, final omen. "Let the Queen return to her ruins."

“是的，”她低声说道，笑容如同一抹华丽而终极的预兆。“让女王回到她的废墟中去吧。”

Chapter 43: The Iron Tide

第四十三章：钢铁潮汐

Chiang Rai Border. 04:00 AM.

清莱边境。凌晨 04:00。

The "Advisors" arrived in a fleet of black, unmarked gunships, their rotors slicing through the humid dawn like knives.

“顾问团”乘着一队全黑、无标识的武装直升机抵达，旋翼如利刃般划破潮湿的黎明。

They were "Blackwood" mercenaries—ex-special forces from three continents, hired by Mishima to surgically remove the "infection" in the jungle.

他们是“黑木”雇佣兵——来自三大洲的前特种部队，受三岛雇佣，旨在外科手术式地切除丛林中的“感染源”。

The lead commander, a man with a scarred throat named Kurtz, looked at the rugged Myanmar border through thermal optics.

领头的指挥官是个脖子上有伤疤、名叫库尔茨的男人，他正通过热成像仪观察崎岖的缅甸边境。

"I don't care about the woman's legend," Kurtz growled into his comms.

“我不关心那个女人的传说，”库尔茨对着通讯器低吼道。

"I only care about the bounty on her head. Level anything that breathes."

“我只在乎她项上人头的赏金。扫平一切活物。”

Below them, the Mekong was a vein of dark mercury, hiding the traps that Miyetsuko had spent weeks laying in the mud.

在他们下方，湄公河如同一条黑暗的水银血脉，掩藏着美悦子花数周时间布下的泥潭陷阱。

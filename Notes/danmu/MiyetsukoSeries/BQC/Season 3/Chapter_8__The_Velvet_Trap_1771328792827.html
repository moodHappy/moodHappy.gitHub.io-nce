Chapter 8: The Velvet Trap

第八章：天鹅绒陷阱

The next evening, the invitations arrived at every major embassy in Tokyo.

次日傍晚，请柬送达了东京的每一个主要大使馆。

"A Celebration of the New Era. Hosted by Rio Pacific Holdings."

“新时代庆典。由里约太平洋控股主办。”

It was a bold move. A public declaration of war disguised as a gala.

这是一个大胆的举动。一场伪装成晚宴的公开宣战。

Miyetsuko stood before the mirror, letting Chen Mo drape a necklace of black diamonds around her neck.

美悦子站在镜子前，任由陈默将一条黑钻项链戴在她的颈间。

"Mishima will come," she said, looking at her reflection. "He can't resist the urge to show me that he’s won the digital key."

“三岛会来的，”她看着镜中的自己说。“他无法抗拒那种冲动，他想向我展示他已经赢得了数字钥匙。”

"He thinks he’s walking into a room where he holds all the cards," Chen Mo replied, adjusting the holster under his tuxedo.

“他以为自己走进的是一个由他掌控全局的房间，”陈默回答道，调整着无尾礼服下的枪套。

"But the 'Omega Files' he downloaded are actually a dormant virus that will trigger when he gets within 10 meters of your phone."

“但他下载的‘奥米加文件’实际上是一个休眠病毒，只要他靠近你的手机10米以内，就会被触发。”

"The proximity trigger," Miyetsuko smiled. "So the closer he gets to me, the more he loses."

“近接触发，”美悦子笑了。“所以他离我越近，失去的就越多。”

"Exactly. His empire will begin to bleed data the moment he tries to touch you."

“没错。在他试图触碰你的那一刻，他的帝国就会开始泄露数据。”

The ballroom of the Ritz-Carlton was a sea of black ties and silk gowns.

丽思卡尔顿酒店的宴会厅里是一片黑领结与丝绸长裙的海洋。

When Miyetsuko entered, the air seemed to thin. She wore a dress made of silver mesh that looked like liquid armor.

当美悦子进入时，空气似乎变得稀薄了。她穿着一件银色网纱礼服，看起来像流动的盔甲。

Every head turned. She was no longer the widow; she was the sun around which the room orbited.

所有的头都转了过来。她不再是那个遗孀；她是整个房间环绕运行的太阳。

Mishima appeared at the top of the stairs, looking immaculate in a white tuxedo.

三岛出现在楼梯顶端，穿着一身洁白的无尾礼服，看起来无懈可击。

He walked down, his eyes never leaving hers. The crowd parted like water before a shark.

他走下楼梯，目光从未离开过她。人群像躲避鲨鱼的水流一样散开。

"Mrs. Kurosaki. Or should I say... the Empress of Rio?" Mishima greeted, taking her hand and kissing it.

“黑崎夫人。或者我该叫你……里约女皇？”三岛打招呼道，抓过她的手吻了一下。

The moment his lips touched her skin, the silent war began.

当他的嘴唇触碰到她皮肤的那一刻，无声的战争开始了。

Inside Chen Mo’s earpiece, a soft chime sounded.

陈默的耳机里传出一声轻柔的鸣响。

"Connection established," Chen Mo’s voice whispered in Miyetsuko’s ear. "The virus is migrating. We are inside his personal network."

“连接已建立，”陈默的声音在美悦子耳边低语。“病毒正在迁移。我们已经进入了他的私人网络。”

Miyetsuko didn't pull her hand away. She leaned in closer, her scent filling Mishima's senses.

美悦子没有移开手。她凑得更近了，她的气息充斥着三岛的感官。

"You look triumphant, Mishima-san," she purred. "Did you find what you were looking for in the dark?"

“你看起来胜券在握，三岛先生，”她娇媚地说道。“你在黑暗中找到了你想要的东西吗？”

"I found everything," Mishima whispered back, his hand sliding to her waist, pulling her flush against him.

“我找到了所有东西，”三岛低语回去，手滑到她的腰间，将她紧贴向自己。

"I found the key to the world. And now, I want the key to you."

“我找到了通往世界的钥匙。而现在，我想要通往你的钥匙。”

Miyetsuko felt the hard line of the Golden Derringer against her thigh, separated from him only by a layer of silver mesh.

美悦子感觉到大腿上的金色德林加那坚硬的轮廓，与他之间仅隔着一层银色网纱。

She didn't kill him. Not yet.

她没有杀他。还不是时候。

She just smiled, a beautiful, terrifying promise of the end.

她只是笑了，那是关于终结的、一个美丽而恐怖的诺言。

"Be careful what you wish for, Mishima. Some keys only unlock the gates of hell."

“小心你的愿望，三岛。有些钥匙只能开启地狱之门。”

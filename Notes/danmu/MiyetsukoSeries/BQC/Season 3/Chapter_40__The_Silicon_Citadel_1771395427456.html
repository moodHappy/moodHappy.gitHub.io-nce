Chapter 40: The Silicon Citadel

第四十章：硅基堡垒

Northern Myanmar. The "Black Lotus" Command Center.

缅北。“黑莲花”指挥中心。

Deep beneath the emerald canopy of the Shan hills, a subterranean fortress hummed with the cold energy of a thousand cooling fans.

在掸邦丘陵浓绿的树冠深处，一座地下堡垒正随着上千台冷却风扇的冷能而嗡鸣。

Miyetsuko stood on a glass walkway overlooking the server farm, her silhouette sharp and commanding.

美悦子站在俯瞰服务器阵列的玻璃走廊上，她的剪影锐利且富有威慑力。

She had traded her silk for a bodysuit of carbon-fiber weave, a second skin that emphasized her lethal curves.

她换掉了丝绸，穿上了一件碳纤维织成的紧身衣，这层“第二皮肤”勾勒出她致命的曲线。

"Tokyo is drowning in the storm you started, Chen," she said, her voice echoing in the sterile, high-tech cavern.

“东京正淹没在你掀起的风暴中，陈，”她说道，声音在冰冷的高科技洞穴中回荡。

"But here, the sun is just rising on our true empire."

“但在此时此地，我们真正帝国的太阳才刚刚升起。”

Chen Mo walked up behind her, the scent of jet fuel and Tokyo rain still clinging to his tactical gear.

陈默走到她身后，战术装备上还残留着航空煤油和东京雨水的味道。

He had returned from his mission, his presence a dark, grounded anchor for her soaring ambition.

他已完成任务归来，他的存在是她飞扬野心的一座黑暗而稳固的锚。

"Mishima's stock is a corpse, but his private army is still breathing," Chen Mo reported, his hand resting on the small of her back.

“三岛的股票已经是一具尸体，但他的私人军队还在呼吸，”陈默报告道，手搭在她的后腰。

"They are sending a fleet of 'advisors' to the Mekong. They are looking for the source of the infection."

“他们正向湄公河派遣一支‘顾问’车队。他们正在寻找感染的源头。”

Chapter 39: The Knight’s Report

第三十九章：骑士的报告

Later that night, Chen Mo sat alone on the balcony, the rain falling in a heavy curtain over Tokyo.

当晚深夜，陈默独自坐在阳台上，大雨如厚重的幕帘般笼罩着东京。

He opened a secure channel to the Mekong villa.

他打开了通往湄公河别墅的安全频道。

Miyetsuko's face appeared on the screen, her pale skin glowing in the low light of her own sanctuary.

美悦子的脸出现在屏幕上，她苍白的肌肤在避风港昏暗的光线下闪着微光。

"It’s done, my Queen," Chen Mo said, his voice flat and professional.

“办妥了，我的女王，”陈默声音平淡而职业。

"Mishima is bleeding. The market is in chaos. He is a dead man walking, even if he doesn't know it yet."

“三岛正在大出血。市场陷入了混乱。即便他还没意识到，他也已经是具行尸走肉了。”

Miyetsuko leaned closer to the camera, her eyes searching his face for any sign of his encounter with Yuki.

美悦子凑近镜头，目光在他脸上搜索，试图寻找他与由纪相遇后的任何蛛丝马迹。

"And did Tokyo provide you with the... relief you needed?" she asked, a dangerous, playful edge to her voice.

“那么，东京是否为你提供了……所需的慰藉？”她问道，声音里带着一种危险且戏谑的锋芒。

Chen Mo looked directly into the camera, his loyalty as absolute as the darkness surrounding him.

陈默直视镜头，他的忠诚如同包围着他的黑暗一般绝对。

"It provided a body, but nothing more," he replied.

“它提供了一个肉体，仅此而已，”他回答道。

"My brain and my soul still belong to you. Always."

“我的大脑和灵魂依然属于你。永远如此。”

Miyetsuko smiled—a slow, beautiful curve that promised both reward and ruin.

美悦子笑了——那是一个缓慢而华丽的弧度，既承诺了奖赏，也预示了毁灭。

"Good. Prepare the final takeover. I’m coming home to watch the empire burn."

“很好。准备最后的收购。我要回家亲眼看着那个帝国化为灰烬。”

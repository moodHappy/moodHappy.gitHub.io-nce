Chapter 44: The Ghost in the Wire

第四十四章：线缆中的幽灵

The Black Lotus Command Center.

“黑莲花”指挥中心。

Inside the fortress, the blue light of the servers turned a violent, pulsing red.

在堡垒内部，服务器的蓝光变成了狂暴且搏动的红色。

"Interpol has launched a Level 7 cyber-strike," Chen Mo said, his fingers a blur across the mechanical keyboard.

“国际刑警组织发起了7级网络打击，”陈默说道，他的手指在机械键盘上快得只剩残影。

"Mishima's moles inside the agency are using high-level decryption keys. They’re trying to burn our sovereignty from the inside out."

“三岛在机构内部的暗桩正在使用高级解密密钥。他们试图从内部彻底烧毁我们的主权。”

Miyetsuko stood behind him, her hand resting on his shoulder, the carbon-fiber of her suit cool and unyielding.

美悦子站在他身后，手搭在他的肩膀上，她那身碳纤维战术服冰冷且坚硬。

She watched the digital battlefield—millions of lines of hostile code crashing against Chen Mo's firewalls.

她注视着数字战场——数百万行敌意代码正猛烈撞击着陈默的防火墙。

"Can they find our physical coordinates?" she asked, her voice a calm, lethal whisper.

“他们能定位我们的物理坐标吗？”她问道，声音是冷静且致命的呢喃。

"Not as long as I’m breathing," Chen Mo replied, his eyes reflecting a digital storm.

“只要我还喘气，他们就别想，”陈默回答，双眼倒映着一场数字风暴。

"But they’re targeting our financial nodes. They want to turn our gold back into worthless lead."

“但他们正瞄准我们的金融节点。他们想把我们的黄金重新变回毫无价值的铅。”

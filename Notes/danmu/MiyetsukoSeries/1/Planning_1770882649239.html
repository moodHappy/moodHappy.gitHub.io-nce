Season 2: Ripples and Radiance
第二季：涟漪与光芒

宗旨与调性 | Mission & Tone

The bond deepens through external challenges and controlled pain.
宗旨：通过外部挑战与受控的苦痛，深化两人之间的羁绊。

Keep conflicts within a "controllable range"—no irreversible tragedies, only emotional fuel.
注意：将冲突控制在“可控范围”内——没有不可逆的悲剧，只有情感的助燃剂。

新登场人物 | New Characters

1. 佐藤 健二 (Sato Kenji)
The Observer / 观察者

An observant student council member who notices the "bite mark" on Miyetsuko's shoulder. He represents the risk of discovery.
敏锐的学生会成员，注意到了美悦子肩上的齿痕。他代表了“被发现”的风险。

2. 丽香 (Reika)
The Provocateur / 挑衅者

A bold girl who openly flirts with Shiraishi, triggering Miyetsuko’s intense jealousy and possessiveness.
一个大胆的女孩，公开挑逗白石，激发出美悦子强烈的嫉妒心与独占欲。

阶段性核心事件 | Plot Arcs

第一阶段：湖边小屋的洗礼 (The Lakeside Cabin)

A private weekend away from school. No walls, no interruptions—only complete surrender.
远离学校的私密周末。没有隔墙，没有打扰——只有彻底的臣服。

第二阶段：入侵的视线 (The Intruding Eyes)

The student council begins to suspect Miyetsuko's frequent absences. The thrill of hiding a secret becomes a painful pressure.
学生会开始怀疑美悦子的频繁缺席。隐藏秘密的快感变成了令人痛苦的压力。

第三阶段：嫉妒的烈火 (The Fire of Jealousy)

Reika tries to get close to Shiraishi. Miyetsuko learns that she must fight to keep what belongs to her.
丽香试图接近白石。美悦子意识到，她必须通过战斗来守护属于她的东西。

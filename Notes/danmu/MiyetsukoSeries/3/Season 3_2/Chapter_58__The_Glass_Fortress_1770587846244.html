Chapter 58: The Glass Fortress

第五十八章：玻璃堡垒

Aman Tokyo. The Corner Suite. 34th Floor.

东京安缦酒店。角房套房。34层。

The suite was a sanctuary of stone, wood, and silence. Floor-to-ceiling windows offered a panoramic view of the Imperial Palace gardens and the glittering city beyond. It was a world away from the humid, rotting bunker in Rio.

套房是石头、木材和寂静的圣所。落地窗提供了皇居花园和远处闪耀城市的无敌全景。这与里约那个潮湿、腐烂的地堡简直是两个世界。

Miyetsuko kicked off her heels. She didn't turn on the lights. The city glow was enough.

美悦子踢掉高跟鞋。她没开灯。城市的微光就足够了。

She walked to the window, pressing her hand against the cold glass.

她走到窗前，手按在冰冷的玻璃上。

"We are back in the spider's web, Chen," she whispered. "Mishima has eyes everywhere. Even in this hotel."

“我们回到了蜘蛛网里，陈，”她低语。“三岛到处都有眼睛。甚至在这家酒店里。”

Chen Mo was sweeping the room with an RF detector. It beeped near a flower vase.

陈默正拿着射频探测器扫描房间。在花瓶附近，它发出了蜂鸣声。

"Found one," he plucked a tiny bug from under a lily. He crushed it between his fingers. "Amateur."

“找到一个，”他从百合花下摘出一个微型窃听器。用手指捏碎。“业余。”

He walked over to her. He was still wearing his suit, tie loosened. He looked tired but alert.

他走向她。他还穿着西装，领带松开了。看起来疲惫但警觉。

"We expected this," he stood behind her, looking at their reflection in the glass. Two predators in a glass cage. "Mishima is scared. Scared men listen."

“意料之中，”他站在她身后，看着玻璃里的倒影。两头困在玻璃笼子里的掠食者。“三岛在害怕。害怕的人才会偷听。”

"Let him listen," Miyetsuko turned around. She reached for his tie, unknotting it slowly. "Let him hear us celebrate."

“让他听，”美悦子转过身。她伸手去解他的领带，动作缓慢。“让他听听我们怎么庆祝。”

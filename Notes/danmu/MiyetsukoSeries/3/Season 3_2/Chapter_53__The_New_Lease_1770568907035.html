Chapter 53: The New Lease

第五十三章：新租约

Fantasma whimpered, looking from the woman above him to the sniper rifle aimed at his manhood.

幽灵呜咽着，看着上方的女人，又看着瞄准他命根子的狙击步枪。

"Please... take it. Take the port. Take the club. Just let me live."

“求你……拿去。港口归你。俱乐部归你。让我活着。”

"I'm not taking it," Miyetsuko corrected him. She took the gun from her thigh holster—the gold derringer. She traced the barrel down his chest, stopping at his belt buckle.

“我不是在拿，”美悦子纠正他。她从大腿枪套里拿出那把枪——那把金色德林加。枪管顺着他的胸口向下滑，停在皮带扣上。

"I'm renting it. And the rent... is your absolute obedience."

“我是在租。而租金……是你的绝对服从。”

"Yes! Yes! I obey!"

“是！是！我服从！”

Miyetsuko stood up. She holstered the gun. She looked at the terrified crowd of gangsters, whores, and thieves.

美悦子站起身。收起枪。她看着那群被吓坏的黑帮、妓女和小偷。

"From today," her voice was calm, authoritative. "Rio is closed to Mishima. Any ship, any plane, any rat that comes from Tokyo... gets burned."

“从今天起，”她的声音平静、威严。“里约对三岛关闭。任何从东京来的船、飞机、老鼠……统统烧掉。”

She turned around and walked towards the stairs where Chen Mo was descending.

她转身走向楼梯，陈默正从那里走下来。

She met him halfway.

她在半路迎上他。

She didn't care about the audience. She wrapped her arms around his neck and kissed him deeply. It was a kiss of victory, tasting of adrenaline and power.

她不在乎观众。她搂住他的脖子，深深地吻了他。那是一个胜利的吻，尝起来有肾上腺素和权力的味道。

"We own the city, Chen," she whispered against his lips.

“我们拥有这座城市了，陈，”她在他唇边低语。

Chen Mo's hand rested on the small of her back, over the tactical gear. He looked over her shoulder at the kneeling drug lord.

陈默的手停在她的后腰，隔着战术装备。他越过她的肩膀看着跪在地上的毒枭。

"We own the cage," he corrected her. "Now... we prepare for the hunter."

“我们拥有了这个笼子，”他纠正她。“现在……我们要为猎人做准备了。”

He lifted her up, carrying her bridal style out of the club, stepping over the broken glass and blood.

他把她抱起来，用公主抱的方式走出俱乐部，跨过碎玻璃和血迹。

"Take me to the penthouse," she murmured, burying her face in his neck. "I want to take a bath. And I want you to scrub every inch of this night off me."

“带我去顶层公寓，”她喃喃道，脸埋进他的脖颈。“我想洗个澡。我要你把今晚的每一寸痕迹都从我身上擦掉。”

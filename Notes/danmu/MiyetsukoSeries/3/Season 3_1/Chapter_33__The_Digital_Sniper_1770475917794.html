第三十三章：数字狙击手

A coin‑operated computer flickered to life. Tor routing. Overseas proxies. Connection established.

投币电脑亮起。 Tor跳转，海外代理。 连接建立。

Message sent: We have the Kurosaki Ledger. Price: 10% of Kurosaki Heavy Industries. Time limit: 1 hour.

信息发送： 黑崎账本在手。 价格：黑崎重工10%股份。 时限：1小时。

Five minutes later— a live video request.

五分钟后—— 实时视频请求。

A synthetic voice spoke: "Mrs. Kurosaki… ruthless as always."

合成声音低沉响起： “黑崎夫人……一如既往心狠。”

"Meet tomorrow night. Yokohama Port. Warehouse 4. Alone."

“明晚见。 横滨港，四号仓库。 单独来。”

Call ended.

通话结束。

Chen Mo exhaled slowly. "That’s a trap."

陈默缓缓呼气。 “这是陷阱。”

"Of course," Miyetsuko said calmly. "But waiting here means death."

“当然。 但等在这里就是等死。”

"We need insurance."

“我们需要保险。”

"We need guns. Lots of guns. And a fast car."

“我们需要枪。 很多枪。 还有一辆快车。”

Chapter 52: The Knee and the Muzzle

第五十二章：膝盖与枪口

"Kill her!" Fantasma screamed at his bodyguards.

“杀了她！”幽灵对保镖尖叫。

BANG!

砰！

A single shot rang out from the shadows of the VIP balcony.

一声枪响从VIP阳台的阴影里传来。

Fantasma's head bodyguard dropped, a hole in his forehead.

幽灵的保镖头子倒下了，额头上有个洞。

Chen Mo stepped out of the shadows above, holding a smoking sniper rifle. He had infiltrated the club while Miyetsuko made her entrance.

陈默从上方的阴影里走出来，手里拿着还在冒烟的狙击步枪。在美悦子进场时，他已经潜入了俱乐部。

"Anyone else moves, they die," Chen Mo's voice was amplified by the club's speakers. It was the voice of God.

“谁再动，谁就死，”陈默的声音通过俱乐部的扬声器放大。那是上帝的声音。

Fantasma looked around. His men were outnumbered. His ally was dead. His money was gone.

幽灵环顾四周。他的人寡不敌众。盟友死了。钱没了。

Miyetsuko walked up to him. She didn't raise her rifle. She used it like a club, smashing the buttstock into Fantasma's knee.

美悦子走到他面前。她没有举起步枪。她把它当棍子用，枪托狠狠砸在幽灵的膝盖上。

CRACK.

咔嚓。

"AHHH!" He fell to the floor, clutching his shattered leg.

“啊！”他倒在地上，捂着碎裂的腿。

She stood over him, placing her combat boot on his chest, pressing down hard enough to crack a rib.

她站在他上方，把战术靴踩在他胸口，用力下压，足以踩断一根肋骨。

"I told you, Fantasma," she leaned down, her face inches from his. Her sweat dripped onto his cheek. "I am the one who eats."

“我告诉过你，幽灵，”她俯下身，脸离他只有几英寸。她的汗水滴在他脸颊上。“我是进食的那一个。”

She grabbed his hair, pulling his head back, exposing his throat.

她抓住他的头发，把他的头向后拉，暴露出喉咙。

"Chen," she called out, looking up at the balcony. "Do we need him?"

“陈，”她抬头看阳台，喊道。“我们需要他吗？”

"We need the port," Chen Mo aimed the rifle at Fantasma's crotch. "But we don't need his balls."

“我们需要港口，”陈默把步枪瞄准幽灵的裤裆。“但不需要他的蛋。”

Chapter 42: The Unbuckling

第四十二章：解开束缚

"Get this off me," Miyetsuko groaned, clawing at the velcro of her Kevlar vest. "I can't breathe."

“帮我脱掉这个，”美悦子呻吟着，抓挠着凯夫拉背心的魔术贴。“我透不过气。”

Chen Mo walked over.

陈默走过去。

RRRIP.

嘶啦。

He tore the velcro straps open.

他撕开了魔术贴带子。

Miyetsuko let out a long, ragged exhale as the pressure released. Her chest heaved, expanding to its full capacity for the first time in hours.

随着压力释放，美悦子发出了一声长长的、破碎的呼气。她的胸廓起伏，几个小时来第一次完全扩张。

Chen Mo lifted the heavy vest over her head and threw it into the corner. It landed with a thud.

陈默把沉重的背心从她头上脱下来，扔到角落里。发出一声闷响。

Underneath, the black tactical bodysuit was soaked through with sweat. It clung to her like a second skin, outlining every curve, every shivering muscle.

在下面，黑色的战术紧身衣已经湿透了。它像第二层皮肤一样贴在她身上，勾勒出每一道曲线，每一块颤抖的肌肉。

"The zipper," she turned her back to him.

“拉链，”她背对着他。

Chen Mo pulled the zipper down slowly. It parted the black fabric, revealing a strip of pale skin that was red and angry from the heat and friction.

陈默慢慢拉下拉链。黑色布料分开，露出一道苍白的皮肤，因为闷热和摩擦而发红、发怒。

She peeled the suit off her shoulders, then her hips, pushing it down to her ankles. She stepped out of it, kicking the boots away.

她把紧身衣从肩头剥下，然后是臀部，一直推到脚踝。她跨出来，踢掉了靴子。

She stood naked in the flickering lantern light.

她在摇曳的灯光中赤裸站立。

Her body was a map of their escape.

A large purple bruise on her right shoulder from the rifle recoil.

Red welts on her ribs where the vest had dug in during the car crash.

Scratches on her legs from the shattered glass.

她的身体是他们逃亡的地图。

右肩上有一块巨大的紫色淤青，那是步枪后坐力留下的。

肋骨上有红色的勒痕，那是车祸时背心勒进去的地方。

腿上有碎玻璃划出的伤痕。

But she didn't look broken. She looked raw. Powerful. Like a weapon that had been used and proven.

但她看起来没有破碎。她看起来原始。有力。像一件经过使用和验证的武器。

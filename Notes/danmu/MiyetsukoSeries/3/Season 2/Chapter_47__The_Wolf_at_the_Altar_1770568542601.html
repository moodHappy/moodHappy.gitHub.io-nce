Chapter 47: The Wolf at the Altar

第四十七章：祭坛上的狼

Three armored trucks smashed through the vegetation. They surrounded the ruins.

三辆装甲卡车撞穿植被。包围了废墟。

The Butcher stepped out. He wore a white suit, mocking the jungle's dirt. His broken finger was bandaged in purple silk.

屠夫走了出来。他穿着白西装，嘲弄着丛林的泥土。他断掉的手指包着紫色的丝绸。

"Mrs. Tanaka," he walked into the church, flanked by ten heavily armed guards. "You look... diminished."

“田中太太，”他走进教堂，两边跟着十个全副武装的卫兵。“你看起来……消瘦了。”

Miyetsuko fell to her knees. She bowed her head, her hair covering her face.

美悦子跪倒在地。她低下头，头发遮住了脸。

"Please..." she held up a USB drive. "Take it. Let us go."

“求你……”她举起一个U盘。“拿去。放我们走。”

The Butcher laughed. He walked up to the altar, looming over her. He grabbed her chin with his good hand, forcing her to look up.

屠夫大笑。他走到祭坛前，笼罩着她。用完好的那只手捏住她的下巴，强迫她抬头。

"Let you go?" he sneered, his eyes roaming over her red dress, lingering on the sweat-soaked fabric clinging to her breasts. "After you broke my finger? No. I'm going to keep you. I have a cage for you in Medellin."

“放你走？”他冷笑，目光游走在她的红裙上，停留在紧贴乳房的湿透布料上。“在你折断我的手指之后？不。我要留着你。我在麦德林有个笼子专门给你留着。”

He reached for the USB drive.

他伸手去拿U盘。

"Where is the boy? The finger-breaker?"

“那个小子呢？那个折手指的？”

"Dead," Miyetsuko whispered. "Fever. In the jungle."

“死了，”美悦子低语。“发烧。在丛林里。”

"Pity. I wanted to skin him," The Butcher grabbed the drive. "Grab her."

“可惜。我还想剥了他的皮，”屠夫抓过U盘。“抓住她。”

Two guards moved forward to seize her arms.

两个卫兵上前抓她的胳膊。

Miyetsuko looked up. Her eyes weren't fearful. They were burning with a dark, manic joy.

美悦子抬起头。她的眼睛里没有恐惧。燃烧着一种黑暗、疯狂的快乐。

"He's not dead," she whispered. "He's the lightning."

“他没死，”她低语。“他是闪电。”

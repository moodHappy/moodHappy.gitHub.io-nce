Chapter 6: The Scorpion’s Den

发布时间：2026/6/21 17:51:47

第六章：蝎子的巢穴

The safehouse in Ciudad Juárez was a brutalist concrete bunker disguised as an abandoned textile factory on the edge of the industrial zone. Outside, the desert wind swirled grey dust against the rusted iron gates; inside, it was a sterile sanctuary of blue monitors and high-end security grids.

华雷斯城的安全屋是一座野兽派风格的混凝土堡垒，伪装成工业区边缘一家废弃的纺织厂。门外，沙漠的风卷着灰色的尘土刮擦着生锈的铁门；门内，则是一个由蓝色显示器和高端安保网络构成的无菌避难所。

"The city is a panopticon, Miyetsuko," Chen Mo said, tapping a key that brought up live feeds from forty different traffic cameras around the Pronaf district—the high-end commercial zone of Juárez. "I’ve intercepted El Alacrán’s communication nodes. He knows Alejandro is here, but he thinks we’re hiding in the slums."

“这座城市是个全景敞视建筑，美悦子，”陈默敲击按键，调出了普罗纳夫区（华雷斯城的高端商业区）周围四十个不同交通摄像头的实时画面，“我拦截了‘蝎子’的通讯节点。他知道亚历杭德罗在这里，但他以为我们躲在贫民窟里。”

Miyetsuko stood before a full-length mirror, adjusting the silk collar of a deep crimson blouse that clung to her chest like spilled wine. She threw the heavy leather jacket over her shoulders, the dark fabric hiding the Golden Derringer strapped to her inner thigh.

美悦子站在全身镜前，整理着一件深绯色丝绸衬衫的衣领，那衣服紧贴在她的胸前，宛如洒出的红酒。她把沉重的皮大衣披在肩上，深色的布料遮住了绑在大腿内侧的金色德林加。

"Then let’s correct his mistake," she whispered, her smile reflecting in the glass. "I didn't come to Mexico to breathe dust in a basement, Chen. I came to take a bank."

“那就来纠正他的错误吧，”她低声呢喃，笑容倒映在镜子里，“陈，我来墨西哥不是为了在地下室里吃土的。我是来夺取一家银行的。”

An hour later, the black Suburban pulled up to the valet of La Presidencia, the most exclusive lounge in Juárez, where politicians and cartel bosses traded favors over glasses of ultra-premium tequila.

一小时后，黑色萨博班停在了La Presidencia的门童前。这是华雷斯城最奢华的会所，政客与卡特尔大佬们在这里一边端着特级龙舌兰，一边进行着利益交换。

Miyetsuko stepped into the crystal-lit room, her bare shoulders catching the glow of the chandeliers, her presence a silent detonation that halted the low murmur of conversation. Chen Mo walked a step behind her, his eyes dead, his hand resting beneath his linen jacket—a human weapon in a room full of predators.

美悦子步入水晶灯闪烁的大厅，赤裸的双肩捕捉着吊灯的光芒，她的出现宛如一场无声的爆炸，让低沉的交谈声戛然而止。陈默跟在她身后一步之遥，眼神毫无生机，手停在亚麻夹克衫内——在一间塞满猎食者的屋子里，他就是一件人形兵器。

She slid into a VIP booth, ordering three glasses of Cristal. Minutes later, the heavy oak doors of the lounge opened, and a man surrounded by four scarred sicarios walked in. He wore a heavy gold chain over a black button-down, his eyes narrow and venomous.

她滑入高级卡座，点了三杯克里斯塔尔香槟。几分钟后，会所沉重的橡木大门被推开，一个被四名满是伤疤的杀手簇拥着的男人走了进来。他黑色衬衫外挂着粗大的金项链，眼神阴鸷如毒蛇。

El Alacrán.

埃尔·阿拉克兰。

He didn't look at the crowd; he walked straight toward Miyetsuko's table, his boots thudding against the polished floor. He stopped at the edge of the booth, his gaze dropping to the swell of her breasts beneath the crimson silk before rising to meet her cold, black eyes.

“蝎子”。

他没有看旁人，径直朝美悦子的桌子走去，皮靴在擦得锃亮的地板上发出沉闷的声响。他在卡座边停下，目光先是落在她绯色丝绸下起伏的胸口，随后抬起，迎上她那双冰冷、漆黑的眼眸。

"You are a long way from Tokyo, gringa," the Scorpion hissed, leaning his hands on the marble table. "And you have something that belongs to me."

“你离东京可够远的，外国人，”蝎子嘶声说道，双手撑在大理石桌面上，“而且你手里有属于我的东西。”

Miyetsuko didn't flinch. She picked up her glass, the crystal catching the light as she took a slow, deliberate sip.

美悦子没有退缩。她端起酒杯，水晶在光线下闪烁，她缓慢而蓄意地抿了一口。

"I have everything that belongs to you, Alacrán," she whispered, her voice carrying the absolute authority of the Shadow Network. "The accounts, the routes, the names of the senators you buy. Alejandro is mine now. The ledger is mine now. And if you want to keep breathing the air of Juárez, you will pay me 40% of every dollar that crosses the bridge."

“我拥有属于你的一切，阿拉克兰，”她低声说道，声音里带着暗影网络绝对的权威，“账户、路线、还有你收买的参议员的名字。亚历杭德罗现在是我的了。账本现在也是我的了。如果你还想继续呼吸华雷斯城的空气，过桥的每一美元，你都要分我40%。”

The silence in the lounge became heavy, suffocating. The Scorpion’s hand slowly drifted toward the small of his back.

会所里的寂静变得沉重、令人窒息。蝎子的手，开始缓缓向他的后腰移去。

Chapter 7: The Mexican Standoff

发布时间：2026/6/21 17:55:08

第七章：墨西哥式的对峙

The tension in the VIP booth was a living thing, a coiled snake ready to strike.

包厢里的紧张气氛化作了实质，像一条盘踞的毒蛇，随时准备发动致命一击。

The Scorpion’s fingers were inches from the grip of his pistol when he felt it—a sharp, cold point pressing directly against the soft tissue of his lower ribs. Chen Mo had materialized behind him, his movements so fluid they seemed to defy human reaction time.

“蝎子”的手指距离枪柄仅有几英寸，就在这时，他感觉到了——一个尖锐、冰冷的点直接抵住了他下肋的软组织。陈默如幽灵般出现在他身后，动作流畅得仿佛违背了人类的反应极限。

"Don't," Chen Mo whispered, his breath a chilling breeze against the gangster's ear. "The knife is ceramic, El Alacrán. It doesn't trip metal detectors, but it cuts deeper than you can imagine."

“别动，”陈默低语道，他的呼吸吹在匪徒耳边，带着刺骨的寒意。“那是陶瓷短刀，“蝎子”。它不会触发金属探测器，但切开你身体的深度会超乎你的想象。”

Meanwhile, beneath the table, Miyetsuko’s movements were just as lethal. She didn't need to brandish the Golden Derringer; she simply angled it upward, the gilded muzzle pressed firmly against the Scorpion’s groin, shielded from the view of the surrounding guards by the heavy marble edge.

与此同时，桌子下方，美悦子的动作同样致命。她无需挥舞金色德林加，只需将其微微上抬，镀金的枪口就稳稳抵住了“蝎子”的裆部，沉重的大理石桌面完美地遮挡了四周保镖的视线。

"You were saying something about 'belonging'?" Miyetsuko asked, her voice light, almost mocking. Her eyes, however, were as dark and void of light as the deep sea.

“你刚才说谁的东西？”美悦子问道，声音轻快得近乎戏谑。但她的眼睛却如深海般漆黑，不见一丝光亮。

She leaned forward, her red blouse straining at the buttons as she maintained the pressure of the gun barrel. "If you try to draw, my friend, you won't have a future, let alone a cartel. I don't care about your guns. I don't care about your reputation. I only care about the 40%."

她向前倾身，绯色衬衫的纽扣因张力紧绷，而她始终维持着枪管的压迫力。“如果你想掏枪，我的朋友，你将不会再有未来，更不用提你的卡特尔了。我不在乎你的枪，也不在乎你的名声。我只在乎那40%。”

The Scorpion’s face drained of color, his bravado crumbling. He looked into Miyetsuko’s eyes and saw not a woman, but a bottomless, hungry abyss. He realized that for her, human life—even his own—was just another ledger entry to be balanced.

“蝎子”的脸色惨白，傲慢荡然无存。他凝视着美悦子的双眼，看到的不是一个女人，而是一个深不见底、贪婪的深渊。他终于意识到，对她而言，人类的生命——哪怕是他自己的——也只不过是账本上一行待处理的数字。

"Fine," he croaked, his voice barely audible over the thump of the club music. "40%. But if I see a single fed or a rival assassin near my shipments... I’ll burn this whole city to the ground before I let you have a penny."

“好，”他嘶哑着嗓子说，声音几乎被迪厅的重音掩盖，“40%。但如果我发现有任何一个联邦探员或者竞争对手出现在我的货运线附近……哪怕要烧掉整座城市，我也不会让你拿到一分钱。”

Miyetsuko pulled the gun back, the tension in the booth instantly dissipating into a cold, transactional silence. She patted the Scorpion’s cheek—a gesture that felt like a slap.

美悦子收回了枪，包厢内的紧张瞬间消散，转为一种冷酷的商业沉默。她拍了拍“蝎子”的脸颊——那个动作更像是一记耳光。

"Don't threaten me with fire, Alacrán. I am the one who lit the match."

“别拿火威胁我，阿拉克兰。我才是那个点火的人。”

As the Scorpion stumbled away, his guards flank-walking him with eyes full of confusion and rage, Miyetsuko took another sip of her drink. Her hand was steady, her pulse unfazed.

当“蝎子”踉跄着离开，保镖们满脸困惑与愤怒地簇拥着他时，美悦子又抿了一口酒。她的手稳如泰山，心跳如初。

"We have our first foothold, Chen," she said, watching the door close. "Now, for the next ten chapters, we bleed him dry. And while he's distracted, we start planning the evacuation."

“我们站稳了第一步，陈，”她看着大门关上，说道，“接下来的十个章节，我们要榨干他。趁他分心，我们开始策划撤离。”

"To New York?" Chen Mo asked, leaning into her space, his presence a comforting darkness.

“去纽约？”陈默问，身体前倾靠近她，那股熟悉的存在感化作一种慰藉的黑暗。

"To the center of the world," she replied, her gaze drifting toward the north, toward the jagged border that led to the heart of the empire. "New York is where the final account settles."

“去世界的中心，”她回答，目光望向北方，望向那条通往帝国心脏的崎岖边界。“纽约，才是最终清算的地方。”

Chapter 20: Prowling in the Snow

发布时间：2026/6/23 19:04:46

第二十章：冰雪中的潜行

Leaving the freezing apartment behind, Miyetsuko and Chen Mo descended the dimly lit, creaking stairwell of their building.

离开冰冷的公寓后，美悦子与陈默走下昏暗、吱呀作响的楼梯间。

They moved with the practiced silence of ghosts, their heavy winter coats doing little to ward off the biting chill that had permeated the peeling hallways.

他们如同幽灵般无声移动，厚重的冬衣几乎无法抵御已经渗入剥落走廊的刺骨寒意。

Stepping out into the harsh New York night, the frigid wind immediately assaulted them, carrying the sharp sting of ice crystals.

踏入严酷的纽约夜色中，寒风立刻袭来，夹杂着冰晶的锋利刺痛。

They quickly crossed the slush-covered street to where their unassuming, secondhand sedan was parked, its windows coated in a thin layer of frost that Chen Mo had to rapidly scrape away with methodical, urgent strokes.

他们迅速穿过泥泞与冰雪覆盖的街道，来到那辆不起眼的二手轿车前，车窗已覆上一层薄霜，陈默必须以冷静而急促的动作迅速刮除。

The drive toward Midtown Manhattan was a slow, cautious navigation through the snow-dusted avenues.

驶向曼哈顿中城的路途，是在覆雪街道上谨慎而缓慢的穿行。

Outside their windows, the city was alive with an oblivious, almost festive energy.

车窗外，这座城市充满一种毫无察觉、近乎节日般的活力。

Neon signs bled into the falling snow, and bundled-up pedestrians hurried past glowing storefronts, completely unaware of the lethal apex predators hiding in plain sight among them.

霓虹灯在飞雪中晕染开来，裹得严实的行人匆匆掠过灯火通明的店铺，全然不知致命的顶级捕食者正隐藏在人群之中。

For a brief moment, Miyetsuko allowed herself to lean against the cold glass, absorbing the deceptive warmth of the city lights.

短暂的一瞬间，美悦子靠在冰冷车窗上，吸收着城市灯光带来的虚假暖意。

It was a perfect camouflage, yet the underlying anxiety of the invisible net closing in on them kept her instincts sharp and her muscles tightly coiled.

这是一层完美的伪装，但那张无形之网不断收紧的潜在焦虑，始终让她保持警觉、肌肉紧绷。

As they finally neared the bustling vicinity of Grand Central Station, the traffic thickened, forcing them to a crawl.

当他们接近中央车站繁忙区域时，车流骤然加密，迫使车辆缓慢爬行。

The majestic, illuminated facade of the terminal loomed ahead like a beacon in the winter storm.

车站宏伟而明亮的外立面，在风雪中如灯塔般矗立前方。

Chen Mo maneuvered the sedan into a shadowy parking spot along a crowded side street, scanning the rearview mirrors with his habitual paranoia.

陈默将车驶入一条拥挤支路的阴影停车位，习惯性地透过后视镜警惕扫描四周。

The plan was simple: slip into the crowded terminal, find a discreet vendor for hot soup, and dissolve into the chaos of the commuters.

计划很简单：混入拥挤的车站大厅，找一个不起眼的摊位喝热汤，然后彻底消失在人群之中。

But as the engine died and silence returned to the cabin, the heavy, unspoken tension between them lingered, setting the stage for the shadows to finally reveal themselves.

但当引擎熄灭、车内重归寂静时，那种沉重而无言的紧张仍然悬在空气中，为黑暗中潜伏的存在揭开帷幕。

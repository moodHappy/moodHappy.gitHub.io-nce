Chapter 43: The Ghost in the Cell

第四十三章：牢房里的幽灵

Chen Mo wet a cloth with water from a canteen and began to wipe the soot and dried sweat from her back. The water was cold. She shivered, her skin bumping.

陈默用水壶里的水沾湿一块布，开始擦拭她背上的烟灰和干汗。水很冷。她打了个寒颤，皮肤起了鸡皮疙瘩。

"The dream..." she started, her voice echoing in the concrete box. "It wasn't just a memory, Chen."

“那个梦……”她开口了，声音在混凝土盒子里回荡。“那不仅仅是回忆，陈。”

Chen Mo stopped wiping. His hand rested on her bruised shoulder.

陈默停止了擦拭。他的手停在她淤青的肩膀上。

"I saw him," she turned around, ignoring her nakedness, her eyes locking onto his. "Not the Kurosaki from seven years ago. The Kurosaki of now. In his cell."

“我看见他了，”她转过身，无视自己的赤裸，眼神锁住他的。“不是七年前的黑崎。是现在的黑崎。在他的牢房里。”

"He is smiling, Chen. He is sitting on his bunk, smiling at the wall. Because he knows."

“他在笑，陈。他坐在铺位上，对着墙笑。因为他知道。”

"Knows what?"

“知道什么？”

"He knows he's not dead. He knows that as long as he breathes, even in a hole in Tokyo, he owns a piece of me. That night... he planted a seed of fear that won't die until the host dies."

“他知道他没死。他知道只要他还呼吸，哪怕是在东京的一个洞里，他就依然占有我的一部分。那一夜……他种下了一颗恐惧的种子，宿主不死，种子不灭。”

She grabbed Chen Mo's hand and placed it on her throat.

她抓起陈默的手，按在自己的喉咙上。

"I need to cut it out. I need to go back."

“我得把它切除。我得回去。”

Chen Mo looked at her. He understood. This wasn't about strategy. It wasn't about survival. It was an exorcism.

陈默看着她。他懂了。这无关战略。无关生存。这是一场驱魔。

"Tokyo Maximum Security Prison," Chen Mo whispered. "It's a fortress. Breaking in is suicide."

“东京最高安全监狱，”陈默低语。“那是一座堡垒。闯进去就是自杀。”

"We don't break in," a cold smile touched her lips, contrasting with the vulnerability of her naked body. "We get invited. Or we become ghosts."

“我们不硬闯，”一抹冷笑浮上她的嘴唇，与她赤裸身体的脆弱形成对比。“我们要被邀请进去。或者变成幽灵进去。”

"Not now," Chen Mo brushed a strand of wet hair from her face. "Now, we survive Rio. But I promise you... we will send him to hell. Personally."

“不是现在，”陈默拨开她脸上的一缕湿发。“现在，我们得在里约活下去。但我向你保证……我们会送他下地狱。亲手送。”

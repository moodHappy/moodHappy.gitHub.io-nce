Chapter 126: The Persistence of the Nurse

发布时间：2026/6/17 07:11:20

第一百二十六章：护士的“补救申请”

Miyetsuko stood by the wall, clutching her stomach as laughter bubbled up, a stark contrast to Fujiwara’s pale, sickly complexion. She had pieced together the scene: the stolen moments, the sudden exit, and the lingering, unmistakable scent of... nature that hung in the air.

美悦子靠在墙边，捂着肚子笑得停不下来，这与藤原那惨白、虚弱的神色形成了鲜明对比。她已经拼凑出了刚才发生的一切：那些偷来的暧昧时刻、突然的落荒而逃，还有空气中那股若有若无、极其微妙的……“自然气息”。

Just as Fujiwara was desperately trying to compose himself, the nurse—clearly not one to be deterred by a bit of gastrointestinal misfortune—approached them. She looked genuinely determined, her eyes locked on Fujiwara with a sincerity that was as baffling as it was bold.

正当藤原拼命试图镇定下来时，那位小护士——显然并不打算被那一丁点肠胃上的“小插曲”劝退——竟走了过来。她看起来神情坚定，目光紧紧锁住藤原，那份诚恳既让人迷惑又显得格外大胆。

"Mr. Fujiwara," she said, ignoring Miyetsuko entirely. "Please, give me another chance. What happened just now... it was truly just a tiny, unfortunate accident. My stomach isn't usually so... expressive."

“藤原先生，”她完全无视了美悦子，直直地说道，“请再给我一次机会。刚才发生的……真的只是个小小的、不幸的意外。我的胃平时并没有这么……‘有表现力’。”

Miyetsuko’s laughter died instantly. Her jaw dropped. She looked at the nurse, then at Fujiwara, who looked like he wanted the hospital floor to swallow him whole.

美悦子的笑声戛然而止。她惊得下巴都快掉了。她看看护士，又看看藤原，此时的藤原看起来恨不得地上裂个缝让他钻进去。

"An... accident?" Miyetsuko repeated, her voice dipping into a dangerously sweet, honeyed tone. She turned her gaze to her husband, who was currently staring intensely at his own shoes. "Well, Fujiwara, darling? It seems you have a 'second chance' awaiting you. Are you going to take it?"

“意外？”美悦子重复道，声音变得危险而甜腻。她转头看向丈夫，而此时的藤原正全神贯注地盯着自己的鞋尖。“怎么啦，藤原亲爱的？看来这儿有个人在等你给个‘第二次机会’呢。你要把握住吗？”

Fujiwara let out a strangled, high-pitched laugh, stepping instinctively behind Miyetsuko. "No, no, thank you! I’ve had... quite enough excitement for one day. My stomach, much like yours, is currently in a state of 'emergency management'."

藤原发出一声压抑的尖笑，本能地躲到美悦子身后。“不，不用了，谢谢！我今天……受到的刺激已经足够多了。我的胃，和你的一样，现在正处于‘紧急管理状态’。”

The nurse looked disappointed but undeterred. "The offer stands, sir. I’m off in an hour."

护士看起来有些失望，但并不死心。“那个提议依然有效，先生。我一小时后下班。”

As the nurse walked away, Miyetsuko folded her arms across her chest and smirked at Fujiwara. "You certainly have a way with... gassy women, don't you? Is this the 'plump and round' charm you were so obsessed with earlier?"

护士走远后，美悦子交叉双臂，斜睨着藤原。“你在……容易胀气的女性中间还真是挺受欢迎的，不是吗？这就是你刚才心心念念的那些‘丰满圆润’的魅力？”

"I'm going to jump out a window," Fujiwara groaned, hiding his face in his hands.

“我干脆跳窗算了，”藤原痛苦地低吟，把脸埋进手里。

"Oh, no you don't," she teased, tugging him toward the exit. "You're coming home with me to change that diaper, remember? And I think you owe me a very long, very detailed explanation about how 'expressive' your future dates might be."

“哦，那可不行，”她调侃着，拽着他往出口走，“你得跟我回家换尿布，还记得吗？而且我觉得，你欠我一个非常漫长、非常详尽的解释——关于你未来的约会对象，到底还有多少这种‘表现力’。”

[Learning Point]

Gastrointestinal misfortune: 肠胃上的不幸（委婉说法）。

Expression: 表现力（此处指排气）。

Strangled, high-pitched laugh: 压抑的尖笑。

Emergency management: 紧急管理。

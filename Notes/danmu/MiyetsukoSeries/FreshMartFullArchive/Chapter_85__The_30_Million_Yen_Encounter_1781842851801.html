Chapter 85: The 30 Million Yen Encounter

发布时间：2026/6/19 12:20:50

第八十五章：三千万日元的“服务”

Fujiwara sat in the dimly lit office, his fingers hovering over the screen of his phone. It was a bizarre, almost morbid curiosity that drove him to the local escort platform. He wanted to understand Aya better—to see what kind of "business" his sister-in-law was actually running.

藤原坐在昏暗的办公室里，手指悬在手机屏幕上方。那是一种近乎病态的好奇心，驱使他点开了本地的援交平台。他想更了解绫——看看他的这位小姨子到底在经营着什么样的“生意”。

When her profile loaded, his heart skipped a beat, then plummeted.

当她的个人资料加载出来时，他的心跳先是漏了一拍，随即沉入谷底。

Fee: 30,000,000 JPY / session.

服务费：30,000,000 日元/次。

"Thirty million?" he whispered, the number ringing in his ears like a gunshot. That wasn't just pocket money. That was enough to buy the entire "Fresh Mart"—inventory, fixtures, and the building itself. "What kind of service justifies a price tag higher than my entire life's inventory?"

“三千万？”他低声喃喃，这数字像枪声一样在耳边炸开。那可不只是零花钱，那足以买下整个“新鲜马特”——包括存货、货架和整个楼房。“什么样级别的服务，能卖到比我这辈子所有家当加起来还贵的价格？”

He was stunned, a mix of shock and a weird, distorted form of pride. Aya wasn't just "turning tricks"; she was operating in a financial stratosphere he hadn't even imagined.

他惊呆了，内心混合着震惊与一种扭曲的自豪感。绫这哪是在做那种下九流的生意；她根本是在他连想都不敢想的金融平流层里搞钱。

He clicked CONFIRM.

他点击了确认。

[The Night of the Encounter / 那个夜晚]

When she arrived, Aya didn't look like a girl selling her time; she looked like an empress reclaiming her territory. She wore a tailored suit that probably cost more than Fujiwara’s annual profit, and her gaze was as cold as a laser.

当她敲门时，绫看起来根本不像是个出卖时间的女孩；她像个巡视领地的女皇。她穿着一身剪裁精良的西装，那身行头估计比藤原全年的利润还贵，眼神像激光一样冰冷。

"Thirty million, Fujiwara-san," she said, stepping inside without waiting for an invitation. "You’re paying for a standard of quality that doesn't exist in your neighborhood."

“三千万，藤原先生，”她没等邀请就迈进屋子，“你付出的代价，买到的是你在你这片街区根本见识不到的质量水准。”

She began the service with a terrifying level of precision. It wasn't "sex" in the clumsy, wet, storage-room sense; it was choreography. She dismantled him, piece by piece, her touch so calculated it felt like she was dissecting his nerve endings.

她以一种近乎恐怖的精准度开始了服务。这不再是那种笨拙的、湿漉漉的仓库式肉体接触；这是一场编排好的舞剧。她将他一点点拆解，触碰得极具计算感，仿佛在解剖他的每一根神经末梢。

She didn't moan; she didn't feign pleasure. She orchestrated it. She led him through a sensory landscape that shifted from icy, clinical detachment to a fever pitch of intensity that made him feel like he was losing his mind.

她没有呻吟，也没有假装快感，她在掌控一切。她引导着他穿过一片感官的荒原，从冰冷的临床式抽离，瞬间跃迁至狂热的巅峰，让他觉得自己彻底疯了。

"This is the difference," she whispered, her voice a sharp, commanding rasp against his ear as she took him in. "You play house with your little family. I master the architecture of desire."

“这就是区别，”她低语，声音带着一种锐利、专制的嘶哑在他耳边回荡，将他彻底吞没。“你和你的小家庭玩过家家，而我，我才是欲望建筑的真正主人。”

The experience was visceral, expensive, and devastatingly efficient. When it was over, Fujiwara felt hollowed out, as if she had extracted his very essence and left him staring at the ceiling, breathless and utterly humbled.

那次经历深刻、昂贵，且致命地高效。结束后，藤原感觉自己被掏空了，仿佛她抽走了他的灵魂，只留下他一个人瘫在那里盯着天花板，气喘吁吁，彻底被折服。

[Learning Point]

Morbid: 病态的。

Stratosphere: 平流层（比喻极高的层次）。

Choreography: 编舞/精心编排。

Visceral: 发自肺腑的/本能的。

Humbled: 谦卑的/被折服的。

Chapter 68: The Cruel Joke

第六十八章：致命的玩笑

The air in the cramped van felt heavy, thick with the scent of sex and the lingering heat of their tangled bodies. A thick, pearly glob of Fujiwara’s seed glistened on Rin’s stomach, stark against her pale, porcelain skin.

狭窄货车里的空气沉闷而浑浊，交织着欢愉过后的汗味与余温。一抹浓稠的乳白色液体在铃的腹部晶莹闪烁，在她那白瓷般的肌肤上显得格外刺眼。

Fujiwara lay back, his breath finally evening out, a look of profound satisfaction on his face. But the silence that followed wasn't peaceful; it was punctured by a sudden, chilling giggle from Rin.

藤原仰躺着，呼吸终于平复，脸上挂着极度的满足。但随之而来的沉默并不宁静，反而被铃突然发出的一阵令人毛骨悚然的轻笑打破了。

"You know, Fujiwara-san," she said, her voice eerily calm and serious as she traced his chest with a fingertip. "You’re done for. I’m HIV positive."

“你知道吗，藤原先生，”她说着，指尖在他胸口画圈，声音异常冷静且认真，“你完了。我有艾滋病。”

Fujiwara’s heart stopped. Every muscle in his body went rigid. The humor of the situation evaporated, replaced by a cold, sharp dread that spiked through his blood. "Impossible," he hissed, his voice trembling. "You're... you're a professional. You're clean."

藤原的心脏漏跳了一拍，浑身肌肉紧绷。方才的温存荡然无存，一股冰冷、尖锐的恐惧瞬间穿透了他的血液。“不可能，”他嘶声说道，声音发颤，“你是……你是专业的。你是干净的。”

Rin sat up, her face a mask of cold, terrifying composure. "I didn't get it from a customer. I injected the virus into my own blood years ago. I wanted to see if I could live with a ticking time bomb inside me."

铃坐起身，脸上的表情冷峻得令人战栗。“我不是从客人那儿传染的。几年前，我往自己血液里注射过病毒。我想看看，带着一颗体内的定时炸弹，我能不能活下去。”

The silence that followed was suffocating. Fujiwara felt his ears ringing. His mind raced—the store, Sayuri’s pregnancy, Miyetsuko, his own life—it all felt like it was collapsing into a black hole.

接下来的沉默让人窒息。藤原耳边嗡嗡作响。他的大脑飞速运转——超市、小百合的孕期、美悦子，还有他自己的命——这一切仿佛正在崩塌进一个黑洞。

Then, Rin broke.

突然，铃“破功”了。

She dissolved into a fit of laughter, shaking so hard she nearly fell off the seat. It was a wild, floral, hysterical sound—she was laughing until she couldn't breathe, clutching her sides.

她彻底崩坏了，爆发出一阵狂笑，笑得浑身发抖，差点从座位上滚下来。那是种狂野的、花枝乱颤的、歇斯底里的笑声——她笑得喘不过气，死死捂着肚子。

"Oh, Fujiwara-san, you should see your face!" she gasped, tears of mirth streaming down her cheeks. "I'm kidding! My god, the look in your eyes! I’ve never been sick a day in my life."

“噢，藤原先生，你该看看自己那副表情！”她喘息着，笑出的泪珠顺着脸颊滑落，“开玩笑的啦！我的天，你那眼神！我这辈子连感冒都没得过几次。”

Fujiwara stared at her, his pulse slowly returning to a rhythmic beat, though his hands were still clammy with sweat. The relief was so intense it almost felt like another kind of pain.

藤原瞪着她，心跳逐渐恢复了节律，尽管手心里全是冷汗。这股如释重负的感觉太过强烈，甚至让他感到另一种形式的疼痛。

Rin quieted down, her expression shifting from mischief to something soft, heavy, and profound. She leaned in, her sightless eyes fixed somewhere near his heart.

铃安静了下来，表情从恶作剧转为某种柔软、沉重而深邃的神情。她凑近他，盲目的双眼似乎看向他心脏的位置。

"But listen to me," she whispered, her voice dropping into a register of absolute, terrifying sincerity. "I grew up in a world where everyone looked away because of my eyes. I made a vow: the first man who pierced my veil—who took my virginity, who touched me not as a 'blind girl' but as a woman—he would own everything I am. My body, my life, my darkness, and my light. That man is you, Fujiwara-san. I am yours. For good."

“但你听我说，”她低语，声音转为一种绝对的、令人心颤的真诚，“我在一个所有人都因为我的眼睛而选择无视我的世界里长大。我立过誓：第一个刺破我面纱的男人——那个夺走我贞洁，那个不把我当作‘瞎眼女孩’而是作为一个女人来触碰的人——他将拥有我的一切。我的身体，我的命，我的黑暗，还有我的光明。那个人就是你，藤原先生。从今往后，我就是你的人了。永远。”

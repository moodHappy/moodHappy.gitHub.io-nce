Chapter 81: The Unraveling Diploma

发布时间：2026/6/19 10:56:12

第八十一章：毕业礼上的“坦诚”

The university auditorium was a sea of black gowns and stifling academic pride. Fujiwara sat in the back row, his arm linked with Rin’s. She was dressed in a soft, modest summer dress, her sightless eyes tilted toward the stage, listening to the rhythmic drone of names being called.

大学礼堂里满是黑色的学士袍，空气中弥漫着令人窒息的学术自豪感。藤原坐在后排，挽着铃的手臂。她穿着一件温柔素雅的夏裙，失明的双眼朝向舞台，聆听着那抑扬顿挫的名字诵读声。

"Is Aya-san’s name close?" Rin whispered, her voice barely audible over the clapping.

“绫小姐的名字快到了吗？”铃轻声问道，掌声掩盖了她的声音。

"Not yet," Fujiwara murmured, scanning the crowd. He was here for her sister, but the atmosphere felt... heavy. There was an undercurrent of tension, a nervous energy vibrating through the wooden floorboards.

“还没呢，”藤原低语，扫视着人群。他是陪绫来的，但气氛总觉得……有些压抑。地板下仿佛暗涌着一股紧张的、颤动的能量。

Suddenly, the drone of the ceremony shattered.

突然，仪式的单调节奏被彻底击碎了。

A girl in the third row, her cap askew and her face tear-stained, leaped onto the stage. She wasn't holding a diploma. She was holding a stack of papers that she threw into the air like confetti.

第三排的一个女生，学士帽歪斜，脸上挂着泪痕，猛地冲上了舞台。她手里没拿毕业证书，而是一叠纸，像撒彩带一样扬向空中。

"You call this justice?!" she shrieked, her voice echoing off the vaulted ceilings. "I’ve spent four years in this hell, and you fail me on a technicality?"

“你们管这叫正义？！”她尖叫着，声音在拱形天花板间回荡。“我在这个地狱里混了四年，最后竟然因为一个技术性问题不让我毕业？”

The guards moved to intercept her, but she was faster. In a single, fluid motion—as if she’d rehearsed the defiance a thousand times in her head—she ripped the gown from her shoulders. Then the blouse. Then the skirt.

保安冲上去想要阻拦，但她更快。动作流畅至极——仿佛她在脑海里已经排演过无数次这种反抗——她一把扯掉肩上的学士袍。接着是衬衫。然后是裙子。

The auditorium went dead silent.

礼堂瞬间死寂。

The girl stood there, completely, unapologetically naked. She was breathtaking—a body of pale, porcelain skin, high, firm breasts, and long, slender legs that looked like they belonged in a Renaissance painting. Her beauty was so sharp, so pure, that it rendered the entire audience motionless. It was a grotesque, jarring contrast: the fierce, unhinged rage in her eyes and the delicate, flawless anatomy of a goddess.

女孩站在台上，彻底地、毫无歉意地赤裸着。她美得惊人——一身白瓷般的肌肤，挺拔傲人的乳房，修长匀称的双腿，仿佛是从文艺复兴的画作中走出来的。她的美是如此锋利、纯粹，让全场观众屏住了呼吸。那是一种怪诞而刺眼的对比：她眼中那股凶猛、失控的愤怒，与她身上那具精致、完美如女神般的躯体，形成了巨大的冲击。

Fujiwara found himself leaning forward, his breath hitching. Even Rin, who could see none of it, seemed to sense the shift in the air—the sharp intake of breath from hundreds of students, the sudden, humid temperature spike in the room.

藤原不由自主地前倾身体，呼吸一滞。就连什么都看不见的铃似乎也察觉到了空气的变化——数百名学生同时倒吸一口凉气，房间里的气温陡然变得燥热起来。

"Fujiwara-san," Rin whispered, her voice tight, picking up on the frantic heartbeat of the room. "Why is everyone so quiet? Did someone die?"

“藤原先生，”铃低声说，声音紧绷，她感知到了全场狂乱的心跳。“为什么大家都这么安静？是有人死了吗？”

Fujiwara couldn't take his eyes off the stage. "No," he muttered, his pulse racing with a mix of shock and low-brow fascination. "Someone is just... shedding their skin."

藤原目不转睛地盯着舞台。“不，”他喃喃道，心中混合着震惊与某种下流的着迷，心跳加速。“有人只是……在脱掉自己的外壳而已。”

[Learning Point]

Stifling: 令人窒息的。

Undercurrent: 暗流。

Vaulted: 拱形的。

Unapologetically: 毫无歉意的。

Shedding: 脱落（指脱皮/脱壳）。

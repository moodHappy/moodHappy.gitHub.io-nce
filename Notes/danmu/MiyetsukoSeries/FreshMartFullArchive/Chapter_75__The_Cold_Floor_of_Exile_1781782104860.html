Chapter 75: The Cold Floor of Exile

发布时间：2026/6/18 19:28:22

第七十五章：走廊上的放逐者

The hotel room door clicked shut with a finality that echoed like a guillotine blade dropping. Fujiwara stood in the narrow, dimly lit corridor, clad only in a pair of crumpled boxers, clutching a single, sad pillow he’d managed to snag before being evicted.

酒店房门发出清脆的响声，合上的瞬间仿佛断头台闸刀落下。藤原站在狭窄昏暗的走廊里，身上只穿着那条褶皱的平角裤，手里紧紧攥着刚才被扫地出门前抢出来的、那个孤零零的枕头。

He stared at the mahogany door, waiting for a reprieve, but there was only silence. Not even a giggle.

他盯着那扇红木门，等待着赦免，然而里面只有一片死寂。连一声轻笑都没有。

"Rin? It was a rounding error! I slapped the third one, it was just... it was just a light tap!" he hissed into the keyhole.

“铃？那是四舍五入的误差！我拍了第三下，只是……只是力道轻了点而已！”他对着锁孔嘶声哀求。

Silence.

依旧死寂。

He slumped against the wall and slid down to the carpeted floor. He was a prisoner of his own bureaucracy. Ever since the "Family Stability Act" was codified—a set of rules designed to prevent domestic anarchy—the boundaries were ironclad. No one was allowed to enter another's room once the "nighttime assignments" were logged. By trying to force his way back in, he’d only risk a total lockdown of all rooms.

他瘫靠在墙上，顺着滑到了地毯上。他成了自己所制定的官僚主义的阶下囚。自从“家庭稳定法”——那套旨在防止家庭无政府状态的条例——生效以来，边界就变得如同钢铁般坚硬。一旦“夜间分配”记录在案，任何人都不得跨入他人的房间。如果他现在硬闯，只会导致所有房间彻底封锁。

He was, quite literally, out in the cold.

他现在，真正意义上被冷落在外了。

A few minutes later, the door to the adjacent room creaked open. Miyetsuko peered out, her eyes scanning the corridor. She saw him sitting on the floor, looking like a discarded piece of laundry.

几分钟后，隔壁房间的门吱呀一声开了。美悦子探出头，目光扫过走廊。她看见他坐在地上，活像一团被丢弃的脏衣服。

"Fujiwara," she said, her voice dripping with mock sympathy. "You’re breaking the protocol. You know the rules: once the door is locked, the room is a sovereign state. You're currently trespassing on international soil."

“藤原，”她语气里带着假惺惺的同情，“你正在违反协议。你知道规矩：门一锁，房间就是主权国家。你现在正非法闯入国际领土。”

"Miyetsuko, be a dear," he pleaded, reaching out a hand. "I’m begging you. My balls are still aching, and I’m pretty sure I caught a draft."

“美悦子，行行好，”他伸出手哀求，“我求你了。我那儿还在隐隐作痛，而且我觉得我快感冒了。”

Miyetsuko smirked, leaning against the doorframe, her gaze lingering on his pathetic posture. "I’d love to help, really. But the ledger is updated, Fujiwara. According to the current roster, you’re currently unassigned. My room is at maximum capacity, and Sayuri is currently playing a podcast about 'How to raise a child in a chaotic environment.' You'd just be a distraction."

美悦子冷笑一声，靠在门框上，视线在他那狼狈的姿态上停留。“我很想帮你，真的。但账本已经更新了，藤原。根据当前花名册，你目前处于无人认领状态。我房间已经满员了，小百合也正在听关于‘如何在混乱环境中养育孩子’的播客。你进去只会碍事。”

She gave him a sharp, playful salute. "Sleep tight, space-man. The corridor is a great place to reflect on your lack of manual dexterity."

她对他行了一个尖锐而戏谑的军礼。“晚安，太空人。走廊是反思你那拙劣手部操作的好地方。”

Click.

咔哒。

The door closed again, leaving him in total isolation.

房门再次合上，将他彻底丢进孤立无援的境地。

Fujiwara pulled his legs up to his chest, hugging the pillow. The air conditioner hummed a mocking, rhythmic tune, and the carpet smelled faintly of industrial cleaner and broken dreams. He realized, with a grim clarity，that he had built a life of such rigid, bizarre rules that he had successfully programmed himself into his own punishment.

藤原把腿缩回胸前，抱着枕头。空调发出的嗡嗡声像是在嘲笑他的节奏，地毯上弥漫着工业清洁剂和破碎梦想的味道。他以一种惨烈的清醒意识到，自己亲手构建了一个如此死板又荒诞的生活规则，以至于成功地把自己编程进了这场惩罚之中。

[Learning Point]

Guillotine: 断头台。

Reprieve: 暂缓/缓刑。

Bureaucracy: 官僚主义/教条。

Sovereign state: 主权国家。

Manual dexterity: 手部灵活性（此处讽刺他拍屁股拍错数）。

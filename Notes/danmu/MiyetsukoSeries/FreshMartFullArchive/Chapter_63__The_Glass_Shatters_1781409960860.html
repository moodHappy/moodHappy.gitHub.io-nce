Chapter 63: The Glass Shatters

发布时间：2026/6/14 12:05:59

第六十三章：碎裂的矜持

The silence that followed Eri’s declaration was thick, heavy, and suffocating. But Eri wasn’t finished. She stepped forward, her movements predatory, her sweet mask slipping to reveal a core of sharp, cold steel. She looked Miyetsuko up and down, her gaze lingering on the slight tremor in Miyetsuko’s hands.

绘里的那番话让办公室陷入了窒息般的沉寂。但她还没打算收场，反而迈进一步，姿态掠夺，那副甜美的面具彻底撕下，露出内里坚硬冷酷的锋芒。她从头到脚审视着美悦子，目光在那双微微颤抖的手上停留了片刻。

"You know," Eri continued, her voice lowered to a sugary, venomous hiss that only those standing nearby could hear. "I’ve seen types like you before. You think you’re different because you know the 'real' him. But look at yourself."

“你知道吗，”绘里继续说道，声音压低，甜腻而毒辣，只有近处的人才能听清。“我见过太多你这种类型的人。以为自己很特别，因为你了解‘真实’的他。可看看你自己吧。”

She laughed, a thin, patronizing sound. "You’re acting exactly like one of his discarded toys. A broken, lonely little doll he played with until he got bored, and now you’re clinging to the shadows of his attention, desperate for him to look at you again. Is that what you are? A used-up, broken little doll?"

她笑了一声，那声音听起来既稀薄又傲慢。“你现在的样子，就像是一个被他玩腻了后丢掉的破布娃娃。一个破碎、寂寞的小玩意儿，现在正死死抓着他余光的影子，渴望他再看你一眼。你就是这种人吗？一个被玩坏了的、廉价的洋娃娃？”

The office seemed to stop breathing. Colleagues shifted uncomfortably, some smirking, some looking away.

整个办公室仿佛停止了呼吸。同事们神色各异，有的幸灾乐祸，有的尴尬地别过头去。

Fujiwara remained seated, his eyes fixed on the screen, though he wasn’t typing. He was watching, silent, an observer in his own war.

藤原依旧坐在椅上，目光盯着屏幕，但他并没有在打字。他就那样静默地看着，像是一个在自己的战争中袖手旁观的看客。

Miyetsuko felt the world tilt. The humiliation she had felt moments ago was nothing compared to this—this cruel, systematic stripping away of her dignity. But as Eri’s words hung in the air, something inside Miyetsuko finally snapped. Not into rage, but into a cold, terrifyingly clear stillness.

美悦子感到天旋地转。刚才的屈辱与此刻相比根本不值一提——那是一种残忍且有系统的尊严剥离。但当绘里的话在空气中回荡时，美悦子体内的某根弦彻底崩断了。不是爆发式的愤怒，而是一种冰冷到极致、清晰到恐怖的沉静。

She didn't scream. She didn't cry. She simply walked over to Eri, her movements fluid and devoid of the hesitant, wounded energy she had been projecting before.

她没有尖叫，也没有哭泣。她只是走到绘里跟前，动作如流水般顺畅，再也没有了之前那种伤感而犹疑的状态。

She reached out—not to strike, but to straighten the collar of Eri’s expensive silk dress. Her touch was calm, almost maternal.

她伸出手——不是为了打人，而是轻轻整理了一下绘里那件昂贵丝绸裙子的领口。她的动作平静，甚至透着一种慈母般的温和。

"You’re very confident in your 'value,' Eri," Miyetsuko murmured, her voice steady and chillingly polite. "But you’re forgetting something. A doll is only broken when its owner decides it’s no longer worth the effort to keep. And I..."

“你对你的‘价值’很有自信，绘里，”美悦子低语，声音平稳且冷得刺骨。“但你忘了一件事。只有当主人觉得不再值得费心时，娃娃才会碎。而我……”

She turned her gaze slowly toward Fujiwara, who had finally looked up. Her eyes weren't filled with longing or pain. They were empty.

她缓缓转头，看向终于抬起头的藤原。她的双眼里没有渴望，也没有痛苦。只剩下空洞。

"I was never his doll, Eri. I was the one holding the inventory keys. And since I’m officially 'discarded'..." Miyetsuko smiled, a slow, hollow expression that didn't reach her eyes. "...I think it’s time I conduct a full-scale liquidation of this department."

“我从来不是他的玩偶，绘里。我才是那个掌握库存钥匙的人。既然我被‘丢弃’了……”美悦子露出一抹缓慢的、虚空的微笑，笑意却未达眼底，“……那我觉得是时候对这个部门进行一次全面的‘清算’了。”

[Learning Point]

Patronizing: 傲慢的 / 屈尊俯就的。

Systematic: 有系统的 / 有计划的。

Liquidation: 清算（商业术语，这里指她要彻底整顿部门，让藤原付出代价）。

Fluid: 流畅的 / 自然的。

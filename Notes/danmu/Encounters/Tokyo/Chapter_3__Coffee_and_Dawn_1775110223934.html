Chapter 3: Coffee and Dawn

第三章：咖啡与破晓

The cool night air eventually made us seek refuge in the bright fluorescent glow of a nearby 24-hour convenience store. We bought two cans of hot coffee from the warming rack, letting the heat seep into our chilly fingers.

夜晚凉爽的空气最终让我们在附近一家24小时便利店明亮的荧光灯下寻求庇护。我们从保温架上买了两罐热咖啡，让热度慢慢渗入冰冷的指尖。

We found a quiet bench in a small neighborhood park, the city humming faintly in the distance. Sitting side by side, our shoulders occasionally brushed against each other as we sipped the sweet, milky beverage.

我们在附近的一个小社区公园里找到了一条安静的长椅，城市在远处发出微弱的嗡嗡声。并肩坐着时，我们一边喝着甜甜的牛奶咖啡，肩膀不时地轻轻擦过。

The sky began to change, slowly transitioning from a deep, velvety black to soft hues of indigo and purple. The first rays of morning light touched her face, illuminating the exhaustion and beauty that the night had left behind.

天空开始变化，慢慢从深邃的丝绒黑过渡到柔和的靛蓝和紫色。第一缕晨光抚触着她的脸庞，照亮了这一夜留下的疲惫与美丽。

"I never thought a rainy night in Tokyo could be this unforgettable," I whispered, turning my gaze away from the sunrise and entirely onto her. She leaned her head gently against my shoulder, a silent and perfect conclusion to our chance encounter.

“我从来没想过，东京的一个雨夜可以如此令人难忘，”我轻声说道，将视线从日出移开，完全落在她的身上。她轻轻地将头靠在我的肩膀上，为我们的这场不期而遇画上了一个无言而完美的句号。

Chapter 1: Neon Lights and Rain

第一章：霓虹与雨

The neon lights of Shinjuku reflected on the wet pavement, creating a kaleidoscope of colors beneath my feet. It was a typical rainy evening in Tokyo, crowded yet somehow lonely.

新宿的霓虹灯倒映在潮湿的路面上，在我的脚下变幻出万花筒般的色彩。这是东京一个典型的雨夜，拥挤却又莫名的孤独。

I ducked into a small, dimly lit izakaya to escape the sudden downpour, shaking the water from my umbrella. The air inside was warm, smelling of grilled yakitori and sake.

为了躲避突如其来的倾盆大雨，我钻进了一家昏暗的小居酒屋，抖落伞上的雨水。里面的空气很温暖，散发着烤串和清酒的香气。

That was when I saw her, sitting alone at the corner of the wooden counter, nursing a glass of plum wine. Her eyes met mine, holding a gentle curiosity that made my heart skip a beat.

就在那时我看到了她，独自坐在木制吧台的角落里，品着一杯梅酒。她的目光与我交汇，带着一种温柔的好奇，让我的心跳漏了半拍。

"Is this seat taken?" I asked hesitantly, pointing to the empty stool beside her. She smiled, a subtle curve of her lips that rivaled the city's glittering skyline, and softly replied, "It is now."

“这个位子有人吗？”我犹豫地问，指着她旁边空着的凳子。她笑了，唇角微微扬起的弧度足以媲美这座城市闪耀的天际线，她轻柔地回答：“现在有了。”

The rain outside continued to fall, blurring the bustling streets, but inside that tiny tavern, time seemed to stand still. We spent the night talking about everything and nothing, entirely lost in the serendipity of our encounter.

外面的雨还在下，模糊了熙熙攘攘的街道，但在这家小酒馆里，时间似乎静止了。我们聊了一整夜，天南海北，完全沉醉在这场不期而遇的缘分中。

Chapter 1: The Unknown Jungle

第一章：未知的丛林

The air was thick with humidity as the expedition team stepped into the dense rainforest. Every rustle of the leaves felt like a hidden warning from nature, reminding them of how small they were.

当探险队步入茂密的热带雨林时，空气中弥漫着浓重的湿气。树叶的每一次沙沙作响都像是大自然隐藏的警告，提醒着他们自身的渺小。

Captain Reynolds checked his compass, only to find the needle spinning wildly. They were completely disconnected from the outside world, forced to rely solely on an ancient map and their own instincts.

雷诺兹队长查看了他的指南针，却发现指针在疯狂旋转。他们与外界完全失去了联系，被迫只能依靠一张古老的地图和他们自己的直觉。

Suddenly, a massive stone structure emerged through the thick vines ahead. It was the lost temple they had been searching for, standing silent and untouched by time for centuries.

突然，前方茂密的藤蔓中浮现出一座巨大的石制建筑。那就是他们一直在寻找的失落神庙，几个世纪以来它静静地矗立在那里，未受时间的侵扰。

As they approached the entrance, a low, rumbling sound echoed from the depths of the ruins. The true adventure was just beginning, and there was no turning back now.

当他们靠近入口时，废墟深处回荡起一阵低沉的隆隆声。真正的探险才刚刚开始，而现在已经没有回头路可走了。

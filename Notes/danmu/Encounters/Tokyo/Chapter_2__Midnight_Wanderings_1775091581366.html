第二章：午夜漫步

We left the izakaya long after midnight, the rain having faded into a gentle, cool mist. The streets of Tokyo had quieted down, offering a rare moment of tranquility in the restless metropolis.

我们在午夜过后才离开居酒屋，雨已经渐渐变成了温柔凉爽的薄雾。东京的街道安静了下来，在这个喧嚣的大都市里难得地呈现出片刻的宁静。

She suggested we walk towards Kabukicho, not for the nightlife, but to see the glowing signs contrast against the dark, cloudy sky. Walking beside her, the distance between us felt both electric and comforting.

她提议我们朝歌舞伎町走去，不是为了体验夜生活，而是为了看发光的招牌与漆黑多云的天空形成的鲜明对比。走在她身旁，我们之间的距离既让人感到触电般的悸动，又令人安心。

As we passed a closed storefront, a stray cat darted across our path, startling her into instinctively grabbing my arm. Her touch was light, yet it sent a sudden warmth rushing through my veins.

当我们经过一家关门的店铺时，一只流浪猫从我们面前窜过，惊得她本能地一把抓住了我的手臂。她的触碰很轻，却让一股暖流瞬间涌遍我的全身。

"Sorry," she laughed softly, releasing her grip, though her hand lingered close to mine. I simply smiled back, looking into her deep eyes, secretly wishing this silent walk could stretch on forever until the break of dawn.

“抱歉，”她轻笑着松开了手，但她的手依然停留在离我很近的地方。我只是回以微笑，注视着她深邃的双眼，在心底暗自期盼这段静谧的漫步能一直延续到破晓。

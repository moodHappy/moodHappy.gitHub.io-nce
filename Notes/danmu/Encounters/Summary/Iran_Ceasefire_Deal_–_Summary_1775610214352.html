Iran Ceasefire Deal – Summary

🔗 阅读原文

伊朗停火协议——简化概要

---

This article reports that the US and Iran have agreed to a two‑week ceasefire, which President Trump announced shortly before a deadline for potential US military strikes.

本文报道美国与伊朗达成两周停火协议，特朗普总统在可能发动军事打击的最后期限前不久宣布了这一消息。

---

The ceasefire requires Iran to halt hostilities and reopen the Strait of Hormuz to commercial shipping, though many uncertainties remain about Iran’s nuclear materials and regional influence.

停火要求伊朗停止敌对行动并重新开放霍尔木兹海峡供商业航运通行，但伊朗的核材料去向及其地区影响力仍存在诸多不确定性。

---

Trump’s earlier threat that Iran’s “civilisation will die” drew strong criticism from Democrats and some Republicans, who argued the remarks were dangerous and unfit for a US president.

特朗普此前威胁称伊朗“文明将灭亡”，此言论引发民主党和部分共和党人的强烈批评，他们认为此类言辞危险且不符合美国总统的身份。

---

Some Republican lawmakers defended Trump, but others warned that carrying out large‑scale strikes would be a serious mistake and inconsistent with American values.

部分共和党议员为特朗普辩护，但也有人警告称实施大规模打击将是严重错误，与美国价值观不符。

---

Iran claims the US accepted parts of its 10‑point plan, including lifting sanctions and withdrawing US forces, though it is unclear whether Trump will agree to such terms.

伊朗声称美国接受了其“十点计划”的部分内容，包括解除制裁和撤军，但目前尚不清楚特朗普是否会真正同意这些条件。

---

The ceasefire is seen as a short‑term political win for Trump, but the long‑term consequences of his rhetoric and the conflict remain uncertain.

此次停火被视为特朗普的短期政治胜利，但其言辞及战争的长期影响仍未可知。

South Korea's runaway wolf finally captured after nine-day search

🔗 阅读原文

韩国出逃的狼在搜捕九天后终被捕获

Neukgu, the two-year-old wolf who broke loose from a South Korean zoo and captured the nation's attention, has finally been captured following a nine-day long search.

纽古（Neukgu）是一只两岁的狼，它从韩国一家动物园逃脱并引起了全国的关注，在经过长达九天的搜捕后，终于被安全捕获。

Neukgu was captured near an expressway on Friday at 00:44 local time (16:44 GMT), according to the government of Daejeon, the central city where his zoo was located. Upon a medical examination, Neukgu's pulse and body temperature were normal, authorities said.

据其所在动物园位于的中部城市大田市政府称，纽古于当地时间周五00:44（格林尼治标准时间16:44）在一条高速公路附近被捕获。当局表示，经体检，纽古的脉搏和体温均正常。

Hundreds of rescue officials had been deployed to find the wolf, and there were a couple of times when authorities came close to getting him - but each time the net seemed to be closing in, Neukgu would slink away.

搜救部门出动了数百名官员来寻找这只狼，当局曾有几次差点将其抓获——但每次眼看包围圈就要缩小收紧时，纽古都会溜走。

Authorities had nearly caught him earlier this week, when the local fire department received a report on Monday night that Neukgu had been spotted on a mountain about 2km (1.2 miles) away from O-World, the zoo and theme park from which he had broken loose. A video of the wolf scampering on a road in the dark, illuminated by the headlights of a vehicle, was also uploaded on social media. Authorities pounced on the lead, launching a search-and-rescue mission, but ultimately he escaped again.

本周早些时候，当局差点就抓住了它。周一晚上，当地消防部门接到报告称，有人在距离它出逃的动物园兼主题公园“O-World”约2公里（1.2英里）的一座山上发现了纽古。一段这只狼在黑暗中被汽车前灯照亮、在道路上奔跑的视频也被上传到了社交媒体上。当局抓住这一线索，发起了一场搜救行动，但最终它再次逃脱了。

The elusive Neukgu has inspired a meme coin, hailed by its creators as a the "wolf that wouldn't stay caged" and a "symbol of independence".

行踪不定的纽古还启发人们发行了一种模因币（meme coin），其创造者称赞它是“不甘被关在笼子里的狼”和“独立的象征”。

Born in 2024, Neukgu is part of a programme at O-World to restore the Korean wolf, which once roamed the Korean Peninsula but is now considered extinct in the wild. But after a life in captivity, some worried that he wouldn't be able to survive in the wild. Animal rights groups were also concerned that Neukgu would be killed during the capture process - something that happened to Porongi, a puma that had escaped from the same zoo in 2018. Even South Korea's president Lee Jae Myung publicly prayed for Neukgu's safe return home, in a post on social media.

纽古出生于2024年，是O-World旨在恢复韩国狼种群计划的一部分。韩国狼曾经漫游在朝鲜半岛，但现在被认为在野外已经灭绝。但是在过惯了圈养生活后，一些人担心它无法在野外生存。动物权利组织也担心纽古会在捕获过程中被击毙——2018年，从同一家动物园逃脱的美洲狮“波龙吉”（Porongi）就遭遇了这样的命运。甚至连韩国总统李在明（Lee Jae Myung）也在社交媒体上发帖，公开祈祷纽古平安回家。

Those fears have been put to rest now that Neukgu has been safely caught.

现在纽古已被安全捕获，这些担忧也随之烟消云散。

Authorities launched another search operation in the Anyeong-dong area on Thursday evening after receiving a tip-off. It was there that they finally caught him, shooting him in the thigh with a tranquilliser gun from 20m (66ft) away before whisking him back to the zoo. He was "moving very fast", authorities said, adding that they then monitored him via a drone. It took about six minutes before he entered a "stable" condition, they said.

星期四晚上，当局在接到举报后，在安永洞（Anyeong-dong）地区发起了另一次搜寻行动。正是在那里，他们终于抓住了它，在20米（66英尺）外用镇静枪射中它的大腿，然后迅速将其带回动物园。当局表示，它当时“移动速度非常快”，并补充说他们随后通过无人机对其进行了监控。他们说，大约六分钟后它才进入“稳定”状态。

A video of the capture operation, posted by the Daejeon city government, showed a limp Neukgu being hauled by rescuers and placed in a carrier. Authorities later posted photos of a sedated Neukgu being attended to in a medical facility.

大田市政府发布的一段抓捕行动视频显示，浑身瘫软的纽古被救援人员拖拽着放入了一个航空箱。当局随后发布了打了镇静剂的纽古在医疗设施中接受护理的照片。

Authorities said Neukgu "hadn't lost much weight" and "looked like he had eaten". They also found a fishing hook inside his stomach, which was removed through endoscopic surgery.

当局表示，纽古“没有掉太多体重”，而且“看起来像是吃过东西”。他们还在它的胃里发现了一个鱼钩，随后通过内窥镜手术将其取出。

"Thank you to everyone who worked hard to bring Neukgu home," the city government wrote on social media. "To everyone who worried about Neukgu's safety and cheered us on, thank you all so much."

市政府在社交媒体上写道：“感谢每一位努力带纽古回家的人。对于所有担心纽古安全并为我们加油的人，我们表示由衷的感谢。”

The Heist's Pulse

窃案的脉搏

The illicit rig sputtered in the dim alleyway, bleeding raw neon light onto the damp, cracked pavement.

那台非法的装置在昏暗的小巷里发出阵阵杂音，将刺眼的霓虹光晕渗入潮湿开裂的路面。

Kael gritted his teeth, fighting to rein in the chaotic energy spikes as the countdown timer ticked closer to zero.

随着倒计时一点点逼近归零，凯尔咬紧牙关，拼命想要控制住那狂乱飙升的能量峰值。

"It's overloading. I need to sever the link," he grunted, his hands slick with sweat as his cutters hovered over a thick, copper-threaded cord.

“超载了。我得切断连接，”他闷声说道，满是汗水的双手握着金属剪，悬停在一根粗壮的铜芯线缆上方。

A heavy, breathless silence fell over the crew, broken only when his partner gave him a firm, reassuring pat on the shoulder. "Make the cut."

团队中陷入了令人窒息的死寂，直到他的搭档在他肩头给了一个坚定而安抚的拍击。“剪吧。”

He clamped down. The screeching alarm died instantly, and the heavy vault doors hummed open into the dark.

他用力剪下。刺耳的警报声瞬间毙命，沉重的金库大门在黑暗中嗡鸣着缓缓敞开。

Chapter 1: The Clever Crow

第一章：聪明的乌鸦

On the edge of a quiet village, there lived a clever crow named Ash. Unlike other crows, Ash loved solving problems and observing the world around him.

在一个宁静的村庄边缘，住着一只聪明的乌鸦，名叫阿什。与其他乌鸦不同，阿什喜欢解决问题，并观察周围的世界。

While other crows spent their days searching for food, Ash often sat on a high branch, watching humans, animals, and everything that moved. He believed that thinking was just as important as flying.

当其他乌鸦整天忙于觅食时，阿什常常停在高高的树枝上，观察人类、动物以及一切活动的事物。他认为，思考和飞行同样重要。

One summer, a terrible drought struck the land. Rivers dried up, and even the smallest puddles disappeared. All the animals began to suffer from thirst.

有一年夏天，一场严重的干旱袭击了这片土地。河流干涸，连最小的水洼也消失了。所有动物都开始饱受口渴之苦。

One day, Ash found a tall clay jar with a little water at the bottom. He tried to reach it with his beak, but the jar was too deep.

一天，阿什发现了一个高高的陶罐，底部还有一点水。他试图用喙去够，但罐子太深了。

Instead of giving up, Ash stopped and thought carefully. Then he picked up a small pebble and dropped it into the jar. The water rose just a little.

阿什没有放弃，而是停下来仔细思考。随后，他叼起一颗小石子丢进罐子里，水面微微上升了一点。

Encouraged, he continued to drop more pebbles into the jar, one by one. Slowly, the water rose higher and higher.

受到鼓舞，他继续一颗一颗地把石子丢进罐子里。水面慢慢地越来越高。

At last, the water reached the top. Ash bent down and drank deeply, finally quenching his thirst.

最终，水面升到了罐口。阿什低下头，畅快地喝水，终于解了渴。

From that day on, the other animals admired Ash not just for his survival, but for his intelligence.

从那天起，其他动物不仅因为他活了下来而敬佩他，更因为他的智慧而赞叹。

And Ash continued to fly across the skies, always ready to face the next challenge with a sharp mind.

而阿什继续在天空中飞翔，时刻准备以敏锐的头脑迎接下一个挑战。

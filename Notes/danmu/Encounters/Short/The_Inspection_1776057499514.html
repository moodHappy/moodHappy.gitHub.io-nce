The Inspection

检查

Inspector Li arrived at Sunflower Kindergarten at exactly 9:00 AM. His clipboard was heavily laden with the Municipal Education Bureau's 400-point safety checklist.

李巡视员在早上九点整准时抵达了向日葵幼儿园。他的文件夹里沉甸甸地夹着市教育局那份包含400个检查项目的安全清单。

The security gate wasn't made of iron, but of intertwined, mildly venomous briars that only parted for those holding a child's hand or a valid employee ID.

大门不是铁做的，而是由互相缠绕、带有轻微毒性的荆棘组成，它们只为牵着孩子的手或持有有效员工证件的人让路。

"Biometric access system," Li muttered, ticking a box. "Ensure the thorns are pruned to eye-level to prevent accidental punctures."

“生物识别门禁系统。”李巡视员嘟囔着，在表格上打了个勾。“确保将荆棘修剪到视线高度以上，以防意外扎伤。”

Principal Wang smiled warmly, handing him a visitor's badge that lightly pulsed with a heartbeat. "Of course, Inspector. Shall we start with fire safety?"

王园长温和地笑了笑，递给他一枚微微跳动着心跳的访客胸牌。“当然，巡视员。我们从消防安全开始好吗？”

In the main hallway, Li inspected the fire suppression equipment. Instead of red steel cylinders, heavy glass jars were bolted to the walls. Inside each jar, a miniature, furious rainstorm swirled, complete with dark cumulonimbus clouds and tiny lightning strikes.

在主走廊里，李巡视员检查了灭火设备。墙上用螺栓固定的不是红色的钢瓶，而是厚重的玻璃罐。每个罐子里都盘旋着一场微型而狂暴的暴风雨，里面甚至还有乌云和微弱的闪电。

"Are they properly pressurized?" Li asked, tapping the glass. A microscopic bolt of lightning flashed against his fingernail.

“内部压力正常吗？”李巡视员敲了敲玻璃问道。一道极其微小的闪电隔着玻璃劈中了他的指甲。

"Regularly fed with ocean mist and low-pressure systems every month," Wang assured him.

“每个月都会定期喂食海雾和低压系统，”王园长保证道。

Li wrote down: Equipment maintained. Precipitation levels adequate. 李巡视员写下：设备维护良好。降水量达标。

Out on the playground, a group of toddlers was taking turns sliding down the long, scaled back of a slumbering river dragon. The beast let out a soft, soapy burp, sending iridescent bubbles floating into the sky.

在操场上，一群蹒跚学步的孩子正轮流从一条沉睡的河龙长满鳞片的背上滑下来。这只巨兽打了一个柔和的、满是肥皂泡的嗝，五颜六色的泡泡飘向了天空。

"Structural integrity of playground equipment?" Li raised an eyebrow, watching the dragon's ribs expand and contract with its breathing.

“游乐设施的结构完整性如何？”李巡视员挑了挑眉毛，看着龙的肋骨随着呼吸而起伏。

"We feed him calcium supplements on Tuesdays and sing him lullabies to ensure he stays dormant during recess," Wang replied.

王园长回答：“我们每周二喂它吃钙片，并给它唱摇篮曲，以确保它在课间休息时保持休眠状态。”

Li squatted down to inspect the ground beneath the dragon. "The landing zone lacks sufficient shock absorption. You need at least three inches of woodchips or crystallized dream-sand here. A dragon's tail can be quite rigid."

李巡视员蹲下来检查龙身下方的地面。“缓冲区的减震效果不足。这里至少需要铺设三英寸厚的木屑或结晶梦境沙。龙的尾巴可是很硬的。”

"Noted," Wang said, jotting it down in her glowing notebook.

“记下了，”王园长说着，记在了她那本发光的笔记本上。

Moving to the cafeteria, the chef was busy kneading morning fog into dough. The lunch menu chalked on the board read: Steamed sunlight with a side of Friday afternoon nostalgia.

来到食堂，厨师正忙着把晨雾揉进面团里。黑板上的午餐菜单写着：清蒸阳光配星期五下午的乡愁。

"No allergens, I hope?" Li sniffed the air, suddenly smelling the distinct scent of his own childhood summer bicycle tires. He blinked away a sudden tear. "Nostalgia can be a choking hazard for the overly sensitive."

“希望没有过敏原？”李巡视员抽了抽鼻子，突然闻到了他自己童年夏天自行车轮胎的独特气味。他眨了眨眼，逼退了突如其来的泪水。“对于过于敏感的孩子来说，乡愁可能会带来窒息风险。”

"It's diluted to a safe, purely conceptual level," Wang promised. "We also have a gluten-free void option for the picky eaters."

“已经稀释到了安全的、纯粹概念的水平，”王园长承诺道。“我们还为挑食的孩子提供了无麸质的虚空套餐。”

Finally, they reached the emergency exits. Wang pushed open the heavy back doors. Instead of the alleyway, it opened directly into a tranquil, eternal autumn forest where the leaves hung suspended in mid-air and time moved at exactly half-speed.

最后，他们来到了紧急出口。王园长推开沉重的后门。门外不是后巷，而是直接通向一片宁静的、永恒的秋日森林，树叶悬浮在半空中，时间流逝的速度精确地只有现实的一半。

"Excellent for rapid evacuation," Li noted, looking into the endless golden woods. "Just ensure the temporal boundary doesn't leak into the nap room. We had an incident last year in District 4 where a class of preschoolers woke up as retired accountants."

“非常有利于快速疏散，”李巡视员看着无尽的金黄色树林记录道。“只要确保时间边界不要泄漏到午休室就行。去年四区发生过一起事故，一个班的学龄前儿童醒来时变成了退休的会计师。”

Li signed the bottom of his glowing parchment and handed Wang her copy. "Passed. But please get that dream-sand for the dragon slide by next week."

李巡视员在他发光的羊皮纸底部签了名，把副本递给王园长。“合格了。但请务必在下周前把龙滑梯下面的梦境沙铺好。”

"Thank you, Inspector," Wang said, as the security briars uncoiled to let him out into the completely ordinary, concrete street.

“谢谢您，巡视员，”王园长说道，同时安保荆棘解开缠绕，让他走到了外面完全普通的、混凝土铺就的街道上。

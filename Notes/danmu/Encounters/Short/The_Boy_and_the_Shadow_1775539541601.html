The Boy and the Shadow

男孩与影子

A curious boy once noticed that his shadow always followed him, no matter where he went.

一个好奇的男孩发现，无论他走到哪里，他的影子总是跟着他。

One day, he decided he would try to outrun it. He ran across fields, through streets, and over hills, but the shadow stayed close behind.

有一天，他决定要甩掉影子。他跑过田野，穿过街道，翻越山丘，但影子始终紧紧跟着他。

Tired and frustrated, the boy shouted, “Why won’t you leave me alone?”

男孩又累又沮丧，大声喊道：“你为什么不肯离开我？”

An old man nearby smiled and said, “Your shadow is part of you. The more you fight it, the closer it stays.”

附近的一位老人微笑着说：“影子是你的一部分。你越是抗拒，它就越紧跟着你。”

The boy stopped running and stood still. As he calmed down, he noticed the shadow quietly resting beside him.

男孩停下脚步，站在原地。当他平静下来时，他发现影子也安静地待在他身旁。

From that day on, he no longer tried to escape it. Instead, he learned to walk with it peacefully.

从那天起，他不再试图逃避影子，而是学会与它平静地同行。

Moral: What you cannot escape, you must learn to accept.

寓意：无法逃避的事物，唯有学会接受。

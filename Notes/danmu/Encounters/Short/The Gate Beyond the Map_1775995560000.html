Chapter 1: The Map That Should Not Exist

第一章：不该存在的地图

The rain fell in thin, silver lines as Elias pressed his back against the cold stone wall, clutching the map tighter than he should.

雨水如细银般落下，伊莱亚斯背靠着冰冷的石墙，比该有的更用力地攥紧那张地图。

He had stolen it only hours ago, yet it already felt like a burden older than time itself.

他不过在几个小时前才偷到它，但它却仿佛承载着比时间更古老的重量。

“Just a map,” he whispered to himself, though his voice betrayed him.

“只是一张地图而已。”他低声自语，但声音却出卖了他。

The ink on the parchment shimmered faintly, as if alive, shifting whenever he dared to look directly at it.

羊皮纸上的墨迹微微闪烁，仿佛有生命一般，每当他直视时就悄然变换。

Somewhere in the distance, a bell tolled.

远处，一声钟鸣响起。

Elias froze.

伊莱亚斯僵住了。

Not because of the bell—no, bells rang every night in this cursed city—but because the map had just changed again.

不是因为钟声——在这座被诅咒的城市里，钟声夜夜都会响——而是因为地图刚刚又变了。

A path had appeared where there had been none before.

原本不存在的一条道路，凭空出现了。

And at the end of that path, a single word glowed:

而在那条路的尽头，一个词正发着光：

“Gate.”

“门。”

Chapter 2: The Living City

第二章：活着的城市

Elias didn’t believe in fate.

伊莱亚斯从不相信命运。

But as he followed the path the map revealed, weaving through alleys that seemed to twist away from memory, doubt began to creep in.

但当他顺着地图指引的路径前行，穿梭于那些仿佛会从记忆中扭曲消失的小巷时，怀疑开始悄然滋生。

He had walked these streets his whole life.

他一生都走在这些街道上。

Yet tonight, they were different.

但今晚，它们变了。

Doors appeared where there had been walls.

原本是墙的地方，出现了门。

Windows blinked open and shut like watchful eyes.

窗户像警觉的眼睛一样忽开忽闭。

And the shadows—

而那些影子——

The shadows followed him.

那些影子在跟着他。

“Alright,” Elias muttered, gripping a dagger at his side. “This is new.”

“好吧。”伊莱亚斯低声说，握紧腰侧的匕首，“这倒是新鲜。”

The map pulsed warmly in his hand, guiding him forward like a heartbeat.

地图在他手中温热地脉动，像心跳一样引导着他前进。

Finally, he reached a narrow staircase spiraling downward into darkness.

最终，他来到一段向下延伸的狭窄螺旋楼梯，通往黑暗深处。

The word on the map shifted again.

地图上的字再次改变。

“Enter.”

“进入。”

Chapter 3: The Gate

第三章：那扇门

The air grew colder with each step.

每走一步，空气就更冷一分。

By the time Elias reached the bottom, his breath had turned to mist.

当伊莱亚斯走到底部时，他的呼吸已经化作白雾。

Before him stood a door unlike any he had ever seen.

在他面前，是一扇他从未见过的门。

It wasn’t made of wood or iron.

它既不是木制的，也不是铁制的。

It looked… woven.

它看起来……像是编织而成的。

Threads of light and darkness twisted together, forming a surface that seemed both solid and endlessly shifting.

光与暗的丝线交织缠绕，形成了一种既坚固又不断变化的表面。

At its center was no handle.

门的中央没有把手。

Only a mark—

只有一个印记——

The same symbol that had begun to appear on the map.

与地图上逐渐浮现的那个符号一模一样。

Elias hesitated.

伊莱亚斯犹豫了。

“Just a door,” he said, though this time, he didn’t believe himself.

“只是一扇门。”他说，但这一次，他自己都不相信。

The map burned suddenly in his hand.

地图突然在他手中灼烧起来。

The symbol on the door flared to life.

门上的符号骤然亮起。

And before he could step back—

还没等他后退——

The door opened.

门开了。

Chapter 4: Beyond

第四章：彼岸

Light swallowed him whole.

光将他彻底吞没。

When Elias opened his eyes again, the city was gone.

当伊莱亚斯再次睁开眼睛时，城市已经消失。

In its place stretched an endless landscape of floating ruins and silent skies.

取而代之的是一片无尽的景象：漂浮的废墟与寂静的天空。

Fragments of towers drifted like forgotten thoughts.

破碎的塔楼像被遗忘的念头般漂浮着。

Rivers flowed upward into the clouds.

河流逆流而上，汇入云端。

And far in the distance—

而在遥远的地方——

Something moved.

有什么在移动。

Something vast.

某种庞然之物。

The map slipped from his hand, now blank.

地图从他手中滑落，如今已变得一片空白。

Elias took a slow breath.

伊莱亚斯缓缓吸了一口气。

“So,” he said, a faint smile forming despite the fear,

“所以，”他开口，尽管恐惧仍在，却浮现出一丝微笑，

“This is where the real story begins.”

“真正的故事，从这里才开始。”

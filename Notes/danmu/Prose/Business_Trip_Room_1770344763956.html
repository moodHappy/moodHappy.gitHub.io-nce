Business Trip Room

出差同住一室

The train arrived at the city just before sunset.

火车在日落前到达这座城市。

She walked ahead of me with a small black suitcase.

她拖着一个黑色小行李箱走在我前面。

I had imagined this trip many times, but the street looked ordinary.

我想象过这次出差很多次，但这条街看起来很普通。

The hotel stood beside a convenience store and a closed bakery.

酒店旁边是一家便利店和一间关门的面包店。

She checked the booking on her phone at the front desk.

她在前台用手机确认预订信息。

The clerk said only one room was left because of a conference.

前台说因为有会议只剩下一间房。

She paused for two seconds and then nodded.

她停顿了两秒，然后点头。

I kept my eyes on the floor tiles and said nothing.

我盯着地砖，没有说话。

The room had two narrow beds pushed close together.

房间里有两张靠得很近的窄床。

A single window faced the parking lot behind the building.

唯一的窗户对着楼后的停车场。

She put her suitcase near the wall and took off her coat.

她把行李箱放在墙边，脱下外套。

I placed my bag beside the desk and waited for instructions.

我把包放在书桌旁，等她开口安排。

She said we should review the client files before dinner.

她说晚饭前先把客户资料再看一遍。

We sat at the small table with our shoulders almost touching.

我们坐在小桌旁，肩膀几乎碰在一起。

Her voice stayed calm while she explained the schedule.

她用平静的语气说明第二天的安排。

I wrote notes slowly to hide my nervous hands.

我写得很慢，好让发抖的手不被看见。

When she leaned over to point at a line, her hair brushed my sleeve.

她俯身指文件时，头发擦过我的袖子。

Neither of us mentioned it, and she continued speaking.

我们都没有提起，她继续往下说。

After the meeting plan was finished, she closed the folder.

计划讲完后，她把文件夹合上。

She asked if I wanted noodles or rice for dinner.

她问我晚饭想吃面还是米饭。

We bought two simple meals from the shop downstairs.

我们在楼下买了两份很普通的便当。

Back in the room, the television played low news sounds.

回到房间，电视里放着很小声的新闻。

She sat on her bed to answer work messages.

她坐在床上回复工作消息。

I stood by the window and watched cars leave the parking lot.

我站在窗边看车一辆一辆离开停车场。

The room grew quiet after ten o’clock.

十点以后房间慢慢安静下来。

She turned off the main light and kept the desk lamp on.

她关掉顶灯，只留下一盏台灯。

Soft yellow light covered half of her face.

柔黄的灯光落在她半边脸上。

She said we needed to wake up at six.

她说明天六点要起床。

I said I understood and lay down on my bed.

我说知道了，然后躺到自己的床上。

The distance between the two beds was less than one step.

两张床之间不到一步的距离。

I could hear her breathing after the lamp was turned off.

台灯关掉后，我能听见她的呼吸声。

No one spoke again before sleep came.

入睡前我们再没有说话。

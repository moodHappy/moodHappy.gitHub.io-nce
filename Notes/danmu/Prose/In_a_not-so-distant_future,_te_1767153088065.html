In a not-so-distant future, technology had advanced to the point where virtual reality had become indistinguishable from reality itself. People wore augmented reality contact lenses, known as "The Upgrade", to enhance their daily lives. With a mere thought, they could access information, communicate with others, or even alter their surroundings.

But as the years passed, the lines between the physical and virtual worlds began to blur. People became trapped in their own personalized realities, oblivious to the world around them. A young woman, struggling to cope with the loss of her loved one, found solace in a virtual reality simulation that allowed her to relive their memories. However, as she became increasingly dependent on the simulation, she began to lose touch with reality, and her digital existence started to consume her.

As she gazed into the virtual void, the boundaries between her digital and real self began to disintegrate, leaving behind a haunting question: what is real, and what is just an illusion?

Chapter 45: The Judas Call

第四十五章：犹大的电话

Morning in the jungle. The mist was lifting, revealing the rotting concrete of the bunker.

丛林的清晨。雾气散去，露出了地堡腐烂的混凝土。

Miyetsuko sat on the hood of the Land Rover, holding the satellite phone. She cleared her throat, adjusting her pitch. She needed to sound desperate. Broken.

美悦子坐在路虎的引擎盖上，拿着卫星电话。她清了清嗓子，调整音调。她需要听起来绝望。破碎。

She dialed The Butcher's private number.

她拨通了屠夫的私人号码。

"Hola, puta (bitch)," The Butcher's voice answered immediately. He was expecting this call.

“你好啊，婊子，”屠夫的声音立刻响起。他在等这个电话。

"I surrender," Miyetsuko whispered, letting a fake sob catch in her throat. "We are... hurt. Chen is dying. I can't run anymore."

“我投降，”美悦子低语，喉咙里卡着一声虚假的啜泣。“我们……受伤了。陈快死了。我跑不动了。”

"Good," The Butcher laughed. "Bring me the codes. And bring yourself."

“很好，”屠夫大笑。“带上代码。还有带上你自己。”

"I'm at the Old Jesuit Ruins. North of the favela," she paused, making her voice tremble. "Please... just kill us quickly. Don't let your men... touch me."

“我在旧耶稣会遗址。贫民窟北边，”她停顿了一下，让声音发抖。“求你……给我们个痛快。别让你的人……碰我。”

"We will see," The Butcher hung up.

“到时候再说，”屠夫挂断了电话。

Miyetsuko lowered the phone. The tears vanished instantly. Her eyes were dry and cold as stones.

美悦子放下电话。眼泪瞬间消失。她的眼睛干涩、冷酷如石。

"He bought it," she looked at Chen Mo, who was wiring a block of C4 to a propane tank. "Greed makes men deaf."

“他信了，”她看着正在把C4炸药接在丙烷罐上的陈默。“贪婪让男人变聋。”

"He's coming with everything," Chen Mo checked the detonator. "Heavy armor. Maybe 20 men."

“他会倾巢而出，”陈默检查引爆器。“重型装甲。大概20个人。”

"Then we need a bigger fire," Miyetsuko hopped off the car. She walked over to Chen Mo, her bare feet silent on the moss. She ran a hand down his sweaty back. "Make it loud, Chen. I want to see them burn."

“那就需要更大的火，”美悦子跳下车。她走到陈默身边，赤脚踩在苔藓上悄无声息。她的手滑过他满是汗水的背脊。“弄得响一点，陈。我想看他们燃烧。”

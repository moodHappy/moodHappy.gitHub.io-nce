Tributes After British Teenager Dies in Vietnam

🔗 阅读原文

英国少女在越南身亡后的悼念

---

Paragraph 1

A 19‑year‑old British woman died in Vietnam after reportedly falling from a motorcycle while travelling the Ha Giang Loop in the country’s northern mountains.

一名 19 岁的英国女孩在越南旅行时不幸身亡，据报道她在北部山区的“河江环线”旅途中从摩托车后座跌落。

---

Paragraph 2

She was taken to a major hospital in Hanoi, where she passed away on 2 April with her parents by her side.

她被送往河内的一家大型医院，并于 4 月 2 日在父母陪伴下去世。

---

Paragraph 3

Her family decided to donate her organs, which doctors say have already helped several critically ill Vietnamese patients.

她的家人决定捐献她的器官，医生表示这些器官已经帮助了多名危重的越南患者。

---

Paragraph 4

Her mother described her as independent, witty, and full of life, saying organ donation was something she believed her daughter would have wanted.

她的母亲形容她独立、机智、热爱生活，并表示器官捐献是女儿生前也会希望做的事。

---

Paragraph 5

Her father said she loved Vietnam deeply, and giving back to the country meant a great deal to the family.

她的父亲表示女儿非常热爱越南，因此能够回馈这个国家对家人意义重大。

---

Paragraph 6

The Ha Giang Loop is a popular but challenging mountain route, and Vietnam continues to face high rates of road accidents each year.

“河江环线”是热门但具有挑战性的山路路线，而越南每年仍面临高发的交通事故问题。

Clock ticks on Trump's Iran ultimatum — Summary

🔗 阅读原文

特朗普对伊朗最后通牒的倒计时 —— 要点概要

---

1. Ultimatum and Threats

The US president has set a strict deadline for Iran, warning of massive strikes if no deal is reached.

美国总统为伊朗设定了严格的最后期限，并警告若无法达成协议将发动大规模打击。

He demands that Iran allow free oil traffic through the Strait of Hormuz as part of any agreement.

他要求伊朗在协议中允许霍尔木兹海峡的石油自由通行。

Trump says the planned strikes would devastate Iran’s infrastructure within hours.

特朗普表示，若开战，美军将在数小时内重创伊朗基础设施。

---

2. Iran’s Response and Stalemate

Iran has rejected a temporary ceasefire and issued its own demands, which US officials call “maximalist.”

伊朗拒绝临时停火，并提出自己的要求，美国官员称其为“最大化诉求”。

There is little sign that Iran will accept the ultimatum as the deadline approaches.

随着最后期限临近，几乎没有迹象显示伊朗会接受这一通牒。

---

3. Risks to US Credibility

If Trump delays the deadline again, it could weaken his credibility after repeated threats.

如果特朗普再次延后期限，可能削弱其在多次威胁后的可信度。

Backing down may signal that the US is not negotiating from a position of true strength.

退缩可能让外界认为美国并非真正处于强势地位。

---

4. Limits of US Military Power

Despite recent successful operations, the US acknowledges that Iran can still disrupt the Strait of Hormuz with low‑cost tactics.

尽管近期行动成功，美国也承认伊朗仍能以低成本手段扰乱霍尔木兹海峡。

Trump notes that even overwhelming US firepower cannot fully eliminate asymmetric threats.

特朗普指出，即便美军火力强大，也无法完全消除这种不对称威胁。

---

5. Humanitarian and Strategic Concerns

Trump says he does not want to destroy Iran’s infrastructure because rebuilding would take decades or longer.

特朗普表示不愿摧毁伊朗基础设施，因为重建可能需要数十年甚至更久。

A major strike could trigger a humanitarian crisis and severe regional retaliation.

大规模打击可能引发人道主义危机，并导致严重的地区报复。

---

6. Possibility of a Last‑Minute Deal

Trump hints that negotiations may be happening behind the scenes but gives no details.

特朗普暗示幕后可能正在谈判，但未透露细节。

He says he believes Iran is negotiating in good faith, though the outcome remains uncertain.

他表示相信伊朗在“真诚谈判”，但结果仍不确定。
